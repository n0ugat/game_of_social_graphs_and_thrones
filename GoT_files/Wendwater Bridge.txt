'''Wendwater Bridge'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a crossing of the [[Wendwater]] river in the [[Crownlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Wendwater Bridge was the site of the Battle of Wendwater Bridge during the [[Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion]].
