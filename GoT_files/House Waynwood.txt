'''House Waynwood'''{{Ref|GOT408}}{{Note|Petyr Baelish refers to Waynwood as a great house of the Vale.}} of [[Ironoaks]] is a vassal house which holds fealty to [[House Arryn]] of the [[Vale of Arryn|Vale]]. It is one of the major houses of Vale of Arryn, its head is the widowed Lady [[Anya Waynwood]].

House Waynwood's [[heraldry]] consists of a black broken wheel on green.

==Members==
*Lady [[Anya Waynwood]], head of the house.
**Ser [[Donnel Waynwood]], her son. A knight.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Waynwoods are powerful vassals of House Arryn. Their seat is the castle of Ironoaks, east of the Eyrie on the coast of the [[Narrow Sea]]. They are known for their insistence on ceremony.

The members of House Waynwood at the time of the novels are:
*Lady Anya Waynwood, Lady of Ironoaks. An elderly woman.
**Ser Morton Waynwood, her eldest son and heir.
***Ser Roland Waynwood, Ser Morton's son and Lady Anya's grandson.
**Ser Donnel Waynwood, her second son. Knight of the Gate.
**Wallace Waynwood, her youngest son.

With unspecified familial relationship to the main branch there are also:
*Carolei Waynwood, married to Ser Geremy Frey.
*{Marsella Waynwood}, the third wife of Ser [[Stevron Frey]]. Died in childbirth
