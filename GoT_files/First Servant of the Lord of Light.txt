'''First Servant of the Lord of Light'''{{Ref|GOT605}} is one of the titles held by [[Kinvara]], a [[red priest]]ess of the [[Lord of Light]], and the High Priestess of the [[Red Temple]] of [[Volantis]].{{Ref|GOT605}}

==Known First Servants of the Lord of Light==
*[[Kinvara]]

==In the books==
In the ''A Song of Ice and Fire'' novels, "First Servant of the Lord of Light" is among the titles held by Benerro, the High Priest of the Red Temple of Volantis.
