The '''Ruby Fern'''<ref>[https://dedalvs.com/work/house-of-the-dragon/misc/shrubbery_v4.pdf ''Shrubbery'' version 4 | dedalvs.com]</ref> is a plant. The [[dragonlord]] [[Jaehaelor Mataeryon]] grew Ruby Ferns in his gardens. He once used the blood of an [[Execution|executed]] [[Slavery|slave]], [[Narha]], to nourish his Ruby Ferns, which then grew to extraordinary heights.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and their supplementary material, there is no mention of Ruby Ferns.
