}}'''Derek Dubery''' is a Northern Irish actor. He has appeared in the film ''{{WPS|The Lost City of Z (film)|The Lost City of Z}}'' and the television series ''{{WPS|Death and Nightingales (TV series)|Death and Nightingales}}''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a Lannister soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]".

He also had uncredited appearances as Brothel Patron #5 in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episode "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]" and Kings Landing Nobelman in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[No One]]".
