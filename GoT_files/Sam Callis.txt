'''Sam Callis''' (29 May 1973) is a British actor.

Callis has appeared in a number of well-known television shows in the UK, including [[wikipedia:London's Burning|London's Burning]], [[wikipedia:Ultimate Force|Ultimate Force]] and [[wikipedia:The Bill|The Bill]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played a [[Gold Cloak (The Night Lands)|Gold Cloak]]. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
