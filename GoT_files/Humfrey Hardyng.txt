Ser '''Humfrey Hardyng'''{{Webref|name=redintel |author=Gravemaster |date=July 26, 2024 |url=https://redanianintelligence.com/2024/07/26/a-knight-of-the-seven-kingdoms-adds-4-more-to-its-cast-including-humfrey-hardyng/ |title=A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms Adds 4 More To Its Cast, Including Humfrey Hardyng |website=Redanian Intelligence |access-date=August 13, 2024 }} is a [[Knighthood|knight]] during the reign of [[Daeron II Targaryen|Daeron II]].{{Ref|H&L416}}

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Humfrey Hardyng was a renowned [[Tournament|tourney]] knight and the brother-in-law of [[Humfrey Beesbury]].

In 208 AC, he won a great mêlée at [[Maidenpool]] in which he defeated Ser [[Donnel of Duskendale]], as well as Lords [[House Arryn|Arryn]] and [[House Royce|Royce]].
