King '''Lucifer Dryland''' was the King of the Brimstone during [[Nymeria's War]].

==Biography==
[[Nymeria]] had Lucifer sent to the [[Wall]] in golden chains after defeating him in Nymeria's War.{{Ref|H&L509}}

==In the books==
Lucifer Dryland is mentioned in ''The World of Ice & Fire''. He was the last member of House Dryland, and thus became known as the "Last of His Ilk".
