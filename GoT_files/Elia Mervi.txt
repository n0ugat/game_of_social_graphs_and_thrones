'''Elia Mervi''' is an artist who made the drawings used in several of the animated ''[[Histories & Lore]]'' shorts in [[Histories & Lore: Season 3|Season 3]] and [[Histories & Lore: Season 4|Season 4]].

Mervi provided the drawings for:

'''Season 3'''
*"[[The Vale]]"
*"[[House Tully (short)|House Tully]]"

'''Season 4'''
*"[[House Martell (short)|House Martell]]"
*"[[The Bastards of Westeros]]"
*"[[House Baelish (short)|House Baelish]]"
