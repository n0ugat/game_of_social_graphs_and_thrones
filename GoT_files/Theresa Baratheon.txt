'''Theresa Baratheon''' was a daughter of [[Orys Baratheon|Orys]] and [[Argella Baratheon]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Theresa Baratheon is an early member of [[House Baratheon]], the daughter of Orys and Argella Baratheon. She married a Ser Kevan{{Note|The house to which Ser Kevan belonged is not clear.}} at [[Sharp Point]]. They had children, including a son called Petyr.{{Ref|GOT106}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT106}}
