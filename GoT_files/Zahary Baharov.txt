|imdb = http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1707712/}}'''Zahary Baharov''' (also spelled '''Zahari''') is a Bulgarian actor. He studied acting at the National Academy for Theatre and Film Arts. He is acting on stage with the National Theatre "Ivan Vazov" Company since 2003. Notable theatrical performances include King Lear (2006), Don Juan (2007), and The Cherry
Orchard (2009). His first major role in a feature film was in ''Zift'', followed by ''Command Performance'' and ''Universal Soldier Regeneration''. In 2010, he appeared in Peter Weir's ''The Way Back''. In 2014 played Commander Elya in George Clooney`s ''The Monuments Men''. His most famous role is the gangster ''Ivo Andonov'' in the Bulgarian criminal series ''Undercover''.

In [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] he portrays [[Loboda]].<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1707712/]</ref>
