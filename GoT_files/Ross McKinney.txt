{{Cast|Title = Ross McKinney|Image = image129.JPG|birthplace = Belfast, Northern Ireland|role = [[Riddell]]|status = |Appearances = [[No One]]|imdb = http://www.imdb.com/name/nm5886289/|twitter = http://www.twitter.com/rossmckinney_}}'''Ross McKinney''' is an actor from Belfast, Northern Ireland. He will be appearing in the upcoming films ''Maze'' and ''Jack Taylor''. He previously worked on ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' as a trainee in the camera and digital imaging departments during the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth]] seasons.

In the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode, "[[No One]]", he played [[Riddell]].
