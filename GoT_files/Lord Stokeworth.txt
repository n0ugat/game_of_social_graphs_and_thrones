'''Lord Stokeworth''' is the head of [[House Stokeworth]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He is the head of [[House Stokeworth]]. He has a daughter, [[Elinor Stokeworth|Elinor]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Lord Stokeworth is mentioned when [[Alicent Hightower]] and [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] gossip about his daughter's betrothal to [[Gormon Massey|Lord Massey]]'s son during the [[Heir's Tournament]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Lord Stokeworth is among the [[Lordship|lords]] who swear to defend the rights of Rhaenyra Targaryen as [[Prince of Dragonstone|Princess of Dragonstone]].
