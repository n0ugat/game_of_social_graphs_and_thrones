'''Steven Blount''' is an actor. He is best known for his role in ''Titanic: Blood and Steele.'' In ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. He plays the part of [[Rickard Karstark]]. Originally [[Vinnie McCabe]] has been announced in the role of Rickard Karstark, however he turned out to be playing a [[House Lannister|Lannister]] bannerman, [[Leo Lefford]], in the same episode. Blount's CV lists him as playing Lord Karstark.<ref>[http://www.firstcallmanagement.ie/STEVE%20BLOUNT.html Steven Blount's CV at First Call Management.]</ref>

For the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] it was announced that [[John Stahl]] was playing Rickard Karstark.<ref>[http://grrm.livejournal.com/243470.html George R.R. Martin's blog]</ref>
