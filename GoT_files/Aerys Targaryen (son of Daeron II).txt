'''Aerys Targaryen'''{{Note|[[High Valyrian]]: ''Aerys Targārien''}} is a [[prince]] of the [[House Targaryen|Targaryen dynasty]].

==Biography==
Aerys Targaryen is the son of King [[Daeron II Targaryen|Daeron the Good]] and Queen [[Myriah Martell]]. He is the younger brother of Prince [[Baelor Targaryen]] and older brother of Princes [[Rhaegel Targaryen|Rhaegel]] and [[Maekar Targaryen]].

==In the books==
[[File:Roman Papsuev - Aerys I Targaryen.jpg|thumb|300px|right|Aerys Targaryen by Roman "Amok" Papsuev.©]]
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Aerys is described as bookish. He was spindly and stooped, with long, straight hair. He had a long, thin face, a long, thin mustache and a long pointed beard.
