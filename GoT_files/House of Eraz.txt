The '''House of Eraz'''{{Ref|GOT307}}{{Note|Razdal's surname is Eraz.}} is one of the noble [[Slavery|slaver]] families from the city of [[Yunkai]].

==Known members==
*{[[Razdal mo Eraz]]}, one of the [[Wise Masters]] of Yunkai. Killed by [[Grey Worm]]

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the envoy sent by the Wise Masters to [[parley]] with [[Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys]] is called Grazdan mo Eraz.

===Known members===
*Grazdan mo Eraz, slaver and diplomat
*{Gorzhak zo Eraz}, also known as "Pudding Face", slaver and commander. Slain by the Tattered Prince during the [[second siege of Meereen]].
