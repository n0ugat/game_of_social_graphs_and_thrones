Lord '''Gormon Massey'''<ref name="WBD" /> is the [[Lordship|Lord]] of [[Stonedance]] and head of [[House Massey]] during the reigns of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]] and [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon II]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Gormon is the father of [[Elinda Massey]], [[Handmaiden|lady-in-waiting]] to [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]]. He also has a [[Gormon Massey's son|son]] who was a [[squire]] to a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Tarly]] in [[112 AC]],{{Ref|HOTD101}} and a younger son named [[Emmon Massey|Emmon]].{{Ref|HOTD203}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Lord Massey is mentioned when [[Alicent Hightower]] and Rhaenyra Targaryen gossip about his son's betrothal to [[Elinor Stokeworth]] during the [[Heir's Tournament]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

At the start of the [[Dance of the Dragons]], [[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]] orders [[Bartimos Celtigar]] to send a raven to Lord Massey. Maester [[Gerardys]] later confirms that House Massey has declared for Rhaenyra.{{Ref|HOTD110}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Gormon is present at the [[Black Council]] when Rhaenyra returns from the [[Stormlands]].{{Ref|HOTD201}}

==Behind the scenes==
Gormon Massey's character description reads as follows:
:''"A member of Queen Rhaenyra’s black council and father to her lady in waiting, Elinda Massey."''<ref name="WBD" />

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Lord Massey sat on the [[Black Council|black council]]. While the TV show clarifies that he is the father of Elinda Massey, their relation in the books is unclear.

Gormon's life is extended in the TV series. In the books, he died attempting to claim [[Vermithor]] during the [[Red Sowing]].
