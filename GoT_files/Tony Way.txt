| twitter= https://twitter.com/tonypaulway|Appearances = 6 episodes}}'''Tony Way''' (born '''Tony Paul Way''' on 7 October 1978) is a British actor. He is best known for his role as Dave in the film ''[[wikipedia:Ali G Indahouse|Ali G Indahouse]]''.

Tony Way and several friends formed a comic collective while still in college in Wickford, Essex. They made several videos, one of which was seen by [[wikipedia:Charlie Higson|Charlie Higson]], who was impressed enough to cast Way in an episode of ''[[wikipedia:The Fast Show|The Fast Show]]''. Way went on to appear in several comedy programmes, including ''[[wikipedia:Randall & Hopkirk (Deceased)|Randall &amp; Hopkirk (Deceased)]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Black Books|Black Books]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Spaced|Spaced]]''. In film he has appeared in ''[[wikipedia:Finding Neverland|Finding Neverland]]'' and the American remake of ''[[wikipedia:The Girl With The Dragon Tattoo|The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', Way is playing the role of Ser [[Dontos Hollard]]. His casting was confirmed on 30 September 2011.<ref>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/Sansas_Own_Florian_Ser_Dontos_Hollard_Cast/ Westeros.org]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
