'''Velos''' is a city on the [[Isle of Cedars]], between [[Slaver's Bay]] and the [[Gulf of Grief]]. It shares an island with the port city [[Ghozai]] in the north. The city appears on a map in [[Archmaester]] [[Ebrose]]'s ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire (Ebrose)|A Song of Ice and Fire]]''.<ref>[https://www.makinggameofthrones.com/production-diary/season-8-episode-6-series-finale-props-song-of-ice-and-fire/ Making ''Game of Thrones'', ASOIAF prop]</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novel series, Velos is a ruined city, destroyed in the [[Doom of Valyria]], during which a tsunami 300 feet high descended on the city, drowning hundreds of thousands of its inhabitants. In ''A Dance with Dragons'', Victarion Greyjoy and the [[Iron Fleet]] makes a stop at Velos on their way to [[Meereen]].
