'''Iain Umber''' was a member of [[House Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Iain was born to Lord [[Gareth Umber]] and [[Hollys Umber]]. He married [[Arwen Swann]], with whom he had at least one child, the son [[Iain Umber]], of the same name. A fearsome warrior noted to have been dangerous to cross, he spent most of his adult life fighting in the [[Northern Wars]], eventually dying at [[Queenscrown]] of wounds sustained during the conflict.{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
Iain Umber is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
