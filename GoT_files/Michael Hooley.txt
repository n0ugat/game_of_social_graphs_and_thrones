}}'''Michael Hooley''' is a British casting director and actor. 

He worked as a casting director for such TV series as ''Derry Girls'' and ''Bloodlands'' and in films such as ''The Last Rifleman'' and ''The Northman''. 

Whilst working as a casting assistant on ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a [[Night's Watch man (Book of the Stranger)|Night's Watch man]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].
