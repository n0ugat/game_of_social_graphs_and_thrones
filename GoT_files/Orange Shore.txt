The '''Orange Shore'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a region of the continent of [[Essos]]. It is located to the west of [[Volantis]], forming the south coast of the continent between the [[Rhoyne]] delta and the [[Disputed Lands]].

==In the books==
In ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'', the Orange Shore is located in the same area.
