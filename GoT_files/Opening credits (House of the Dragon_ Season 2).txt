{{DISPLAYTITLE:Opening credits {{C|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2}}}}
[[File:Title card (House of the Dragon).png|thumb|right|200px|The ''House of the Dragon'': Season 2 title card.]]
The '''opening credits''' of [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]] introduce each episode starting with "[[A Son for a Son]]." Overhauling the original [[Opening credits (House of the Dragon: Season 1)|opening credits]] from [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|Season 1]], it features the weaving of a tapestry of [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|Targaryen family]] history. The ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' theme music by [[Ramin Djawadi]] also returns.{{Ref|HOTD201}}

==Scenes==
The following events are depicted, with new scenes being added as the season progresses:

==="[[A Son for a Son]]"===
*The founding of the [[Valyrian Freehold]].
*A woman being sacrificed before a [[dragon]].
*The [[Doom of Valyria]].
*[[Aegon's Conquest]].
**[[Aegon I Targaryen|Aegon I]] flying on [[Balerion]], [[Rhaenys Targaryen (sister of Aegon I)|Rhaenys]] flying on [[Meraxes]], and [[Visenya Targaryen|Visenya]] flying on [[Vhagar]], above the [[Velaryon fleet]].
**The [[Field of Fire]]. Dead men with the shields of [[House Gardener]] and [[House Lannister/House of the Dragon|House Lannister]] being devoured by Vhagar and Balerion.
**[[Ronnel Arryn]], [[Sharra Arryn]], [[Torrhen Stark]], and [[Edmyn Tully]] kneeling.
*The construction of the [[Red Keep]] and [[King's Landing]].
*[[Maegor Targaryen]] dead on the [[Iron Throne]], flanked by [[Vermithor]] and [[Silverwing]].
*[[Jaehaerys I Targaryen|Jaehaerys]] and [[Alysanne Targaryen]] reconciling with the [[Faith of the Seven]].
*The [[Great Council at Harrenhal]]. From left to right: [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys]], [[Aemma Arryn|Aemma]], the [[High Septon (Viserys I)|High Septon]], Jaehaerys, [[Ryam Redwyne]], [[Corlys Velaryon|Corlys]], and [[Rhaenys Targaryen|Rhaenys]].
*The rivalry between [[Alicent Hightower]] and [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]].
**On the [[Blacks|Black]] side, from left to right: [[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]], Rhaenys, Corlys, [[Jacaerys Velaryon|Jacaerys]], [[Lucerys Velaryon|Lucerys]], and [[Joffrey Velaryon|Joffrey]].
**On the [[Greens|Green]] side, from left to right (opposite the Black side): [[Otto Hightower|Otto]], [[Criston Cole|Criston]], [[Aemond Targaryen|Aemond]], [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon]], and [[Helaena Targaryen|Helaena]].
*Aegon II Targaryen sitting on the Iron Throne and Rhaenyra Targaryen sitting on [[Dragonstone]]'s throne, both surrounded by the shields of various [[noble house]]s of [[Westeros]]:
**Shields on Rhaenyra's side, clockwise: [[House Dustin|Dustin]], [[House Greyjoy|Greyjoy]], [[House Stark/House of the Dragon|Stark]], [[House Arryn/House of the Dragon|Arryn]], [[House Velaryon|Velaryon]], [[House Tully/House of the Dragon|Tully]], [[House Frey|Frey]], [[House Beesbury|Beesbury]], [[House Bar Emmon|Bar Emmon]].
**Shields on Aegon's side, counter-clockwise: [[House Tarbeck|Tarbeck]], [[House Fossoway|Fossoway]], [[House Peake|Peake]], [[House Lannister/House of the Dragon|Lannister]], [[House Hightower/House of the Dragon|Hightower]], [[House Baratheon/House of the Dragon|Baratheon]], Ambrose, [[House Redwyne|Redwyne]], [[House Stokeworth|Stokeworth]].
*Vhagar [[Fight above Shipbreaker Bay|killing]] [[Arrax]].

==="[[The Burning Mill]]"===
*The [[assassination of Jaehaerys Targaryen]].
*The hanging of the Red Keep's ratcatchers.

==="[[Smallfolk (episode)|Smallfolk]]"===
*The [[Battle at Rook's Rest]].

==="[[The Queen Who Ever Was]]"===
*Vhagar being confronted by Syrax, [[Silverwing]], [[Vermax]], [[Vermithor]], [[Caraxes]], [[Moondancer]], and [[Seasmoke]], with [[Dragonstone]] behind them.
