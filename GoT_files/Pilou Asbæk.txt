'''Johan Philip Asbæk''', known professionally as '''Pilou Asbæk''',{{Note|[pʰiˈlu ˈæspek]}} is a Danish actor. He is best known for his roles in the Danish TV show ''Borgen'' and the films ''A Hijacking'', ''Lucy'' and "Ghost in the Shell (2017)".

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrays [[Euron Greyjoy]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth]] seasons.<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/sources-report-euron-cast-major-scene-filmed-today-in-ballintoy-harbour/ Watchers on the Wall — Euron Cast]</ref>

His name is pronounced "Pe-lou As-beck".<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JxEtuv2CizE</ref>

== Background ==
Johan Philip "Pilou" Asbæk is a Danish actor. He got the nickname "Pilou" at a young age. Pilou means "little Phillip" in French.

In the summer of 2008, he graduated from the Danish National School of Performing Arts.

He co-presented the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Copenhagen alongside Lise Rønne and Nikolaj Koppel.
