'''Katrina Umber''', née '''Cerwyn''', was a member of [[House Cerwyn]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Katrina was born to [[Cleos Umber]] and [[Vivian Cerwyn]] and married [[Joseth Umber]], with whom she had two children.{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She is mentioned in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}
