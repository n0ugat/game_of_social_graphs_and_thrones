|Appearances = "[[Valar Morghulis]]"}}'''Wren Ros Elliot-Sloan''' is a child actress.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' she portrays [[Rhaego]] (in [[Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys]]'s vision). She joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].

She is the "surrogate niece" of [[Emilia Clarke]].<ref>http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Di04J5BrfW0</ref> [[Jason Momoa]] had to hold her during the scene, as she would cry whenever Emilia tried to hold her.
