'''Lord Wibberley''' is the head of [[House Wibberley]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Lord Wibberley sends a message to [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch|Lord Commander]] [[Jon Snow]], in which he agrees to send men to the [[Night's Watch]].{{Ref|GOT504}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of any House Wibberley; the name is a reference to Cormac and Marianne Wibberley, a married team of scriptwriters who are also friends of the TV show writers.<ref>[https://twitter.com/clmazin/status/720763097967755265 Craig Mazin Twitter]</ref>
