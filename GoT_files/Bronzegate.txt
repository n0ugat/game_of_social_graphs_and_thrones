'''Bronzegate'''{{Ref|H&L204}} is a castle in the [[Stormlands]], the region controlled by [[House Baratheon]] of [[Storm's End]]. The castle is located north of Storm's End just off the [[Kingsroad]], southwest of [[Haystack Hall]].<ref>"[[Robert's Rebellion - Stannis Baratheon]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Bronzegate is the seat of House Buckler. It is located south-east of the Wendwater.
