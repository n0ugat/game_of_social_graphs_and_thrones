| imdb= http://www.imdb.com/name/nm4422626/|Appearances = 8 episodes}}'''Callum Wharry''' is a child actor from Northern Ireland who played Prince [[Tommen Baratheon]]. ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' is his first acting experience. He initially appeared as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and returned in this role for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].

For [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|Season 4]], [[Dean-Charles Chapman]], who had previously played Tommen's cousin [[Martyn Lannister]], took over the role of Tommen.
