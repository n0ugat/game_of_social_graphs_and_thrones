'''Hanan Tobias Simpson Menzies''' is a British actor, best known for his regular role as Brutus on the [[HBO]] series ''[[wikipedia:Rome (TV series)|Rome]]'', Andrew Lawrence on the BBC series ''[[wikipedia:Spooks|Spooks]]'' and as "Black Jack" Randall in Starz's ''[[wikipedia:Outlander (TV series)|Outlander]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he plays [[Edmure Tully]]. He was announced as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] at San Diego Comic-Con 2012.<ref name=ComicCon>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/Massive_Comic-Con_Casting_Reveals/ "Massive Comic-Con Casting Reveals" from Westeros.org, 13 July 2012]</ref>

Menzies was cast in ''Rome'' alongside [[Ciarán Hinds]] by ''Game of Thrones'' casting director [[Nina Gold]]. He has also appeared in the films ''Finding Neverland'' (2004), ''Casino Royale'' (2006), ''Atonement'' (2007), and ''Forget Me Not'' (2010).
