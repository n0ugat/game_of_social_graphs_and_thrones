|Last = "[[The Rains of Castamere (episode)|The Rains of Castamere]]"}}'''Tom Brooke''' is a British actor. He has appeared on TV in ''Sherlock, Foyle's War'', ''The Hollow Crown'', ''Room at the Top'', ''Preacher'', and ''Rock and Chips'' (with [[Samantha Spiro]]). He was a production assistant and appeared uncredited in ''Bridget Jones: The Edge of Reason''. His father, actor Paul Brooke, was in the previous film, ''Bridget Jones Diary'', as Mr. Tits Pervert.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he is playing [[Lothar Frey]]. On August 02 2012, it was announced that he had a role in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]].<ref name="WiC Tom">[http://winteriscoming.net/2012/08/tom-brooke-joins-game-of-thrones/ "Tom Brooke"], Winter is Coming.net, August 02 2012</ref> Winter is Coming reported his role on January 10 2013.<ref name="WiC 10-01">[http://winteriscoming.net/2013/01/season-three-casting-update/]</ref> His role was recast in the sixth season with [[Daniel Tuite]] taking over as Lothar.
