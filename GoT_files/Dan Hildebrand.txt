'''Dan Hildebrand''' is a British actor best known for playing the roles of Tim Driscoll (in the first season) and Shaughnessy (in the third) on the [[HBO]] series ''[[wikipedia:Deadwood (TV series)|Deadwood]]'', as well as the role of Sean Casey on ''[[wikipedia:Sons of Anarchy|Sons of Anarchy]]''.

On ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Hildebrand plays the role of [[Kraznys mo Nakloz]] in three episodes of [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|Season 3]]. He was announced in the role on 18 August 2012.<ref>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/Two_More_for_S3_Ramon_Tikaram_Dan_Hildebrand_Join_Cast/ Westeros.org]</ref>
