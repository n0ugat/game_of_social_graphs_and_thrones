}}'''Ian David McShane''' is an English actor, director, producer and voice artist. He is known for his role in television series such as ''Jesus of Nazareth'', ''Lovejoy'', ''Deadwood'', ''The Pillars of the Earth'', ''American Horror Story: Asylum'', and ''Ray Donovan'' and films such as ''Sexy Beast'', ''The Golden Compass'', ''Kung Fu Panda'', ''Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides'' (alongside [[DeObia Oparei]] and [[Anton Lesser]]) , ''Snow White and the Huntsman'' and ''John Wick''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrays Brother [[Ray]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/ian-mcshane-hints-at-his-game-of-thrones-season-six-role-reveals-details/ Watchers on the Wall — Ian McShane hints at his Game of Thrones Season Six role, reveals details]</ref><ref>[http://tumblrofthrones.tumblr.com/post/144979231743/the-for-your-consideration-dvd-with-credits-hbo-is Watchers on the Wall - Sue the Fury Tumblr]</ref>
