'''''The Life and Adventures of Elyo Grivas, First Sword of Braavos'''''{{Ref|GOT403}}{{Note|The comma is implied by Davos's diction.}} is a book which details the exploits of [[Elyo Grivas]], who was the [[First Sword of Braavos|First Sword]] of [[Braavos]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
[[Shireen Baratheon|Shireen]] gives the book to [[Davos Seaworth|Davos]] as part of his reading lessons. It is one of Shireen's favorite books, because it is filled with pirates and swordfights.{{Ref|GOT403}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of a book by this name, as there is no character named Elyo Grivas.
