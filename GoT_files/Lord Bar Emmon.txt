'''Lord Bar Emmon''' is the head of [[House Bar Emmon]] and Lord of [[Sharp Point]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]] commands [[Bartimos Celtigar]] to send a raven to Lord Bar Emmon. Maester [[Gerardys]] later confirms that House Bar Emmon has declared for [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]].{{Ref|HOTD110}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Lord Bar Emmon was a member of the [[Black Council|black council]]. At the [[Great Council at Harrenhal|Great Council of 101 AC]], a Lord Bar Emmon supported the claim of [[Laenor Velaryon]], but it is unknown if this is the same person.
