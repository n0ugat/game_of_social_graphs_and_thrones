The '''Redgrass Field'''{{Ref|GOT104}} is a field near [[King's Landing]]. The [[Battle of Redgrass Field]], the final battle of the [[Blackfyre Rebellion]], was fought there.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Redgrass Field was named for the bloodbath of the battle that took place there during the Blackfyre Rebellion. After the war, the field became a memorial to [[Daemon Blackfyre]], with many [[House Blackfyre|Blackfyre]] supporters coming there to pay respects to him.
