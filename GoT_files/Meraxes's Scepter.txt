'''Meraxes's Scepter'''<ref>[https://dedalvs.com/work/house-of-the-dragon/misc/shrubbery_v4.pdf ''Shrubbery'' version 4 | dedalvs.com]</ref> was a fruit native to the [[Valyrian peninsula]]. It was named after the [[Old Gods of Valyria|Valyrian god]] [[Meraxes (god)|Meraxes]]. The extremely sour scent of [[jeweled moss]] is said to be similar to that of Meraxes's Scepter.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and their supplementary material, there is no mention of Meraxes's Scepter.
