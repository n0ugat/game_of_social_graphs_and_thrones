'''Derek''' is a sworn brother of the [[Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Derek and [[Brant]] are present during the funeral for [[Order of Maesters|Maester]] [[Aemon]]; [[Samwell Tarly|Sam]] notices them staring at him hatefully after [[Alliser Thorne]] warns Sam of his vulnerability. During their guard duty, he and his friend Brant attempt to rape [[Gilly]]. After severely beating Sam, who intervened, they are scared off by a snarling [[Ghost]].{{Ref|GOT507}}



==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no Derek or any analogous character.
