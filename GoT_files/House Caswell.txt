'''House Caswell'''{{Ref|HOTD106}}{{Note|Allun's surname is Caswell.}} of [[Bitterbridge]] is a vassal house that holds fealty to Lord [[Bronn]] of Highgarden. Their seat is Bitterbridge, which is located in the east of the region where the [[Mander]] crosses the [[Roseroad]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Lord [[Allun Caswell]] is a supporter of Princess [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] at court, and thus a member of the [[Blacks]].{{Ref|HOTD106}}

==Relationships==
===Ancestors===
*Lord {[[Allun Caswell]]}, Lord of Bitterbridge. Head of House Caswell during the reign of King [[Viserys I Targaryen]]. Hanged for supporting [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]].
**His [[Lady Caswell|wife]], Lady of Bitterbridge.

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', House Caswell were supporters of [[Maegor Targaryen]] in his war against [[Aegon Targaryen (son of Aenys)|Aegon the Uncrowned]]. Late in the reign of Viserys I, Lord Caswell was noted as a supporter of Rhaenyra Targaryen at court.

By the time of the main ''A Song of Ice and Fire'' novels, House Caswell is still a notable vassal of House Tyrell, and [[Renly Baratheon]]'s host camps beneath Bitterbridge during the [[War of the Five Kings]]. It is there that [[Catelyn Stark|Catelyn]] meets with Renly.

===Known members===
*Lord Lorent Caswell, Lord of Bitterbridge and Defender of the Fords.
**Two unnamed daughters.
*Four unnamed sisters.
