The '''Golden Fields'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} are a region in western [[Essos]]. This area is located between [[Dagger Lake]] and a tributary of the [[Rhoyne]]. The [[Flatlands]] lie to the northwest and the [[Disputed Lands]] and the [[Free Cities|Free City]] of [[Myr]] to the south-west.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Golden Fields are located in the same area. The tributary of the Rhoyne that lies along their south-western edge is called the Lhorulu.
