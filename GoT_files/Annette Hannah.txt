'''Annette Hannah''' is an actress who appears in ''Game of Thrones'' [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|Season 6]]. She was 13 years old in 2018.

She plays [[Frances]], one of the ''little birds'', street children used as spies within [[King's Landing]], in the episode "[[Oathbreaker]]".<ref>[http://www.pqacademy.com/news/pqa-derby-students-stars-game-thrones/ PQAcademy Website]</ref> She was also one of the children who kill [[Pycelle]] in the episode [[The Winds of Winter]].
Annette shot a scene for Season 7, Episode 7, but this was deleted during the edit process. The characters name was spoken during the dialogue, this time she was known as Trella.

Annette has achondroplasia (a common type of [[dwarf]]ism).
