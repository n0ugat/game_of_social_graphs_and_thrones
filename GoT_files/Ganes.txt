'''Ganes''' is a butcher from a village in the [[Riverlands]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
A [[tortured prisoner]] at [[Harrenhal]] reveals that Ganes and his son aided [[Beric Dondarrion]] and the [[Brotherhood Without Banners|Brotherhood]].{{Ref|GOT204}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the brotherhood has many allies and sympathizers in the riverlands. Ganes specifically is not mentioned.
