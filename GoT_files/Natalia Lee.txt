|Appearances = "[[The North Remembers]]"}}'''Natalia Lee''' is an actress and armorer.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' she portrayed [[Chella]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[The North Remembers]]".

She was also a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episode "[[Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken]]" and an armorer, a lead armorer, a senior armorer, and an armorer coordinator in the series.
