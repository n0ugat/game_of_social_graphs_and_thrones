'''Warden of the East'''{{Ref|H&L122}} is a title traditionally held by the [[Defender of the Vale]] and the head of [[House Arryn]] of the [[Eyrie]], since [[Ronnel Arryn]], the last [[King of the Mountain and the Vale]], [[Bending the knee|submitted]] to [[Aegon I Targaryen]]. Previously held by [[Lordship|Lord]] [[Jon Arryn]] and then held by [[Robin Arryn]], until the Vale [[Battle of the Bastards|joined]] the reinstated [[North|Kingdom of the North]]. [[Robin Arryn]], having come of age, can be legally considered the Warden of the East after choosing to elect [[Bran Stark]] as [[Lord of the Seven Kingdoms|Lord of the Six Kingdoms]].
