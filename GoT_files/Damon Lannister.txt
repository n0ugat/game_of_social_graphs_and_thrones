Lord '''Damon Lannister''' is the [[Lord of Casterly Rock]] during the reign of [[Daeron II Targaryen]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lord Damon is a renowned tourney knight, even when ruling as Lord of Casterly Rock. He has two sons, Tybolt and Gerold. Damon is the great-grandfather of [[Tywin Lannister]] and the great-great-grandfather of [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]], [[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]], and [[Cersei Lannister]].
