'''Aurelia Casterly''' was the daughter of Lady [[Morven Casterly|Morven]] who lived during the [[Age of Heroes]]. She was a relative of Princess [[Ianthe Casterly|Ianthe]] and the [[Casterly king]].

==Behind the scenes==
On July 21, 2020, ''Redanian Intelligence'' revealed that [[Amy McPherson]] had portrayed a character named Aurelia in ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', the rejected ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' [[World of Westeros|prequel]] [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]]. It was also revealed that her character was the daughter of [[Naomi Watts]]'s character,<ref name="Redanian" /> whose name was later confirmed to be [[Morven Casterly]].<ref name="Storyboard" />

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and its supplementary material, there is no mention of a character named Aurelia Casterly. The character was invented by [[Jane Goldman]] for ''Bloodmoon''.
