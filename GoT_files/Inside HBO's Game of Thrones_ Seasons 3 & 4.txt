'''''Inside HBO’s Game of Thrones: Seasons 3 & 4''''' is a companion book about the ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' TV series adaptation produced by [[HBO]]. It is a sequel to ''[[Inside HBO's Game of Thrones]]'', which provided behind-the-scenes interviews and information about the production of [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|Season 1]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|Season 2]] of the TV series, and was written by scriptwriter and loremaster [[Bryan Cogman]].

The sequel contains new behind-the-scenes interviews and information from the production of [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|Season 3]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|Season 4]], and was written by Cat Taylor, the former personal assistant to showrunners [[David Benioff]] and [[D.B. Weiss]]. She is best known to fans as the original writer of the "Making Game of Thrones" official HBO production blog (which she stopped writing for by the end of Season 4). The book includes a preface by Benioff and Weiss. It was released on November 11, 2014.<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/exclusive-preview-inside-hbos-game-thrones-seasons-3-4/ Exclusive preview of Inside HBO’s Game of Thrones: Seasons 3 & 4!]</ref>
