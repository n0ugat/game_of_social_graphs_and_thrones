'''Katharine Pickering''' is a British professional stunt rider and trainer.

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', she was a stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[Second of His Name]]" and the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[The Red Sowing]]".
