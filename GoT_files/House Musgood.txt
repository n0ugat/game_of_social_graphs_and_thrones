'''House Musgood'''{{Ref|GOT403}} is a vassal house from the [[Stormlands]] that holds fealty to [[House Baratheon]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
[[Davos Seaworth|Davos]] reports to [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]] he managed to secure the support of some noble houses, among them House Musgood.{{Ref|GOT403}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Musgood has only been mentioned very briefly. According to "[[The Citadel]]", their sigil is a golden pavilion on blue and a green laurel crown on white, quartered.

The only known member of the house is Jack Musgood, who was [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch]] during the invasion of [[Raymun Redbeard]], [[King-Beyond-the-Wall]]. He was first known as Jolly Jack. He didn't notice Raymun's invasion and was known after that as Sleepy Jack.
