'''William John Postlethwaite''' is an English actor. He is the son of the late {{WPS|Pete Postlethwaite}} and has appeared in television series such as ''{{WPS|Midsomer Murders}}'' and ''{{WPS|Holby City}}''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he portrayed [[Theo]] in "[[Dragonstone (episode)|Dragonstone]]".
