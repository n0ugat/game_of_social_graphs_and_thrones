Ser '''Donnel of Duskendale'''{{Con|D&EN}} is a [[Knighthood|knight]] of the [[Kingsguard]] of [[Daeron II Targaryen|Daeron II]].{{Ref|H&L416}}

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Donnel has red hair and a beard. He is of [[Smallfolk|low birth]] and hails from [[Duskendale]].

In 208 AC, Donnel was defeated by Ser [[Humfrey Hardyng]] in a [[Tournament|tourney]] at [[Maidenpool]].
