'''Wylla Waynwood''' was a member of [[House Waynwood]] and the wife of [[Paxter Royce]]. She is the mother of [[Edwina Royce]].

==Biography==
Wylla Waynwood married [[Paxter Royce]] and bore him a daugher, [[Edwina Royce]].<ref name = "s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Wylla Waynwood is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Wylla Waynwood is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books. The name of Yohn Royce's mother has not been mentioned in the books.
