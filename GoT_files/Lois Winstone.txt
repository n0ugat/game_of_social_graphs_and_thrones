'''Lois Elaine Winstone''' is an actress. She is best known for her role in the film ''Tamara Drewe''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' she portrayed a [[Mole's Town whore]].

She is the daughter of actor [[wikipedia: Ray Winstone|Ray Winstone]]. Her younger sister [[wikipedia: Jaime Winstone|Jaime]] is also an actress and was previously engaged to [[Alfie Allen]].
