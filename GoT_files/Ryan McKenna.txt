}}'''Ryan McKenna''' is an actor from Dungannon, Northern Ireland. He won the [[Ciarán Hinds]] Most Promising Actor Award at the 2011 Belfast Cinemagic Festival. He also participated in the ''Game of Thrones'' panel at the festival alongside [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|Season 2]] actress [[Hannah Murray]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he plays Ser [[Willis Wode]].
