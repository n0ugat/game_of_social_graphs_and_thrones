The '''siege of White Harbor'''{{Ref|GOT806}} was a large-scale outlaw attack on [[White Harbor]] by the [[Sea Kings]]. Lord [[Rickard Stark]] defended the city and almost drowned in the process, but was saved by Ser [[Gwayne Gaunt]],{{Note|name=Gwayne Gaunt}} who broke the siege.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of this siege.
