'''Oswin''' is a sailor in service to [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Oswin is the captain of [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]]'s personal yacht.{{Ref|HOTD104}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
When Rhaenyra decides to leave [[Storm's End]] and return to [[King's Landing]] after her progress, she has Captain Oswin prepare the ship.{{Ref|HOTD104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the only character by that name is Oswin Arryn, who was a [[King of the Mountain and the Vale|King of Mountain and Vale]] from [[House Arryn]].
