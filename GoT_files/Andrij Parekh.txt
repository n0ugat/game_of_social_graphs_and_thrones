'''Andrij Parekh''' (born 20 September 1971) is an American cinematographer and director. He is a director on the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]] of ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.<ref>[https://www.eyeforfilm.co.uk/feature/2023-03-21-sophie-barthes-on-the-feminist-design-and-ethos-of-the-pod-generation-feature-story-by-amber-wilkinson Rounding out sci-fi, Sophie Barthes on the feminist design and ethos of The Pod Generation | ''Eye For Film'']</ref>

Parekh previously directed one episode of HBO's ''Watchmen'', and four episodes of HBO's ''Succession''. He won the Emmy Award for Outstanding Directing in a Drama Series for the third episode of ''Succession'' Season 2, "[[wikipedia:Hunting (Succession)|Hunting]]."
