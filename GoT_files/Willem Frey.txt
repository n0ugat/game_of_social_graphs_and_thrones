{{Quote|I went to Willem Frey's wedding?|[[Jaime Lannister]] to his cousin, [[Alton Lannister|Alton]]|A Man Without Honor}}
Ser '''Willem Frey''' was a member of [[House Frey]] and a [[Knighthood|knight]] whose wedding was celebrated with a tournament.

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
[[Alton Lannister]] recalls squiring for [[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]] at a tournament, the same day Willem's wedding was held.{{Ref|GOT207}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
A [[Riverlands traveller]] tells [[Locke]] that he saw Jaime fight in a tournament at Willem Frey's wedding.{{Ref|GOT302}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
He is supposedly murdered, along with his other male family members, by [[Arya Stark|Arya]], in retaliation for the [[Red Wedding]].{{Ref|GOT701}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is a character named Willem Frey, but he is a young grandson of Lord [[Walder Frey]], the second son of Cleos Frey (the analogous character of [[Alton Lannister]]) and Jeyne Darry. This "Willem Frey" is only ten years old, thus was never married. He serves as a page at [[Ashemark]]. He has not made an appearance in the books so far, only mentioned in the appendices.
