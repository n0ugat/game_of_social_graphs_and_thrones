In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he has portrayed a Bolton Soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episode "[[The Bear and the Maiden Fair (episode)|The Bear and the Maiden Fair]]", a Stark Bannerman in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episode "[[Mhysa]]" and a Night's Watch Officer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episodes "[[Breaker of Chains]]", "[[Oathkeeper (episode)|Oathkeeper]]", "[[Mockingbird]]", "[[The Watchers on the Wall]]" and "[[The Children]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[The Wars To Come]]", "[[The House of Black and White]]", "[[High Sparrow (episode)|High Sparrow]]", "[[Kill the Boy]]", "[[The Gift]]", "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]" and "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[The Red Woman]]", "[[Oathbreaker]]" and "[[Book of the Stranger]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[The Long Night]]" and "[[The Last of the Starks]]".
