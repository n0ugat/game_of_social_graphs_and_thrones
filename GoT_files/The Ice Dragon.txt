"'''The Ice Dragon'''"{{Ref|ATGS06}} is the sixth and final episode of ''[[Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series]]''. It premiered on November 17, 2015. It was written by Andrew Grant and James Windeler, and directed by Kent Mudle.
