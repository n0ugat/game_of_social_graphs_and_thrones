'''Dolf''' is a member of the [[Stone Crows]], one of the [[hill tribes]] of the [[Mountains of the Moon]] in the [[Vale of Arryn]]. He is the father of [[Shagga]], the current leader of the Stone Crows.{{Ref|GOT108}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Dolf, father of Shagga, is a Stone Crow and the son of Holger. Shagga claims that Dolf taught him everything he knew about warfare.
