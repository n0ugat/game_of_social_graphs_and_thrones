In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a [[Free Folk|wildling]] in the episode "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]" alongside his bandmates [[Bill Kelliher]] and [[Brann Dailor]]. He reprised the role in "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]".

== Death ==
Hinds was killed while riding his Harley Davidson when a BMW SUV crashed into him after failing to yield while making a turn, killing him in the process.<ref>[https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/aug/21/brent-hinds-mastodon-dies-motorcycle-crash The Guardian]</ref>
