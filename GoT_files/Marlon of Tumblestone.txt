Ser '''Marlon of Tumblestone''' is a [[Knighthood|knight]], and a patron of [[Petyr Baelish|Littlefinger]]'s brothels, where he is said to prefer amputees.

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Ser Marlon and his unusual tastes are mentioned by [[Varys]] when the latter attempts to intimidate Petyr Baelish with the knowledge of what transpires in his brothels.{{Ref|GOT105}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of a character named Marlon of Tumblestone. The only character by that name is Ser Marlon Manderly, Lord [[Wyman Manderly]]'s cousin and the commander of the [[White Harbor]] garrison.
