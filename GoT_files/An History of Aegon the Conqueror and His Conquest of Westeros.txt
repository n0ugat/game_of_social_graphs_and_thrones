'''''An History of Aegon the Conqueror and His Conquest of Westeros'''''{{Ref|GOT310}} is a history book that narrates [[Aegon's Conquest]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
''An History of Aegon the Conqueror and His Conquest of Westeros'' is one of the books Princess [[Shireen Baratheon]] gives Ser [[Davos Seaworth]] while he is imprisoned at [[Dragonstone]].{{Ref|GOT310}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there's no mention of a book of this title. Given that it was a major, epoch-defining historical event in Westeros, numerous books have probably been written on the subject.
