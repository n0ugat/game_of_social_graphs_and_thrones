| imdb=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0821471/}}'''John Macdonald Steele''' (born 1953 in Sauchie, Clackmannanshire, Scotland; died 2 March, 2022<ref>Game Of Thrones actor John Stahl dies aged 68 https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-scotland-tayside-central-60624212</ref>), known professionally as '''John Stahl''', was a Scottish actor, arguably best known for playing the recurring character of Quinn on the second season of ''[[wikipedia:Being Human (TV series)|Being Human]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played the role of [[Rickard Karstark]], starting in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]]. He replaced [[Steven Blount]], who was announced in the role, but was not identified by name on screen. [[George R.R. Martin]] confirmed him in the role on 13 October 2011.<ref>[http://grrm.livejournal.com/243470.html George R.R. Martin's blog]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
