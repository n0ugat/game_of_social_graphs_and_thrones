'''Tristan Mudd''' was a [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
Tristan Mudd at some point joined the [[Night's Watch]], and eventually rose to become its [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch|Lord Commander]]. He is remembered as one of the worst to hold the title.{{Ref|H&L507}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, his story is the same.
