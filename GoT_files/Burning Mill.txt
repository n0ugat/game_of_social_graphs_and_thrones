The '''Burning Mill'''{{Ref|HOTD203}} is a mill on the borderlands of [[House Bracken]] and [[House Blackwood]] in the [[Riverlands]]. It is named for a [[Battle at the Burning Mill|battle]] that was fought there during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', the Burning Mill is located near [[Stone Hedge]].
