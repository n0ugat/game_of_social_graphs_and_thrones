'''Lemonwood'''{{Ref|GOT401}} is a castle in [[Dorne]], the region controlled by [[House Martell]] of [[Sunspear]], and the seat of [[House Dalt]]. The castle is located on the east coast of Dorne, south of the mouth of the [[Greenblood]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lemonwood is the seat of [[House Dalt]], loyal vassals of the Martells. It lies just south of the Planky Town.
