'''House Sunglass'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} of [[Sweetport]] is a [[noble house]] from the [[Crownlands]]. Their heraldry displays a ring of seven gold seven-pointed stars on a white field.<ref name="Shield" />

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Man-at-arms|Men-at-arms]] from House Sunglass take part in the [[War for the Stepstones]].{{Ref|HOTD103}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the seat of House Sunglass is Sweetport Sound in or along [[Blackwater Bay]]. They are among the vassals of [[Stannis Baratheon]].

===Known members===
*Lord {Guncer Sunglass}, Lord of Sweetport Sound, a pious follower of the Faith. Burned alive at [[Selyse Baratheon]]'s orders as a sacrifice to [[Lord of Light|R'hllor]].
**Lord Sunglass, his unnamed brother and heir, the new Lord of Sweetport Sound. Self-exiled in [[Volantis]] for fear of being burned by [[Melisandre]].
