|Last = "[[And Now His Watch Is Ended]]"}}'''Robert Pugh''' (born 11 October 1950) is a Welsh actor. Pugh is known for his roles in ''[[wikipedia:The Ghost Writer (film)|The Ghost Writer]]'' (with [[David Rintoul]]), ''[[wikipedia:Robin Hood (2010 film)|Robin Hood]]'' (with [[Mark Addy]] and [[Max von Sydow]]) and ''[[wikipedia:Master and Commander: The Far Side of the World|Master and Commander: The Far Side of the World]]'', as well as numerous other TV and film credits.

He appeared in an episode of the British crime drama ''Agatha Christie's Poirot'' alongside future ''Game of Thrones'' cast member [[Alexander Siddig]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he plays [[Craster]], the master of [[Craster's Keep]], a sordid place beyond the [[Wall]]. He was confirmed in the role on August 10, 2011.<ref>[http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/game-thrones-meet-craster-220687 Hollywood Reporter]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]], and he remained a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]].
