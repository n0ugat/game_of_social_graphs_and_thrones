A '''White Walker''' traveled to the [[Haunted Forest]] near [[Craster's Keep]] and seized [[Craster's last son]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
[[File:White Walker Oathkeeper with baby.jpg|thumb|left|The White Walker takes Craster's last son to the Land of Always Winter, riding an undead wight-horse.]]
The White Walker travels to the [[Haunted Forest]] near [[Craster's Keep]] and seizes [[Craster's last son]], who had been left out in the snow by [[Rast]]. He takes the child deep into the [[Land of Always Winter]], where he places him on an icy altar. The [[Night King]] then takes the baby and turns him into a [[White Walkers|White Walker]].{{Ref|GOT404}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Craster's wives are convinced that the baby boys given by Craster to the Others are, in turn, transformed into new Others. However, no such event has ever been witnessed in the books so far.
