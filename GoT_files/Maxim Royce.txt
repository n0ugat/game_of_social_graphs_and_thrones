'''Maxim Royce''' is a member of [[House Royce]] and a younger brother of [[Yohn Royce]], the current [[Lord of Runestone]]. His current status is uncertain.

==Biography==
Maxim Royce is the secondborn son of Lord [[Andar Royce]] and Lady [[Elena Egen]], the younger brother of [[Yohn Royce]] and older brother of [[Waymar Royce (son of Andar)|Waymar Royce]].<ref name = "s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Maxim Royce is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Maxim Royce is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books. No siblings of Yohn Royce have been mentioned in the books.
