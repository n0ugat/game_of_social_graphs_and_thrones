Lord '''Daragh Umber''' was a head of [[House Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
With his wife [[Morag Umber|Morag]], Daragh had a son, [[Hothor Umber]], who continued the family line with seven children of his own with [[Amarylis Umber|Amarylis Manderly]].{{Ref|GOT104}}
