'''Simeon of Ashemark''' was a [[First Builder]] of the [[Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Simeon was appointed First Builder by [[Robert of Oxbridge]], and continued to hold the position under [[Willem of the Dun Fort]].<ref>[[:File:5x2 Night's Watch Book.jpg]]</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Simeon is mentioned in a book that [[Samwell Tarly]] is reading.{{Ref|GOT502}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Simeon of Ashemark.
