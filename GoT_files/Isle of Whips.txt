The '''Isle of Whips'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is an island in the [[Jade Sea]], off the eastern coast of [[Great Moraq]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Isle of Whips is a mostly barren island serving as a waystation for [[Slavery|slavers]].
