King '''Dorren Stark''' was a [[King in the North]] before [[Aegon's Conquest]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
[[Shireen Baratheon]] mentions that Dorren's tomb is in the ancient crypts underneath [[Winterfell]], along with [[Brandon the Builder]].{{Ref|GOT505}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Dorren Stark is mentioned only in passing. According to legend, during the time of his rule the [[Night's Watch]] fought [[Giants]] and actively traded with the [[Children of the Forest]].
