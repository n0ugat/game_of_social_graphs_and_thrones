'''House Wylde'''{{Guideref|Jasper Wylde}}{{Note|Jasper's surname is Wylde.}} of [[Rain House]] is a vassal house which holds fealty to [[House Baratheon]] of [[Storm's End]].

==Known members==
*Lord [[Jasper Wylde]], [[Master of Laws]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen]] and [[Aegon II Targaryen]].
*Ser {[[Willem Wylde]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] of the [[Kingsguard]] of [[King of the Andals and the Rhoynar and the First Men|King]] [[Daeron II Targaryen]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Wylde is a noble house from [[Rain House]] in the Stormlands. It is one of the principal houses sworn to House Baratheon. According to semi-canon sources they blazon their arms with a blue-green maelstrom on gold.
