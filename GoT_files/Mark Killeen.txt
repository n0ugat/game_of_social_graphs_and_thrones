'''Mark Killeen''' is a British film and television actor. His first major role was in ''[[Wikipedia:Rise of the Footsoldier|Rise of the Footsoldier]]''. On television he appeared in the Doctor Who episode ''Let's Kill Hitler''.<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2075330/</ref> In 2012 he played the lead role in two romantic comedies ''[[Wikipedia:The Callback Queen|The Callback Queen]]'' and ''[[Wikipedia:The Right Juice|The Right Juice]]'', filmed in the Algarve.<ref>http://www.algarveresident.com/45048-0/algarve/a-star-studded-film-launch</ref> He featured in ''[[Wikipedia:The Dark Knight Rises|The Dark Knight Rises]]'' and is currently filming the role of Greek Commander in'' [[Wikipedia:300: Battle of Artemisia|300: Battle of Artemisia]]''.<ref>http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1253863/</ref> He is represented by the Felix De Wolfe Agency in London.<ref>http://www.felixdewolfe.com/</ref>

He has been cast for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] of ''Game of Thrones'' in the role of [[Mero]], also known as the "Titan's Bastard". He was announced in the role on December 3 2012.<ref name="WiC Killeen">[http://winteriscoming.net/2012/12/mero-cast/]</ref>
