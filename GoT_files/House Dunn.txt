'''House Dunn'''{{Scriptref|GOT510}}{{Note|Tom's surname is Dunn.}} is a [[noble house]] from the [[Reach]].

==Members==
*{[[Tom Dunn]]}, a member of the [[Night's Watch]].

==Behind the scenes==
House Dunn is only mentioned in the script for "[[Mother's Mercy]]."{{Scriptref|GOT510}} In the filmed version, the line mentioning it is cut.{{Ref|GOT510}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Dunn is among the Reach houses that declare for [[Renly Baratheon]] during the [[War of the Five Kings]].{{Bookref|ACOK|31}} According to a semi-canon source, their coat of arms is per pale black and pink, a countercharged two-headed pelican.<ref>https://www.westeros.org/Citadel/Heraldry/Houses/7/</ref>
