'''House Morrigen'''{{Ref|H&L502}}{{Note|Damon's surname is Morrigen.}} of [[Crow's Nest]] is a [[noble house]] of the [[Stormlands]], and vassals to [[House Baratheon]]. Their [[Heraldry|sigil]] is a black crow in flight on a storm-green field.

==Members==
===Historical members===
[[File:HL5 Maegor trial of seven versus Damon Morrigen.png|thumb|Ser Damon Morrigen leading the Faith Millitant during the [[trial by seven of Maegor Targaryen]].]]
*Ser {[[Damon Morrigen]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] who commanded the [[Faith Militant]] during its [[Faith Militant uprising|uprising]]. He challenged [[Maegor Targaryen]] to a [[Trial by seven of Maegor Targaryen|trial of seven]], and was killed by him.{{Ref|H&L502}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Morrigen is among the Stormlords who declare for [[Renly Baratheon]] and later go over to [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]. Unlike most Stormlords that bend the knee to [[Joffrey Baratheon|Joffrey]] after the [[Battle of the Blackwater]], House Morrigen remains loyal to Stannis.

House Morrigen has a rivalry with [[House Connington]].

The known members of House Morrigen are:

*Lord Lester Morrigen, Lord of Crow's Nest.
*Ser Richard Morrigen, Lester's younger brother and heir.
*Ser {Guyard Morrigen}, known as "Guyard the Green", a sworn brother of Renly Baratheon's Rainbow Guard. Slain at the battle of the Blackwater.

They blazon their arms with a black crow in flight on a storm-green field. Their words have not been revealed in the books.
