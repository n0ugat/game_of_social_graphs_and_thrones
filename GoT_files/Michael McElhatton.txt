'''Michael McElhatton''' is an Irish actor and scriptwriter. He won the [[wikipedia:IFTA Award|IFTA Award for Best Actor]] in 2003 for his role in the comedy series ''[[wikipedia:Paths to Freedom|Paths to Freedom]]'', which he also co-wrote.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', McElhatton played the role of Lord [[Roose Bolton]]. He was confirmed in the role on August 10, 2011.<ref>[http://grrm.livejournal.com/233352.html?thread=14494344#t14494344 George R.R. Martin's blog]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] and returned for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third]], and [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth]], and was promoted to starring cast member for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth]], and continued on until the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth]].

He has been cast in a supporting role in the upcoming fantasy TV series ''[https://wot.fandom.com/wiki/A_beginning The Wheel of Time]'', based on the books of the same name by the late [https://wot.fandom.com/wiki/Robert_Jordan Robert Jordan], of whom [[George R.R. Martin]] was a friend.
