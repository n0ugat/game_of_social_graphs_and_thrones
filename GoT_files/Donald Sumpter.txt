'''Donald Sumpter''' (born 13 February 1943) is a British actor who has worked regularly since 1966. He has appeared three times in [[wikipedia:Doctor Who|''Doctor Who'']] and once in its spin-off, [[wikipedia:The Sarah-Jane Adventures|''The Sarah-Jane Adventures'']]. He recently had a major role as Kemp, the primary antagonist in the second season of ''[[wikipedia:Being Human (TV series)|Being Human]]'', and has also been cast as Pythol in the long-awaited ''[[wikipedia:Warhammer 40,000|Warhammer 40,000]]'' film ''[[wikipedia:Ultramarines: The Movie|Ultramarines: The Movie]]''.

He portrayed framed murder suspect Alexander Bonaparte Cust in an episode of ''[[Wikipedia:Agatha Christie's Poirot|Agatha Christie's Poirot]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Sumpter is playing [[Order of Maesters|Maester]] [[Luwin]]. He was announced in the role on 20 October 2009. He initially appeared as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and returned in this role for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
