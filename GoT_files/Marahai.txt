'''Marahai'''<ref name="Map">[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is a crescent-shaped island in the central [[Jade Sea]], off the southern coast of [[Essos]].

==In the books==
In ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', Marahai is called a paradise isle. Within its curvature are two small volcanic isles which often emit smoke and flame.
