'''House Mullendore'''<ref>[[:File:Catspaw Lineages.jpg]]</ref> of [[Uplands]] is a vassal house from the [[Reach]] that holds fealty to Lord [[Bronn]] of [[Highgarden]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
[[Martyna Mullendore]] is mentioned in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' as the wife of [[William Baratheon]], an early member of [[House Baratheon]].<ref>''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop ([https://www.factinate.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/Catspaw-Season-1.png see image], Willam's entry is in the lower right)</ref>

==Members==
*{[[Martyna Mullendore]]} - wife of [[William Baratheon]], an early member of House Baratheon.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Mullendore is one of the principal houses of the Reach, sworn to [[House Hightower]]. In the [[Dance of the Dragons]], House Mullendore supported the [[blacks]], going against their Hightower overlords. During the [[War of the Five Kings]], House Mullendore initially declared for [[Renly Baratheon]], but went over to [[Stannis Baratheon]] following Renly's death. After the [[Battle of the Blackwater]], House Mullendore bent the knee to [[Joffrey Baratheon]].
