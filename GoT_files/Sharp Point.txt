'''Sharp Point'''{{Ref|GOT105}} is a town in the [[Crownlands]], the region controlled directly by the [[Iron Throne]] in [[King's Landing]]. The town is located north of [[Stonedance]], at the end of a peninsula where the southern portion of [[Blackwater Bay]] meets the [[Narrow Sea]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
In a rage after the [[Red Sowing]], [[Aemond Targaryen]] flies to the [[Blacks|Black]]-aligned Sharp Point on [[Vhagar]] and [[Burning of Sharp Point|burns]] it to its stones.{{Ref|HOTD208}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Sharp Point is located at the end of Massey's Hook and is the seat of [[House Bar Emmon]], a noble house sworn to [[Dragonstone]]. The castle is a large watchtower upon which a great fire burns atop.

There is no mention in the books of any event that took place in Sharp Point.
