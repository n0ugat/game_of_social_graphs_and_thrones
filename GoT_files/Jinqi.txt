'''Jinqi'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is a city in [[Yi Ti]], located directly north of the island of [[Leng]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Jinqi was the capital of the Golden Empire during the reign of the maroon emperors. They kept a martial court in the city.
