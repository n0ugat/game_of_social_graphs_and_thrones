|twitter = https://twitter.com/Markgatiss|First = "[[The Laws of Gods and Men]]"|Last = "[[The Spoils of War]]"}}'''Mark Gatiss'''​ (born 17 October, 1966) is an English actor, screenwriter and novelist. He plays the role of [[Tycho Nestoris]], an envoy from the [[Iron Bank of Braavos]], in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh]] seasons.<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2013/07/sherlock-actor-mark-gatiss-cast-in-season-4/ Winter Is Coming]</ref><ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/mark-gatiss-returning-game-thrones-season-5/]</ref>

He attended [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heighington_CE_Primary_School Heighington CE Primary School] and [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Woodham_Community_Technology_College Woodham Comprehensive School] in [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newton_Aycliffe Newton Aycliffe].<ref>[http://www.accessmylibrary.com/coms2/summary_0286-30117544_ITM Remembering Heighington's past with pride - and looking to the future.]</ref>

Gatiss is best known as a writer and actor for his involvement in the TV series ''[[wikipedia:The League of Gentlemen|The League of Gentlemen]]'', ''[[wikipedia: Doctor Who|Doctor Who]]'' and ''[[wikipedia: Sherlock (TV series)|Sherlock]]''.

He is currently starring the BBC/FX period drama series ''Taboo'', along with his ''Game of Thrones'' co-stars [[Jonathan Pryce]], [[Nicholas Blane]], [[Oona Chaplin]], [[Roger Ashton-Griffiths]], and [[Jefferson Hall]].
