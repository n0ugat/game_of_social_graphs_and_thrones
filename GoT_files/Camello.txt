'''Camello''' is a member of [[Izembaro]]'s comedic theatre [[Mummers|troupe]] in [[Braavos]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
Camello portrays [[Eddard Stark]] in the production of ''[[The Bloody Hand]]'', and is later seen by [[Arya Stark]] backstage. Arya frowns as she watches how her late father is made fun of. He portrays Eddard as crude and dim-witted.{{Ref|GOT605}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Camello. It has not been mentioned yet whether Eddard Stark's character appears in ''The Bloody Hand'', and who plays him.
