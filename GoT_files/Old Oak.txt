'''Old Oak'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in the [[Reach]]. The castle is located in the north-west of the Reach. It is north west of [[Highgarden]] on the [[Searoad]] near the border with the [[Westerlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Old Oak is the home of Ser Arys Oakheart, a noted member of the [[Kingsguard]] who accompanies [[Myrcella Baratheon]] to [[Dorne]]. He recalls that his home contains tapestries showing the Oakhearts fighting the Dornish in ancient times.
