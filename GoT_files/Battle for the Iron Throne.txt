'''''Battle for the Iron Throne''''' is a Facebook-based app game based on ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', that was launched in 2011.

To battle for the [[Iron Throne]], the player must prove his strength, skill and knowledge. Ten levels will be unlocked throughout the first season. The player must achieve a minimum score per level, or he will die and his sword will be added to the Iron Throne.
