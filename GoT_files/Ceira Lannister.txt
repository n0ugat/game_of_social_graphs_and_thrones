Lady '''Ceira Lannister'''{{Note|Pronounced the same as "Kira"}} is the mother of [[Jason Lannister|Jason]] and [[Tyland Lannister]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Ceira is the widow of a previous [[Lord of Casterly Rock]]. Her original house is unknown.{{Ref|HOTD103}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Ceira and her sons join [[Viserys I Targaryen|King Viserys]]'s hunting party in the [[Kingswood]] on Prince [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon]]'s second [[nameday]]. She socializes with [[Alicent Hightower]], [[Lynesse Hightower]], and [[Joselyn Redwyne]] in the royal tent.{{Ref|HOTD103}}

Ceira attends the opening feast of the wedding of [[Laenor Velaryon]] and [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]].{{Ref|HOTD105}}
