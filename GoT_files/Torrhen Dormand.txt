Lord '''Torrhen Dormand''' was the first [[lord]] of [[Dormand Hall]] and second head of [[House Dormand]].

==Biography==
Torrhen was the son of Ser [[Artys Dormand]] and was named after Lord [[Torrhen Stark]]. He was raised to lordship some years after inheriting Dormand Hall, which was hitherto a [[Knighthood|knightly]] fief.<ref>https://entertainment.ha.com/itm/movie-tv-memorabilia/props/tywin-lannister-charles-dance-leather-bound-histories-of-the-greater-and-lesser-houses-book-from-game-of-th/a/7366-89163.s</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Torrhen Dormand.
