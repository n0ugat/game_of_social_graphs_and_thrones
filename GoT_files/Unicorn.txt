A unicorn is a large goat-like animal, with a long single horn on its head. There are ancient tales of "Hairy Men" in [[Essos]] riding unicorns to battle<ref>* ''The World of Ice & Fire'', Beyond the Sunset Kingdom: Beyond the Free Cities (The Grasslands).
*</ref>, and [[Skagos|Skagosi]] lords are also said to ride unicorns.<ref>''The World of Ice & Fire'', Beyond the Sunset Kingdom: Beyond the Free Cities (The Grasslands).</ref>
