'''Yin'''<ref name="Map">[[:File:HotD Nine Voyages Map.png]]</ref> is a city in [[Yi Ti]].

==In the books==
In ''The World of Ice & Fire'', Yin is the capital of Yi Ti and seat of the God-Emperor. By the time of the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Yin is ruled by Bu Gai, the seventeenth azure emperor, one of three claimants to the rulership of all Yi Ti.{{Bookref|TWOI&F|8|4|1}}
