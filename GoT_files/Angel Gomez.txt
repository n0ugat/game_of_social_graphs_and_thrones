'''Ángel Gómez Fernández''' is a stuntman and horse master.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episode "[[The Spoils of War]]", "[[Eastwatch]]" and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]".
