{{stub}}'''Anette Haellmigk''' is a cinematographer working on ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.<ref>[https://library.creativecow.net/haellmigk_anette/Behind-the-Lens_Game-of-Thrones/1 ]</ref>

Her first work on the show as director of photography was for Season 3 episode 4 "[[And Now His Watch Is Ended]]"<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/cogman-tweets-anon/ ]</ref>

She was nominated for the Season 5 was nominated at the 2015 American Society of Cinematographers Awards, in the "Episode of a Regular Series" category for her work on the Season 4 finale "[[The Children]]", though she did not win.<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/game-of-thrones-nominated-at-the-asc-awards/ Game of Thrones nominated at the ASC Awards]</ref>
