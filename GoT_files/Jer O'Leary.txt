'''Jer O'Leary''' is an actor based in Dublin, Ireland.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played the role of a [[Lordsport dockhand]]. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].

He has made small appearances in movies such as ''In the Name of the Father'', ''The Boxer'', ''Angela's Ashes'', and ''In America''.
