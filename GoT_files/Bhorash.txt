'''Bhorash'''{{Con|ASOIAF}}{{Note|Misspelled as "Bhonash."}} is a ruined city in [[Essos]]. The city is located on the northern shore of [[Slaver's Bay]]. It is northeast of [[Tolos]] and west of [[Meereen]].{{Mapref|Viewer's Guide}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the city lies to the northeast of the [[Black Cliffs]]. Bhorash is connected by [[Valyrian Freehold|Valyria]]n roads to [[Mantarys]] and Tolos. It is in ruins, presumably destroyed in the [[Doom of Valyria]].
