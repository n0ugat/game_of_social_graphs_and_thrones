'''''A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms'': Season 1, Episode 1''' is the first episode of the [[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms: Season 1|first season]] of ''[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. It is the first episode of the series overall. It will premiere in 2026 on [[HBO]] and [[HBO Max]]. It was written by [[Ira Parker]] and directed by [[Owen Harris]].
