'''House Bulwer'''{{Webref|name=Spotlight |author=Unknown author |date=Unknown date |url=https://app.spotlight.com/0274-6723-1457 |title=EDDIE EYRE |website=Spotlight |access-date=July 21, 2024 }}{{Note|Axell's surname is Bulwer.}} of [[Blackcrown]] is a vassal house from the [[Reach]] sworn to [[House Hightower]]. Their seat is located southwest of [[Oldtown]].

==Known members==
*Ser {[[Axell Bulwer]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] sworn to [[Aegon II Targaryen]].
*{[[Jack Bulwer]]}, nicknamed Black Jack Bulwer, a Ranger in the [[Night's Watch]]. Killed in a [[Free Folk|wildling]] [[Sack of Mole's Town|attack on Mole's Town]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Bulwer is headed by Lady Alysanne Bulwer, a child of seven. According to semi-canon sources, their sigil is a bull's skull, bone over blood, and their words are "Death Before Disgrace".

===Known members===
*Lady Alysanne Bulwer, the Lady of Blackcrown. A young girl.
**Lord {Jon Bulwer}, her late father. The previous lord of the house, died of a summer fever.
**Victaria Tyrell, her mother and Jon's widow.
**Her brother.
With unspecified familiar relationship with the lord of the house there are also:
*{Black Jack Bulwer}, one of the rangers at Castle Black. Killed by the Weeper.
