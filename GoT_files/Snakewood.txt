'''Snakewood'''<ref>[[:File:Cersei Westeros Map Puzzle.jpg]]</ref> is a castle on the [[Fingers]] in the [[Vale of Arryn]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Snakewood is located within the snakewood forest.<ref>''A Song of Ice and Fire Campaign Guide''</ref> In ''A Storm of Swords'', [[Sansa Stark|Alayne Stone]] stops at Snakewood while traveling to the [[Eyrie]].<ref>''A Storm of Swords, Chapter 68''</ref>
