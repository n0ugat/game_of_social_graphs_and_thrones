'''Mark Huffam''' is a Irish producer. He was awarded the CBE (Commander of the Order of the British Empire) in the 2011 Queen's Birthday Honours List for his services to the film and television industries. He is a producer at Pinewood Studios, Pinewood Road, Iver, England.

Mark Huffam was the producer of the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] of the HBO series ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.
