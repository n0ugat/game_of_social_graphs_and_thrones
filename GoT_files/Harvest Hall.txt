'''Harvest Hall'''{{Ref|GOT806}} is a settlement in the [[Dornish Marches]], serving as the seat of [[House Selmy]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
Harvest Hall is mentioned in [[Barristan Selmy]]'s entry in the ''[[Book of Brothers]]''.<ref>"[[The Iron Throne]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the land around Harvest Hall is more fertile than most of the [[Stormlands]].
