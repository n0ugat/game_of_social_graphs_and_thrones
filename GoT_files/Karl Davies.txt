|Appearances = 3 episodes}}'''Karl Davies''' (born 6 August 1982) is a British actor. It was confirmed that he had joined the cast on August 10, 2011. Davies is best known for his role in the British series ''[[wikipedia:Emmerdale|Emmerdale]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Kingdom (TV series)|Kingdom]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Davies plays the newly-created role of [[Alton Lannister]]. He was announced in the role on 8 August 2011.<ref>[http://www.accesshollywood.com/skins-uk-actress-and-former-british-soap-star-join-game-of-thrones-season-2_article_51810 Access Hollywood]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
