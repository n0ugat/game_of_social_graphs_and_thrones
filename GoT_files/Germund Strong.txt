'''Germund Strong''' is a member of [[House Strong]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Germund is a grandson of Ser [[Simon Strong]]{{Con|F&B}} and either the brother or cousin of [[Paxter Strong]].

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Germund is present when [[Daemon Targaryen]] arrives at [[Harrenhal]] to [[Taking of Harrenhal|claim]] the castle for [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]]. He later eats supper with Daemon, Simon, and [[Paxter Strong]].{{Ref|HOTD203}}
