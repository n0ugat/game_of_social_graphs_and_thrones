}}'''Tara Anne Cassandra Fitzgerald''' (born 18 September 1967) is a British actress, best known for her roles in the TV series ''[[wikipedia:The Camomile Lawn|The Camomile Lawn]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Waking the Dead|Waking the Dead]]''. She has also had roles in the films ''[[wikipedia:Sirens (film)|Sirens]]'', ''[[wikipedia:The Englishman Who Went Up a Hill But Came Down a Mountain|The Englishman Who Went Up a Hill But Came Down a Mountain]]'', and ''[[wikipedia:Brassed Off|Brassed Off]]''.

It was revealed in a newspaper article that Fitzgerald would be joining the cast of ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' in its [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]].<ref>[http://www.standard.co.uk/news/londoners-diary/why-tara-fitzgerald-has-the-mindset-of-an-athlete-7896158.html "Why Tara Fitzgerald Has the Mindset of an Athlete, London Evening Standard, 28 June 2012]</ref> She was later confirmed as playing [[Selyse Baratheon]].<ref name="ComicCon">[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/Massive_Comic-Con_Casting_Reveals/ "Massive Comic-Con Casting Reveals" from Westeros.org, 13 July 2012]</ref>

Fitzgerald previously starred in the TV series ''[[wikipedia:The Body Farm (TV series)|The Body Farm]]'' along with [[wikipedia:Keith Allen (actor)|Keith Allen]], real life father of actor [[Alfie Allen]].
