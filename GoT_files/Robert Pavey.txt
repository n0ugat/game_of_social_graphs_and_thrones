'''Robert Pavey''' is a stuntman and actor. He is the brother of [[James Pavey]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[High Sparrow (episode)|High Sparrow]]" and "[[Sons of the Harpy (episode)|Sons of the Harpy]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]".
