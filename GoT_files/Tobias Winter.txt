'''Tobias Winter''' is an actor. In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' and plays the role of [[Timett]]. He initially appeared uncredited as a featured extra in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and returned in this role for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]]. He had his first lines and a guest starring credit in the second season episode "[[What Is Dead May Never Die]]".

He had a role in ''[[wikipedia:Your Highness|Your Highness]]''.
