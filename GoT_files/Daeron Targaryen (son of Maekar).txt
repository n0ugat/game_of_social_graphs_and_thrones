Prince '''Daeron Targaryen'''{{Note|[[High Valyrian]]: ''Dāeron Targārien''}} is a [[prince]] of the [[House Targaryen|Targaryen dynasty]].

==Biography==
Daeron is the firstborn son of [[Maekar Targaryen]] and [[Dyanna Dayne]].

==Family==
{{Targaryen tree (A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms)}}

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Daeron has sallow skin, sandy brown hair, and a blond beard. Known as "Daeron the Drunken," he is not much of a [[Knighthood|knight]], disliking horses and swords.
