}}'''Jóhannes Haukur Jóhannesson''' is an Icelandic actor. He is best known for his roles in the Icelandic film ''Svartur á leik'' and the American television series ''A.D. The Bible Continues''. In his country, he has played a number of roles in musical theater performances of ''Les Miserables'', ''Little Shop of Horrors'', ''Hair'', ''Grease'' and ''The Rocky Horror Picture Show''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrays [[Lem]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].
