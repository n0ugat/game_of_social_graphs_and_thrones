'''Alden''' was an individual who lived during the [[Age of Heroes]].

==Behind the scenes==
On February 10, 2024, Wake the Dragon leaked storyboards from the [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]] of ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', one of which depicted a character named Alden helping another character named [[Sylvi Stark|Sylvi]] exit a gondola at [[Casterly Rock]] as members of [[House Casterly]] arrive.{{Webref|name=WTD |author=Wake the Dragon |date=February 10, 2024 |url=https://twitter.com/WtDasoiaf/status/1756284710118998245 |title=üö®üêâ Cancelled GoT prequel 'BLOOD MOON' storyboards |website=X |access-date=February 11, 2024 }}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and its supplementary material, there is no mention of a character named Alden. He was an invention of [[Jane Goldman]] for ''Bloodmoon''.
