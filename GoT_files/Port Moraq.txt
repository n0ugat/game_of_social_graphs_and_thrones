'''Port Moraq''' is a city on the southern coast of [[Great Moraq]].<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Port Moraq sits directly south of a dense rainforest. It is a popular stop for traders returning from the [[Jade Sea]].
