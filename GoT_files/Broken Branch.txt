The '''Broken Branch'''{{Mapref|Viewer's Guide}} is a river in the [[North]]. The river is so-called for its winding and branching nature. It flows out of the forests and hills near [[Hornwood]] into the [[Bite]]. The castle of [[Ramsgate]] sits near its mouth.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Broken Branch's headwaters are in the Sheepshead Hills.
