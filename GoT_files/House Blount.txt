'''House Blount'''{{Ref|H&L411}}{{Note|Boros's surname is Blount.}} is a [[Noble house|vassal house]] from the [[Crownlands]]. It holds fealty directly to the elected monarchs of the [[Seven Kingdoms|Six Kingdoms]].

==Members==
* Ser {[[Boros Blount]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] of the [[Kingsguard]]. Killed by [[Sandor Clegane]] during the [[Battle of King's Landing]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Blount is a vassal House sworn to the [[Iron Throne]]. They blazon their arms with a red bend sinister between two black porcupines on green.
