'''Sheila Atim''' (born 1991) is an English actress, singer, composer, and model born in Uganda, Africa. She is best known for her {{WPS|Laurence Olivier Award}}-winning role as Marianne Laine in {{WPS|Conor McPherson}}'s ''{{WPS|Girl from the North Country (musical)|Girl from the North Country}}''.

She portrayed a character in the [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]] of ''[[Bloodmoon]]''.<ref>[https://www.thewrap.com/game-of-thrones-prequel-cast-naomi-ackie-jamie-campbell-bower-toby-regbo/ ‘Game of Thrones’ Prequel Adds 8 to Cast]</ref>
