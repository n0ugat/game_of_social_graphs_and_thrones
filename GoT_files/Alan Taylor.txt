'''Alan Taylor''' (born 13 January 1959) is an American Emmy winning director. He has directed extensively for television and has also directed three theatrically-released movies, ''[[wikipedia:Palookaville (film)|Palookaville]]'', ''[[wikipedia:The Emperor's New Clothes (2001 film)|The Emperor's New Clothes]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Kill the Poor (film)|Kill the Poor]]''. For [[HBO]] he has directed episodes of ''[[wikipedia:Rome (TV series)|Rome]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Deadwood (TV series)|Deadwood]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Carnivale (TV series)|Carnivale]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Six Feet Under (TV series)|Six Feet Under]]'', ''[[wikipedia:The Sopranos|The Sopranos]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Sex and the City|Sex and the City]]''. His other TV directing credits include ''[[wikipedia:Mad Men (TV series)|Mad Men]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Lost (TV series)|Lost]]''.

For ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Taylor has directed episodes 9 and 10 of the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and was the only Season 1 director to return for [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|Season 2]], where he directed episodes 1, 2, 8 and 10. He also joined the production team as a co-executive producer for the second season.

Taylor's availability for the third season of ''Thrones'' was reduced after it was announced that he would be directing the movie ''[[wikipedia: Thor: The Dark World| Thor: The Dark World]]'' for Marvel Studios in 2012. It was initially hoped that he would still be able to direct one episode of the season,<ref>Hoare, James (March 29, 2012).  [http://www.scifinow.co.uk/interviews/game-of-thrones-season-2-director-alan-taylor-interview-exclusive/ Game of Thrones Season 2 Director Alan Taylor Interview Exclusive]. SciFiNow.</ref> but it was later revealed that the ''Thor: The Dark World'' schedule would not allow this.<ref>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/Taylor_Out_Nutter_Returns/ Westeros.org]</ref> Taylor is also directing the fifth installment of Terminator film series ''Terminator Genisys''.

On June 29, 2016, it was announced that Taylor would be returning to direct in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]].<ref>[http://www.ew.com/article/2016/06/29/game-thrones-season-7 EW: GOT Season 7 Directors]</ref>
