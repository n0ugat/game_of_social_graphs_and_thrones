'''Forbes KB''' is the stage name of '''Forbes Keir-Bucknall''' (born 29 May 1965), a London based Scottish actor. He has appeared in numerous movies and TV series, including the [[wikipedia:Michael Caine|Michael Caine]] movie ''[[wikipedia:Harry Brown (film)|Harry Brown]]'', where he appeared alongside fellow ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' actors [[Iain Glen]], [[Liam Cunningham]] and [[David Bradley]].

In ''Game of Thrones'' he is playing the role of [[Black Lorren]]. He was announced in the role on 19 August 2011.<ref>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/The_Ironman_and_the_Director/ Westeros.org]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
