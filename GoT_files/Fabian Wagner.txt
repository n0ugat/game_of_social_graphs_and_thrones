'''Fabian Wagner''' (born 25 April 1978) is a German cinematographer working on ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' and ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.

Wagner was nominated at the 2015 American Society of Cinematographers Awards in the "Episode of a Regular Series" category for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|Season 4]] episode "[[Mockingbird]]", but did not win.  Wagner was again nominated that the 2016 ASC Awards for "Episode of a Regular Series", for the episode "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]".<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/game-of-thrones-nominated-at-the-asc-awards/ Game of Thrones nominated at the ASC Awards]</ref>
