'''Lambswold'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a village in the [[Riverlands]], part of the demesne of [[House Bracken]].{{Ref|HOTD205}}

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Lambswold is brutally raided by [[House Blackwood]] after [[Amos Bracken]] refuses to surrender to [[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]].{{Ref|HOTD205}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lambswold is located near the Inn of the Kneeling Man. It is not specified which house the village belongs to.
