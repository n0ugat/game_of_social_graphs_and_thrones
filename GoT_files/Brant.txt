'''Brant''' is a sworn brother of the [[Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Brant's dialogue indicates that he is probably from [[White Harbor]].{{Ref|GOT507}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Brant and his friend [[Derek]] are present during the funeral for [[Order of Maesters|Maester]] [[Aemon]]. [[Samwell Tarly|Sam]] notices them staring at him hatefully after being warned of such dangers by [[Alliser Thorne]]. During their guard duty, he and Derek attempt to rape [[Gilly]]. After severely beating Sam, who intervened, they are scared off by a snarling [[Ghost]].{{Ref|GOT507}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no Brant or any analogous character.
