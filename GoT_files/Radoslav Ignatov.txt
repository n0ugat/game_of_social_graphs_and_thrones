'''Radoslav Borislavov Ignatov''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[Sons of the Harpy (episode)|Sons of the Harpy]]", "[[The Gift]]", "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]" and "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[Book of the Stranger]]" and "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[Dragonstone (episode)|Dragonstone]]", "[[Eastwatch]]", "[[Beyond the Wall (episode)|Beyond the Wall]]" and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[The Long Night]]", "[[The Last of the Starks]]" and "[[The Bells]]".
