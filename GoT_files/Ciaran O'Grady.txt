'''​Ciaran O'Grady '''​is an Irish ​​actor, stage combat instructor, stunt performer, and fight director. He is regularly cast in violent roles and has appeared in numerous TV shows and movies such as ''[[wikipedia:The Tudors|The Tudors]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Ripper Street|Ripper Street]]'', ''Dawn of the Dragonslayer'', and ''Saving the Titanic''.

He currently teaches stage combat at the Gaiety School of Acting and the Lir Academy in Dublin. He has choreographed fights for various stage and screen productions including Digging for Fire (Rough Magic Theatre Co.), Big Maggie (Druid Theatre Co.), White Star of the North (Lyric Theatre) and Irish band Kodaline's music video for their hit song "All I Want". He has also performed many stunts for the historical drama ''[[wikipedia:Vikings|Vikings]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]],'' O'Grady plays [[Frey soldier 1]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] [[Mhysa|finale]].
