A '''Lordsport dockhand''' lives in the [[Iron Islands]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
He is working when [[Theon Greyjoy|Theon]] arrives in [[Lordsport]]. He is unmoved by Theon introducing himself as the heir to the [[Iron Islands]] and last living son of [[Balon Greyjoy]]. Theon pays him to find a horse to take him to [[Pyke]]. [[Yara Greyjoy|Yara]] offers to take Theon, and Theon pays the dockhand a little more to ensure that his belongings are sent to Pyke when they are unloaded.{{Ref|GOT202}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no equivalent character. Theon pays the captain of the ''[[Myraham]]'' to forward his belongings; as he is about to buy a horse from an innkeeper, he is met by his uncle [[Aeron Greyjoy|Aeron]].
