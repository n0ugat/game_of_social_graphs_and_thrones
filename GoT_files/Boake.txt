'''Boake''' was a member of the [[Faith Militant]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Boake is a member of the [[Faith Militant]], the recently reinstated military arm of the [[Faith of the Seven]].{{Ref|GOT504}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Boake is one of the Faith Militant who ransacks [[Littlefinger's brothel]].{{Ref|GOT504}}

Boake escorts Queen Mother [[Cersei Lannister]] when she visits Queen [[Margaery Tyrell]] in her cell in the [[Great Sept of Baelor]]. He then escorts her to the [[High Sparrow]], who thanks him and dismisses him.{{Ref|GOT507}}

He escorts [[Septa]] [[Unella]] when she comes to Cersei's cell to tempt her to confess her sins with the promise of water. He opens the door to Cersei's cell for [[Qyburn]] also.{{Ref|GOT508}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
Boake is inside the [[Great Sept of Baelor|Great Sept]] for the trials of [[Loras Tyrell|Loras]] and Cersei, standing directly behind Loras who is beside Margaery seconds before it explodes with [[wildfire]], killing everyone inside.{{Ref|GOT610}}

==Behind the scenes==
The character may be named after Robert Boake, one of the [[Filming locations (Game of Thrones)|filming location]] managers for the TV series.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Boake.
