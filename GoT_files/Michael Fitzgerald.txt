| twitter = https://twitter.com/michealfitzg/|First = "[[The Broken Man]]"|Last = "[[The Last of the Starks]]"}}'''Michael Fitzgerald''' is an Irish actor. He has appeared in the television series ''{{WPS|Home and Away}}'' and ''{{WPS|Reign (TV series)|Reign}}''. He is also one of the creators of the ''{{WPS|Star Wars}}'' fan film ''Stormtroopers''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a [[House Glover|Glover]] general in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[The Broken Man]]" and "[[The Winds of Winter]]" and a Knight of the [[Vale of Arryn|Vale]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]", "[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms (Game of Thrones)|A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]", "[[The Long Night]]" and "[[The Last of the Starks]]".
