'''Rebecca Benson''' is a Scottish actress. She stars in HBO's ''Game of Thrones'' as [[Talla Tarly]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].

Benson graduated from the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland in 2011.

She previously appeared as s Eli in the National Theatre of Scotland’s ''Let the Right One In'', which later transferred to London and New York.

She appeared in the film ''[http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1672845/?ref_=nm_flmg_act_8 You Instead]'' alongside [[Natalia Tena]], who portrays [[Osha]].

Benson has also guested on episodes of ''Shetland'', ''Vera'', and ''Holby City''.

More recently she appeared in the 2015 ''Macbeth'' film adaptation starring Michael Fassbender.
