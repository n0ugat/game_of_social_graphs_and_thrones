'''Meera Baratheon''' was the wife of [[Symeon Baratheon]] and the mother of [[Lyonel Baratheon]]. Through Lyonel she is the great-great-grandmother of [[Robert Baratheon|Robert]], [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]] and [[Renly Baratheon]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She is mentioned in [[Lyonel Baratheon]]'s entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. It is unknown which house she was from and what her name was before she married [[Symeon Baratheon]].{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
