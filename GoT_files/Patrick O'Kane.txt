|imdb = http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0641433/|status = }}'''Patrick O'Kane''' is an Irish actor born in Belfast. He has appeared in TV shows such as ''The Borgias'' and ''The Fall''. He's also been in films ''Exorcist: The Beginning'' and the Danish film ''Rembrandt'' alongside fellow ''Game of Thrones'' cast member [[Nikolaj Coster-Waldau]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he portrayed [[Jaqen H'ghar]] after the character had changed his appearance. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
