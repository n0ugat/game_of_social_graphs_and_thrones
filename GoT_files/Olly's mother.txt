'''Olly's mother''' was a farmer who lived in a village close to the [[Wall]] with her husband [[Guymon]] and her son [[Olly]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
After her husband is killed in a [[Free Folk|wildling]] attack on their village, she orders her son to hide. She is then killed by [[Styr]]. He scares Olly by saying he will eat his dead parents.{{Ref|GOT403}}

During the [[battle for the Wall]], she is avenged by [[Jon Snow]].{{Ref|GOT409}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character parallel to her.
