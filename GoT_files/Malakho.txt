'''Malakho''' is an old [[Dothraki]] warrior in the ''[[khalasar]]'' of [[Daenerys Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Malakho is an aged Dothraki in ''[[Khal]]'' [[Drogo]]'s ''[[khalasar]]''. After ''Ko'' [[Pono]] and ''Ko'' [[Jhaqo]] abandoned the ''khalasar'' following Drogo's incapacitation and death, Malakho remained. After Daenerys hatched her three [[dragon]] eggs, Malakho pledged loyalty to the ''[[khaleesi]]''.{{Ref|GOT201}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
Malakho can be seen in the background when Daenerys's silver horse dies of exhaustion in the [[Red Waste]],{{Ref|GOT201}} and when the the [[Thirteen]] receive Daenerys at the gates of [[Qarth]].{{Ref|GOT204}} Malakho attends the reception for Daenerys in the home of [[Xaro Xhoan Daxos]]. He and [[Kovarro]] have to be prevented from stealing Xaro's statuary.{{Ref|GOT205}} He guards Daenerys during her return from a meeting with the [[Spice King]] and is present when she discovers the theft of her dragons.{{Ref|GOT206}} He guards Daenerys's room after the theft.{{Ref|GOT207}}

After the duplicity of Xaro Xhoan Daxos is discovered, Malakho is the one who forces the would-be [[King of Qarth]] into his empty vault.{{Ref|GOT210}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Malakho.
