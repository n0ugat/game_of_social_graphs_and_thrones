'''Greenshield'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is one of the four [[Shield Islands]], west of the [[Reach]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Greenshield is ruled by House Chester. After he is elected King of the Isles and the North, [[Euron Greyjoy]] conquers the island and names Maron Volmark its new lord.
