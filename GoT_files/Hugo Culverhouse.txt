'''Hugo Culverhouse''' is a young actor from the UK. ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' was his first major project. He played the role of the [[Red Keep]] [[stableboy]] who tries to stop [[Arya Stark]] fleeing the castle and is killed by her in the [[The Pointy End|eighth episode]] of the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]]. He has since appeared as the 'First Croney' in the feature film ''[[wikipedia:Will (2011 TV series)|Will]]''.
