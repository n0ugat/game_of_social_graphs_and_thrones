Princess '''Jaehaera Targaryen''' is a [[prince]]ss of the [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|Targaryen dynasty]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Jaehaera is the daughter of King [[Aegon II Targaryen]] and Queen [[Helaena Targaryen]]. She is the twin sister of Prince [[Jaehaerys Targaryen (son of Aegon II)|Jaehaerys Targaryen]].{{Guideref|Character Guide}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Jaehaera is playing with her brother as their mother is doing her needlework.{{Ref|HOTD109}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Jaehaera is playing in the [[Red Keep]]'s nursery with her mother and servants present, [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon]] enters the room, searching for her twin brother, she does not acknowledge the presence of her father. Jaehaera is abed when [[Blood]] and [[Cheese]] stumble upon her bedchamber, threaten her mother and [[Assassination of Jaehaerys Targaryen|murder]] her [[Jaehaerys Targaryen (son of Aegon II)|twin brother]], she is awoken by her mother and they flee the room.{{Ref|HOTD201}}

After her brother's death, Jaehaera is passed over as heir to the throne in favor of Aegon's brother [[Aemond Targaryen|Aemond]].{{Ref|HOTD205}}

==Behind the scenes==
[[Lulu Barker]] began playing Jaehaera in Season 2. Her sister, [[Ziggy Barker|Ziggy]], previously portrayed the infant [[Helaena Targaryen]] in Season 1.

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Jaehaera was born in 123 AC, the twin sister of Jaehaerys. She did not cry or smile or act as babies normally did, and her lack of emotion continued as she grew older.
