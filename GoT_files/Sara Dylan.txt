'''Sara Dylan''' is an actress based in Ireland. She starred in the independent film ''Fiddler's Walk'' in 2006. In 2009 she appeared in the canabalistic slasher film ''Gnaw''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' she is playing the role of [[Bernadette]]. She joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] and continued in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh]] seasons.
