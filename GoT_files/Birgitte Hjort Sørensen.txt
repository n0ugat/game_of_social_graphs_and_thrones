'''Birgitte Hjort Sørensen''' (born January 16, 1982) is a Danish actress. She is best known for the TV show ''Borgen'' and the films ''Marie Krøyer'', ''Automata'', and ''Pitch Perfect 2''.

In [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] she portrays the wildling chieftess [[Karsi]].<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/photos-released-from-game-of-thrones-season-5-episode-8-hardhome/]</ref><ref>[https://twitter.com/GoTKingsLanding/status/604010934965780481]</ref>

According to her Twitter account, her name is pronounced "Beer-GI-deh Yord SIR-ehn-sehn (ish)". Ir is pronounced like in the word "''stir".''
