'''Oxcross'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} is a village in the [[Westerlands]]. It is located behind the defensive mountain range that protects the border with the [[Riverlands]] and is thought to be protected by the stronghold [[Golden Tooth]]. It is the site of [[Battle of Oxcross|a major battle]] in the [[War of the Five Kings]].<ref>"[[Garden of Bones (episode)|Garden of Bones]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Oxcross is three days ride from [[Lannisport]].
