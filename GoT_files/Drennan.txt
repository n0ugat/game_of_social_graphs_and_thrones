'''Drennan'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} was an [[Ironborn]] raider and part of the crew of the ''[[Sea Bitch]]''.

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
Drennan is a crewman of the ''Sea Bitch'', the ship [[Theon Greyjoy|Theon]]'s father [[Balon Greyjoy]] gave him to raid the [[Stony Shore]]. He takes part in the [[Capture of Winterfell|seizing]] of [[Winterfell]]. When the people of the castle are brought together in the courtyard for [[Bran Stark|Bran]] to officially surrender to Theon, [[Farlen]] yells insults at Theon, and Drennan hits him with a club.{{Ref|GOT206}}

[[File:Osha kills.jpg|thumb|left|Osha kills Drennan.]]
Afterwards, Drennan is assigned guard duty. During the night, Osha seduces him and slits his throat, allowing Bran and [[Rickon Stark|Rickon]] to escape Winterfell alongside [[Hodor]], [[Summer]] and [[Shaggydog]] and Osha herself.{{Ref|GOT206}}

[[File:Theon beats Lorren.jpg|thumb|Theon beats [[Black Lorren]] next to Drennan's dead body.]]
Drennan's body is discovered and brought into the courtyard the following day.{{Ref|GOT207}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Drennan's background is the same.

After Winterfell is taken by the ironborn, he and another ironborn rape [[Palla]], the daughter of Farlen. Theon orders them whipped to show the people of Winterfell that he will treat them fairly as their new lord. 

To ease the escape of Bran, Rickon, Hodor, Summer, and Shaggydog, Osha comes to him with a plate of food. He is found slumped over a table, with his pants around his ankles and his throat cut with his own knife.
