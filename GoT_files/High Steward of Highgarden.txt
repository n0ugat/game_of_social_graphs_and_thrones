The '''High Steward of Highgarden'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} was a title held by [[House Tyrell]].

The [[Andals]] [[Coming of the Andals|invaded]] [[Westeros]], with [[Alester Tyrell]] leading an army to [[Highgarden]]. Rather than battle them, [[House Gardener]] welcomed the Tyrells, naming them the High Stewards of Highgarden to sate their ambition.<ref>"[[Highgarden (short)|Highgarden]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the title of High Steward of Highgarden was abolished when [[House Tyrell]] were awarded [[Highgarden]] and the titles of [[Lord Paramount of the Reach|Lord Paramount of the Mander]] and [[Warden of the South|Wardens of the South]] for submitting to King [[Aegon I Targaryen]].

Known High Stewards of Highgarden:

*Gareth Tyrell.
*Leo Tyrell, Gareth's son and successor.
*Ser Osmund Tyrell, High Steward to King Garth X Gardener and later King Mern VI Gardener.
*Ser Robert Tyrell, High Steward to King Mern VI, Osmund's son and successor.
*Lorent Tyrell, High Steward to King Mern VI, Robert's son and successor.
*Harlen Tyrell, High Steward to King [[Mern IX Gardener]]. Last High Steward of Highgarden, raised to Lord Paramount of the Mander.
