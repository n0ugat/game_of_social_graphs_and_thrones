'''Master-at-arms''',{{Ref|GOT103}} or '''master of arms''',{{Ref|GOT704}} is a title hold by a man, usually a [[Knighthood|knight]], responsible for the training of soldiers in a castle in peacetime. This can includes sons and [[ward]]s of the lord he serves. In wartime, he is actively involved in leading the defence of the castle along with the [[captain of the guard]] and the lord himself.

==Known masters-at-arms==
* Lord {[[Quenton Qoherys]]}, master-at-arms at [[Dragonstone]]. Later promoted to the rank of [[Lord of Harrenhal]] by [[Aegon I Targaryen]].
*Ser {[[Alliser Thorne]]}, former master-at-arms at [[Castle Black]]. Executed by hanging by [[Jon Snow]] for treason.
*Ser {[[Rodrik Cassel]]}, master-at-arms at [[Winterfell]]. Executed by [[Theon Greyjoy]].
* {[[Locke]]}, Master-at-Arms at the [[Dreadfort]]. Sent to the [[Wall]] and neck snapped by [[Bran Stark]] while warging into [[Hodor]].
* Ser [[Samwell Stone]], master-at-arms at [[Runestone]].
* [[Endrew Tarth]], master-at-arms at Castle Black.
* Unidentified master-at-arms at [[Bear Island]].
