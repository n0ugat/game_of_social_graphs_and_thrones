'''''Khaleesi'' of the Great Grass Sea'''{{Ref|GOT307}}{{Note|[[Dothraki (language)|Dothraki]]: ''Khaleesi Havazhofi Hranni''}} is a title claimed by [[Daenerys Targaryen]]. The [[Dothraki Sea|Great Grass Sea]] is the region in [[Essos]] where the [[Dothraki]] roam, and ''[[khaleesi]]'' is the title given to the wife of a ''[[khal]]'' in [[Dothraki]] culture.

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Daenerys became a ''[[khaleesi]]'' upon her marriage to [[Drogo]] as arranged by [[Viserys Targaryen (son of Aerys II)|Viserys Targaryen]] and [[Illyrio Mopatis]], a powerful ''[[khal]]'' who commanded a ''[[khalasar]]'' at least 40,000 strong.<ref>"[[Winter Is Coming]]"</ref> Drogo eventually impregnated Daenerys, and it was believed by the ''[[Dosh Khaleen]]'' that her son, [[Rhaego]], would be the [[stallion who mounts the world]].<ref>"[[A Golden Crown]]"</ref> After Drogo's death, Daenerys, whose son is stillborn, assumes control of the remnants of his ''khalasar'' that remain loyal to her.<ref>"[[Fire and Blood]]"</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
Daenerys is brought before [[Khal]] [[Moro]], who states that she must return to [[Vaes Dothrak]] to live among with ''[[khaleesi]]'' widows of the ''[[Dosh Khaleen]]''.<ref>"[[The Red Woman]]"</ref> At the meeting of the ''[[Khalar Vezhven]]'', however, Daenerys [[Massacre of the Khalar Vezhven|burns]] the gathered ''[[khal]]s'' and unites their ''[[khalasar]]s'' behind her.<ref>"[[Book of the Stranger]]"</ref> For the first time in the [[history]] of the [[Dothraki]], they cross the [[Narrow Sea]], following Daenerys.<ref>"[[The Winds of Winter]]"</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
When [[Jon Snow]] is brought before Daenerys at [[Dragonstone]], [[Missandei]] formally introduces her with her many aliases and titles, ''[[Khaleesi]]'' of the [[Dothraki Sea|Great Grass Sea]] among them.<ref>"[[The Queen's Justice]]"</ref> The ''khaleesi'' Daenerys, mounted atop [[Drogon]], later uses her united [[Dothraki]] ''[[khalasar]]'' to attack the [[House Lannister|Lannister]]-[[House Tarly|Tarly]] army at the [[Battle of the Goldroad]].<ref>"[[The Spoils of War]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels by [[George R.R. Martin]], similar to the [[Game of Thrones|TV series]], ''[[Khaleesi]]'' of the [[Dothraki Sea|Great Grass Sea]] is one of the many titles claimed by [[Daenerys Targaryen]].
