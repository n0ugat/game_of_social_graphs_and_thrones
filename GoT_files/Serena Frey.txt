Lady '''Serena Frey'''{{Webref|name=Charlist |author=HeroOfRodden |date=September 30, 2023 |url=https://redanianintelligence.com/2023/09/30/every-new-cast-member-house-of-the-dragon-season-2/ |title=List: Every New Cast Member of House of the Dragon Season 2 So Far |website=Redanian Intelligence |access-date=July 15, 2024 }} is the wife of Lord [[Forrest Frey]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Serena and her husband [[Forrest Frey|Forrest]] treat with Prince [[Jacaerys Velaryon]] at the [[Twins]]. She swears allegiance to [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] in exchange for the promise of being granted [[Harrenhal]] after the war.{{Ref|HOTD205}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', the wife of Forrest Frey was named Sabitha, originally of House Vypren. She was considered merciless and grasping, and was rumored to prefer women over men.
