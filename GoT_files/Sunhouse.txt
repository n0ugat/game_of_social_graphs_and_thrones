'''Sunhouse''',{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} also known as '''Sunflower Hall''',{{Ref|GOT701}} is a castle in the [[Reach]], the region controlled by [[House Tyrell]] of [[Highgarden]]. The castle is located on the south coast of [[Westeros]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels Sunhouse is the seat of House Cuy and is located on the [[Summer Sea]] near the mouth of the Torentine. It is called "Sunflower Hall" in the appendix of ''A Feast for Crows''.
