'''Rosby'''{{Ref|GOT701}} is a settlement in the [[Crownlands]], consisting of a small{{Ref|HOTD201}} castle and a surrounding town. It is the seat of [[House Rosby]], a vassal house holding fealty to [[House Baratheon of King's Landing]]. The castle is located north of [[King's Landing]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels Rosby is surrounded by a town of daub and wattle huts. It sits on the road from King's Landing to [[Duskendale]] and is surrounded by rich farms, which grow a lot of the food that helps feed the capital.
