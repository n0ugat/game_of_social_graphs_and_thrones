'''Son of the Sea Wind'''{{Ref|H&L202}} is one of the self-styled titles held by the head of [[House Greyjoy]], ruler of the [[Ironborn]] and [[Lord of the Iron Islands]]. It was first held by [[Lordship|Lord]] [[Vickon Greyjoy]]{{Ref|H&L209}} and is currently held by Lady [[Yara Greyjoy]].
