'''Kelvyn''' is a [[Order of Maesters|maester]] of the [[Citadel]] in service to [[House Velaryon]] at [[High Tide]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
After the funeral of [[Laena Velaryon]], Kelvyn tends to [[Aemond Targaryen|Aemond]]'s eye injury, [[Lucerys Velaryon|Luke]]'s broken nose, and [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]]'s cut.{{Ref|HOTD107}}

Kelvyn informs [[Rhaenys Targaryen]] that [[Corlys Velaryon|Corlys]] has been gravely injured in the [[Stepstones]].{{Ref|HOTD108}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Kelvyn. The name of the maester in service of House Velaryon during the rule of Corlys Velaryon is unknown.
