Ser '''Rymun Mallister''' is a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Mallister]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Rymun is the son of Lord [[Lymond Mallister]] of [[Seagard]]. He was knighted at the age of 18. He won a melee at [[Cider Hall]].{{Ref|HOTD102}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Rymun is brought before [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] in [[Maegor's Holdfast]] as a candidate to replace Ser [[Ryam Redwyne]] on the [[Kingsguard/House of the Dragon|Kingsguard]]. On account of his lack of real combat experience, Ser [[Criston Cole]] is chosen instead.{{Ref|HOTD102}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Rymun Mallister, and it is unknown whether Lymond Mallister had any children.
