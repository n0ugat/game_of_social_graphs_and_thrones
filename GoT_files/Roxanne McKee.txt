'''Roxanne McKee''' (born 10 August 1980) is an English model and actress. After a successful modelling career, McKee was cast in the role of [[wikipedia:Louise Summers|Louise Summers]] in the British soap ''[[wikipedia:Hollyoaks|Hollyoaks]]'' in 2004, after impressing producers during an open audition. She played the role until 2008 in which the character was killed off, intending to have a temporary departure. However, Roxanne never returned to the role and the character's death was confirmed in December 2011.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' she plays the role of [[Doreah]]. She was confirmed in the role on 28 July 2010. She initially appeared as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and returned in this role for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
