'''Moelle'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} is a [[septa]] of the [[Faith of the Seven]] stationed at the [[Great Sept of Baelor]] in [[King's Landing]]. 

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Moelle is present along with Septas [[Unella]] and [[Scolera]] during the arrest of [[Cersei Lannister|Cersei]]. She watches as Unella throws Cersei into a cell under the orders of the [[High Sparrow]].{{Ref|GOT507}}

Before Cersei performs the [[walk of atonement]], Moelle and Scolera escort and help remove her clothes.{{Ref|GOT510}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Moelle is described as having stiff white hair, small eyes, and lips twisted in perpetual disapproval.
