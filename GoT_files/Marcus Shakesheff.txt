'''Marcus Shakesheff''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]", "[[The Old Gods and the New]]", "[[A Man Without Honor]]", "[[The Prince of Winterfell]]", "[[Blackwater]]" and "[[Valar Morghulis]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episodes "[[First of His Name]]" and "[[The Laws of Gods and Men]]".

He was also a stunt double for [[Kit Harington]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[A Man Without Honor]]" and "[[The Prince of Winterfell]]".
