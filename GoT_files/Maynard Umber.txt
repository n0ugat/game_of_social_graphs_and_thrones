'''Maynard Umber''' was a member of [[House Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Maynard Umber was the son of Lord [[Joseth Umber]] and his wife, Lady [[Katrina Umber|Katrina Cerwyn]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there are several characters whose first name is Maynard, but none of them is a member of House Umber.
