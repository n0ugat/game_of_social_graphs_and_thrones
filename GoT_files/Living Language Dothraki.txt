'''''Living Language Dothraki''''' is an official teach-yourself guide for the [[Dothraki (language)|Dothraki language]]. The book was written by the inventor of the Dothraki language, [[David J. Peterson]], who was hired as the official linguist for [[HBO]]'s ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', to develop the fictional languages used in the TV series. It was produced in partnership with Living Language and HBO Global Licensing.

''Living Language Dothraki'' will come in three forms: a printed book, an online course, and an app for iOS devices.

It was released on October 7, 2014.
