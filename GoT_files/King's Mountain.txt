'''King's Mountain'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a place in the [[Stormlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, King's Mountain is the origin of Ser Patrek of King's Mountain. Patrek holds lands in the south, but it is unknown if they are at King's Mountain.

The name "King's Mountain" is a translation of "Montreal," being part of a reference to [[George R.R. Martin]]'s friend Patrick St. Denis, who is from Montreal.
