'''Allan Gildea''' is an actor and director from Northern Ireland. He has appeared in films such as ''Bloody Sunday'' and ''Good Vibrations'' (the latter alongside [[Richard Dormer]], [[Liam Cunningham]] and [[Kerr Logan]]).

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he portrays [[Samwell Stone]].
