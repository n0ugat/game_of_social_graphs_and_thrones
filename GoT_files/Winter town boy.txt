A '''Winter town boy''' is present in the [[winter town]] during the arrival of [[Daenerys Targaryen]]'s army.

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
The boy rushes from the woods into the [[winter town]] when he hears that the army of [[Daenerys Targaryen]] has arrived. Unable to see the army due to taller people standing in his way, he climbs a tree to get a better view.{{Ref|GOT801}}
