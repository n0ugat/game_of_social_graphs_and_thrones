'''Todor Petrov Lazarov''' (born March 10, 1979) is a Bulgarian stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episode "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]" and ''[[Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series]]''.
