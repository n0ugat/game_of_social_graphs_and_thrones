'''Guymon''' was a farmer who lived with his [[Olly's mother|wife]] and [[Olly|son]] in a village close to the [[Wall]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
When he and his son are on their way home for dinner, their village is attacked by a group of [[Free Folk|wildlings]]. He is killed by an arrow to the head from [[Ygritte]]. His body is later eaten by [[Styr]].{{Ref|GOT403}}

During the [[battle for the Wall]], Guymon is avenged by his son who kills Ygritte.{{Ref|GOT409}}

==Behind the scenes==
He is named after [[Guymon Casady]], the co-executive producer of the TV series.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character parallel to him.
