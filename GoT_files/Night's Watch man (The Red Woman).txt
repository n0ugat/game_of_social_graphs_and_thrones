This '''unnamed man''' is a Sworn Brother of the [[Night's Watch]], serving at [[Castle Black]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
This watchman is present when [[Alliser Thorne]] reveals that he killed [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch|Lord Commander]] [[Jon Snow]] alongside the other officers in the [[Night's Watch]]. He angrily calls the officers out as murderers, but later appears placated by Thorne's explanation for his actions.{{Ref|GOT601}}

He is present when the [[Free Folk|wildlings]] break into [[Castle Black]] and he is one of the first to surrender.{{Ref|GOT602}}
