Ser '''Jore Blackbrow''' was a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Blackbrow]]. He was the husband of [[Emilia Umber]], a daughter of Lord [[Gareth Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Jore Blackbrow was the son of Lord [[Erich Blackbrow]] and [[Conn Blackbrow]]. He married [[Emilia Umber]], who bore him two sons, [[Marek Blackbrow]] and [[Helos Blackbrow]], and a daughter, [[Emilia Blackbrow]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Jore, his parents, and his children are mentioned in his wife's entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
Jore Blackbrow is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
