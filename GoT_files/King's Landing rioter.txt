A ''' King's Landing rioter''' was killed by [[Sandor Clegane]] for attempting to rape [[Sansa Stark]] during the [[riots in King's Landing]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
He accosts [[Sansa Stark]] during the [[riots in King's Landing]]. He chases her into a back alley and grabs her. She slaps him and he punches her, breaking the skin of her cheek. He pins her on the ground and attempts to rape her. He is lifted bodily from the ground by the neck and then disemboweled by [[Sandor Clegane]].{{Ref|GOT206}} Sansa is troubled by dreams of the rioter attacking her after the event.{{Ref|GOT207}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, an unnamed rioter tries to drag Sansa from her horse and has his arm cut off by the Hound. (''A Clash of Kings'' Chapter 41, Tyrion IX)
