Lord '''Beric Dayne''' was a [[Lord of Starfall]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Beric Dayne was the Lord of Starfall and the father of Ser [[Arthur Dayne]], the [[Sword of the Morning]].{{Ref|GOT401}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
He is mentioned in his son Arthur's entry in the ''[[Book of Brothers]]'', which King [[Joffrey Baratheon]] was reading.{{Ref|GOT401}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
The new [[Lord Commander of the Kingsguard]] [[Brienne of Tarth]] while flicking through the ''Book of Brothers'', comes across Arthur Dayne's entry, which mentions Beric.{{Ref|GOT806}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Ser Arthur Dayne's father is never named or mentioned, and due to the equal primogeniture in Dorne it is unknown whether Ser Arthur was the son of a male or female ruler of Starfall. Ser Arthur's nephew Edric Dayne is the [[Lord of Starfall]] during the events of the series.
