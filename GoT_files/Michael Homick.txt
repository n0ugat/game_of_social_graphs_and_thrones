'''Michael Homick''' is a stuntman.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[The Long Night]]" and "[[The Last of the Starks]]".

He was also a stunt double for [[Hafþór Júlíus Björnsson]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[The Bells]]" and "[[The Iron Throne]]".
