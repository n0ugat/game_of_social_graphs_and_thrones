Ser '''Rodrik Velaryon''' was a [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Rodrik was a member of [[House Velaryon]]. He was elected Lord Commander of the [[Night's Watch]] after the death of [[Lord Commander Stone]]. At Maester [[Gavin]]'s counsel, he appointed [[Dagon Harlaw]] as [[First Ranger]]. He held the [[Wall]] for one year before succumbing to a chill in the night, and was succeeded by Ser [[Jasper Bracken]].<ref>[[:File:5x2 Night's Watch Book.jpg]]</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Rodrik is mentioned in a book that [[Samwell Tarly]] is reading.{{Ref|GOT502}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Rodrik Velaryon.
