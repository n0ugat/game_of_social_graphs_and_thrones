'''Burn Hugh Winchester Gorman''' is a British actor and musician. He was born in Hollywood, to English parents, while his father was a professor at the University of California.

Gorman is best known for his roles as Owen Harper in ''Torchwood'', as Gottlieb in ''Pacific Rim, ''as William Guppy in ''Bleak House'', as Phillip Stryver in ''The Dark Knight Rises'' (with [[Aidan Gillen]]), as well as appearances in British soap opera ''Coronation Street'', ''Johnny English Reborn'' (with [[Rupert Vansittart]]),  2004 film ''Layer Cake'' and the 2012 film ''Red Lights''.

He guest stars in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] of ''Game of Thrones'' as [[Karl Tanner]], a deserter of the [[Night's Watch]]. <ref name="Casting update 10-01">[http://winteriscoming.net/2013/01/season-three-casting-update/]</ref>
