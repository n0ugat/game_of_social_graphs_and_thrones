'''Will Farquarson''' (born 22 September 1983) is a British musician. He is a member of the British rock band {{WPS|Bastille (band)|Bastille}}, in which he plays guitar, bass, piano, and keyboards and sings backing vocals.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he (along with Bastille bandmates [[Dan Smith]], [[Kyle Simmons]], and [[Chris "Woody" Wood]]) portrayed a [[wight]] in "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]."<ref>[https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/tv/news/game-of-thrones-bastille-cameo-season-7-finale-the-dragon-and-the-wold-a7917566.html ''Independent'': You probably missed Bastille's cameo in the season 7 finale]</ref>
