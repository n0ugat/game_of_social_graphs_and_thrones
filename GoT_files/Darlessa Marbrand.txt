'''Darlessa Marbrand''' is a member of [[House Marbrand]], wife of [[Tygett Lannister]], and mother of his son, [[Tyrek Lannister]].<ref name="finalebook">[https://www.makinggameofthrones.com/production-diary/season-8-episode-6-series-finale-props-song-of-ice-and-fire See] the family tree of [[House Lannister]] in Archmaester [[Ebrose]]'s ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire (Ebrose)|A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' (as featured in "[[The Iron Throne]]")</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Darlessa is featured in the family tree of House Lannister in Archmaester [[Ebrose]]'s ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire (Ebrose)|A Song of Ice and Fire]]''.<ref name="finalebook" /> She is not mentioned or featured elsewhere in the series.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Darlessa is only featured in the appendices, not appearing in the story itself. As in the season 8 prop's family tree, she is the wife of Tygett Lannister and the mother of Tyrek Lannister.
