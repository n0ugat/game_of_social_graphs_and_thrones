'''Maiev Stark''' was a [[prince]]ss of [[House Stark]] who lived during the [[Age of Heroes]]. She was a relative of Princess [[Sylvi Stark|Sylvi]] and [[Stark (Bloodmoon)|another Stark]].

==Behind the scenes==
On March 27, 2019, ''Variety'' announced that [[Dixie Egerickx]] had been cast as a series regular in ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', a ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' [[World of Westeros|prequel]] [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]].{{Webref|name=Variety |author=Joe Otterson |date=March 27, 2019 |url=https://variety.com/2019/tv/news/game-of-thrones-prequel-pilot-cast-1203173822/ |title=‘Game of Thrones’ Prequel Adds Five More Series Regulars |website=Variety |access-date=December 6, 2023 }} On July 21, 2020, ''Redanian Intelligence'' revealed that her character was named Maiev.{{Webref|name=Redanian |author=Gravemaster |date=July 21, 2020 |url=https://redanianintelligence.com/2020/07/21/new-tidbits-about-the-cancelled-game-of-thrones-prequel-leaf/ |title=The cancelled Game of Thrones prequel featured a character from the original show and other new tidbits |website=Redanian Intelligence |access-date=March 30, 2023 }} On February 18, 2024, Wake the Dragon revealed that Maiev was a Stark princess.<ref name="Starks" />

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and its supplementary material, there is no mention of a character named Maiev Stark. The character was invented by [[Jane Goldman]] for ''Bloodmoon''.
