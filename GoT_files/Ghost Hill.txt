'''Ghost Hill'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in [[Dorne]], the region controlled by [[House Martell]] of [[Sunspear]]. The castle is located in the north-east of Dorne on the shores of the [[Sea of Dorne]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Ghost Hill is the seat of House Toland. It is located on the Broken Arm of Dorne.
