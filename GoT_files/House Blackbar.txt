'''House Blackbar'''{{Con|F&B}} of [[Bandallon]] is a [[noble house]] of the [[Reach]]. Their sigil displays a black fess on a silver field.<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Blackbar is part of [[Renly Baratheon]]'s army during the [[War of the Five Kings]].{{Bookref|ACOK|31}}

===Known members===
*Lord Blackbar, hosted King [[Jaehaerys I Targaryen]] at Bandallon in 89 AC.{{Bookref|F&B|11}}
**His daughters, the youngest of whom attempted to seduce King Jaehaerys.{{Bookref|F&B|11}}
*Ser Elwood Blackbar, a participant in the tourney at [[Ashford]] Meadow in 209 AC.{{Bookref|THKGN}}
*Ser Leo Blackbar, a knight.{{Bookref|ASOS|Appendix}}
**Olene Tyrell, his wife.{{Bookref|ASOS|Appendix}}
