'''Ronnel Arryn''' was the son of [[Jasper Arryn]], the head of [[House Arryn]].

His ancestor and namesake, King [[Ronnel Arryn]], lived during the [[Aegon's Conquest]] almost three centuries before.

==Biography==
Ronnel was the son of [[Jasper Arryn]], the head of [[House Arryn]]. He had two older siblings: [[Jon Arryn|Jon]] and [[Alys Arryn|Alys]].{{Guideref|Viewer's Guide}}

{{Arryn tree}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Ronnel's family was unchanged. Ronnel died around the same time his son Elbert was born, however, Elbert himself died when he was executed by the Mad King [[Aerys II Targaryen]] alongside [[Rickard Stark|Rickard]] and [[Brandon Stark]] at the beginning of [[Robert's Rebellion]], extinguishing Ronnel's line.
