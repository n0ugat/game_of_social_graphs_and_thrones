|Appearances = "[[The Watchers on the Wall]]"}}'''Neil Fingleton''' was a 7' 7" tall British actor and former basketball player. He was best known for a role in ''X-Men: First Class''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played [[Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg]] in the episode "[[The Watchers on the Wall]]".

Neil died from heart failure on February 25, 2017, at the age of 36.
