'''Jon''' is an attendant in the [[Red Keep]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Jon is a child serving as an attendant to King [[Viserys I Targaryen]].{{Ref|HOTD109}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
In the early morning, Jon finds Viserys dead in his bedchamber. He immediately goes to inform [[Talya]], Queen [[Alicent Hightower|Alicent]]'s [[handmaiden]] and [[Mysaria]]'s informant.{{Ref|HOTD109}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', the servant who found Viserys dead directly informed Alicent, not her handmaiden.
