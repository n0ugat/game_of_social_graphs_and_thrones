|twitter = https://twitter.com/kaealexander}}'''Kae Yukawa''', known professionally as '''Kae Alexander''', is an English-Japanese actress. She was born in {{WPS|Kobe}}, {{WPS|Japan}}; however, she spent parts of her childhood in {{WPS|Tokyo}} and {{WPS|Hong Kong}} as well. At age 10, her family moved to London, where they settled. She studied at the {{WPS|Guildhall School of Music & Drama}}, which she graduated from in 2011. She was awarded the Lord Mayor's Prize for Acting. She is known for her roles in ''{{WPS|Bad Education (TV series)|Bad Education}}'' and ''{{WPS|House of Anubis}}''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she portrays [[Leaf]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]], taking over from [[Octavia Alexandru]].

She has been cast in the upcoming fantasy TV series ''[https://wot.fandom.com/wiki/A_beginning The Wheel of Time]'', based on the books of the same name by the late [https://wot.fandom.com/wiki/Robert_Jordan Robert Jordan], of whom [[George R.R. Martin]] was a friend.
