'''Paul Vincent Lowe''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] episodes "[[Winter Is Coming]]", "[[Cripples, Bastards, and Broken Things]]", "[[The Wolf and the Lion]]" and "[[You Win or You Die]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episode "[[Kill the Boy]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[The Winds of Winter]]" the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[Beyond the Wall (episode)|Beyond the Wall]]" and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]", "[[The Long Night]]" and "[[The Bells]]".
