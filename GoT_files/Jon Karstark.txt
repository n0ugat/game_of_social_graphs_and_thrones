'''Jon Karstark''' was a member of [[House Karstark]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Jon Karstark was the fourthborn son of [[Tommard Karstark]] and his wife, [[Regan Karstark|Regan]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
Jon Karstark is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
