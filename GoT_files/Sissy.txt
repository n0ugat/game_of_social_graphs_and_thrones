'''Sissy''' is one of [[Craster]]'s daughters whom he has married.

==Biography==
===Background===
She is one of [[Craster]]'s daughters whom he has married.{{Ref|GOT404}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
After Craster is killed in the [[Mutiny at Craster's Keep|mutiny]], she is taken by [[Karl Tanner]], who frequently beats her. When [[Morag]] brings in [[Craster's last son]], she joins her sisters in chanting that the child is a gift for [[White Walkers|the gods]].{{Ref|GOT404}}

During a [[Raid on Craster's Keep|raid]] on [[Craster's Keep]], she stabs Karl Tanner in the back, preventing him from killing [[Jon Snow]] and giving the latter a chance to kill Karl.{{Ref|GOT405}}

==Behind the scenes==
The character's name is never mentioned onscreen or in credits. It is mentioned in the actress's CV.<ref>http://www.lisarichards.ie/actorsf/jane-mcgrath-actor#.U3hWt_l5MkK</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there's no mention of a daughter-wife of Craster called Sissy.
