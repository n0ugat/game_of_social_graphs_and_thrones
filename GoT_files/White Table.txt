The '''White Table'''{{Webref|name=WIC |author=Dan Selcke |date=February 28, 2022 |url=https://winteriscoming.net/2022/02/28/details-white-table-hbos-cancelled-game-of-thrones-spinoff/ |title=Info on “the White Table” from HBO’s cancelled Game of Thrones spinoff |website=Winter is Coming |access-date=March 1, 2022 }} was a carved [[weirwood]] table depicting the [[North]], similar to the [[Painted Table]] at [[Dragonstone]], which depicts all of [[Westeros]]. It was owned by [[House Stark]] prior to the [[Long Night]].

==Behind the scenes==
The White Table would have appeared in the canceled TV series ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', a prequel about the Long Night.<ref name="WIC" />

==In the books==
There is no mention in the books that House Stark owned such a table.
