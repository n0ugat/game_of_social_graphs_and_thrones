'''Hannah Waddingham''' (born 1974) is an English actress. She has appeared in films such as ''Les Misérables'', ''How to Lose Friends & Alienate People'' and ''The Only Boy for Me'', and TV Shows such as ''Benidorm'', but she is better known in musical theater productions, such as ''A Little Night Music'', ''Into the Woods'', ''The Wizard of Oz'' and ''Spamalot''.

In the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth]] seasons, she portrays [[Septa]] [[Unella]], a member of the [[Most Devout]], the ruling council of the [[Faith of the Seven]].<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/british-actress-cast-septa-unella/ Watchers on the Wall]</ref>

Waddingham "had a 10 week old baby in tow" when she went to Croatia to film her scenes. On her Twitter feed she posted as her background picture a photo she took in front of the Iron Throne while still in full costume, holding her baby.<ref>[http://www.vulture.com/2015/06/hannah-waddingham-on-cersei-drama-got-fashion.html ]</ref>
