'''Crow's Nest'''{{Ref|GOT105}} is a castle in the [[Stormlands]], the region controlled by [[House Baratheon]] of [[Storm's End]]. The castle is located in a wooded area east of [[Blackhaven]].<ref>"[[The Wolf and the Lion]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Crow's Nest is the seat of House Morrigen.

In ''A Dance with Dragons'', a force of the [[Golden Company]] captures Crow's Nest on behalf of the presumed-dead [[Aegon Targaryen (son of Elia Martell)|Aegon Targaryen]], the son of Rhaegar and Elia who has returned to Westeros to claim the [[Iron Throne]].
