'''Elena Egen''' was a member of [[House Egen]] and the wife of [[Andar Royce]], [[Lord of Runestone]]. She is the mother of [[Yohn Royce]], as well as of his brothers [[Maxim Royce]] and [[Waymar Royce (son of Andar)|Waymar Royce]].

==Biography==
Elena Egen was a daughter of Lord [[Xander Egen]] and [[Rusalia Egen]]. She married [[Andar Royce]] on his eighteenth nameday and bore him three sons [[Yohn Royce|Yohn]], [[Maxim Royce|Maxim]], and [[Waymar Royce (son of Andar)|Waymar Royce]].<ref name = "s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Elena Egen is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Elena Egen is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books. The name of Yohn Royce's mother has not been mentioned in the books.
