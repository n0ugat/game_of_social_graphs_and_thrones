'''Wyvern Point'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is a peninsula in northeastern [[Sothoryos]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Wyvern Point is richly populated by [[wyvern]]s, a predatory species of winged reptiles related to [[dragon]]s.
