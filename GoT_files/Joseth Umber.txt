'''Joseth Umber''' was a member of [[House Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Joseth Umber was the fifth born son of Lord [[Hothor Umber]] and his wife, Lady [[Amarylis Umber]]. On his eighteenth nameday, he married [[Katrina Umber|Katrina Cerwyn]], with whom he had two children. He died at the age of forty from wounds sustained in a tourney melee.{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
