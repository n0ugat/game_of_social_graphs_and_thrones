'''Irene Kelleher''' is as Irish actress. She has appeared in the short films ''Outside the Box'', ''Hatch'', ''The Trouble with Aoibhe'', ''Sonnet 90'' and ''Spellbound''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she portrayed an inn waitress in the episode "[[The House of Black and White]]".
