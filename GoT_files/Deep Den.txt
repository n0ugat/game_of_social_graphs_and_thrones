'''Deep Den'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in the [[Westerlands]], the region controlled by [[House Lannister]] of [[Casterly Rock]]. The castle is located just off the [[Goldroad]] to the east of Casterly Rock, between [[Hornvale]] and [[Silverhill]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Deep Den is the seat of House Lydden.
