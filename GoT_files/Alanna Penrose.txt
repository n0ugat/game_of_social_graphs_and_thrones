'''Alanna Penrose''' was a member of [[House Penrose]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Alanna Penrose [[Marriage|married]] [[Axel Baratheon]], a son of [[Orys Baratheon|Orys]] and [[Argella Baratheon]], in 20 AC. 24 years later, Axel fought a duel with [[Steffon Trant]] to defend Alanna's honor, but was defeated and succumbed to his wounds. Alanna and Axel had no children.{{Ref|GOT106}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She is referenced in her husband's entry of ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character whose first name is Alanna, either of [[House Penrose]] or any other Westerosi house.
