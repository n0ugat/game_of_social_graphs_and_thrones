Ser '''Wyllis Bracken''' is a [[Knighthood|knight]] from the [[Riverlands]] and a member of [[House Bracken]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
[[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]] tells [[Bronn]] that [[Cersei Lannister|Cersei]] has broken the arrangement to wed [[Lollys Stokeworth]] to him; instead, Lollys will marry Ser Wyllis.{{Ref|GOT502}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of a character named Ser Wyllis Bracken, and Bronn is the one who marries Lollys Stokeworth.
