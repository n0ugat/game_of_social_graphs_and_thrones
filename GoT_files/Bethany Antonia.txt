'''Bethany Antonia'''<!--Some wikis list her full name as "Bethany-Antonia Clarke." This is an error and Antonia herself has requested that this be corrected. Do not add it back.--> (born 25 December 1997) is an English actress. She is playing [[Baela Targaryen]] in ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.<ref>[https://pressroom.warnermedia.com/us/bio/house-dragon-character-descriptions WarnerMedia]</ref> 

==Biography==
Bethany Antonia was born in Birmingham, UK. She is best known for the Netflix series ''{{WPS|Get Even|Get Even}}'' (an adaptation of a popular book series) and ''{{WPS|Stay Close|Stay Close}}'' (also an adaptation, of a mystery novel).
