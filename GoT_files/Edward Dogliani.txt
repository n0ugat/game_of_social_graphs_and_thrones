'''Edward Dogliani''' is a British actor who has appeared in the short films ''Jump'' and ''VGL Hung'' and the full-length film ''Full Firearms''. He has appeared on TV in ''The Mystery of Edwin Drood''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', Dogliani played the [[Lord of Bones]]. He was announced in the role on 15 November 2011.<ref>[http://grrm.livejournal.com/249766.html George R.R. Martin's blog]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]], and returned for [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|the next season]]. However, [[Ross O'Hennessy]] took over the role for [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]].
