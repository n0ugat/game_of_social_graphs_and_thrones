'''Tanselle'''{{Webref|name=GRRMegg |author=George R.R. Martin |date=May 21, 2024 |url=https://georgerrmartin.com/notablog/2024/05/21/heres-egg/ |title=Here's Egg! |website=Not a Blog |access-date=May 21, 2024 }} is a [[Dornishmen|Dornish]] woman living during the reign of [[Daeron II Targaryen]].

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Tanselle is tall and slim, with small breasts. She has black hair and olive skin. Due to her height, she is nicknamed "Tanselle Too-Tall." She is part of a group of traveling puppeteers.
