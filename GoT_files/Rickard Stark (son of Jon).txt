King '''Rickard Stark''' was a [[King in the North|King]] of the [[House Stark|Stark]] dynasty.

==Biography==
During the [[Thousand Years War]], he fought the [[Marsh King]] and killed him, taking his daughter as his wife. He gave the dominion of the the [[Neck]] to his bannermen, [[House Reed]].{{Ref|H&L609}}

==In the books==
King Rickard, who was sometimes called the Laughing Wolf for his good nature, took the Neck away from the Marsh King and took his daughter as his wife.
