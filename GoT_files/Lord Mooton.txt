'''Lord Mooton''' was the head of [[House Mooton]] during the youth of [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Lord Mooton<ref>https://www.instagram.com/p/CidR7nFKoGC/?igshid=MDJmNzVkMjY%3D</ref> is among a group of suitors gathered at [[Storm's End]], vying for Rhaenyra's hand in marriage.{{Ref|HOTD104}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Lord Mooton is not mentioned during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]]. It is unknown if Lord Walys Mooton, the Lord of Maidenpool during the [[Dance of the Dragons]], was also lord during Rhaenyra's youth.
