'''Lordsport'''{{Ref|H&L202}} is a port town on the island of [[Pyke (island)|Pyke]], in the [[Iron Islands]], and the seat of [[House Botley]]. It is the largest town in the Iron Islands, and the main port for Pyke island. This also makes it the main port in service to nearby [[Pyke]] castle, the seat of [[House Greyjoy]].<ref name="S2E02">"[[The Night Lands]]"</ref>

==History==
===Background===
Lordsport was burned to the ground during the [[Greyjoy Rebellion]] as a prelude to the [[Siege of Pyke]] castle, but was rebuilt in intervening years.<ref>"[[Greyjoy Rebellion - Theon Greyjoy]]"</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
[[Theon Greyjoy]] arrives to Lordsport aboard the ''[[Myraham]]'', sent by [[Robb Stark]] as an envoy to discuss an alliance with [[Balon Greyjoy]].<ref name="S2E02"/>

After choosing to side with his father in his conquest of the [[North]], Theon returns to Lordsport to meet the crew of the ''[[Sea Bitch]]'' and set sail.<ref>"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]"</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
The on-location scenes in Lordsport were filmed at the coastal town of [[Wikipedia:Ballintoy|Ballintoy]], County Antrim, Northern Ireland.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lordsport is ruled for the Greyjoys by House Botley. It is the largest town in the Iron Islands, and the main port for Pyke island.

Lordsport is known for its forges and metalworkers, who produce excellent swords, axes, ringmail, and plate.
