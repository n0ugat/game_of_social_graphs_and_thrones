Lady '''Lynesse Hightower''' is the wife of Lord [[Hobert Hightower]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Lynesse and her husband join [[Viserys I Targaryen|King Viserys]]'s hunting party on Prince [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon]]'s second [[nameday]]. She socializes with [[Ceira Lannister]], [[Joselyn Redwyne]], and her niece [[Alicent Hightower|Alicent]] in the royal tent.{{Ref|HOTD103}}

Lynesse attends the wedding of [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] and [[Laenor Velaryon]]. [[Harrold Westerling]] announces her name as she enters the [[throne room]] with her husband.{{Ref|HOTD105}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Lynesse Hightower. In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, [[Lynesse Hightower (daughter of Leyton)|Lynesse Hightower]] is the estranged wife of [[Jorah Mormont]].

Another Lynesse Hightower appears in the approved online roleplaying game ''Blood of Dragons'', created by Elio García and Linda Antonsson, the authors of ''The World of Ice & Fire''. This Lynesse is the second daughter of Lord Ormund Hightower, and would be alive in the time period of ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.<ref>[https://www.westeros.org/BoD/Houses/Entry/House_Hightower/ Blood of Dragons: Houses - House Hightower of the Hightower]</ref>
