'''Duncan Muggoch''' is a British Producer who supervised filming in Croatia in [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|Season 4]], Croatia and Spain in [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]], and Spain in Seasons [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|6]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|7]] of ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. He was a producer of [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|Season 8]].

Prior to ''Game of Thrones'', he had worked on many feature films and television shows such as: ''Thor: The Dark World'', ''Jack the Giant Killer'', ''Salmon Fishing in the Yemen'', ''The Wolfman'', ''The Bourne Ultimatum'', ''Stardust'', ''The Da Vinci Code'', and the HBO movie ''RKO 281''.
