'''''Game of Thrones: House of the Dragon: Inside the Creation of a Targaryen Dynasty''''' is a companion book to the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] of ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', written by Gina McIntyre. It was first published on December 13, 2022 as an e-book<ref>https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/63234422-game-of-thrones</ref> and was published in hardcover format on January 31, 2023.<ref>https://www.amazon.com/Making-HBOs-House-Dragon/dp/1647225280</ref>

==Contents==
The book offers an in-depth behind-the-scenes look at the development and production of the first season of ''House of the Dragon''. It includes exclusive interviews with the showrunners and concept art by Constantine Sekeris.
