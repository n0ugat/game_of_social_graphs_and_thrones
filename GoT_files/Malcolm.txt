'''Malcolm''' is a man-at-arms sworn to [[House Frey]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
Following the [[Red Wedding]], along with [[Talbot]] and [[Frey soldier 1|another man-at-arms]], Malcolm helps to sew the head of [[Robb Stark]]'s direwolf, [[Grey Wind]], onto Robb's body.{{Ref|GOT310}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the only character named Malcolm is a landed knight who lived near [[King's Landing]]. No information is given about him, except that he sent someone named Lync to the [[Wall]] for stealing peppers.
