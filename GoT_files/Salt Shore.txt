'''Salt Shore'''{{Ref|GOT106}} is a castle in [[Dorne]], the region controlled by [[House Martell]] of [[Sunspear]]. The castle is located on the southern coast of Dorne, on the [[Summer Sea]]. It is the seat of [[House Gargalen]]

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
[[Joffrey Baratheon]] claims that lords will come from all of Westeros, from the [[Last Hearth]] in the north to the Salt Shore in the south, to his and [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]]'s wedding.{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Salt Shore is the same.
