'''Pylos''' was an [[Archmaester]] of the [[Citadel]], one of the governing members of the [[Order of Maesters]]. He is the author of ''[[On Rare Diseases]]''.

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
[[Samwell Tarly|Sam]] mentions that he may be able to cure [[Jorah Mormont]]'s [[greyscale]] using Pylos's methods, but is rebuked by [[Ebrose]], who says that Pylos died of the same disease; because of this, Pylos's methods are forbidden to be used again.{{Ref|GOT702}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no archmaester named Pylos. Archmaester Pylos shares his name with [[awoiaf:Pylos|Maester Pylos]] from the books.
