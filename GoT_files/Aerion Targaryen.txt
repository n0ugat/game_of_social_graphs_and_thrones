'''Aerion Targaryen''', nicknamed '''Aerion Brightflame''', is a [[prince]] of the [[House Targaryen|Targaryen dynasty]].

==Biography==
Aerion is the second son of Prince [[Maekar Targaryen]] and Lady [[Dyanna Dayne]], the younger brother of Prince [[Daeron Targaryen (son of Maekar)|Daeron Targaryen]] and the older brother of Princes [[Aemon]] and [[Aegon Targaryen (son of Maekar)|Aegon Targaryen]].

==Family==
{{Targaryen tree (A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms)}}

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Aerion is slim and of average height with curly silver hair, an imperious face of sharp cheekbones, straight nose, pale skin, and violet eyes. He is [[Targaryen madness|mad]], believing himself a [[dragon]] in human form. His personal sigil is the Targaryen dragon with one head red, one orange, and one gold.
