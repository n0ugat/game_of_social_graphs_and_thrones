'''Tatiana S. Riegel''', also known as '''Tanya Riegel''', is a film and television editor. She was an additional editor for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[Winter Is Coming]]". She has edited the films ''Pulp Fiction'' and ''Fright Night''.
