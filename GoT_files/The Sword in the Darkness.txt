"'''The Sword in the Darkness'''"{{Ref|ATGS03}} is the third episode of ''[[Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series]]''. It premiered on March 24, 2015. It was written by Dan Martin, John Dombrow, and Joshua Rubin, and directed by Graham Ross.
