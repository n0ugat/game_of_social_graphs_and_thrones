A '''Valyrian slave''' is one of the slaves held in [[Meereen]] during [[Daenerys Targaryen]]'s conquest of the [[Slaver's Bay]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He is one of the slaves held in [[Meereen]] during [[Daenerys Targaryen]]'s conquest of the [[Slaver's Bay]].{{Ref|GOT404}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
He is one of the slaves that are persuaded by [[Grey Worm]] to [[Siege of Meereen|revolt]] against the [[Great Masters]] of Meereen, despite being hesitant at first and claiming the revolts always end with the Masters winning and punishing the slaves. He also points out that the slaves have no training and no weapons.{{Ref|GOT404}}
