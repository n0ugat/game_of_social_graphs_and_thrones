'''Steffon Trant''' was a member of [[House Trant]].

==Biography==
===Background===
In 44 AC, he dishonored [[Axel Baratheon]]'s wife [[Alanna Penrose|Alanna]], and then killed Axel in a duel at [[Gallowsgrey]].{{Ref|GOT106}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He is mentioned in Axel Baratheon's entry of ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
