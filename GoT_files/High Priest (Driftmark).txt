A '''High Priest''' of the [[Old Gods of Valyria]] serves [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] at [[Dragonstone]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
In [[126 AC]],{{Dateref|Driftmark}} the [[High Priest (Old Gods of Valyria)|High Priest]] performs a traditional [[Valyrians|Valyrian]] [[Marriage|wedding]] for Rhaenyra and [[Daemon Targaryen]].{{Ref|HOTD107}}

In [[132 AC]],{{Dateref|The Black Queen}} the High Priest attends the funeral of Rhaenyra's stillborn daughter [[Visenya Targaryen (daughter of Rhaenyra)|Visenya]]. He joins the others present in kneeling when Rhaenyra is crowned queen.{{Ref|HOTD110}}
