'''Isabella May Ramsey''' (born 30 September 2003) is an English actor.

In the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh]], and [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth]] seasons of ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', they play Lady [[Lyanna Mormont]]. It was their first acting role of any kind.

After Season 6, they starred in the CBBC adaptation of the ''Worst Witch'' book series.<ref>[http://www.makinggameofthrones.com/production-diary/bella-ramsey-lyanna-mormont We Know No Scene-Stealer but the One on Bear Island, Whose Name Is Bella Ramsey], June 27, 2016.
</ref>
