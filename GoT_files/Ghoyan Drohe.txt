'''Ghoyan Drohe'''<ref>https://entertainment.ha.com/itm/movie-tv-memorabilia/props/essos-map-from-game-of-thrones-hbo-r-original-2011-2019-/a/7366-89866.s</ref> is a city in western [[Essos]], along one of the [[Rhoyne]]'s tributaries.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Ghoyan Drohe is a ruined city in the Velvet Hills, on the banks of the Little Rhoyne. It was destroyed by the [[dragonlord]]s during the Rhoynish Wars. Before its destruction, it is said to have been "a fair place, green and flowering, a city of canals and fountains." In the present day, it is abandoned and sinking back into the earth.

[[Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys]] passes Ghoyan Drohe on her and the ''[[khalasar]]''<nowiki>'</nowiki>s journey from [[Pentos]] to [[Vaes Dothrak]].

[[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] meets the crew of the ''Shy Maid'' at Ghoyan Drohe.
