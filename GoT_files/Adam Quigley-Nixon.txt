}}'''Adam Quigley-Nixon''' is a Northern Irish actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a Lannister guard in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]".<ref>https://www.imdb.com/title/tt5924366/?ref_=nm_flmg_act_1</ref>

== Personal life ==
Quigley-Nixon was born in Derry, Northern Ireland on December 19, 1995. His acting career began in 2015 with his small role in a play. In the episode he is wearing a full set of armor and his face is unable to be seen.
