'''Eddard''' is a servant of [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]] in the [[Red Keep]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Eddard works on King [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys]]'s model of [[Old Valyria]].{{Ref|HOTD106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the name "Eddard" is only known to be given in the [[north]]. There are two characters by that name: [[Eddard Stark]], and his namesake Eddard Karstark, one of [[Rickard Karstark]]'s sons. There is no character named Eddard in ''[[Fire & Blood]]''.
