'''Harlon the Hunter''' was a son of [[Garth the Gardener]] who raised [[Horn Hill]] with his brother, [[Herndon of the Horn]].

==Biography==
Harlon was the son of [[Garth the Gardener]] and brother of [[Herndon of the Horn]]. He lived during the [[Age of Heroes]]. Along with his brother, he raised the castle of [[Horn Hill]]. There he shared the castle and a wife with him. This way of life went on for them for a hundred years, until one of the two died.{{Ref|H&L616}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Harlon's story is the same. It was said that the wife he shared with Herndon was a beautiful [[woods witch]] and that they would not age as long as they had sex with her when there was a full moon.
