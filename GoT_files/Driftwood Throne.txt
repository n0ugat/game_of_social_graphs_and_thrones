The '''Driftwood Throne'''{{Ref|HOTD102}} is the throne of [[House Velaryon]]. It is located in the Hall of Nine in [[High Tide]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[File:WLTW Corlys Velaryon Still 1.jpg|thumb|Corlys sitting the throne in the Hall of Nine.]]When [[Viserys I Targaryen]] visits High Tide, [[Corlys Velaryon]] waits on the Driftwood Throne to greet him.<ref>"[[We Light the Way]]"</ref>

[[Rhaenys Targaryen]] sits on the Driftwood Throne upon hearing the news that her husband Corlys has contracted blood fever after being severely wounded in an ambush during the [[War for the Stepstones]].<ref>"[[The Lord of the Tides]]"</ref>
==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', the Driftwood Throne was originally housed in [[Castle Driftmark]] before being moved to High Tide. It is said to have been gifted to the Velaryons by the [[Merling King]], a god associated with the [[Narrow Sea|narrow sea]].
