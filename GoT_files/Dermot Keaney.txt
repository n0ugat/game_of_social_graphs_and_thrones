'''Dermot Keaney''' (born 28 August 1964) is a British actor. He is best known for playing the role of Maccus, the second-in-command to [[wikipedia:Bill Nighy|Bill Nighy]]'s [[wikipedia:Davey Jones|Davey Jones]] in the second and third [[wikipedia:Pirates of the Caribbean (film series)|''Pirates of the Caribbean'']] movies.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Keaney played the role of [[Gared]], replacing [[Richard Ridings]] who played the role in the unaired pilot. He was confirmed in the role on 16 July 2010.
