Ser '''Chester Mooton'''<ref name="Script" /> was a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Mooton]], a member of the [[Kingsguard/House of the Dragon|Kingsguard]] of [[King of the Andals and the Rhoynar and the First Men|King]] [[Jaehaerys I Targaryen]], and the sworn protector of [[Prince]]ss [[Saera Targaryen]]. Due to the nature of the knight's relationship with the princess, Jaehaerys had Chester gelded and sent to the [[Wall]].

==Behind the scenes==
Chester was mentioned in an earlier draft of the script for "[[Driftmark (episode)|Driftmark]]," in which [[Harrold Westerling]] tells of the fate he met as a warning to [[Criston Cole]].<ref name="Script" />

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Chester Mooton. However, his fate is comparable to those of Braxton Beesbury and Lucamore Strong.
