'''Sophia Pettit''' (born 13 April 2003) is an English actress.

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', she portrays a to-be-confirmed character in at least 4 [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|Season 1]] episodes directed by [[Miguel Sapochnik]], [[Greg Yaitanes]], [[Clare Kilner]], and [[Geeta Vasant Patel]].<ref>https://www.curtisbrown.co.uk/client/sophia-pettit/cv</ref>

==Background==
Sophia Pettit is a friend of [[Emily Carey]], who is portraying young [[Alicent Hightower]].
