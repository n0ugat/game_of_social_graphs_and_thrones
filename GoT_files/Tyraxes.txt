'''Tyraxes'''{{Note|[[High Valyrian]]: ''Tyrakses''}} is a [[dragon]] bred by [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]]. He is bonded to [[Joffrey Velaryon]].{{Ref|HOTD110}}

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
[[File:Tyraxes inside a kennel heading for a ship..png|thumb|Tyraxes inside a kennel being transported by Dragonkeepers.]]
Tyraxes, alongside [[Stormcloud]], is transported inside a kennel carried by [[Dragonkeepers]] as he and Joffrey prepare to depart for the [[Eyrie]] to secure [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]]'s alliance with [[House Arryn]].{{Ref|HOTD203}} Lady [[Jeyne Arryn]] is discontent, claiming the two dragons are too small.{{Ref|HOTD205}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Tyraxes was bound to Joffrey Velaryon, and by the beginning of the dance he was already big enough to fly with his rider.
