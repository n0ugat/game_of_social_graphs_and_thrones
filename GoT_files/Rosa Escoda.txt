'''Rosa Escoda''' is an actress and writer. She is known for her role as Amanda Yeo in the television series ''{{WPS|Line of Duty}}''. She has also appeared in films such as ''{{WPS|Justice League (film)|Justice League}}'' and ''{{WPS|Greta (film)|Greta}}'' and television series such as ''{{WPS|Four Weddings and a Funeral (miniseries)|Four Weddings and a Funeral}}'' and ''{{WPS|Holby City}}''.

She portrays a younger midwife in ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.<ref>[https://www.spotlight.com/interactive/cv/3332-4533-8019 Rosa Escoda on Spotlight]</ref>
