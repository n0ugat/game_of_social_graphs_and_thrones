'''James Ronald Gordon Copeland''' (born 1947), known professionally as '''James Cosmo''', is a Scottish actor. He is best known for playing older, veteran warriors in historical dramas and epics, such as Campbell in ''[[wikipedia:Braveheart|Braveheart]]'' (which also featured [[Rupert Vansittart]]), Glaucus in ''[[wikipedia:Troy (film)|Troy]]'' (written by [[David Benioff]] and featuring [[Sean Bean]] and [[Julian Glover]]), Hrothgar in ''[[wikipedia:The Last Legion|The Last Legion]]'' (with [[Nonso Anozie]], [[Owen Teale]] and [[Robert Pugh]]), and Hengist in the British TV series ''[[wikipedia:Merlin (TV series)|Merlin]]''. His other roles include ''[[wikipedia:The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe|The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe]]'' (as [[wikipedia:Father Christmas|Father Christmas]]) and recent guest roles on American TV series ''[[wikipedia:FlashForward (TV series)|FlashForward]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Castle (TV series)|Castle]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Sons of Anarchy|Sons of Anarchy]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Cosmo is playing [[Jeor Mormont]], the "Old Bear". He was confirmed in the role on 16 July 2010. He initially appeared as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and joined the starring cast for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]]. He also voices the role of Jeor Mormont in the ''Game of Thrones'' role-playing [[Game of Thrones (game)|game]].

The first scene Cosmo filmed for was the opening scene of the first episode of [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|Season 3]], when he burns the [[wight]] and saves Samwell Tarly.<ref>[http://www.accesshollywood.com/game-of-thrones-james-cosmo-talks-lord-commander-mormonts-dramatic-scene-at-crasters-keep-spoiler-alert_article_78360 Game Of Thrones: James Cosmo Talks Lord Commander Mormont’s Dramatic Scene At Craster’s Keep]</ref>

Cosmo's final scene in the Game of Thrones series is during Season 3 episode 4 ("[[And Now His Watch Is Ended]]") during the mutiny at [[Craster's Keep]]. He is killed by the [[Night's Watch]]man [[Rast]].
