'''Rowley Irlam''' is an English professional stunt coordinator.

He became the new stunt coordinator for ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' starting in [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]].<ref>[http://www.makinggameofthrones.com/production-diary/go-inside-the-stunts-of-season-5?rq=stunt ]</ref>

He received an Emmy Award for "Outstanding Stunt Coordination For A Drama Series, Limited Series Or Movie" for his work on ''Game of Thrones'' Season 5.<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/game-of-thrones-breaks-the-record-for-most-emmy-wins-in-a-year/ ]</ref>

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he was the stunt coordinator for the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] and [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]].
