'''Lord Belgrave'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} lived during the reign of King [[Baelor I Targaryen]].

==Biography==
Lord Belgrave was forced to wash a beggar's feet in public by King Baelor.{{Ref|H&L618}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lord Belgrave was a proud man who was famous for the face he made when King Baelor Targaryen commanded him to wash the ulcerous feet of beggars. His given name is unknown.
