Prince '''Valerion Targaryen'''{{Con|F&B}} was the twelfth child of King [[Jaehaerys I Targaryen|Jaehaerys I]] and Good Queen [[Alysanne Targaryen|Alysanne]].{{Guideref|Jaehaerys I Targaryen}}

==Family==
{{Targaryen tree (House of the Dragon)}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Valerion was born in 77 AC after a difficult labor that left the queen bedridden for half a year. He was sickly, and almost as small as his brother [[Gaemon Targaryen|Gaemon]] at birth. He died in 78 AC, just short of his first [[nameday]].
