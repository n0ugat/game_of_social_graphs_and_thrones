'''Mory'''{{Ref|HOTD205}} is a settlement in the [[Riverlands]], part of the demesne of [[House Bracken]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Mory is brutally raided by [[House Blackwood]] after [[Amos Bracken]] refuses to surrender to [[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]].{{Ref|HOTD205}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, nothing is known of Mory except that Pate of Mory hails from there. It is not specified which house the settlement belongs to.
