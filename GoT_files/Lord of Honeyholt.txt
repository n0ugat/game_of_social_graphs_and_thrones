'''Lord of Honeyholt'''{{Guideref|Lyman Beesbury}} is a title given to the ruler of the castle [[Honeyholt]] in the [[Reach]], located between [[Oldtown]] and [[Brightwater Keep]], on the banks of the Honeywine river. The title is traditionally held by the head of [[House Beesbury]].
