'''Gregor Clegane's father''' was the second head of [[House Clegane]]. He was the son of [[Gregor Clegane's grandfather]], the founder of the house.

==Biography==
The second head of [[House Clegane]] wanted a [[Knighthood|knight]] within his family, a wish that was fulfilled by his son Gregor, who was personally anointed by [[Rhaegar Targaryen]] with the influence of [[Tywin Lannister|Tywin]]. Later, he was killed in a suspicious hunting accident.{{Ref|H&L210}}

This man was known to have favored Gregor over Sandor, going so far as to protect Gregor when the former burned half of Sandor's face.{{Ref|GOT407}}
