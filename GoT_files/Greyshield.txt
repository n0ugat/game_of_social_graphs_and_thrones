'''Greyshield'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is one of the four [[Shield Islands]], west of the [[Reach]]. It has at least one settlement, [[Grimston]].{{Ref|THTDB201}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Greyshield is ruled by House Grimm. It served as Torgon Greyiron's seat when he raided along the [[Mander]].

After he is elected King of the Isles and the North, [[Euron Greyjoy]] conquers the island and names Harras Harlaw its new lord.
