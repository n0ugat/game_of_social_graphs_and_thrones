'''Michael Condron''' is a Canadian-born British actor.<ref>[http://www.castingcallpro.com/uk/actor/profile/michael-condron Casting Call Pro]</ref> He has appeared in TV Shows such as ''Pulling Moves'' or ''The Tudors''.

In [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] he portrays [[Bowen Marsh]], First Steward of the [[Night's Watch]].<ref>[http://www.universalartists.co.uk/Producer/Artist.aspx?ID=c6acc2b6-9bb0-4b5c-adc1-630992f3b578]</ref> Although it was believed [[Brian Fortune]] was playing the role in [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|Season 1]], his character was identified as [[Othell Yarwyck]] in [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|Season 4]].
