'''House Rosby'''{{Ref|THTDB204}} of [[Rosby]] is a vassal house that holds fealty directly to the elected monarchs of the [[Seven Kingdoms|Six Kingdoms]]. Their seat is the eponymous town of Rosby, located in the [[Crownlands]], north of [[King's Landing]].

==Members==
*Lord [[Gyles Rosby]], an ever present figure in the court at the [[Red Keep]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Rosby was one of the first to kneel to [[Aegon I Targaryen|Aegon the Conqueror]]. Rosbys are not known for their robustness, tending to be sickly persons. Bethany Rosby is the deceased sixth wife of Lord [[Walder Frey]] and mother to five Freys including [[Olyvar Frey|Olyvar]] and [[Roslin Tully|Roslin Frey]]. According to the semi-canonical ''A Song of Ice and Fire Roleplaying Campaign Guide: A Game of Thrones Edition'' from [[wikipedia:Green Ronin Publishing|Green Ronin Publishing]] Bethany Rosby is Lord Gyles Rosby's daughter.

During the [[First Dornish War]], Jon Rosby briefly ruled [[Dorne]] as [[Warden]] of the Sands, in addition to governing [[Sunspear]] as [[castellan]] in King Aegon's name.

Their blazon consists of three red chevronels on ermine. Their motto is not mentioned in the books. 

===Members===
*Lord {Gyles Rosby}, Lord of Rosby and [[Master of Coin]]. Died of an illness in the [[Red Keep]]
**{Two unnamed wives}
With unknown familiar relationship with the main branch:
*Lady {Bethany Rosby}, the late sixth wife of Lord [[Walder Frey]]. Mother of Perwyn, Benfrey, Willamen, [[Olyvar Frey|Olyvar]], and [[Roslin Tully|Roslin Frey]].

===Historical members===
*Lord {Jon Rosby}, Lord of Rosby, [[Castellan]] of [[Sunspear]], and [[Warden]] of the Sands. Killed in the Defenestration of Sunspear during the [[First Dornish War]].
*Ser {Rayford Rosby}, a [[Knighthood|knight]]. Fought and died for King [[Maegor Targaryen]] during his [[Trial by seven of Maegor Targaryen|trial by seven]] in 42 AC.
