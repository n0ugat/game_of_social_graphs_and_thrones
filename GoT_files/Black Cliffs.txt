The '''Black Cliffs'''{{Ref|GOT410}}{{Note|Valyrian: ''Gavori Zobri''}} are a series of cliffs on the northwest coast of [[Slaver's Bay]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
When [[Daenerys Targaryen]] asks for the whereabouts of her largest dragon, [[Drogon]], [[Grey Worm]] informs he was last seen flying over the Black Cliffs three days earlier.<ref>"[[The Children]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Black Cliffs are a series of cliffs that are situated on the northwest coast of Slaver's Bay, south of the Painted Mountains. They lie between [[Tolos]] and the ruined city of [[Bhorash]], south of the Demon Road.
