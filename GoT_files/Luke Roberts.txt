'''Luke Roberts''' (born 1977) is an English actor. He is best known for his role as {{WPS|Joseph Byrne (Holby City)|Joseph Byrne}} in the television series ''{{WPS|Holby City}}''. He has also appeared in films such as ''{{WPS|Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides}}'' (with [[Ian McShane]], [[DeObia Oparei]] and [[Anton Lesser]]) and ''{{WPS|300: Rise of an Empire}}'' and television series such as ''{{WPS|Band of Brothers (miniseries)|Band of Brothers}}'', ''{{WPS|Crossroads (soap opera)|Crossroads}}'', ''{{WPS|Mile High}}'', ''{{WPS|Law & Order: UK}}'', ''{{WPS|Beauty & the Beast (2012 TV series)|Beauty & the Beast}}'', ''{{WPS|Reign (TV series)|Reign}}'' and ''{{WPS|Wolf Hall (miniseries)|Wolf Hall}}''. He also plays an important role in [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_Sails_(TV_series) Black Sails] season 3.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he played Ser [[Arthur Dayne]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Oathbreaker]]".<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/more-confirmation-of-luke-roberts-nathalie-emmanuel-arrives-in-peniscola-and-new-set-photos-from-spain-and-saintfield/ Watchers on the Wall — More confirmation of Luke Roberts, Nathalie Emmanuel arrives in Peñíscola, and new set photos from Spain and Saintfield!]</ref>
