====Notes====
*Dinklage was the last starring cast member to appear in the first season's opening credits, receiving the "and" modifier. He is now the first credited.
*First season "guest starring" cast members promoted to the starring cast for the second season are [[Charles Dance]], [[John Bradley]], [[James Cosmo]], [[Jerome Flynn]], [[Conleth Hill]] and [[Sibel Kekilli]].
*[[Kit Harington]] is now credited individually onscreen.
*New cast members joining the starring cast for the second season are [[Liam Cunningham]], [[Stephen Dillane]], [[Carice van Houten]] and [[Natalie Dormer]].
*Headey is credited ahead of Coster-Waldau and Fairley for the first time.

====Notes====
*Fifth season "guest starring" cast member promoted to the starring cast for the sixth season is [[Jonathan Pryce]]. In episode ten he is also credited individually onscreen.
*[[Sophie Turner]] and [[Nathalie Emmanuel]] are now credited individually onscreen.
*[[Isaac Hempstead-Wright]] returns to the starring cast for this season, as his character ([[Bran Stark]]) did not appear in the fifth season.
*[[Rory McCann]] returns to the starring cast for this season, as his character ([[Sandor Clegane]]) did not appear in the fifth season.
*Episodes nine and ten of season six are the only episodes in the whole series in which [[Kit Harington]] is credited ahead of [[Emilia Clarke]]. In every other episode in which they both appear, she is credited before him in the opening titles.
*Episode ten of season six is the first episode in which [[Maisie Williams]] is credited individually onscreen, which she will subsequently be for the rest of the series.
