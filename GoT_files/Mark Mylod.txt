'''Mark Mylod''' is a British Emmy winning film and television director and producer. His most notable early directing credits are the films ''[[wikipedia:Ali G Indahouse|Ali G Indahouse]]'', ''[[wikipedia:The Big White|The Big White]]'', and ''[[wikipedia:What's Your Number?|What's Your Number?]]'', and the television shows ''[[wikipedia:Cold Feet|Cold Feet]]'', ''[[wikipedia:The Royle Family|The Royle Family]]'', and ''[[wikipedia:Bang Bang, It's Reeves and Mortimer|Bang Bang, It's Reeves and Mortimer]]''. Mylod later co-produced and directed many episodes of ''[[wikipedia:Entourage (TV Series)|Entourage]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Shameless (TV Series)|Shameless]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Once Upon a Time (TV Series)|Once Upon a Time]]''.
[[File:Mark Mylod.png|thumb|left|Mark Mylod filming in Season 5.]]
On July 15, 2014, it was announced that Mylod would be directing two episodes in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]], the [[High Sparrow (episode)|third]] and [[Sons of the Harpy (episode)|fourth]] episodes.<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2014/07/15/directors-game-thrones-season-5-announced/]</ref> On June 25, 2015, it was revealed he would come back for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] to direct the [[The Broken Man|seventh]] and [[No One|eighth]] episodes.<ref>[http://www.ew.com/article/2015/06/23/game-thrones-season-6-directors]</ref> On June 29, 2016, it was announced he would return for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]].<ref>[http://www.ew.com/article/2016/06/29/game-thrones-season-7 <nowiki>[3]</nowiki>]</ref>
