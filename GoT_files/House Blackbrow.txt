'''House Blackbrow'''{{Ref|GOT104}}{{Note|Jore's surname is Blackbrow.}} is a noble house in the [[Seven Kingdoms]]. Their region of origin is not known.

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Several members of House Blackbrow are recorded in the entry of [[Emilia Umber]] (who married into the house) in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==Members==
===Historical members===
*Lord {[[Erich Blackbrow]]}, head of the house.
**Lady {[[Conn Blackbrow|Conn]]}, his wife of an unknown house.
***{[[Jore Blackbrow]]}, their son
****Lady {[[Emilia Umber|Emilia]]}, his wife of [[House Umber]].
*****{[[Marek Blackbrow]]}, their son.
*****{[[Helos Blackbrow]]}, their son.
*****{[[Emilia Blackbrow]]}, their daughter

==In the books==
House Blackbrow is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
