'''Godsgrace'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in [[Dorne]], the region controlled by [[House Martell]] of [[Sunspear]]. The castle is located in eastern Dorne close to where the [[Greenblood]] is born from the [[Scourge]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Godsgrace is the seat of House Allyrion.
