'''Saath'''<ref name="Map">[[:File:HotD Nine Voyages Map.png]]</ref> is a city in northern [[Essos]].

==In the books==
In ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', Saath is a small port city populated by the Tall Men (Tagaez Fen), descendants of the ancient [[Sarnori]]. It was the only city of the [[Kingdom of Sarnor]] to survive the rise of the [[Dothraki]] during the [[Century of Blood]].
