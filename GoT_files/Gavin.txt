'''Gavin''' was a [[Order of Maesters|maester]] serving in the [[Night's Watch]] during the command of [[Rodrik Velaryon]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Maester Gavin counseled Lord Commander Velaryon to appoint [[Dagon Harlaw]] as [[First Ranger]].<ref>[[:File:5x2 Night's Watch Book.jpg]]</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Maester Gavin is mentioned in a book that [[Samwell Tarly]] is reading.{{Ref|GOT502}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Maester Gavin.
