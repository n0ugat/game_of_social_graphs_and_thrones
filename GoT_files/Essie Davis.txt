'''Esther "Essie" Davis''' is an award-winning Australian actress. She is best known for her role as Phryne Fisher in the ''Miss Fisher Murder Mysteries'', in addition to being known for her roles in films such as ''The Matrix Reloaded'', ''The Matrix Revolutions'', ''Girl with a Pearl Earring'', ''Charlotte's Web'', ''Legend of the Guardians: The Owls of Ga'Hoole'' and ''The Babadook''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she portrayed [[Lady Crane]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2015/09/12/richard-e-grants-role-revealed-plus-the-babadooks-essie-davis-joins-the-cast/ Richard E. Grant’s role revealed, plus The Babadook’s Essie Davis joins the cast]</ref>
