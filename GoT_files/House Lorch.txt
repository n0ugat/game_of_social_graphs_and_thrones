'''House Lorch'''{{Ref|GOT203}}{{Note|Amory's surname is Lorch.}} is a knightly house of the [[Westerlands]]. They are sworn to [[House Lannister]] of [[Casterly Rock]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
Ser [[Amory Lorch]] fights for [[House Lannister]] during the [[War of the Five Kings]].{{Ref|GOT203}}

==Members==
*Ser {[[Amory Lorch]]}, a knight in the service of Lord [[Tywin Lannister]]. Poisoned by [[Jaqen H'ghar]] at [[Arya Stark]]'s behest.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the only known members of House Lorch are Ser Amory and Ser Lorent Lorch. Ser Amory serves as the castellan of [[Harrenhal]] in the absence of [[Tywin Lannister]].
