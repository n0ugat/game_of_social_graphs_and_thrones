Prince '''Gaemon Targaryen'''{{Con|F&B}} was the eleventh child of King [[Jaehaerys I Targaryen|Jaehaerys I]] and Good Queen [[Alysanne Targaryen|Alysanne]].{{Guideref|Jaehaerys I Targaryen}}

==Family==
{{Targaryen tree (House of the Dragon)}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Gaemon was born prematurely in 73 AC, named after Gaemon the Glorious, a pre-[[Aegon's Conquest]] Lord of Dragonstone. He was scrawny, almost half the size of his brother [[Vaegon Targaryen|Vaegon]] at birth. He died less than three months later in 74 AC.
