'''Robert Samuel Boyd Rankin''' (1 November 1945 - 19 October 2019<ref>https://pharmaceutical-journal.com/article/news/deaths-robert-samuel-boyd-rankin</ref>) was an Irish weapon smith and armorer. He was a co-founder of the company Irish Arms. He was a senior supervising armorer for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second]] seasons of ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. He played [[Mikken]] in the first season.
