'''Brett Lannister''' is a member of [[House Lannister/House of the Dragon|House Lannister]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Brett is a member of House Lannister. His exact relationship to the main branch is unknown.{{Ref|HOTD103}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Brett accompanies [[Ceira Lannister|Ceira]], [[Jason Lannister|Jason]], and [[Tyland Lannister]] to the [[Kingswood]] to participate in the royal hunt on Prince [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon]]'s second [[nameday]].{{Ref|HOTD103}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Brett.
