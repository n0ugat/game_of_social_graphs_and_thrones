Ser '''Axell Bulwer'''{{Webref|name=Spotlight |author=Unknown author |date=Unknown date |url=https://app.spotlight.com/0274-6723-1457 |title=EDDIE EYRE |website=Spotlight |access-date=July 21, 2024 }} is a [[Knighthood|knight]] of the [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|Targaryen]] household in [[King's Landing]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Axell is from [[House Bulwer]], a [[noble house]] of the [[Reach]] sworn to [[House Hightower/House of the Dragon|House Hightower]].

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Axell joins Ser [[Criston Cole]] and Ser [[Gwayne Hightower]] on their campaign in the [[Crownlands]]{{Ref|HOTD203}} and later fights in the [[Battle at Rook's Rest]].{{Ref|HOTD204}}

Axell heralds [[Meleys]]'s severed head as the [[Greens]] parade it through the streets of King's Landing.{{Ref|HOTD205}}

Axell witnesses Gwayne's confrontation of Criston about the latter's affair with [[Alicent Hightower|Alicent]].{{Ref|HOTD208}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Axell Bulwer. House Bulwer's side during the Dance of the Dragons is not mentioned in the source material, but it can be inferred they followed their liege lords of House Hightower.
