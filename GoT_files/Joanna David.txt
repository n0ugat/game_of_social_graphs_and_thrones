'''Joanna David''' (born '''Joanna Elizabeth Hacking'''; 17 January, 1947) is an English actress. She has appeared in the films ''{{WPS|Anna Karenina (1985 film)|Anna Karenina}}'' and ''{{WPS|You Will Meet a Tall Dark Stranger}}'' and the television series ''{{WPS|Sense and Sensibility (1971)|Sense and Sensibility}}'', ''{{WPS|The Last of the Mohicans (TV series)|The Last of the Mohicans}}'', ''{{WPS|War and Peace (1972 TV series)|War and Peace}}'', ''{{WPS|Ballet Shoes (TV serial)|Ballet Shoes}}'', ''{{WPS|Rebecca (1979 TV series)|Rebecca}}'', ''{{WPS|The Agatha Christie Hour}}'', ''{{WPS|Jackanory}}'', ''{{WPS|First Among Equals (TV series)|First Among Equals}}'', ''{{WPS|Prince Caspian and The Voyage of the Dawn Treader (1989 TV serial)|Prince Caspian and the Voyage of the Dawn Treader}}'', ''{{WPS|Inspector Morse (TV series)|Inspector Morse}}'', ''{{WPS|Pride and Prejudice (1995 TV series)|Pride and Prejudice}}'', ''{{WPS|A Touch of Frost}}'', ''{{WPS|Dalziel and Pascoe (TV series)|Dalziel and Pascoe}}'', ''{{WPS|Foyle's War}}'', ''{{WPS|Heartbeat (British TV series)|Heartbeat}}'', ''{{WPS|Midsomer Murders}}'', ''{{WPS|Agatha Christie's Marple}}'', ''{{WPS|Holby City}}'', ''{{WPS|Downton Abbey}}'', ''{{WPS|Death in Paradise (TV series)|Death in Paradise}}'', ''{{WPS|Doctors (2000 TV series)|Doctors}}'', and ''{{WPS|Casualty (TV series)|Casualty}}''.
