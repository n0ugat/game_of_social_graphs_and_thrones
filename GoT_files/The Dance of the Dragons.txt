'''''The Dance of the Dragons'''''<ref name="THR" /> was a pitched television series of the ''[[World of Westeros]]'' based on the [[Dance of the Dragons|civil war of the same name]]. It was initially helmed by [[Carly Wray]] before passing onto [[Bryan Cogman]]. The story of the Dance of the Dragons was eventually adapted in ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.
