'''Derwa Frey '''is one of the many daughters of [[Walder Frey]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Derwa is one of the many daughters of [[Walder Frey]], the [[Lord of the Crossing]].{{Ref|GOT309}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
Along with several of Lord Frey's other daughters and granddaughters, [[Robb Stark]] had the option of marrying her. When he arrives at the [[Twins]] for his uncle [[Edmure Tully|Edmure]]'s [[Red Wedding|wedding]], Robb apologizes for his slight against them.{{Ref|GOT309}}

{{Frey tree}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Walder Frey has no daughter named "Derwa" - nor does he have any daughters or granddaughters named "Derwa". For that matter, no character with the first name "Derwa" has ever appeared in the books.
