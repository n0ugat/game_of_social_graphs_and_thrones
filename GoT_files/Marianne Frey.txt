'''Marianne Frey''' is one of the many granddaughters of [[Walder Frey]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Marianne is one of the many granddaughters of [[Walder Frey]], the [[Lord of the Crossing]].{{Ref|GOT309}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
Along with several of [[Lordship|Lord]] Frey's other daughters and granddaughters, [[Robb Stark]] had the option of marrying her. When he arrives at the [[Twins]] for his uncle [[Edmure Tully|Edmure]]'s [[Red Wedding|wedding]], Robb apologizes for his slight against them.{{Ref|GOT309}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
Marianne is present when [[Edmure Tully]] is brought into the hall by Walder Frey.{{Ref|GOT606}}

After the [[second siege of Riverrun]], Marianne is seen celebrating in the background at the victory feast after the Freys and [[House Lannister|Lannisters]] have taken [[Riverrun]].{{Ref|GOT610}}

{{Frey tree}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lord Walder has no granddaughter named "Marianne". He does have a great-granddaughter named Marianne Vance, daughter of Ser Dafyn Vance and Maegelle Frey. This Marianne is herself the granddaughter of Walder's eldest son, Ser [[Stevron Frey]]. She is among the Frey girls presented to Robb before Edmure's wedding.
