"'''Valar Dohaeris'''" is the first short of the [[Game of Thrones: Inside the Episode: Season 3|third season]] of ''[[Game of Thrones: Inside the Episode]]''. It is the twenty-first short of the series overall. It was released on April 1, 2013.
