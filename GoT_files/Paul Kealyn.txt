'''Paul Kealyn''' is an Irish actor who has been most active in stage productions.

''[[Game of Thrones]]'' is his first major television role. He plays the role of [[Weasel]]. He was discovered to be in the role by the Winter is Coming website on 7 January 2012<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2012/01/season-two-director-breakdown-and-a-minor-casting-update/ Winter is Coming]</ref>.
