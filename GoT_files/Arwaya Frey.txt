'''Arwaya Frey''' is one of the many daughters of [[Walder Frey]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Arwaya is one of the many daughters of [[Walder Frey]], the [[Lord of the Crossing]].{{Ref|GOT109}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Arwaya is present when her father confers with Lady [[Catelyn Stark]], standing beside her [[bastard]] half-brother [[Ryger Rivers]].{{Ref|GOT109}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
Along with several of Lord Frey's other daughters and granddaughters, [[Robb Stark]] had the option of marrying her. When he arrives at the [[Twins]] for his uncle [[Edmure Tully|Edmure]]'s [[Red Wedding|wedding]], Robb apologizes for his slight against them.{{Ref|GOT309}}
