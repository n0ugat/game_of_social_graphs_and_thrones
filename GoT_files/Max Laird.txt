'''Max Laird''' is a dwarf actor and stunt performer.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he portrayed a [[Dwarf 2|dwarf]] dressed as [[Stannis Baratheon|King Stannis Baratheon]] in the episode "[[The Lion and the Rose]]". 

He played a Gringotts goblin in ''Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2''.
