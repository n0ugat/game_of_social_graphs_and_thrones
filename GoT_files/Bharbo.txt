'''Bharbo''' was a [[Dothraki]] ''[[khal]]'' and the father of ''Khal'' [[Drogo]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Bharbo was a revered ''khal'' amongst the Dothraki. He is the father of Drogo.{{Ref|GOT103}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
[[Rakharo]] tells [[Jorah Mormont]] that his father was a [[bloodrider]] to Khal Bharbo and was a great warrior.{{Ref|GOT103}} When Drogo vows to cross the [[Narrow Sea]] and conquer the [[Seven Kingdoms]] in response to the attempted assassination of [[Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys]], he swears by his father's name.{{Ref|GOT107}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
Bharbo is briefly mentioned when Daenerys is taken before ''Khal'' [[Moro]] - again specifically in reference to his son as "Drogo, son of Bharbo".{{Ref|GOT601}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Bharbo is mentioned in the same way as in the TV series; Drogo refers to him when swearing to retake the [[Iron Throne]] for Daenerys and for their son [[Rhaego]].
