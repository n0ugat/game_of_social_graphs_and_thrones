'''Redfort'''{{Ref|GOT701}} is a castle in the south east of the [[Vale of Arryn]], the region controlled by [[House Arryn]].<ref>[http://www.makinggameofthrones.com/production-diary/2011/4/15/the-artisans-jim-stanes.html The Artisans: Jim Stanes (at 0:14)]</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Redfort is the seat of the eponymous House Redfort, loyal and powerful vassals of House Arryn. House Redfort, like [[House Royce]], is noted by [[Catelyn Stark]] as being descended from the [[First Men]].

[[Domeric Bolton]] was fostered at the Redfort for three years, and served as a squire to Lord Horton Redfort.
