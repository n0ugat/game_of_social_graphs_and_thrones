}}'''Wilf Scolding''' is a British TV and theatre actor.

He appears in the [[The Dragon and the Wolf|finale]] of the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh]] season of ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' as [[Rhaegar Targaryen]].

Outside of acting, Scolding is a professional chef, and works as the head chef of The Dove pub in Hammersmith, London.<ref>https://www.instagram.com/p/BQ_PzNrFL89/?taken-by=wilf.scolding</ref>
