'''James Sebastian Faulkner''' (born 18 July 1948) is an actor who has been cast as [[Randyll Tarly]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].

Recently Faulkner has been well-known for playing Lord Sinderby on ''Downton Abbey'', and also Pope Sixtus IV on ''Da Vinci's Demons''.

Over his long career Faulkner has appeared in ''X-Men: First Class'', ''MI-5'', ''Tatort'', ''Highlander'', ''La Femme Nikita'', ''Covington Cross'' and ''The Hound of the Baskervilles'' (Granada). He also appeared in ''Bridget Jones's Diary'' and its sequel.

Faulkner notably co-starred in the classic series ''I, Claudius'' as Herod Agrippa ([[George R.R. Martin]] has said that he is a huge fan of ''I, Claudius'').
