In 2013, Rob graduated from London's Guildhall School of Music and Drama. He had a minor role in the film, ''Pride and Prejudice and Zombies'', and in the Syfy original series, ''Dominion''.

In the episode, "[[The Door]]", he plays [[Clarenzo]].
