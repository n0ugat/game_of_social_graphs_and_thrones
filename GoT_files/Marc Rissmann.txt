'''Marc Rissmann''', also spelled '''Marc Rißmann''', is a German freelance actor and director, who appears in [[Game of Thrones: Season 8]] as [[Harry Strickland]], the captain of the [[Golden Company]].<ref>https://winteriscoming.net/2017/10/16/actor-marc-rissmann-cast-book-character-game-thrones-season-8/</ref>

In addition to ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he is known for ''The Last Kingdom'' and ''Overlord''.

==Biography==
===Career===
Marc Rissmann was born in Berlin, Germany, in 1980. He studied acting at the College of Dramatic Arts "Ernst Busch" Berlin from October 2005 to October 2009. His first role in film was Jan in ''Nazi Goreng'' (2011), and his first television role was Mark Schildt in ''SOKO Stuttgart''. As a director, he was worked on ''Lisa & Frank'' - a commercial campaign for the German National Lotto - and the music video for ''Please Tell Rossie'' by Alle Farben.
