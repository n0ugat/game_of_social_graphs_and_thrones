'''House Tully'''{{Ref|GOT106}} of [[Riverrun]] is one of the [[Great House]]s of [[Westeros]]. They were granted dominion over the lands surrounding the [[Trident]] river by the [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|Targaryens]] when they [[Aegon's Conquest|conquered]] the [[Seven Kingdoms]], and have ruled the [[Riverlands]] since.

==History==
===Background===
House Tully rose to prominence during [[Aegon's Conquest]]. Lord [[Edmyn Tully]] rebelled against the [[King of the Iron Islands]], [[Harren Hoare|Harren the Black]], to support the invading [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]]. Edmyn's fellow riverlords followed his lead. When [[Aegon I Targaryen|Aegon Targaryen]] established his rule from the [[Iron Throne]], he rewarded Edmyn by elevating the Tullys to [[Lord Paramount of the Trident|Lords Paramount of the Trident]].

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
A member of House Tully travels to [[King's Landing]] to participate in the [[Heir's Tournament]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

The [[Small Council]] discusses a land dispute in the [[Riverlands]] between [[House Blackwood]] and [[House Bracken]], as the ailing Lord [[Grover Tully|Grover]] is unable to handle the issue, [[Grover Tully's son|his son]] now ruling Riverrun in all but name.{{Ref|HOTD106}}

At the [[Green Council]], Ser [[Otto Hightower]] refers to Riverrun as one of their allies, and commands [[raven]]s be sent to them.{{Ref|HOTD109}} At the [[Black Council]], Maester [[Gerardys]] also sends ravens to Lord [[Grover Tully]], while Prince [[Daemon Targaryen]] announces his intentions to personally treat with him.{{Ref|HOTD110}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
At the beginning stages of the [[Dance of the Dragons]], House Tully remains undeclared and has not sided with the [[Blacks]] or the [[Greens]].{{Ref|HOTD201}}
