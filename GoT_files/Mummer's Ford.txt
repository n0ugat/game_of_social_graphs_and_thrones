The '''Mummer's Ford'''{{Ref|GOT304}} is a town in the [[Riverlands]], the region controlled by [[House Frey]] of the [[Twins]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
After receiving reports about raiding in the Riverlands, committed by the [[Gregor Clegane|Mountain]], [[Eddard Stark]] sends against him a force of 100 men under the command of Lord [[Beric Dondarrion]].<ref>"[[A Golden Crown]]"</ref> The subsequent encounter, known as [[Battle at the Mummer's Ford]], ends in victory for the Lannisters, and leads to the formation of the [[Brotherhood Without Banners]] by Beric and the survivors.<ref>"[[Brotherhood Without Banners (short)|Brotherhood Without Banners]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Mummer's Ford is so named because it is on a ford across the [[Red Fork]] of the [[Trident]].
