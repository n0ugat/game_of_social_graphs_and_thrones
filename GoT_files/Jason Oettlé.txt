'''Jason Oettlé''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt wireman in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[First of His Name]]" and "[[The Watchers on the Wall]]", a stunt rigger in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episode "[[Kill the Boy]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[The Door]]" and "[[Blood of My Blood]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episode "[[Beyond the Wall (episode)|Beyond the Wall]]" and [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]", "[[The Long Night]]", "[[The Last of the Starks]]" and "[[The Iron Throne]]" and a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]" and "[[Mother's Mercy]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[The Door]]", "[[No One]]", "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]" and "[[The Winds of Winter]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[The Spoils of War]]" and "[[Eastwatch]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]" and a utility stunts performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]".
