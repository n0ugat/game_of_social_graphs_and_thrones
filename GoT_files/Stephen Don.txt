}}'''Stephen Don''' is a Northern Irish actor, born in County Down. He is known for his theater work in London and also for a number of television appearances, most notably in TV movies ''[[wikipedia:Mo (film)|Mo]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Omagh (film)|Omagh]]''. In 2008 he founded Silver Sombrero Pictures Ltd, which he runs as co-director.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Stephen Don played the role of [[Stiv]]. He was announced in the role on 5 November 2010.
