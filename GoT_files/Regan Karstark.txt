Lady '''Regan Karstark''', née '''Umber''', was a member of [[House Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
[[File:Umber lineage.jpg|thumb|Regan's entry in ''The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms''.]]
Regan Karstark is an early member of [[House Umber]]. She was the daughter of Lord [[Hothor Umber]] and his wife, Lady [[Amarylis Umber]]. She married [[Tommard Karstark]] and bore him five sons. She died peacefully in her sleep at the age of seventy.{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
