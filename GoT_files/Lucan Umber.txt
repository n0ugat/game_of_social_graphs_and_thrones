'''Lucan Umber''' was a member of [[House Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Lucan Umber was the third born son of Lord [[Hothor Umber]] and his wife, Lady [[Amarylis Umber]]. He died from a fever at the age of three.{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lucan Umber has no corresponding counterpart.
