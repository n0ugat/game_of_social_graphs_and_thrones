'''''House of the Dragon: DracARys''''' is an augmented reality app in which the user raises a personalized virtual [[dragon]]. It was released on App Store and Google Play on July 25, 2022. Early access was given to attendees at San Diego Comic-Con on July 20. The app serves as promotional material for the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] of ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.<ref>https://www.hbo.com/house-of-the-dragon</ref>
