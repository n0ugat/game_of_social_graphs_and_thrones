'''Velizar Plamenov Peev''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]" and "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episode "[[Beyond the Wall (episode)|Beyond the Wall]]".
