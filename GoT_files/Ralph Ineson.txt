'''Ralph Michael Ineson''' (born 15 December 1969) is a British actor, best known for numerous roles on television. He is arguably most famous for playing the recurring character of Chris Finch on the original British version of ''[[wikipedia:The Office (UK)|The Office]]'', but is also known from roles on ''[[wikipedia:Playing the Field|Playing the Field]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Coronation Street|Coronation Street]]'', and ''Spooks''. He has appeared in films including ''[[wikipedia:First Knight|First Knight]]'', ''[[wikipedia:From Hell (film)|From Hell]]'', ''[[wikipedia:The Damned United|The Damned United]]''. Most recently he has appeared as Death Eater Amycus Carrow in the [[wikipedia:Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|sixth]], [[wikipedia:Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 1|seventh]] and [[wikipedia:Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 2|eighth]] ''[[wikipedia:Harry Potter|Harry Potter]]'' films.

He was once a teacher and a cricket coach. He is an avid supporter of Leeds United A.F.C.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]],'' Ineson played the role of [[Dagmer|Dagmer Cleftjaw]]. He was confirmed in the role on 25 August 2011.<ref>[http://winter-is-coming.net/2011/08/one-more-ironborn-dagmer-cleftjaw-cast/ Winter is Coming]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] and appeared in five episodes.

In 2015, he starred alongside his fellow ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' cast member [[Kate Dickie]] in the critically acclaimed horror film ''{{WPS|The Witch (2015 film)|The Witch}}''.
