'''Karlon Stark''' was a younger son of the [[King in the North]] who was awarded lands on the eastern region of the [[North]] after putting down a rebellion of [[House Bolton]] of the [[Dreadfort]].

==Biography==
Karlon was a younger son of the [[King in the North]] who was awarded lands on the eastern region of the [[North]] after putting down a rebellion of [[House Bolton]] of the [[Dreadfort]].{{Ref|H&L311}}

Over time, Karlon's seat, known as Karl's Hold, became known as [[Karhold]], and his descendants became known as the [[House Karstark|Karstarks]].<ref>[http://viewers-guide.hbo.com/game-of-thrones/season1/#!/locations/karhold/ HBO viewers guide, season 1 map, Karhold entry]</ref><ref>[http://viewers-guide.hbo.com/game-of-thrones/season2/#!/locations/karhold/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Karhold entry]</ref>{{Ref|H&L311}}{{Ref|H&L609}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Karlon Stark's story is the same. The rebellion that he helped put down was aided by another cadet branch of the Starks, House Greystark.
