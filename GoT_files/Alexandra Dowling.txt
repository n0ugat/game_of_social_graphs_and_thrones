'''Alexandra Dowling''' (born 22 May 1990) is a British actress. She is a graduate from Oxford School of Drama and has a number of theater credits. She appeared in BBC's ''Merlin ''and will play the female lead in the feature film ''Hammer of the Gods'' opposite [[James Cosmo]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she is playing the role of [[Roslin Tully]]. She joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]].
