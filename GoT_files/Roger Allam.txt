'''Roger William Allam''' (born 26 October 1953) is an English actor noted for his roles on stage and on screen. He has won the [[wikipedia:Laurence Olivier Award|Laurence Olivier Award]] for Best Actor once and been nominated twice more. On stage he is best known for playing Inspector Javert in the original London run of ''[[wikipedia:Les Miserables|Les Miserables]]'', while on television he is known for recurring roles in political satire ''[[wikipedia:The Thick of It|The Thick of It]]'' and in the second season of ''[[wikipedia:Ashes to Ashes (TV series)|Ashes to Ashes]]''. In film he is noted for roles in ''[[wikipedia:V For Vendetta|V For Vendetta]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Tamara Drewe|Tamara Drewe]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Allam plays the role of [[Illyrio Mopatis]], replacing [[Ian McNeice]] who had played the role in the unscreened [[Pilot (Game of Thrones)|pilot]] and been forced to withdraw due to a scheduling conflict. Allam was announced in the role on 19 September 2010.
