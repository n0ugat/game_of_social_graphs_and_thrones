'''Jessica Jensen''' is a 22 year old award winning British adult actress who has featured in over thirty hardcore movies since she first entered the adult industry in late 2011. In 2012 she was crowned "Best Newcomer" at the UKAP awards ceremony.  

She was involved in the production of ''Game of Thrones'''s [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]], working as an extra. She was also seen in Season 6 episode 4, "[[Book of the Stranger]]".
