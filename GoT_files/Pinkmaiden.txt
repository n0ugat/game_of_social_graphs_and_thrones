'''Pinkmaiden'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in the [[Riverlands]], the area controlled by [[House Tully]] of [[Riverrun]]. The castle is located south of Riverrun, near the border with the [[Westerlands]] and a tributary of the [[Red Fork]] of the [[Trident]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Pinkmaiden is the seat of House Piper. It is one of the settlements sacked by [[Gregor Clegane]] at the beginning of the [[War of the Five Kings]].
