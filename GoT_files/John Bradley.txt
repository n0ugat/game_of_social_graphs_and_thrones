'''John Bradley West''' (credited as '''John Bradley''' in the series) is an English actor, born in Wythenshawe, Manchester in September 1988. He is a graduate of the Manchester Metropolitan University, where he gained an honors degree in acting.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', Bradley West is playing [[Samwell Tarly]]. The news was announced on the university website on July 6, 2010. He initially appeared as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and joined the starring cast for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
