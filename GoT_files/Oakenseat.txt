The '''Oakenseat'''{{Ref|H&L704}} was a living throne of the [[Reach|Kingdom of the Reach]].

==History==
The Oakenseat was planted by [[Garth the Gardener|Garth Greenhand]]. It was destroyed during a [[Dornish sack of Highgarden]].{{Ref|H&L704}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Oakenseat was the ancestral throne of the [[King of the Reach]]. Legend states that it was a living throne that was carved from an oak by [[Garth the Gardener]]. It was destroyed during a [[Dornishmen|Dornish]] sack of [[Highgarden]] late in the reign of Garth, who had grown senile by that point.
