==History==

[[Archmaester]] [[Ebrose]] hands this book to [[Samwell Tarly]], claiming that it might be useful when writing ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire (Ebrose)|A Chronicle of the Wars Following the Death of King Robert I]]''.{{Ref|GOT702}}
