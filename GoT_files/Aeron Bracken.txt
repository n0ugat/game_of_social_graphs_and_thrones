Ser '''Aeron Bracken'''{{Note|Pronounced the same as "Aaron."{{Ref|THTDB203}}}} was a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Bracken]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Aeron is a nephew of Lord [[Amos Bracken]] of [[Stone Hedge]].{{Ref|HOTD203}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Aeron is confronted by [[Davos Blackwood]] whilst moving boundary stones near the [[Burning Mill]], at the [[House Bracken|Bracken]]-[[House Blackwood|Blackwood]] border. The fight between the two of them eventually escalates into a [[Battle at the Burning Mill|full-scale battle]] between the two houses, in which Aeron is slain.{{Ref|HOTD203}}
