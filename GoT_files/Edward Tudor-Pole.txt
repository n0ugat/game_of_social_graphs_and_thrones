'''Edward Felix Tudor-Pole''' is an actor based in the UK. He presented the gameshow ''The Crystal Maze ''from 1993 to 1995, taking over from Richard O'Brien. He is a singer, guitarist and songwriter and has performed with the Sex Pistols, The Damned and The Boys, as well as his own band, Tenpole Tudor. Through his mother, he is a direct descendant of Sir Owen Tudor, the grandfather of King Henry VII of England.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he is playing the role of a [[protestor]]. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
