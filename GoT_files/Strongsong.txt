'''Strongsong'''{{Ref|C&R07}} is a castle in the [[Vale of Arryn]] and the seat of [[House Belmore]]. It is situated by a series of lakes in the [[Mountains of the Moon]].{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Strongsong is the seat of House Belmore, loyal and powerful vassals of [[House Arryn]]. Strongsong is within the southernmost river valley of the Snakewood Forest. Lord Benedar Belmore is one of the six Lords Declarant.
