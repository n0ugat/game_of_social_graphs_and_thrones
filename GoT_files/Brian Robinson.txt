'''Brian Robinson''' is a stuntman.

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he was a stunt double for [[Bill Paterson]] in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[The Green Council]]" and a stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[Rhaenyra the Cruel]]".
