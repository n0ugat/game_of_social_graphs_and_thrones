The '''Old Stone Bridge'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is an inn in the [[Crownlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Old Stone Bridge inn is a tall timbered building next to an old stone bridge. [[Brienne of Tarth]] stays there during her search for [[Sansa Stark]].
