Ser '''Steffon Fossoway''' is a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Fossoway]] of [[Cider Hall]] during the reign of [[Daeron II Targaryen|Daeron II]].{{Ref|H&L416}}

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Steffon is quick and muscular. His destrier is named Wrath. His cousin [[Raymun Fossoway|Raymun]] is his [[squire]].
