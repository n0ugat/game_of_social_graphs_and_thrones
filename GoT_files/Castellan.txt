'''Castellan'''{{Ref|HOTD203}} is a senior position granted by a [[Lordship|lord]] to a member of his household. It confers responsibility for the defense and governance of a castle or territory when the lord is away or cannot attend to those tasks himself, such as in the case of a lord's minority.
