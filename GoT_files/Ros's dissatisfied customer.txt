==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
He pays for the services of [[Ros]] in the brothel. She begins to cry as they are just beginning. He walks out, dissatisfied. Littlefinger himself emerges to answer his complaints and arranges for him to see [[Armeca]] instead, an arrangement that he has no objection to.{{Ref|GOT202}}
