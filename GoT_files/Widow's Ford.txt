The '''Widow's Ford'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a ford in the [[Riverlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Widow's Ford was the site of a battle during the [[coming of the Andals]], in which three sons of Lord [[House Darry|Darry]] held off the [[Andals|Andal]] warlord Vorian Vypren for a day and a night.
