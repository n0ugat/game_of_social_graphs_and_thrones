'''Starpike'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a castle in the [[Reach]] and the seat of [[House Peake]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Starpike is the seat of House Peake. Additionally, they used to own the castles [[Dunstonbury]] and [[Whitegrove]]. The current Lord of Starpike is Titus Peake.
