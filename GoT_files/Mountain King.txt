'''Mountain King'''{{Ref|H&L122}} was a title held by the [[First Men]] rulers of the [[Vale of Arryn|Vale]] until the [[coming of the Andals]]. The last of the Mountain Kings was the [[Griffin King]], who was defeated by the "Winged Knight", Ser [[Artys Arryn]]. Afterward, [[House Arryn]] replaced the line of the Mountain Kings as the rulers of the [[Vale of Arryn|Kingdom of the Mountain and the Vale]].
