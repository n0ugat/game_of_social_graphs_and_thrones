| twitter= }}'''Marquis Kofi Rodriguez''' is an American actor. He has appeared in films such as ''{{WPS|Phoebe in Wonderland}}'', ''{{WPS|Definitely, Maybe}}'' and ''{{WPS|Saturday Church}}'' and television series such as ''{{WPS|Law & Order: Special Victims Unit}}'', ''{{WPS|Blue Bloods}}'', ''{{WPS|The Affair (TV series)|The Affair}}'', ''{{WPS|Chicago Fire (TV series)|Chicago Fire}}'' and ''{{WPS|When They See Us}}''. He also appeared in the {{WPS|Marvel Cinematic Universe}} {{WPS|Netflix}} series ''{{WPS|Luke Cage (TV series)|Luke Cage}}'' and ''{{WPS|Iron Fist (TV series)|Iron Fist}}'', working with [[Finn Jones]] and [[Jessica Henwick]] in the latter. He has also played young Simba in the Broadway version of ''The Lion King''.
