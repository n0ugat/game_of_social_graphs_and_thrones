'''Francis Magee''' is an Irish actor. He is best known for his role as Liam in British soap opera ''[[wikipedia:EastEnders|EastEnders]]''. More recent roles have included as surgeon [[wikipedia:William Beatty (surgeon)|William Beatty]] (the ship's doctor on [[wikipedia:HMS Victory|HMS ''Victory'']] at the [[wikipedia:Battle of Trafalgar|Battle of Trafalgar]] in ''Trafalgar Battle Surgeon'', and as the father of singer [[wikipedia:Boy George|Boy George]] in ''[[wikipedia:Worried About the Boy|Worried About the Boy]]'' (which also features [[Richard Madden]]).

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Magee is playing [[Yoren]]. He was confirmed in the role on 16 July 2010. He initially appeared as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and returned in this role for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].

He is also most commonly seen all over the world by teenagers on the Nickelodeon TV show, '''''House of Anubis '''''(which also features [[Eugene Simon]]). In the show, he plays the grumpy caretaker Victor Rudenmaar, who is searching for eternal life. He has been in two seasons already.
