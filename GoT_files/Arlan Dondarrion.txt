Ser '''Arlan Dondarrion''' was a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Dondarrion]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Ser Arlan fought in a series of border conflicts with [[Dorne]]. He knighted [[Criston Cole]], a footsoldier and son of the [[Criston Cole's father|steward]] of [[Blackhaven]], after the razing of two Dornish watchtowers along the [[Boneway]].{{Ref|HOTD102}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Criston mentions Ser Arlan when he is brought before [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] in [[Maegor's Holdfast]] as a candidate to replace Ser [[Ryam Redwyne]] on the [[Kingsguard/House of the Dragon|Kingsguard]].{{Ref|HOTD102}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Arlan Dondarrion.
