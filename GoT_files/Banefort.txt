'''Banefort'''{{Ref|GOT701}} is a castle in the [[Westerlands]]. It is located south-westernmost point of [[Ironman's Bay]], on the shores of the [[Sunset Sea]] and north of the [[Crag]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Banefort is located in the same point. It is the seat of House Banefort, a noble house sworn to [[House Lannister]] of [[Casterly Rock]].
