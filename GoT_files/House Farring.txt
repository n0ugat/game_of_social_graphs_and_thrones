'''House Farring'''{{Ref|HOTD203}} is a [[noble house]] of the [[Crownlands]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
House Farring supports [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].{{Ref|HOTD203}}

==Members==
*[[Lord Farring]], head of House Farring during the Dance of the Dragons.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Farring is among the principal supporters of [[Stannis Baratheon]] in the [[War of the Five Kings]]. Their coat of arms is per pale purple and white, two knights combatant with swords, counter-charged.

House Farring's involvement in the Dance of the Dragons is unknown.

===Members===
*Annara Farring, the seventh wife of [[Walder Frey]]
*Ser Gilbert Farring, commander of the [[Storm's End]] garrison.
**Bryen Farring, his son. [[Squire]] to Stannis Baratheon.
*Ser Godry Farring, Gilbert's cousin, called Giantslayer after killing a fleeing [[giant]] during the battle beneath the [[Wall]].
