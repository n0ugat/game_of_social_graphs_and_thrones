| imdb= http://www.imdb.com/name/nm4475182/}}'''Dennis McKeever''' is a British actor based in Northern Ireland.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played the role of an unnamed [[Night's Watch officer]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third]] seasons. At first he was thought to be playing [[Othell Yarwyck]], but the character [[Brian Fortune]] had been playing was identified as such in [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|Season 4]].
