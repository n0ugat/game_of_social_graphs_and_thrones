== Themes and leitmotifs ==
A list of the [[Themes and leitmotifs (Game of Thrones)|themes and leitmotifs]] that are introduced in this soundtrack, in order of appearance.

* [[Themes and leitmotifs (Game of Thrones)#The Night King's theme|The Night King's theme]]

==Appearances==
#"Main Title": Opening credits.
#"The Rains of Castamere": Unused.
#"Arrival at Winterfell": Jon and Dany arrive at Winterfell. ("[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]")
#"Flight of Dragons": Jon and Dany ride Rhaegal and Drogon respectively. ("Winterfell")
#"Heir to the Throne": Jon finds out about his parentage. ("Winterfell")
#"Jenny of Oldstones": Unused instrumental version of Pod's singing. ("[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms (Game of Thrones)|A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]")
#"A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms": Jaime knights Brienne. ("A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms")
#"The Battle of Winterfell": Dothraki charge into the army of the dead. ("[[The Long Night]]")
#"The Dead are Already Here": Melisandre lights the trench. ("The Long Night")
#"Battle for the Skies": The dragons fight above Winterfell. ("The Long Night")
#"The Long Night, Pt. 1": Arya fights wights, Sandor panics, Lyanna slays a wight giant. ("The Long Night")
#"The Long Night, Pt. 2": Jorah and Dany fight wights. ("The Long Night")
#"The Night King": The Night King approaches Bran. ("The Long Night")
#"Dead Before the Dawn": The White Walkers are defeated. ("The Long Night")
#"Not Today": End credits of "The Long Night".
#"Farewell": Mass funeral at Winterfell. ("[[The Last of the Starks]]")
#"Outside the Gates": Parley at the gates of King's Landing. ("The Last of the Starks")
#"The Bells": Arya and Sandor approach the Red Keep. ("[[The Bells]]")
#"The Last War": The Targaryens attack King's Landing. ("The Bells")
#"Into the Fire": Sandor tackles Gregor into a fiery chasm. ("The Bells")
#"For Cersei": Cersei and Jaime's death, end credits. ("The Bells")
#"Believe": Arya wakes up. ("The Bells")
#"Stay a Thousand Years": Unused. A rendition of "Truth".
#"Nothing Else Matters": Tyrion finds the corpses of Jaime and Cersei. ("[[The Iron Throne]]")
#"Master of War": Dany's speech to her armies, Dany approaches the Iron Throne. ("The Iron Throne")
#"Be with Me": Dany is assassinated. ("The Iron Throne")
#"The Iron Throne": The Iron Throne is melted. ("The Iron Throne")
#"Break the Wheel": The Great Council. ("The Iron Throne")
#"You Have a Choice": Jon leaves King's Landing. ("The Iron Throne")
#"The White Book": Brienne finishes Jaime's entry in ''The Book of Brothers''. ("The Iron Throne")
#"The Last of the Starks": Jon arrives at Castle Black, Sansa is crowned Queen in the North, Arya sails across the Sunset Sea. ("The Iron Throne")
#"A Song of Ice and Fire": Jon takes the wildlings north of the Wall. ("The Iron Throne")
