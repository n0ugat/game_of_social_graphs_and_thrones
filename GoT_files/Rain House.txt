'''Rain House'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in the [[Stormlands]], the region controlled by [[House Baratheon]] of [[Storm's End]]. It is the seat of [[House Wylde]]. The castle is located south-east of Storm's End, near the eastern tip of a peninsula extending into the [[Narrow Sea]]. It overlooks the southern coastline of [[Shipbreaker Bay]], south of the island of [[Tarth]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Rain House is the seat of House Wylde. It lies on the far eastern edge of Cape Wrath.

In ''A Dance with Dragons'', a force of the [[Golden Company]] captures Rain House on behalf of the presumed-dead [[Aegon Targaryen (son of Elia Martell)|Aegon Targaryen]], the son of Rhaegar and Elia who has returned to Westeros to claim the [[Iron Throne]].
