Lady '''Arwen Swann''' was a member of [[House Swann]]. 

==Biography==
===Background===
Arwen Swann was the daughter of [[Ector Swann]] and [[Eleanor Swann]]. She married [[Iain Umber (son of Gareth)|Iain Umber]] and bore him a son, also named [[Iain Umber]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She is referenced in her husband's entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
