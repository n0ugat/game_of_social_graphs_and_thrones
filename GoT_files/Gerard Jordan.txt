'''Gerard Jordan-Quinn''' is a Northern Irish actor, best known for his role in the film ''[[wikipedia:Five Minutes of Heaven|Five Minutes of Heaven]]'' (alongside [[wikipedia:James Nesbitt|James Nesbitt]] and [[wikipedia:Liam Neeson|Liam Neeson]]) and the TV series ''[[wikipedia:Pulling Moves|Pulling Moves]]'' (where he encountered ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' director [[Brian Kirk]]).

In ''Game of Thrones'' Jordan is playing the role of [[Biter]] and is credited as "Gerard Jordan". He was confirmed in the role on 2 September 2011.<ref>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/Rorge_and_Biter_Confirmed/ Westeros.org]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
