Queen '''Visneya Targaryen''' was the wife and queen of [[Viserys Targaryen (son of Rhaenyra)|Viserys II Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She is mentioned under [[Aemon Targaryen (son of Viserys II)|Aemon the Dragonknight]]'s entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'', which records her being the wife and queen of Viserys II and the mother of his children.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Viserys II did not marry one of his relatives; he married Larra Rogare, a [[Lys]]ene noblewoman. Viserys did have a younger sister named [[Visenya Targaryen (daughter of Rhaenyra)|Visenya]], but she was stillborn.
