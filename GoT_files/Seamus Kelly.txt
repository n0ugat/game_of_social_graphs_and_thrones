In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a Gold Cloak in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[Lord Snow]]".

He was also a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[The Wolf and the Lion]]".
