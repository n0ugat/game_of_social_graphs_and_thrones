The '''Spear Tower'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} is the dominant structure in [[Sunspear]], the seat of [[House Martell]]. It is a 150-foot structure with a pinnacle of shining steel.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Spear Tower is a massive tower of stone, thrusts up from the surrounding community, stretching one hundred and fifty feet tall, with a spear of gilded steel atop that adds a further thirty feet. Highborn prisoners might be confined in cells at the top of the Spear Tower.
