'''Bryan''' served as [[Jaime Lannister]]'s [[squire]] on the day of [[Willem Frey]]'s wedding tournament.

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
While imprisoned by [[House Stark]], [[Alton Lannister|Alton]] and Jaime talk about [[Willem Frey]]'s wedding. Alton mentions he filled in as Jaime's squire, when Bryan threw up on his horse. Jaime said it was [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] who made him so drunk.{{Ref|GOT207}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there are several characters whose first name is Bryan (Bryan Fossoway, Bryan Frey), but none of them have ever squired for Jaime. 

In the fourth novel, during the journey to resolve the [[Second siege of Riverrun|siege of Riverrun]], Jaime is escorted by three squires - Lewys Piper, Garrett Paege, and Josmyn Peckledon. There is no mention that he had any squires before that.
