'''Alejandro Martínez''' is a Mexican cinematographer working on ''[[House of the Dragon]]'' with director [[Clare Kilner]].

Martínez was nominated for the 2023 {{WPS|American Society of Cinematographers|ASC Awards}} in the "Episode of a One-Hour Non-Commercial Television Series" category for his work on the episode "[[The Green Council]]."<ref>https://deadline.com/2023/01/asc-awards-nominations-2023-cinematograhpers-guild-1235214726/</ref>
