'''House Gargalen'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} of [[Salt Shore]] is a [[Noble house|vassal house]] which holds fealty to [[House Martell]] of [[Dorne]]. Their seat is at Salt Shore, the southernmost castle in [[Westeros]]. House Gargalen's heraldry is a red cockatrice on a golden field.

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
[[Joffrey Baratheon|Joffrey]] mentions Salt Shore to [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]], saying how lords will travel to their wedding from [[Last Hearth]] in the north to Salt Shore in the south.{{Ref|GOT106}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
[[File:401 Gargalen heraldry.jpg|thumb|200px|At far right, blurry and not in focus, a banner displaying House Gargalen's heraldry (a red cockatrice on a gold field).]]
A House Gargalen banner is seen amongst the delegation of Dornish lords who accompany Prince [[Oberyn Martell]] to [[King's Landing]].{{Ref|GOT401}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Gargalen rules Salt Shore. Prince [[Doran Martell]] squired for Lord Tremond Gargalen in his youth. 

Lord Gargalen is among the Dornish nobles that accompany Oberyn Martell to King's Landing.

===Known members===
*Lord Tremond Gargalen, the Lord of Salt Shore.
