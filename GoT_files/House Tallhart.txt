'''House Tallhart'''{{Ref|GOT205}}{{Note|Referred to as the "House of Tallhart."}} of [[Torrhen's Square]] is a vassal house that holds fealty to [[House Stark]] of [[Winterfell]]. House Tallhart's lands are south-west of Winterfell. Their seat is Torrhen's Square.

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
[[Theon Greyjoy|Theon]] consults with [[Dagmer]] how he can prove himself as a true Iron Islander. Theon mentions that Torrhen's Square lies near to the [[Stony Shore]]; Dagmer says that the seat of House Tallhart is more worthy prize than raiding fishermen's villages.<ref>"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Tallhart is a masterly house sworn to the Starks of Winterfell as Masters of Torrhen's Square. According to a semi-canon source, House Tallhart descends from the [[First Men]].

In the novels, House Tallhart's heraldry consists of three green sentinel trees on a brown background. The TV series changed this to one green sentinel tree, on a white background.{{Cite}}

===Known members===
*Ser {Helman Tallhart}, the head of the family and Master of Torrhen's Square. Slain in the Battle of [[Duskendale]].{{Bookref|ASOS|37}}
**{Benfred Tallhart}, Helman's eldest son and heir. Slain by the [[ironborn]] during the harrying of the [[Stony Shore]].{{Bookref|ACOK|37}}
**Eddara Tallhart, Helman's only daughter. Held captive by the ironborn at Torrhen's Square.{{Bookref|AFFC|Appendix}}
*{Leobald Tallhart}, Helman's brother and Castellan of Torrhen's Square. Slain by [[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay Snow]] during the [[Sack of Winterfell#In the books|Sack of Winterfell]].{{Bookref|ACOK|66}}
*Berena Tallhart, his wife of [[House Hornwood]]. Held captive by the ironborn at Torrhen's Square.{{Bookref|AFFC|Appendix}}
***Brandon Tallhart, their eldest son. Held captive by the ironborn at Torrhen's Square.{{Bookref|AFFC|Appendix}}
***Beren Tallhart, their younger son. Held captive by the ironborn at Torrhen's Square.{{Bookref|AFFC|Appendix}}

In ''A Dance with Dragons'', following the [[fight by Deepwood Motte]], House Tallhart is one of the northern houses who join [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]] in [[Battle in the ice#In the books|his campaign against the Boltons]].{{Bookref|ADWD|35}}
