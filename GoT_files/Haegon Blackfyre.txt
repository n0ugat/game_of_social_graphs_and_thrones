'''Haegon Blackfyre''' is a son of [[Daemon Blackfyre]].

==Biography==
Haegon is the younger brother and heir of [[Daemon Blackfyre (son of Daemon)|Daemon the Younger]]. He has a son, also named [[Daemon Blackfyre (son of Haegon)|Daemon]], either after his father or his brother. He lives in [[exile]] with his family.{{Ref|H&L803}}

==Family==
{{Blackfyre tree (A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms)}}

==In the books==
In ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', following the [[Battle of Redgrass Field|Battle of the Redgrass Field]], his uncle [[Aegor Rivers|Bittersteel]] took him to [[Tyrosh]]. In exile he had at least two sons, the eldest of which he named Daemon.
