'''Vivian Cerwyn''' was a head of [[House Cerwyn]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Vivian married [[Cleos Umber]], with whom she had a daughter, [[Katrina Umber|Katrina]]. Katrina later married [[Joseth Umber]] and had two sons, [[Maynard Umber|Maynard]] and [[Cedric Umber|Cedric]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She is mentioned in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
