Lord '''Dorian Hightower''' was the head of [[House Hightower]] during the [[coming of the Andals]].

==Biography==
When the [[Andals]] invaded Westeros, Dorian told [[Dorian Hightower's first wife|his wife]], to whom he had been married for two decades, that wars have a negative impact on trade, and abandoned her to marry [[Dorian Hightower's second wife|a young Andal princess]].{{Ref|H&L612}}

==In the books==
In ''The World of Ice & Fire'', Dorian Hightower's story is the same.
