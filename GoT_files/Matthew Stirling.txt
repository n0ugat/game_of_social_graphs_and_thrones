'''Matthew Stirling''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[Blackwater]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episode "[[Walk of Punishment (episode)|Walk of Punishment]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[Two Swords]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[Stormborn]]", "[[The Spoils of War]]", "[[Eastwatch]]" and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]".
