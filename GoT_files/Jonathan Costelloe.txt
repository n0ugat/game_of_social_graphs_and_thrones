'''Jonathan Costelloe''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episode "[[The Spoils of War]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Long Night]]".
