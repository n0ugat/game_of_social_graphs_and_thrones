'''Vanessa Taylor''' is an American scriptwriter and producer. She has written several episodes of ''[[wikipedia:Alias (TV series)|Alias]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Everwood (TV series)|Everwood]]''. She was a writer and producer for ''[[wikipedia:Tell Me You Love Me|Tell Me You Love Me]]''.

On ''[[Game of Thrones]],'' Taylor is a co-executive producer<ref>[http://www.deadline.com/2011/09/movie-lit-agent-jumping-to-icm-from-uta/ Deadline]</ref> and scriptwriter, penning two episodes of the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]]<ref>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/One_More_Season_2_Writer/ Westeros.org]</ref> and one of the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third]].<ref>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/HBO_Releases_Season_3_Episode_Writers_and_Directors Westeros.org]</ref>

Taylor left the ''Game of Thrones'' crew after Season 3, to work on the film adaptation of the young adult novel ''Divergent'' (2014).
