'''Master of Revels'''{{Scriptref|HOTD101}} is a title in the [[Seven Kingdoms]]. The holder of the office is tasked with overseeing royal festivities.

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
The [[Master of Revels (The Heirs of the Dragon)|Master of Revels]] serves as the herald for the [[Heir's Tournament]].<ref name="Heirs">"[[The Heirs of the Dragon]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is a master of revels in the royal court of [[Joffrey Baratheon|Joffrey I]].{{Bookref|ACOK|2}} In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], there is a "master of the games" who enrolls and pairs participants in tourneys.{{Bookref|THK}}
