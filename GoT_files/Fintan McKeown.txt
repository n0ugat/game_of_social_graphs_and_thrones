| imdb=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0571654/}}'''Fintan McKeown''' is an Irish actor, noted for appearing in episodes of ''[[wikipedia:Merlin (TV series)|Merlin]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Star Trek: Voyager|Star Trek: Voyager]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played the role of Ser [[Amory Lorch]]. He was confirmed in the role on 12 September 2011<ref>[http://winter-is-coming.net/2011/09/amory-lorch-cast-gregor-recast/ Winter is Coming]</ref>. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
