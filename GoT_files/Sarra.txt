'''Sarra''' is present at [[Winterfell]] during the celebration of the victory in the [[Battle of Winterfell]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
Sarra and [[Willa (The Last of the Starks)|Willa]] approach [[Tormund]] and [[Sandor Clegane|the Hound]] at the feast. Willa seduces Tormund who recommends to the Hound that they drown their sorrows, to which Clegane replies that he has not finished his drink. Sarra sits beside him as he downs his cup and asks if he is ready for her. He reveals that he was referring to a jug of wine. She continues her attempt at seduction, and he angrily rejects her. She is then approached by [[Podrick Payne|Pod]] and [[Winterfell girl|another woman]]. Pod charms her, and the three of them leave the hall together.{{Ref|GOT804}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the only characters whose name is Sarra are [[Sarra Frey]] and Sarra Stark, both of whom share no similarities with this character.
