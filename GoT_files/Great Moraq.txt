'''Great Moraq''',<ref name="Map1">[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> also known simply as '''Moraq''',<ref name="Map2">[[:File:HotD Nine Voyages Map.png]]</ref> is an island between the [[Summer Sea]] and the [[Jade Sea]], directly south of [[Qarth]].<ref name="Map1" /> The city of [[Port Moraq]] lies on its southern coast.<ref name="Map1" /> [[Corlys Velaryon]] passed by Great Moraq on at least two of his nine voyages.<ref name="Map2" />

==In the books==
In ''The Lands of Ice and Fire'', Great Moraq is the largest island in the [[known world]]. It is home to the city of Faros in the north, and Port Moraq in the south. The island was once conquered by Jar Joq, the seventh sea-green emperor of [[Yi Ti]].
