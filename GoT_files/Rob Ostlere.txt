'''Robert Ostlere''' is a British actor. He is an alumnus of [[wikipedia:RADA|RADA]] with extensive stage work in productions at the academy. On TV he has appeared in the British soap ''[[wikipedia:Doctors (2000 TV series)|Doctors]]'' and in the film ''Tortoise''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Ostlere played the role of Ser [[Waymar Royce]], replacing [[Jamie Campbell Bower]] who had played the role in the unaired [[Pilot (Game of Thrones)|pilot]]. Bower was unavailable for the reshoots of the prologue sequence.
