'''Ciarán Hinds''' (born February 9, 1953) is a Northern Irish actor. He is best known for his starring role as [[w:c:hbo-rome:Gaius Julius Caesar|Gaius Julius Caesar]] on the [[HBO]] series ''Rome'' and as [[w:c:harrypotter:Aberforth Dumbledore|Aberforth Dumbledore]] in ''[[w:c:harrypotter:Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2|Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2]]''. He has also appeared in the Steven Spielberg film ''Munich'', the British crime drama ''Above Suspicion'', ''Ivanhoe'', and ''Cold Lazarus''. He is also known for his roles as Russian President Alexander Nemerov in the 2002 film adaptation of Tom Clancy's book ''​The Sum of All Fears''​, as Richard Firmin in the 2004 film version of Andrew Lloyd Webber's musical ''​Phantom of the Opera'', and as Roarke/The Devil (alias [[w:c:marvel:Mephisto_(Earth-121347)|Mephistopheles]]) in the 2012 film ''[[w:c:marvel:Ghost Rider: Spirit of Vengeance|Ghost Rider: Spirit of Vengeance]]'', taking over the role from Peter Fonda. He portrayed [[w:c:dcextendeduniverse:Steppenwolf|Steppenwolf]] in the 2017 film ''[[w:c:dcextendeduniverse: Justice League (film)|Justice League]]'', alongside fellow ''Thrones'' actors [[Jason Momoa]] and [[Michael McElhatton]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played [[Mance Rayder]]. He was announced as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] on August 17, 2012.<ref>[http://insidetv.ew.com/2012/08/17/game-of-thrones-mance-rayder/ Game of Thrones' casts 'Rome' actor as Mance Rayder -- EXCLUSIVE]</ref>
