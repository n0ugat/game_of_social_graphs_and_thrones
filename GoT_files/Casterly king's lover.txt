The '''Casterly king's lover''' lived during the [[Age of Heroes]].

==Behind the scenes==
On January 8, 2019, ''Entertainment Weekly'' announced that [[Alex Sharp]] had been cast as a series regular in ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', a ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' [[World of Westeros|prequel]] [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]].{{Webref|name=EW |author=James Hibberd |date=January 8, 2019 |url=https://ew.com/tv/2019/01/08/game-of-thrones-prequel-cast-director/ |title=''Game of Thrones'' prequel announces diverse full cast, female director |website=Entertainment Weekly |access-date=January 9, 2019 }} On February 18, 2024, Wake the Dragon revealed that he had portrayed the young Casterly king's lover.<ref name="Lover" />

==In the books==
The Casterly king's lover was an invention of [[Jane Goldman]] for ''[[Bloodmoon]]''.
