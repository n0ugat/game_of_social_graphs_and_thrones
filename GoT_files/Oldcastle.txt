'''Oldcastle'''{{Ref|GOT402}} is a castle in the [[North]]. It is located on the northern coast of the [[Bite]], south-east of [[White Harbor]], and not far from the mouth of the [[White Knife]] river.

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
Oldcastle is seen on a map of the North that [[Roose Bolton]] has in the [[Dreadfort]], when he is pointing at [[Moat Cailin]] to the west.{{Ref|GOT402}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Oldcastle is the seat of House Locke.
