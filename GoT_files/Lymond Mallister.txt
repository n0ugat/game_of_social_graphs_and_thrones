Lord '''Lymond Mallister''' was the head of [[House Mallister]] and a vassal of [[House Tully]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Lymond Mallister's son, Ser [[Rymun Mallister]], is presented before Princess [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] when [[Lord Commander of the Kingsguard|Lord Commander]] [[Ryam Redwyne]] of the [[Kingsguard/House of the Dragon|Kingsguard]] passes away, and a vacancy becomes available. Ser [[Harrold Westerling]] speaks of Ser Rymun's father when he lists his various accomplishments.{{Ref|HOTD102}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Lymond Mallister is the Lord of Seagard during the early reign of King [[Viserys I Targaryen]]. He is notable for having unhorsed Ser [[Criston Cole]] during the [[Tournament|tourney]] at [[Maidenpool]] to celebrate the king's ascension to the [[Iron Throne]].
