The '''Weeping Water'''{{Ref|GOT406}} is a river in the [[North]]. It runs past the [[Dreadfort]] and through the lands of [[House Bolton]] into the [[Shivering Sea]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
[[File:Weeping-Water.jpg|thumb|250px|[[Yara Greyjoy]] and her crew paddling up the Weeping Water.]]
[[Yara Greyjoy]] sails from [[Pyke (island)|Pyke]] to lead an [[assault on the Dreadfort]] to rescue her captured brother [[Theon Greyjoy|Theon]]. She and her crew paddle up the Weeping Water.<ref>"[[The Laws of Gods and Men]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Ramsay Snow was raised by his mother at a mill along the Weeping Water up river from the Dreadfort.
