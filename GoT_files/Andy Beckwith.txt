| imdb=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0065874/}}'''Andy Beckwith''' is a British actor, best known for portraying the characters of Clanker in the second and third ''[[wikipedia:Pirates of the Caribbean (film series)|Pirates of the Caribbean]]'' films and Errol in the [[wikipedia:Guy Ritchie|Guy Ritchie]] movie ''[[wikipedia:Snatch (film)|Snatch]]''. He appeared in ''Les Miserables'' (2012), as the Innkeeper. On TV, he appeared in ''Dinotopia'', ''The Bill'', ''Sins'', and ''Eastenders''.

He worked in the building trade, as a plasterer, and was a boxer, before becoming an actor. He also manages a Sunday League football team in the UK. He is described as not shy; when his team won the Cup, he took off all of his clothes and danced naked around the pitch.<ref>Andy Beckwith at Webspanner</ref>

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Beckwith plays the role of [[Rorge]]. He was announced in the role on 28 August 2011.<ref>[http://winter-is-coming.net/2011/08/rorge-cast/ Winter is Coming]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]], and returned for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]].<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2013/06/slynt-lysa-and-rorge-set-to-return-for-s4/ Winter is Coming]</ref>
