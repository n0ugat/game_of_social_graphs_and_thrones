"'''Sons of Winter'''"{{Ref|ATGS04}} is the fourth episode of ''[[Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series]]''. It premiered on May 26, 2015. It was written by Nicole Martinez and Brad Kane, and directed by Kent Mudle.
