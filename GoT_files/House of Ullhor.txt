==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, King [[Cleon]] is a former slave of [[Greizhen mo Ullhor|Grazdan mo Ullhor]].

The survivors of a failed assault against the besieging [[Yunkai]]'i forces hold a great feast in the pyramid of Ullhor, where they commit mass suicide by drinking [[poison]].
