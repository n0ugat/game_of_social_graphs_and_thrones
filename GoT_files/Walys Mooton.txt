Lord '''Walys Mooton'''{{Con|F&B}} is the Lord of [[Maidenpool]] and head of [[House Mooton]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].<ref name="Mooton" />

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Walys Mooton and three other riverlords arrive at [[Harrenhal]] to complain to [[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]] about the [[Raids on Bracken lands|atrocities]] performed by the [[House Blackwood|Blackwoods]] at the [[House Bracken|Brackens]]' lands. He says that punishing [[Amos Bracken|Lord Bracken]] for a treason is one thing, but not in such manner, and furiously points out "those beasts" proudly carried the banner of [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]].{{Ref|HOTD205}}

Lord Mooton is tasked by [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] to reclaim [[Rook's Rest]] for the [[Blacks]].{{Ref|HOTD206}}

After the death of Lord Grover, the riverlords are summoned once again to Harrenhal. At a meeting in the castle's [[godswood]], Lord Mooton witnesses the execution of Ser [[Willem Blackwood]].{{Ref|HOTD207}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Walys had two younger brothers, the eldest of whom was his heir presumptive Manfryd.
