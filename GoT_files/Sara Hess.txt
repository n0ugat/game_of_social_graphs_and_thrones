'''Sara Lee Hess''' is an American screenwriter and producer. She is an executive producer of ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', as well as a member of the writing staff.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2020/10/house-of-the-dragon-paddy-considine-cast-star-hbo-game-of-thrones-prequel-series-1234591696/ Deadline: 'House Of the Dragon': Paddy Considine To Star As King Viserys Targaryen in HBO's 'Game Of Thrones' Prequel]</ref>

==Biography==
In 2005, Hess joined the writing staff of HBO's ''Deadwood'', writing the second season episode "Advances, None Miraculous".

From 2005 to 2012, Hess wrote 17 episodes of the Fox drama series ''House''. In addition, she served as story editor from 2006 to 2007, executive story editor from 2007 to 2008, and producer from 2008 to 2012.

Since 2013, Hess has written 8 episodes of the Netflix comedy-drama series ''Orange Is the New Black'', for which she is an executive producer.

In 2014, Hess married the musician and actress Talia Osteen.

In 2019, Hess was attached as executive producer on [[Jane Goldman]]'s prequel [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]], working title ''[[Bloodmoon]]''.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2019/02/game-of-thrones-prequel-pilot-exec-producer-sara-lee-hess-overall-deal-hbo-1202566122/ Deadline: 'Game of Thrones' Prequel Pilot Exec Producer Sara Lee Hess Inks Overall Deal With HBO]</ref> However, the prequel was officially rejected on October 29, 2019.<ref>[https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/game-thrones-naomi-watts-led-prequel-dead-at-hbo-1250795/ The Hollywood Reporter: 'Game of Thrones': Naomi Watts-Led Prequel Dead at HBO]</ref>
