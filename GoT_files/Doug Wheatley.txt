'''Douglas H. Tropea-Wheatley''' is a Canadian comic book artist. Within ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'', he is best known for illustrating the book ''[[Fire & Blood]]''. He is also known for several ''Star Wars'' comic books, concept art, and cover art.

Martin]]," a video from the Blu-ray release{{Note|Pre-released on YouTube.}} of [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]].{{Webref|name=YouTube |author=GameofThrones |date=August 5, 2022 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qZX5JXJ8UoQ |title=Before the Dance: An Illustrated History with George R.R.
