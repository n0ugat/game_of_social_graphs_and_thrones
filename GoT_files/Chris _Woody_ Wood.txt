'''Chris "Woody" Wood''' (born 6 July 1985) is a British musician. He was a member of the British rock band {{WPS|Bastille (band)|Bastille}}, in which he played drums and percussion and sang backing vocals. Wood was replaced in the band sometime in the late 2010s by David Kendrick (who formerly played drums for Sparks and Devo).

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a [[wight]] in "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]".<ref>[https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/tv/news/game-of-thrones-bastille-cameo-season-7-finale-the-dragon-and-the-wold-a7917566.html Game of Thrones: You probably missed Bastille's cameo in the season 7 finale]</ref>
