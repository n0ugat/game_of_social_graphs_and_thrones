'''Freedmen'''{{Ref|H&L503}} are former [[Slavery|slaves]] in the [[Free Cities]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the term "freedmen" refers to slaves from [[Astapor]], [[Yunkai]], and [[Meereen]] who were freed by [[Daenerys Targaryen]].
