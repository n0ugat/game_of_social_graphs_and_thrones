'''Gormon Massey's son''' is a member of [[House Massey]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He is the firstborn son and heir of Lord [[Gormon Massey]]{{Ref|HOTD101}} and the brother of [[Elinda Massey|Elinda]]<ref name="WBD" /> and [[Emmon Massey]].{{Ref|HOTD203}} He is a squire to a knight of [[House Tarly]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] and [[Alicent Hightower|Alicent]] discuss the squire's betrothal to [[Elinor Stokeworth]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', a son of Gormon Massey is not mentioned.
