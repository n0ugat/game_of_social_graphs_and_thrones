'''Mormont Keep'''{{Note|Conjecture based on information from ''A Song of Ice and Fire Campaign Guide''; may be subject to change.}} is a castle on [[Bear Island]] in the [[North]]. It is named for [[House Mormont]], its historical rulers.

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
[[Jon Snow]], [[Sansa Stark]], and [[Davos Seaworth]] visit Mormont Keep to seek an audience with [[Lyanna Mormont]], the [[Lord of Bear Island|Lady of Bear Island]]. Lyanna musters 62 [[Man-at-arms|men-at-arms]] from her household to fight in Jon's army.{{Ref|GOT607}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Mormont Keep is a castle with wooden walls and a smoky keep.{{Bookref|AWOIAF|Bear Island}} The main hall is built with large logs and surrounded by a palisade of earth.{{Bookref|ACOK|12}} A carving on the gate depicts a woman with a babe in one hand and a battleaxe in the other.{{Bookref|ASOS|45}} The name "Mormont Keep" is not mentioned in the books, but is provided in the ''A Song of Ice and Fire'' Campaign Guide.
