'''Cornfield'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a settlement in the [[Westerlands]]. It the seat of [[House Swyft]], a knightly house sworn to [[House Lannister]] of [[Casterly Rock]]. It is located southeast of Casterly Rock and [[Lannisport]] and northeast of [[Crakehall]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Cornfield is the seat of House Swyft.
