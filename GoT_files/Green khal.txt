The '''green ''khal'''''<ref>https://wiki.languageinvention.com/index.php?title=Season_6_Game_of_Thrones_Dialogue</ref> was a [[Dothraki]] ''[[khal]]''.

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
He is part of the ''[[Khalar Vezhven]]'' assembled at the [[Temple of the Dosh Khaleen|Temple of the ''Dosh Khaleen'']] to decide the fate of [[Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys]]. He accuses another ''khal'' of killing [[Iggo]], a member of his ''[[khalasar]]'' (who was actually killed by [[Daario Naharis|Daario]]). He is soon after killed when Daenerys sets the temple on fire.{{Ref|GOT604}}
