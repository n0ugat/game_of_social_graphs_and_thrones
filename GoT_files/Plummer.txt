'''Plummer''' is the [[steward]] of [[Ashford]] during the reign of [[Daeron II Targaryen]].

==In the books==
In ''The Hedge Knight'', Plummer has a narrow, pinched face and thin grey hair. He acts as the master of the games during the [[Tournament at Ashford Meadow|tourney at Ashford Meadow]].
