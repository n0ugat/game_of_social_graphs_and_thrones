'''Sahara Knite''' <!--Please do not add real name--> (born 4 February 1975) is an English model, actress and adult film star. She worked as a clothing technologist in the fashion industry for six years before beginning her adult film career.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' she is playing [[Armeca]] and was confirmed in the role on 10 November 2010, although it had previously been rumored she was involved in the project. She initially appeared as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and returned in this role for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
