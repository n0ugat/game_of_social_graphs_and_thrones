'''Moe Dunford''' is an actor based in Ireland.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he is playing the role of a [[Stark messenger]] in "[[The Prince of Winterfell]]". He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].

From 2014 to present, Moe Dunford joined the cast of History Channel's Vikings as Aethelwulf, son of King Ecbert of Wessex.
