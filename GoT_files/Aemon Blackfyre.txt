'''Aemon Blackfyre''' was the second son of [[Daemon Blackfyre]], a legitimized [[bastard]] of King [[Aegon IV Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Aemon Blackfyre was the second son of Daemon Blackfyre, a legitimized [[bastard]] of King Aegon IV Targaryen and his cousin [[Daena Targaryen]]. He had three brothers: [[Aegon Blackfyre|Aegon]], his twin, as well as [[Daemon Blackfyre (son of Daemon)|Daemon Blackfyre]] and [[Haegon Blackfyre]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Aemon, along with his siblings, is mentioned in the [[House Targaryen]] entry of the book ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Aemon's background is the same. He squired for his father along with his brother Aegon.

Aemon was killed in the [[Battle of Redgrass Field|Battle of the Redgrass Field]] alongside his father and Aegon.
