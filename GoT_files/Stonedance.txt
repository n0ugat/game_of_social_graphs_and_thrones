'''Stonedance'''{{Ref|GOT105}} is a castle in the [[Crownlands]], the region controlled directly by the [[Iron Throne]] in [[King's Landing]]. The castle is located south of [[Sharp Point]], on the peninsula south of [[Blackwater Bay]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Stonedance is the seat of [[House Massey]], which gives the peninsula its name of Massey's Hook.
