'''Of Monsters and Men''' is a five-piece indie folk/indie rock band hailing from Keflavík, Iceland. Formed in 2010, the band have currently released two full studio albums, as well as one EP, and one live album.

All the band's lyrics are performed in English.

The full band lineup appeared in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] of ''Game of Thrones'', as the stage musicians in the play that [[Arya Stark|Arya]] sees in [[Braavos]], ''[[The Bloody Hand]]''.<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/icelandic-band-of-monsters-and-men-appearing-in-game-of-thrones-season-6/ Icelandic band Of Monsters and Men appearing in Game of Thrones season 6]</ref>
