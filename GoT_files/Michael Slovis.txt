'''Michael Slovis''' is an American Emmy winning director and cinematographer for film and television. He is best known for his work on ''Law and Order: SVU'', ''Elementary'', ''Chicago Fire'', ''CSI'', and was the cinematographer for ''[[w:c:breakingbad:Breaking Bad|Breaking Bad]]''. He also previously directed [[Nikolaj Coster-Waldau]]'s 2008 TV series ''New Amsterdam''.

On July 2, 2014, it was announced that Slovis would be directing two episodes in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]], the [[The Wars To Come|season premiere]] and the [[The House of Black and White|second episode]].<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2014/07/02/michael-slovis-joins-the-lineup-of-directors-for-game-of-thrones-season-5/#comment-420830 Michael Slovis joins the lineup of directors for Game of Thrones season 5]</ref>
