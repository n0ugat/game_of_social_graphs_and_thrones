The '''Giant's Lance'''{{Ref|H&L615}} is a mountain in the [[Vale of Arryn]].{{Ref|H&L615}}

==History==
The Giant's Lance was the site of the [[Battle of the Seven Stars]] where Ser [[Artys Arryn]] and the [[Griffin King]] fought for dominion over the Vale. Arryn eventually overcame and killed the Griffin King. [[Qyle Corbray]] was also among the dead there.

==In the books==
In the [[A Song of Ice and Fire|''A Song of Ice and Fire'']] novels, the same information is given about the Giant's Lance.
