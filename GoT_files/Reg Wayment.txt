'''Reg Wayment''' is an actor and stuntman based in the UK.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he is playing the role of a [[King's Landing rioter|rioter]]. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].

He was also a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[The Old Gods and the New]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episodes "[[First of His Name]]" and "[[The Mountain and the Viper]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]"
