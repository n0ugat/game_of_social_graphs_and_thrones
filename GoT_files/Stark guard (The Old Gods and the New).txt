This '''unnamed man''' is a [[House Stark]] guardsman fighting for King [[Robb Stark]] in the [[War of the Five Kings]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
He is a House Stark guardsman fighting for King [[Robb Stark]] in the [[War of the Five Kings]]. He is congratulated by Robb for having taken a [[House Lannister|Lannister]] helm.{{Ref|GOT206}}

He is present when [[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]] is recaptured and defends the prisoner against a vengeful [[Rickard Karstark]].{{Ref|GOT207}}
