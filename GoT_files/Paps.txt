The '''Paps'''{{Guideref|War of the Five Kings}} is an island located northeast of the [[Fingers]] in the [[Narrow Sea]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels the Paps is the seat of House Elesham, sworn to the [[Eyrie]].
