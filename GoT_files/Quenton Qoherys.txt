Lord '''Quenton Qoherys'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} was the [[Lord of Harrenhal]] after [[Aegon's Conquest]].

==Biography==
Quenton served as a general of [[Aegon I Targaryen]]. After the [[Burning of Harrenhal]], the castle was given to him by Aegon, although his line eventually went extinct.{{Ref|H&L214}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Quenton Qoherys was the master-at-arms of Dragonstone castle, and was granted Harrenhal by Aegon the Conqueror, after which he married the daughter of his liege lord, [[Edmyn Tully]].
