'''Milen Kaleichev''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[Sons of the Harpy (episode)|Sons of the Harpy]]", "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]" and "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[The Red Woman]]", "[[Oathbreaker]]", "[[The Door]]", "[[Blood of My Blood]]" and "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[Stormborn]]" and "[[The Queen's Justice]]".
