Ser '''Jaremy Mallister''' was a [[Knighthood|knight]] and member of [[House Mallister]]. He was a loyal bannerman of [[House Tully]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
Ser Jaremy was among the prisoners of the joint [[North|Northern]]-[[Riverlands]] army held captive at [[Harrenhal]]. Before abandoning the castle, Ser [[Gregor Clegane]] had all the prisoners put to the sword. When the Northern army, led by [[Robb Stark]], discovered the corpses, [[Catelyn Stark]] recognizes Ser Jaremy.{{Ref|GOT301}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of a member of House Mallister named Jaremy.
