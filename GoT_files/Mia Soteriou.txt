}}'''Mia Soteriou''' is an actress and musician. She is best known for her role in ''[[wikipedia:Mamma Mia! (film)|Mamma Mia!]]'', in which she acted as well as working as a vocal coach. She has also appeared in British TV series such as ''[[wikipedia:Holby City|Holby City]]'', ''[[wikipedia:The Bill|The Bill]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Absolutely Fabulous|Absolutely Fabulous]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' she played the role of [[Mirri Maz Duur]] and was confirmed in the role on 29 September 2010.
