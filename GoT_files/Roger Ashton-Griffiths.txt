|Last ="[[The Winds of Winter]]"}}'''Roger Ashton-Griffths''' is a British actor that was cast as Lord [[Mace Tyrell]], debuting in [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|Season 4]].<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2013/07/roger-ashton-griffiths-cast-as-mace-tyrell/ ]</ref>

He graduated from Lancaster University (BMus), and began his career as a singer with [[wikipedia:English National Opera|English National Opera]] at the [[wikipedia:London Coliseum|London Coliseum]].

He has appeared in numerous high-profile films, including Terry Gilliam's ''[[wikipedia:The Brothers Grimm (film)|The Brothers Grimm]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Brazil (1985 film)|Brazil]]'', ''[[wikipedia:A Knight's Tale|A Knight's Tale]]'', Martin Scorsese's ''[[wikipedia:Gangs of New York|Gangs of New York]]'', Roman Polanski's ''[[wikipedia:Pirates (1986 film)|Pirates]]'', Peter Greenaway's ''[[wikipedia:The Cook, the Thief, His Wife & Her Lover|The Cook]]'', Woody Allen's ''[[wikipedia:You Will Meet a Tall Dark Stranger|You Will Meet a Tall Dark Stranger]]'' and Olivier Dahan's ''[[wikipedia:Grace of Monaco (film)|Grace of Monaco]]''.

He has also worked extensively in television, including ''[[wikipedia:Margaret (2009 film)|Margaret]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:The Tudors|The Tudors]]''.

On his character, [[Mace Tyrell]], Ashton Griffiths says: "''Well, Mace is described variably by others as an oaf; the Lord of Oaf. However, in my view he's rather a wise old cookie, and is just bumbling along.''"<ref>http://fuckyeahiwanrheon.tumblr.com/</ref>

He is currently starring the BBC/FX period drama series ''[[wikipedia:Taboo (2017 TV series)|Taboo]]'', along with his ''Game of Thrones'' co-stars [[Jonathan Pryce]], [[Nicholas Blane]], [[Oona Chaplin]], [[Jefferson Hall]], and [[Mark Gatiss]].
