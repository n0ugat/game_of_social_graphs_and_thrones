'''Iain Umber''' was a member of [[House Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Iain Umber was born to [[Iain Umber (son of Gareth)|Iain Umber]], his father by the same name, and [[Arwen Swann]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He is mentioned in his father's entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
Iain Umber is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
