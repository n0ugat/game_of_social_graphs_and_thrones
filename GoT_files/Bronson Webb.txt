'''Bronson John Webb''' (born 20 February 1983) is a British actor. Webb has an extensive string of TV and film appearances, including roles in [[wikipedia:The Bill|''The Bill'']], [[wikipedia:Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|''Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban'']], [[wikipedia:Kingdom of Heaven|''Kingdom of Heaven'']] and [[wikipedia:The Dark Knight (film)|''The Dark Knight'']].

In [[HBO]]'s ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played the role of [[Will]] in the unaired pilot episode. He announced via his website he had been cast in the role on 1 October 2009.

On 7 July 2010 it was revealed that the prologue sequence was being reshot and Webb was not available for the reshoots, necessitating the recasting of the role. However, on 2 September 2010 HBO confirmed that the availability issues had been resolved and Webb would be returning to the role.
