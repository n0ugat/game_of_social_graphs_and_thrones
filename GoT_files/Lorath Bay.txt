'''Lorath Bay'''<ref>https://entertainment.ha.com/itm/movie-tv-memorabilia/props/essos-map-from-game-of-thrones-hbo-r-original-2011-2019-/a/7366-89866.s</ref> is a bay located south of [[Lorath]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there are leviathans, seals, and walruses in Lorath Bay. It is claimed by Lorath, but the more powerful nations of [[Braavos]] and [[Ibben]] can move through it and fish its waters with impunity.
