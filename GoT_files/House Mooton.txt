'''House Mooton'''{{Ref|GOT205}} of [[Maidenpool]] is a vassal house that holds fealty to [[House Tully]] of [[Riverrun]]. They control Maidenpool, a port town near the border between the [[Riverlands]] and the [[Crownlands]], on the Bay of Crabs. Their sigil is a red salmon to sinister within a gold tressure on white.{{Ref|GOT205}}{{Ref|HOTD104}}

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Lord Mooton]] travels to [[Storm's End]] to put himself forward as a suitor to Princess [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]].{{Ref|HOTD104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
To protect her identity, [[Arya Stark|Arya]] claims to be from Maidenpool while serving as [[Tywin Lannister|Tywin]]'s [[cupbearer]] at [[Harrenhal]]. Tywin catches her out in her lie when she cannot identify the sigil of House Mooton.{{Ref|GOT205}}

==Members==
*{[[Lord Mooton]]}, the head of House Mooton during the reign of King [[Viserys I Targaryen]].
*Lord {[[Walys Mooton]]}, the head of House Mooton during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Mooton are the rulers of Maidenpool. According to semi-canon sources, their motto is "Wisdom and Strength." Their current lord is William Mooton.
