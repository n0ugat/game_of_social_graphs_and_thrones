'''Jellicoe''' was a [[Order of Maesters|maester]] of the [[Citadel]].

==Biography==
Jellicoe claimed that the settlement that later became [[Oldtown]] began as a trading post where ships from [[Old Valyria|Valyria]], [[Old Ghis]] and the [[Summer Isles]] put in to replenish provisions and conduct repairs.{{Ref|H&L612}}

==In the books==
In ''The World of Ice & Fire'', Maester Jellicoe supposedly lived among the [[Children of the Forest]]. Based on his theory about the foundation of [[Oldtown]], he dated the city to the [[Dawn Age]] before the arrival of the [[First Men]] in [[Westeros]].
