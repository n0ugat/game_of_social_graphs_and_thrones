'''Rowly Bolton''' was a [[Red King]] of [[House Bolton]] who lived during the [[Age of Heroes]].

==Behind the scenes==
On November 21, 2024, ''Redanian Intelligence'' revealed that [[James O'Driscoll]] had portrayed a character named King Rowly Bolton in ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', the rejected ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' [[World of Westeros|prequel]] [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]].<ref name="Redanian" />

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and its supplementary material, there is no mention of a character named Rowly Bolton. The character was invented by [[Jane Goldman]] for ''Bloodmoon''.
