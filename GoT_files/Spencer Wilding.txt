}}'''Spencer Wilding''' is a Welsh actor. He guest starred as a [[White Walkers|White Walker]] in "[[Winter Is Coming]]". Wilding played the role in the original version of the pilot shot by [[Thomas McCarthy]] and was replaced by [[Ian Whyte]] in the role when the pilot was re-shot by [[Tim Van Patten]]. Footage of Wilding remained in the final cut of the episode so he retained his credit.

He and his fellow ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' actor [[Daniel Naprous]] share the role of {{WPS|Darth Vader}} in the ''{{WPS|Star Wars}}'' anthology film ''{{WPS|Rogue One}}''.
