'''Shrykos'''{{Con|F&B}} is a [[dragon]] bred by [[House Targaryen]].{{Ref|H&L403}}

==Behind the scenes==
Though previously depicted in ''[[Histories & Lore]]'' and ''[[The Dance of Dragons]]'', dragon egg incubators are visible beneath [[Jaehaerys Targaryen (son of Aegon II)|Jaehaerys]] and [[Jaehaera Targaryen|Jaehaera]]'s beds in "[[A Son for a Son]]," indicating that Shrykos and [[Morghul]] have not yet hatched within the timeline of ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Shrykos was a young she-dragon bound to Prince [[Jaehaerys Targaryen (son of Aegon II)|Jaehaerys Targaryen]], son of King [[Aegon II Targaryen]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].
