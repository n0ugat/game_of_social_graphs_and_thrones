'''Flavia''' was an individual who lived during the [[Age of Heroes]].

==Behind the scenes==
On July 21, 2020, ''Redanian Intelligence'' revealed that [[Georgina Beedle]] had portrayed a character named Flavia in ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', the rejected ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' [[World of Westeros|prequel]] [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]].{{Webref|name=Redanian |author=Gravemaster |date=July 21, 2020 |url=https://redanianintelligence.com/2020/07/21/new-tidbits-about-the-cancelled-game-of-thrones-prequel-leaf/ |title=The cancelled Game of Thrones prequel featured a character from the original show and other new tidbits |website=Redanian Intelligence |access-date=March 30, 2023 }}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and its supplementary material, there is no mention of a character named Flavia. The character was invented by [[Jane Goldman]] for ''Bloodmoon''.
