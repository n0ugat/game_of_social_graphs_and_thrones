'''Leigh Maddern''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[The Red Woman]]", "[[Oathbreaker]]", "[[The Door]]", "[[Blood of My Blood]]", "[[The Broken Man]]" and "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[Dragonstone (episode)|Dragonstone]]", "[[Stormborn]]", "[[The Queen's Justice]]", "[[The Spoils of War]]", "[[Eastwatch]]", "[[Beyond the Wall (episode)|Beyond the Wall]]" and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]", "[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms (Game of Thrones)|A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]", "[[The Long Night]]", "[[The Last of the Starks]]" and "[[The Bells]]" and a utility stunts performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episode "[[Beyond the Wall (episode)|Beyond the Wall]]".

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[The Heirs of the Dragon]]", a core stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episodes "[[The Rogue Prince]]", "[[Second of His Name]]", "[[King of the Narrow Sea (episode)|King of the Narrow Sea]]", "[[We Light the Way]]", "[[The Princess and the Queen]]", "[[Driftmark (episode)|Driftmark]]" and "[[The Lord of the Tides]]" and an assistant stunt coordinator in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episodes "[[The Green Council]]" and "[[The Black Queen]]" and the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[A Son for a Son]]", "[[Rhaenyra the Cruel]]", "[[The Burning Mill]]", "[[The Red Dragon and the Gold]]", "[[The Red Sowing]]" and "[[The Queen Who Ever Was]]".
