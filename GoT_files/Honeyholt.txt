'''Honeyholt'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in the [[Reach]], the region controlled by [[House Tyrell]] of [[Highgarden]], and the seat of [[House Beesbury]].{{Guideref|Lyman Beesbury}} The castle is located between [[Oldtown]] and [[Brightwater Keep]], on the banks of the [[Honeywine]] river.{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Honeyholt is the seat of House Beesbury, a vassal of the Tyrells.
