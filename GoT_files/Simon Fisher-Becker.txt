'''Simon Fisher-Becker''' (born November 1961 in Ruislip, UK) is a British actor, best known for playing the recurring role of Dorium Maldovar on ''[[wikipedia:Doctor Who|Doctor Who]]'', as well as playing the Fat Friar in the film ''[[wikipedia:Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Fisher-Becker was supposed to play the [[High Septon (Joffrey)|High Septon]] of the [[Faith of the Seven]]. He confirmed in an interview he was playing this role on 22 September 2011. However, he was replaced by [[David Verrey]].<ref>[http://www.cultbox.co.uk/interviews/exclusives/1955-simon-fisher-becker-doctor-who-the-wedding-of-river-song-interview Cultbox]</ref>
