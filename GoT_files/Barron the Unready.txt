'''Barron the Unready''' was a [[Tournament|tourney]] fighter in [[Westeros]].

==Biography==
Barron was defeated by Ser [[Barristan Selmy]] at the great tourney of [[Hellholt]].{{Webref|name=MGOT|author=Making Game of Thrones Blog |date=May 28, 2019 |url=https://www.makinggameofthrones.com/production-diary/season-8-episode-6-series-finale-props-song-of-ice-and-fire |title=The GoT Series Finale Was One for the Books |website=Making Game of Thrones |access-date=January 8, 2024 }}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the only character named Barron is Storm King Barron Durrandon.
