'''House Egen'''{{Ref|GOT105}}{{Note|Vardis's surname is Egen.}} is a noble house from the [[Vale of Arryn]], sworn to [[House Arryn]], the [[Defender of the Vale|Defenders of the Vale]].

==Known members==
*Ser {[[Vardis Egen]]}, [[captain of the guard]] at the [[Eyrie]]. Slain in trial by combat by the sellsword [[Bronn]] while championing for Lord [[Robin Arryn]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Ser Vardis Egen is stated as having one young son. Their sigil is a yellow sun, white crescent moon, and silver star on blue chief above white. Their words are "By Day or Night".
