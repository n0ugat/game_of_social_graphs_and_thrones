'''Jan Hadyn Rowles''' is a Dialogue Coach in [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] of ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.  

Among her jobs is coaching the cast in proper pronunciation of the constructed [[Languages]] developed for the TV series.  She also coaches actors whose characters speak in regional or class accents.
