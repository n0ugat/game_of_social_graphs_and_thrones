'''House Charlton'''<ref name="Book">[[:File:5x2 Night's Watch Book.jpg]]</ref>{{Note|Morros's surname is Charlton.}} is a noble house of the [[Riverlands]]. They are sworn to [[House Frey]] of the [[Twins]].

==Historical members==
*{[[Morros Charlton]]}, a [[Steward (Night's Watch)|steward]] of the [[Night's Watch]].<ref name="Book" />

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the coat of arms of House Charlton is three sprigs of mistletoe, green and red, on a gold field within a green border.

House Charlton fights for [[Robb Stark]] during the [[War of the Five Kings]]. After the [[Red Wedding]], they follow the Freys in supporting the [[Iron Throne]].
