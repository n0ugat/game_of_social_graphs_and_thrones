Lord '''Symon Gaunt''' is the head of [[House Gaunt]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He is a member of House Gaunt. He is the father of at least three sons, the youngest of whom was Ser [[Gwayne Gaunt]] of the [[Kingsguard]].{{Ref|GOT806}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
He is mentioned in Ser [[Arthur Dayne]]'s{{Note|One paragraph, apparently dedicated to Ser Gwayne Gaunt, is erroneously placed in Arthur Dayne's entry.}} entry in the ''[[Book of Brothers]]''.{{Ref|GOT806}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, no such character is mentioned.
