The '''liberation of Stone's Hedge''' is a military engagement in the [[War of the Five Kings]]. It is part of the Northern campaign to liberate the [[Riverlands]] from the [[House Lannister|Lannister]] occupation.{{Guideref|Retaking the Riverlands}}

==History==
Following the liberation of [[Riverrun]] at the [[Battle of the Camps]], [[Robb Stark|Robb]] tasks [[Greatjon Umber]] with the expulsion of Lannister forces from the castles of the Riverlords, while he invades the [[Westerlands]]. Umber first [[Liberation of Raventree Hall|liberates Raventree Hall]], the seat of [[House Blackwood]], and then [[Stone Hedge]], the castle of the [[House Bracken]], thus forcing the Lannister host to fall back to [[Harrenhal]] and cutting [[Tywin Lannister|Tywin]] from [[Casterly Rock]].{{Guideref|Retaking the Riverlands}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, after the liberation of Riverrun and Robb's proclamation as [[King in the North]], he gives leave to the Riverlords to reclaim their seats,{{Bookref|ACOK|7}} among them Lord [[Jonos Bracken]], whose castle has been sacked and burned by the [[Gregor Clegane|Mountain]].{{Bookref|AGOT|56}}

Lord Bracken reclaims his castle in a fierce battle in which he is injured, while his nephew and heir, Hendry Bracken, is killed.{{Bookref|ACOK|7}}
