'''Christopher Reilly''', also called '''Chris Reilly,''' is an actor based in London, born in Glasgow.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he has played two roles. The first was [[Tom]], a [[House Stark|Stark]] soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]]. He later played [[Morgan (Two Swords)|Morgan]], a [[House Lannister|Lannister]] soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]].
