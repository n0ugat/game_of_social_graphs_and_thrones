{{Cast|Type = Dragon|role = [[Howland Reed]]|Appearances = "[[Oathbreaker]]"|imdb = http://www.imdb.com/name/nm4547262|Image = Leo Woodruff.png|status = |Title = Leo Woodruff}}'''Leo Woodruff''' is a British stunt performer and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed [[Howland Reed]] in [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Oathbreaker]]".

He was also a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[Oathbreaker]]", "[[Book of the Stranger]]", "[[The Broken Man]]" and "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seveth season]] episodes "[[Dragonstone (episode)|Dragonstone]]", "[[Stormborn]]", "[[The Queen's Justice]]", "[[The Spoils of War]]", "[[Eastwatch]]", "[[Beyond the Wall (episode)|Beyond the Wall]]" and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]", and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms (Game of Thrones)|A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]",Â "[[The Long Night]]", "[[The Last of the Starks]]" and "[[The Bells]]".

He was also a stunt double for [[Nikolaj Coster-Waldau]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[A Man Without Honor]]".

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episodes "[[Second of His Name]]", "[[Driftmark (episode)|Driftmark]]" and "[[The Lord of the Tides]]" and the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[A Son for a Son]]", "[[The Red Dragon and the Gold]]" and "[[The Red Sowing]]".
