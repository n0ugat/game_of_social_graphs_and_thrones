'''House Towers'''{{Ref|H&L609}} is an extinct house from the [[North]].

==History==
House Towers fought against [[House Stark]] in the [[Thousand Years War]] and subsequently lost. This led to their extinction.{{Ref|H&L609}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Towers' story is the same.
