A '''Triarchy lieutenant''' fought in the [[War for the Stepstones]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He is an officer in the [[Triarchy]]'s military.{{Ref|HOTD103}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
The lieutenant commands a group of archers during the assault in the [[siege of Bloodstone]]. He fails numerous attempts to kill [[Daemon Targaryen]], who survives three arrows. [[Laenor Velaryon]] later arrives on his [[dragon]] [[Seasmoke]], who burns the lieutenant and his men alive.{{Ref|HOTD103}}
