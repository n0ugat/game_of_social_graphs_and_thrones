}}'''Hannah Dominique E. John-Kamen''' is an English actress.

She was born in Anlaby, East Yorkshire, and is the daughter of a Nigerian forensic psychologist and a Norwegian fashion model.

John-Kamen plays Dutch in the sci-fi space series ''Killjoys'', and appeared as a First Order officer in ''Star Wars: Episode VII - The Force Awakens''.

In the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] of ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she portrays a [[Lhazareen]] member of the [[Dothraki]] ''[[Dosh Khaleen]]'' named [[Ornela]].
