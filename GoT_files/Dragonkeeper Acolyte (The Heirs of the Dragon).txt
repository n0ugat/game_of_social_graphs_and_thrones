A '''Dragonkeeper Acolyte''' serves [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]] in the [[Dragonpit]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He is a young member of the [[Dragonkeepers]], a monastic order guarding and raising House Targaryen's [[dragon]]s. He is apprenticed to a [[Dragonkeeper Elder (The Heirs of the Dragon)|Dragonkeeper Elder]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
The Acolyte and three other Dragonkeepers bring [[Syrax]] into the Dragonpit after [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] lands her at the entrance. He is timid when approaching Syrax, but the Elder takes over and shows him the proper way of handling a dragon.{{Ref|HOTD101}}

The Elder brings the [[Small Council]] news of [[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]] attacking him and his Acolyte, then stealing one of [[Dreamfyre]]'s eggs.{{Ref|HOTD102}}
