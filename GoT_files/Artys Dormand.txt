Ser '''Artys Dormand''' was a landed [[Knighthood|knight]] and the founder of [[House Dormand]].

==Biography==
Artys was a [[squire]] to Lord [[Torrhen Stark]]. In [[7 AC]],{{Dateref|Artys Dormand}} he saved Torrhen's life from [[Free Folk|wildlings]] during a hunt in the [[Wolfswood]], fighting and killing them with only a crude steel dagger. For this action he was knighted on the spot and given a small tract of land south of [[Winterfell]], where he raised [[Dormand Hall]]. He had at least one son, named [[Torrhen Dormand|Torrhen]] after his liege lord.<ref>https://entertainment.ha.com/itm/movie-tv-memorabilia/props/tywin-lannister-charles-dance-leather-bound-histories-of-the-greater-and-lesser-houses-book-from-game-of-th/a/7366-89163.s</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Artys Dormand.
