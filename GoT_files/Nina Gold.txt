'''Nina Gold''' is the Emmy-winning London [[Wikipedia:Casting (performing arts)|casting director]] for ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.
==Career==
Apart from casting the various actors for the show, she has worked on numerous other shows and movies, including ''Hot Fuzz'', ''The King's Speech'', ''Prometheus'', ''Les Misérables'', and ''Star Wars: The Force Awakens''. She was also the casting director for the previous [[HBO]] show, ''Rome''.

She lives in northwest London, England.

==''Game of Thrones''==
Nina Gold won the Outstanding Casting for a Drama Series 2015 Emmy Award for [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] of ''Game of Thrones.<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/game-of-thrones-breaks-the-record-for-most-emmy-wins-in-a-year/ ]</ref>''

A phrase often circulated around fansites is "In Nina Gold we trust" (or "In Nina we trust"), a joking reference that while viewers may be uncertain about casting speculation for upcoming major roles in the TV series, in past seasons Gold has consistently picked actors (some of whom were relatively unknown at the time) who turned out to be excellent.<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/lexicon/ ]</ref>
