'''''House of the Dragon: Season 1 (Soundtrack from the HBO Series)''''' was composed by [[Ramin Djawadi]]. A single, "The Prince That Was Promised", was released on August 18, 2022. Another single, "Protector of the Realm", was released on October 9, 2022. Two final singles, "Lament" and "Fate of the Kingdoms", were released on October 16, 2022. The soundtrack was released in full on October 23, 2022.
