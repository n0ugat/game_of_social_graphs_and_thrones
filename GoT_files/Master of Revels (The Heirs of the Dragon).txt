A '''Master of Revels''' served [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]] at [[King's Landing]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===Background===
As [[Master of Revels]], he is tasked with overseeing royal festivities.{{Ref|HOTD101}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
The Master of Revels is the herald at the [[Heir's Tournament]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', this character has no equivalent and the title of "Master of Revels" is not mentioned.
