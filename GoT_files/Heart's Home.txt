'''Heart's Home'''{{Ref|C&R07}} is a castle located in the [[Vale of Arryn]].

==In the books==
In the books Heart's Home is the seat of [[House Corbray]]. It is located on the northern shore where a glacial river flows into a narrow bay of the [[Narrow Sea]]. Heart's Home is within the southernmost river valley of the Snakewood.
