'''Master of Driftmark''',{{Ref|HOTD101}} or '''Lord of Driftmark''',{{Ref|HOTD107}} is a title held by the ruler of the island [[Driftmark]] in [[Blackwater Bay]]. The title is traditionally held by the head of [[House Velaryon]], along with their primary title of [[Lord of the Tides]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the title is the same.
