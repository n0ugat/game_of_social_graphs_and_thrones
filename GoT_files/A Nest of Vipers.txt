"'''A Nest of Vipers'''"{{Ref|ATGS05}} is the fifth episode of ''[[Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series]]''. It premiered on July 21, 2015. It was written by Meghan Thornton and Brad Kane, and directed by Jason Latino.
