'''House Piper'''{{Ref|HOTD205}}{{Note|Lord Piper's surname is Piper.}} of [[Pinkmaiden]] is a [[Noble house|vassal house]] of the [[Riverlands]].

==Known members==
*Lord [[Petyr Piper]], the head of the house and the Lord of Pinkmaiden.
**His sons.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Piper is the same.

Its seat at Pinkmaiden Castle is located on the Red Fork close to the border with the [[Westerlands]]. Their blazon is a pink dancing maiden in a swirl of white silk, over blue. Their motto is not mentioned in the books, but in semi-canon sources it is listed as "Brave and Beautiful".

===Known members===
*Lord Clement Piper, Lord of Pinkmaiden.
**Ser Marq Piper, his eldest son. Heir to Pinkmaiden Castle. Taken captive at the [[Red Wedding]].
**Lewys Piper, his younger son. A squire to [[Jaime Lannister]].
** Five Piper knights who are Clement's cousins and nephews.
