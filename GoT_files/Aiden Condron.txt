'''Aiden Condron''' is an actor, director, and teacher based in Northern Ireland. He had minor parts in the TV show ''[[Wikipedia:The Tudors|The Tudors]]'' and the movie ''[[Wikipedia:Saving Private Ryan|Saving Private Ryan]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he is playing the role of a Lannister captain. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
