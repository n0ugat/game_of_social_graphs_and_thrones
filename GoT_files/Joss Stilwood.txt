'''Joss Stilwood'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} is the [[squire]] of [[Knighthood|Ser]] [[Gregor Clegane]], the Mountain.

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Joss Stilwood squires for the Mountain in the [[Tournament#Tourney of the Hand|Tourney of the Hand]]. After Gregor is defeated in a joust by [[Loras Tyrell]], he orders Joss to bring him his sword, then beheads his horse in anger.{{Ref|GOT105}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
Joss squires for Gregor when he faces [[Oberyn Martell]] in the [[second trial by combat of Tyrion Lannister]].{{Ref|GOT408}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Joss Stilwood is the squire of Ser [[Gregor Clegane]] and belongs to his group of men-at-arms from the Westerlands, known as the ''Mountain's men''. He takes part in the gang rape of Layna, an innkeeper's daughter.
