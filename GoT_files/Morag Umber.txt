Lady '''Morag Umber''' was a member of [[House Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Morag married into House Umber of the [[Last Hearth]], and bore her husband [[Daragh Umber|Daragh]] a son, [[Hothor Umber]], who continued the family line with seven children of his own with [[Amarylis Umber|Amarylis Manderly]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
