'''''Game of Thrones: Season 2 (Music from the HBO Series)''''' was composed by [[Ramin Djawadi]] and was released on June 19, 2012. It was preceded by ''[[Game of Thrones (Music from the HBO Series)]]'', released in 2011, and followed by ''[[Game of Thrones: Season 3 (Music from the HBO Series)]]'', released in 2013.
