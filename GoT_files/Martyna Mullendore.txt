'''Martyna Mullendore''' was a member of [[House Mullendore]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Martyna Mullendore was the wife of [[William Baratheon]] and the mother of his numerous children.{{Ref|GOT106}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She is referenced in her husband's entry of ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
