A '''Stark guard''' is a guardsman sworn to [[House Stark]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
He is guarding the recaptured [[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]] when he is dismissed by [[Catelyn Stark|Catelyn]].{{Ref|GOT207}}

He is assigned to guard Catelyn after she betrays [[Robb Stark|Robb]] by releasing Jaime.{{Ref|GOT208}}
