'''Ben Wright''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[The Spoils of War]]" and "[[Eastwatch]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[The Long Night]]" and "[[The Bells]]" and an assistant stunt coordinator in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]", "[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms (Game of Thrones)|A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]", "[[The Long Night]]", "[[The Last of the Starks]]" , "[[The Bells]]" and "[[The Iron Throne]]".

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he was a key assistant stunt coordinator in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[A Son for a Son]]", "[[Rhaenyra the Cruel]]", "[[The Burning Mill]]", "[[The Red Dragon and the Gold]]", "[[Regent (episode)|Regent]]", "[[Smallfolk (episode)|Smallfolk]]", "[[The Red Sowing]]" and "[[The Queen Who Ever Was]]".

In the [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]] of ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', he was an assistant stunt coordinator.
