'''Kate Rhodes James''' is a [[Wikipedia:Casting (performing arts)|casting director]] for ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.

==Career==
Kate Rhodes James has previously done casting work for ''The Diary of Anne Frank'' 2009 miniseries (starring [[Ellie Kendrick]]), ''The Tunnel'' (starring [[Stephen Dillane]]), ''You, Man and the Apocalypse'' (with [[Joel Fry]] and [[Diana Rigg]]), ''Jekyll and Hyde'' (2015) (with [[Donald Sumpter]]), ''Ripper Street'' (with [[Jerome Flynn]]), ''Sherlock'' (2012) (with Benedict Cumberbatch, Martin Freeman, and [[Mark Gatiss]]), ''The Terror'' (with [[Tobias Menzies]] and [[Ciar√°n Hinds]]), ''Catherine the Great'' 2019 (with Helen Mirren), ''Cursed'', ''Raised by Wolves'', and ''House of Gucci''.
