'''Stephen Warbeck''' (born 1953) is an English [[wikipedia:Oscar Awards|Oscar Award]]-winning composer, best known for his work on television and film. He has composed the music for ''[[wikipedia:Prime Suspect|Prime Suspect]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Mrs. Brown|Mrs. Brown]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Mystery Men|Mystery Men]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Billy Elliot|Billy Elliot]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Quills|Quills]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Captain Corelli's Mandolin (film)|Captain Corelli's Mandolin]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Charlotte Gray (film)|Charlotte Gray]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Proof (2005 film)|Proof]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Princess Kaiulani (film)|Princess Kaiulani]]'', amongst others. He won the Oscar for Best Soundtrack for his work on ''[[wikipedia:Shakespeare in Love|Shakespeare in Love]]'' in 1999.

On ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Warbeck was originally announced as composing the music for the series on 23 October 2010. However, on 3 February 2011 it was announced he had departed the project and been replaced by [[Ramin Djawadi]].
