'''Oona Castilla Chaplin''' (born 4 June 1986) is a Spanish-Swiss actress and dancer.

==Biography==
Born in Madrid, Spain, Chaplin also grew up in Switzerland. From the age of 15 she attended {{WPS|Gordonstoun|Gordonstoun School}} in Scotland and later attended {{WPS|RADA}} in London. Chaplin's parents are American actress {{WPS|Geraldine Chaplin}} and Chilean cinematographer Patricio Castilla. Her namesake is her maternal grandmother {{WPS|Oona O'Neill}}, American playwright {{WPS|Eugene O'Neill}}'s only daughter and British comedian and filmmaker {{WPS|Charlie Chaplin}}'s fourth wife.<ref>Holly Williams, Holly (July 16, 2011). [http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/profiles/funny-girl-the-notso-silent-star-oona-chaplin-2312991.html Funny girl: The not-so silent star Oona Chaplin]. The Independent.</ref>

Her first screen appearances was on the British drama series {{WPS|Spooks (TV series)|''Spooks''}} in 2007. She had a minor part in the ''{{WPS|James Bond}}'' movie ''{{WPS|Quantum of Solace}}''. She had a recurring role as Marnie Madden on the BBC drama series {{WPS|The Hour (2011 TV series)|''The Hour''}}, and she appeared in the BBC series {{WPS|Sherlock (TV series)|''Sherlock''}} as John Watson's girlfriend Jeanette.

She starred in the BBC/FX period drama series {{WPS|Taboo (2017 TV series)|''Taboo''}}, along with her ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' co-stars [[Jonathan Pryce]], [[Nicholas Blane]], [[Jefferson Hall]], [[Roger Ashton-Griffiths]], and [[Mark Gatiss]].

In an interview with ''[[wikipedia:The Daily Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]]'' that was published on August 11, 2011, Chaplin confirmed she had secured a role in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] of ''Game of Thrones''.<ref>Tyzack, Anna (August 11, 2011). [http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/celebritynews/my-perfect-weekend/8695705/My-perfect-weekend-Oona-Chaplin.html My perfect weekend: Oona Chaplin]. The Daily Telegraph.</ref> This was later suggested to be Jeyne Westerling.<ref>[http://www.comicbookmovie.com/fansites/rorschachsrants/news/?a=44507 ComicBookMovie.com]</ref> However, her character introduces herself as [[Talisa Stark|Talisa Maegyr]] rather than Jeyne when she first appears in "[[Garden of Bones (episode)|Garden of Bones]]."<ref>"[[Garden of Bones (episode)|Garden of Bones]]"</ref> It was later revealed that the original plan had been for Chaplin to play Jeyne, but the character was dramatically changed in rewrites. Eventually [[George R.R. Martin]] himself suggested renaming her and came up with her character's new name.<ref>Ryan, Maureen (September 4, 2012). [http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/09/04/game-of-thrones-season-3-characters_n_1854918.html 'Game of Thrones' Season 3 Characters And Scoop From Creator George R.R. Martin]. Huffington Post.</ref>

Chaplin has described herself as a "massive fan" of both ''Game of Thrones'' as well as the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novel series prior to her own casting.<ref>(April 19, 2012). [http://winteriscoming.net/2012/04/interview-with-oona-chaplin Interview with Oona Chaplin]. WinterIsComing.Net.</ref>
