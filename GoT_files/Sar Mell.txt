'''Sar Mell'''{{Mapref|Viewer's Guide}} is a ruined city in western [[Essos]], located on the eastern bank of the [[Rhoyne]] just north of its delta. The ruin is located north-west of [[Volantis]] and directly opposite the town of [[Volon Therys]]. The eastern banks of the Rhoyne lie at the far south-west of [[Dothraki]] influence.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Sar Mell is a ruined Rhoynar city.
