'''House Strickland'''{{Ref|GOT801}}{{Note|Harry's surname is Strickland.}} is an [[exile]]d [[noble house]] from [[Westeros]]. Their last known [[Lordship|lord]] was [[Harry Strickland]], captain of the [[Golden Company]].

==History==
===Background===
House Strickland supported [[House Blackfyre]] in one of the [[Blackfyre Rebellion]]s. As a result, they were exiled to [[Essos]].

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
Captain [[Harry Strickland]] is hired by [[Cersei Lannister]] to fight against [[Daenerys Targaryen]] in the [[Last War]].<ref>"[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]"</ref>

Harry Strickland is killed by [[Grey Worm]] during the [[Battle of King's Landing]]. The fate of his House is unknown.<ref>"[[The Bells]]"</ref>

==Members==
*{[[Harry Strickland]]}, captain of the [[Golden Company]]. Killed by [[Grey Worm]] during the [[Battle of King's Landing]].

==Military==
When Harry Strickland was the head of both the Golden Company and House Strickland, the 20,000 men of the company served as the ''de facto'' military of the House.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lord Strickland fought for [[Daemon Blackfyre]] in the Blackfyre Rebellion. After the [[Battle of Redgrass Field|Battle of the Redgrass Field]], he fled with [[Knighthood|Ser]] [[Aegor Rivers]] across the [[Narrow Sea|narrow sea]]. Since then, four generations of Stricklands have served in the Golden Company.
