==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
The Westerosi trader is speaking with his companion concerning the [[Ironborn]] fleet's recent arrival at [[Slaver's Bay]], when he is approached by [[Arya Stark|Arya]], who recognizes him as a Westerosi due to his usage of the [[Common Tongue]]. She then pays him with two bags of coins in order to secure passage for her journey back to Westeros.{{Ref|GOT607}}
