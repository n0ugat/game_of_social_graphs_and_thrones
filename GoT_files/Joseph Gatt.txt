'''Joseph Gatt''' is an English actor and model. In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|Season 4]], he plays a [[Thenn warg]].

Gatt is perhaps best known as the motion capture performer and likeness for Kratos from the acclaimed ''[[wikipedia:God of War (series)|God of War]]'' video game franchise. He appeared as the Frost Giant, Grundroth in ''[[wikipedia:Thor (film)|Thor]]'', and Starfleet Science Officer 0718 in ''[[wikipedia:Star Trek Into Darkness|Star Trek Into Darkness]]''.

Gatt has a condition called alopecia universalis which made all of his hair fall out at the age of 14.

Gatt was arrested by the LAPD on April 6, 2022 for allegedly engaging in "online sexually explicit communication" with a minor. He was released the same day on $5,000 bail.<ref>https://www.tmz.com/2022/04/13/game-of-thrones-joseph-gatt-arrested-contact-minor-sexual-offense/</ref>
