}}'''Mark Quigley''' is a Northern Irish actor. He has appeared in the television series ''{{WPS|Halo: Nightfall}}'', ''{{WPS|Vikings (TV series)|Vikings}}'' and ''{{WPS|Penny Dreadful (TV series)|Penny Dreadful}}'' and the film ''{{WPS|Suicide Squad (film)|Suicide Squad}}''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a Bolton Guard in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[The Mountain and the Viper]]", a Baratheon Soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[The Children]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[The Wars To Come]]", "[[The Gift]]" and "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]", a Mormont soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]", a Bannerman in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]", "[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms (Game of Thrones)|A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]", "[[The Long Night]]", "[[The Last of the Starks]]" and "[[The Bells]]" and a member of the Golden Company in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]".
