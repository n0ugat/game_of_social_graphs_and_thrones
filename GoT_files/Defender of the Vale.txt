'''Defender of the Vale''',{{Ref|GOT604}} informally '''Lord of all the Vale'''{{Ref|GOT105}} and '''Lord of the Vale''',{{Ref|GOT303}} and also referred to as '''Lord Paramount of the Vale''',{{Ref|H&L122}} is the title held by the [[Great House]] of the [[Vale of Arryn]] under the authority of the [[King of the Andals and the Rhoynar and the First Men|King of the Andals and the First Men]]. Traditionally, this is the patriarch of [[House Arryn]].
