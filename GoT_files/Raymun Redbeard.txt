'''Raymun Redbeard'''{{Ref|H&L409}} is a wildling of the [[Free Folk]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Raymun Redbeard had a younger brother named the Red Raven, who is an ancestor of Gerrick Kingsblood, though Gerrick prefers to boast that he is a descendant of Raymun. When he is made a lord by [[Stannis Baratheon]], he takes the name "Redbeard" for his new house.
