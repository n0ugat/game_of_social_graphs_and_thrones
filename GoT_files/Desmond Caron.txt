Ser '''Desmond Caron''' is a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Caron]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Desmond is the son of Ser [[Royce Caron]] of [[Nightsong]].{{Ref|HOTD102}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Desmond is brought before [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] in [[Maegor's Holdfast]] as a candidate to replace Ser [[Ryam Redwyne]] on the [[Kingsguard/House of the Dragon|Kingsguard]]. Despite Ser [[Harrold Westerling]] praising him, Rhaenyra dislikes him, and Ser [[Criston Cole]] is chosen instead.{{Ref|HOTD102}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Desmond Caron.
