A servant girl in [[Winterfell]] works as a '''spy''' for [[Petyr Baelish|Littlefinger]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
While spying on Littlefinger herself, [[Arya Stark|Arya]] overlooks a conversation between the girl and Littlefinger from a distance; she is too far to hear what they say. Littlefinger gives the girl a coin, and she leaves.{{Ref|GOT705}}
