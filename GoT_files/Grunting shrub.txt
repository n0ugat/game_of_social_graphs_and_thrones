{{Quote|I must surely start taking note of where his grunts start and stop, for twice now I've arisen to find this plant growing from the ceiling and then the floor.|Jaehaelor Mataeryon|Shrubbery, Being a History on the Flora of the Valyrian Freehold}}
A '''grunting shrub'''<ref>[https://dedalvs.com/work/house-of-the-dragon/misc/shrubbery_v4.pdf ''Shrubbery'' version 4 | dedalvs.com]</ref> allegedly capable of uprooting itself and walking at nightfall was grown by [[Jaehaelor Mataeryon]] in his gardens. Jaehaelor thought himself mad for almost a year because of this plant, but he later attributed its abilities to [[magic]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and their supplementary material, there is no mention of this plant.
