'''David Michael Scott''' is an Australian actor, but has lived in the Republic of Ireland since 2004. He has extensive theater experience and has appeared in various shows, including ''Vikings ''and [[wikipedia:The Tudors|''The Tudors'']].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he portrayed Lord [[Beric Dondarrion]] in the episode "[[A Golden Crown]]", though his status as a "featured extra" indicated the role was subject to a possible recast in [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|Season 3]] - which indeed happened as he was replaced by [[Richard Dormer]]. He confirmed he was playing the role via Twitter on November 15, 2010.
