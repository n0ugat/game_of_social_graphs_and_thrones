The '''Isle of Elephants'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is an island in the [[Jade Sea]], located south of [[Great Moraq]]. The port city of [[Zabhad]] lies on its northern shore.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'', the only settlement on the Isle of Elephants is the city of Zabhad, which is ruled by a ''shan'' from a palace of ivory.
