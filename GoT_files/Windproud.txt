'''''Windproud'''''{{Ref|GOT106}} was a [[Ships|ship]] belonging to [[Steffon Baratheon]], [[Lord of Storm's End]]. It sank during a raging storm on [[Shipbreaker Bay]] in [[278 AC]],{{Dateref|Windproud}} killing Lord Steffon and his wife, [[Cassana Baratheon|Cassana]].{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the ''Windproud'' was a two-masted galley with a hundred oarsmen and sailors. Lord Steffon and Lady Cassana traveled to the [[Free Cities]] on the galley to find a potential wife for [[Rhaegar Targaryen]]. On their return, the ''Windproud'' broke up during a storm on Shipbreaker Bay as [[Robert Baratheon|Robert]] and [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]] looked on. There were no survivors, save the [[fool]] Patchface, who washed up on shore half-mad.
