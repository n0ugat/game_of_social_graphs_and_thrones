'''Catherine Goldschmidt''' (born 21 October 1983) is an American-British cinematographer working on ''[[House of the Dragon]]'' with director [[Geeta Vasant Patel]].

Goldschmidt was nominated for the 2023 {{WPS|American Society of Cinematographers|ASC Awards}} in the "Episode of a One-Hour Non-Commercial Television Series" category for her work on the episode "[[The Lord of the Tides]]."<ref>https://deadline.com/2023/01/asc-awards-nominations-2023-cinematograhpers-guild-1235214726/</ref>
