'''House Arryn'''{{Ref|GOT604}} of the [[Eyrie]] is one of the [[Great House]]s of [[Westeros]], and is one of the oldest dynasties of the [[Seven Kingdoms]]. Their seat is the Eyrie, a castle on a shoulder of the [[Giant's Lance]], from where they rule over their domain, the [[Vale of Arryn]].

==Members==
*Lady [[Jeyne Arryn]], [[Defender of the Vale]], [[Warden of the East|Wardeness of the East]].
*Princess {[[Daella Targaryen]]}, the wife of the Defender of the Vale, Jeyne's cousin or uncle.
**Queen {[[Aemma Arryn]]}, her daughter. First wife of King [[Viserys I Targaryen]]. Died of blood loss of a risky medical procedure during the birth of her son, [[Baelon Targaryen|Baelon]], at the end of her seventh pregnancy.
***(See [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]])
