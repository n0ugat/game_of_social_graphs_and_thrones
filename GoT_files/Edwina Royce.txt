'''Edwina Royce''' is a member of [[House Royce]], the daughter of [[Paxter Royce]]. Her current status is uncertain.

==Biography==
Edwina Royce is the daughter, and only child, of [[Paxter Royce]], an uncle of [[Yohn Royce]], and [[Wylla Waynwood]].<ref name = "s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Edwina Royce is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Edwina Royce is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
