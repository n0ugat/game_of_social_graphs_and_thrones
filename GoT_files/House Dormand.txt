'''House Dormand'''{{Ref|GOT206}} of [[Dormand Hall]]<ref name="Auction" /> is a [[Noble house|vassal house]] that holds fealty to [[House Stark]] of [[Winterfell]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
During the [[War of the Five Kings]], a letter detailing the infantry movements of [[House Lannister]]'s forces was accidentally sent to [[Lordship|Lord]] [[Marlyn Dormand|Marlyn]] of House Dormand, instead of Lord [[Damon Marbrand]], due to the illiteracy of [[Amory Lorch]].{{Ref|GOT206}}

==Members==
*Lord [[Marlyn Dormand]], the current head of the family.

===Historical members===
*Ser {[[Artys Dormand]]}, a landed knight and founder of the house.<ref name="Auction" />
**Lord {[[Torrhen Dormand]]}, his son and successor. Raised to lordship some years after inheriting Dormand Hall.<ref name="Auction" />

==In the books==
No House Dormand or Lord Marlyn are mentioned in the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels.
