'''Freddie Stroma''' is an English actor and model from London, United Kingdom. He is best known for his role as Cormac McLaggen in the ''[[wikipedia:Harry Potter|Harry Potter]]'' film series, and his role in the TV series ''UnREAL''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed [[Dickon Tarly]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].<ref>[http://www.ew.com/article/2015/09/04/game-thrones-freddie-stroma-samwell-brother?hootPostID=68a8cf9cc424cf0041450a32b1efd505/ Entertainment Weekly — Game of Thrones casts Samwell Tarly's brother]</ref>

Due to being cast in the ABC TV series ''Time After Time'', Stroma was unable to return for [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|Season 7]], so the role of Dickon Tarly was recast with actor [[Tom Hopper]].<ref>[ http://watchersonthewall.com/freddie-stroma-confirmed-not-return/ ]</ref>
