'''Eileen Shim''' is a Korean-American screenwriter. She is a member of the writing staff on ''[[House of the Dragon]]'' prequel TV series, credited with writing episode 8 of [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|Season 1]].

==Biography==
Eileen Shim was born in South Korea: when she was nine years old she moved with her family to New York City, USA. A Yale graduate, she studied comparative literature focusing on Gothic horror. She worked as a journalist for several years at NBC before getting into screenwriting.

She developed the horror series ''Light as a Feather'' which ran for one season on Hulu in 2019.
