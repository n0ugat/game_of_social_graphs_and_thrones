A '''High Septon''' served as head of the [[Faith of the Seven]] during the reigns of [[Jaehaerys I Targaryen|Jaehaerys I]] and [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
The [[High Septon]] is present at the [[Great Council at Harrenhal]]. Eleven years later, he also attends the ceremony in which [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] is named [[Prince of Dragonstone|Princess of Dragonstone]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}
[[File:WLTW Rhaenyra and Laenor Still 7.jpg|thumb|Royal wedding.]]
The High Septon weds Rhaenyra and [[Laenor Velaryon]] in a small and grim ceremony.{{Ref|HOTD105}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', this High Septon is not mentioned as being present when Rhaenyra was named heir.
