'''Jamie Michie''' is a Scottish actor. In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', Michie plays [[Steelshanks]]. He was announced in the role October 31, 2012.<ref>[http://wicnet.tumblr.com/post/34714077285/jamie-michie-will-be-appearing-in-season-three-as. Winter is Coming.net]</ref>

He has starred in the movie ''Route Irish'' (2010), and in the series ''Midsomer Murders'' and ''The Bill''. His uncle is John Michie star of the long-running Scottish detective series ''Taggart''. Before his uncle achieved success, he advised his nephew not to make acting his profession.<ref>[http://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/entertainment/celebrity/my-uncle---john-michie---1060314 Daily Record interview here]</ref>
