'''House Penrose'''{{Ref|GOT106}} is a [[noble house]] from the [[Stormlands]].

==Historical members==
*{[[Alanna Penrose]]}, wed to [[Axel Baratheon]].

==In the books==
House Penrose is a noble house. Their seat is Parchments, a castle in the Stormlands. Their blazon is tenné, two quills crossed argent. According to semi-canon sources, their words are "Set Down Our Deeds".

===Known members===
*Lord Penrose, Lord of Parchments. An old man, sickly and failing.
**Ser {Cortnay Penrose}, his son. The former castellan of [[Storm's End]]. Killed by [[Melisandre]]'s second [[shadow]].

===Historical members===
*{Lucinda Penrose}, a lady companion to Queen Daenaera Velaryon. 
*{Unnamed Lady Penrose}, whose sons fought in the [[Blackfyre Rebellion]].
**At least three sons. All slain by Ser Quentyn Ball, during the Blackfyre Rebellion, except for the youngest one.
*{Ronnel Penrose}, who married Princess [[Elaena Targaryen]] and became the [[Master of Coin]] on King [[Daeron II Targaryen]]'s [[Small Council]].
**{Robin Penrose}, his son.
**{Laena Penrose}, his daughter.
**{Jocelyn Penrose}, his daughter.
**{Joy Penrose}, his daughter.
*Queen {Aelinor Penrose}, the wife of King [[Aerys Targaryen (son of Daeron II)|Aerys I Targaryen]].
*Ser {Jon Penrose}, who fought at the Ashford Tourney.
