'''Ira Parker''' is an American screenwriter and producer. He was a member of the writing staff on ''[[House of the Dragon]]'' for its [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]].

Parker is the showrunner of ''[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.<ref name="KnightandSquire">[https://georgerrmartin.com/notablog/2023/04/14/a-knight-and-a-squire/ George R.R. Martin | Not A Blog | A Knight and a Squire, April 14, 2023]</ref>

==Biography==
Ira Parker worked as a staff writer on ''Rogue'' (2015), ''Four in the Morning'' (2016), and ''The Last Ship'' (2016-2018). He then worked as a writer/co-executive producer on ''Better Things'' (2019-2020).
