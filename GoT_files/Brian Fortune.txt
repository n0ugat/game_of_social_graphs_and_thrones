In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he plays the role of [[Othell Yarwyck]], the [[Night's Watch]] [[First Builder]]. The actor had appeared in [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|Season 1]] as an unnamed 'Night's Watch Officer' at first believed to be [[Bowen Marsh]], but the character was explicitly identified as Yarwyck in Season 4 and [[Michael Condron]] was cast as Bowen Marsh for Season 5.
