Ser '''Samwell Stone'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} is a [[Knighthood|knight]] of the [[Vale of Arryn|Vale]] in service to [[House Royce]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Sam Stone is a [[bastard]] from the Vale. He serves as the [[master-at-arms]] of [[Runestone]].{{Ref|GOT501}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Sam Stone is training [[Robin Arryn]] in swordfighting while he is being [[Ward|fostered]] at Runestone by Lord [[Yohn Royce]].{{Ref|GOT501}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'', novels Ser Samwell Stone, dubbed "Strong Sam Stone", is a renowned knight and the master-at-arms of Runestone. He supports his liege lord Yohn Royce when the latter gathers a group of nobles, dubbed the "Lords Declarant", to denounce [[Petyr Baelish|Littlefinger]] and oppose his rule over the Vale.
