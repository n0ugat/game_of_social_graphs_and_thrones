'''Crakehall'''{{Ref|GOT701}} is a stronghold in the [[Westerlands]]. It is the seat of [[House Crakehall]], bannermen to [[House Lannister]] of [[Casterly Rock]]. Located on the [[Searoad]], close to the shores of the [[Sunset Sea]], south of Casterly Rock and [[Lannisport]] and close to the border of the [[Westerlands]] and the [[Reach]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Crakehall is also known as Crakehall Castle.

[[Aegon Targaryen (son of Aenys)|Aegon]] and [[Rhaena Targaryen (daughter of Aenys)|Rhaena Targaryen]] were besieged at Crakehall during the [[Faith Militant uprising]]. When King [[Aenys Targaryen|Aenys]] heard of this, he collapsed and died three days later.

[[Jaime Lannister]] was a [[squire]] at Crakehall along with Merrett [[House Frey|Frey]].
