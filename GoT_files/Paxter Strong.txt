'''Paxter Strong''' is a member of [[House Strong]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Paxter is a grandson of Ser [[Simon Strong]]{{Con|F&B}} and either the brother or cousin of [[Germund Strong]].

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Paxter Strong is present when [[Daemon Targaryen]] arrives at [[Harrenhal]] to claim the castle for [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]]. Paxter and Simon Strong serve Daemon dinner while discussing how to make [[House Tully]] declare for the [[Blacks]].{{Ref|HOTD203}}

==Family==
{{Strong tree}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Paxter Strong. The only character whose first name is Paxter is Lord [[Paxter Redwyne]]. Simon Strong's grandsons are mentioned but unnamed.
