'''Whispering Sound'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a bay on the southwestern coast of the [[Reach]]. The river [[Honeywine]] empties into it at [[Oldtown]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, [[Euron Greyjoy]]'s raiders harass ships on Whispering Sound during his invasion of the Reach.
