The '''Blue Fork'''{{Mapref|Viewer's Guide}} is one of the three main rivers of the [[Trident]]. The shortest and least noteworthy of the three branches, it rises in the hills southeast of [[Seagard]] and [[Ironman's Bay]]. It flows southeasterly into the main body of the Trident.
