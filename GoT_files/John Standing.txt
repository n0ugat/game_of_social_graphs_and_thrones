'''Sir John Ronald Leon, 4th Baronet''' (born 16 August 1934), known professionally as '''John Standing''', is a British actor. He has a very long career in television and film, as well as theatrical work. His most recent genre role was in ''[[wikipedia:V For Vendetta (film)|V For Vendetta]]''. He has a previous [[George R.R. Martin]] connection, as he appeared in the 1987 film based on Martin's novella ''[[wikipedia:Nightflyers|Nightflyers]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he plays Lord [[Jon Arryn]] (as a corpse). He was confirmed in the role on 16 July 2010.
