'''Ashford Castle'''{{Ref|H&L114}} is a castle in the [[Reach]]. It is located in the town of [[Ashford]] and ruled by [[House Ashford]].

==Behind the scenes==
The exterior of Ashford Castle is portrayed by Myra Castle in County Down, Northern Ireland. It is the same castle that portrays the exterior of the [[Dreadfort]].{{Webref|name=Redanian |author=Gravemaster |date=April 23, 2024 |url=https://redanianintelligence.com/2024/04/23/the-hedge-knight-revisit-iconic-game-of-thrones-filming-locations/ |title=The Hedge Knight To Revisit Iconic Game of Thrones Filming Locations |website=Redanian Intelligence |access-date=April 23, 2024}}

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Ashford Castle is a small, triangular castle with round thirty-foot towers connected by thick crenelated walls. Its Great Hall is decorated with tapestries of fruits and flowers.
