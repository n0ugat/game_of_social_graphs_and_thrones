King '''Jon Stark''' was a [[King in the North]] of [[House Stark]].

==Biography==
Jon drove pirates out of the [[White Knife]] and claimed the eastern coast of the [[North]] for the Starks.{{Ref|H&L304}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Jon Stark built a castle, the Wolf's Den, along the White Knife after taking it from the pirates there, and was succeeded by his son, Rickard.
