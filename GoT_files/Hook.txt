The '''Hook'''{{Ref|GOT504}} is a street in [[King's Landing]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
[[Barristan Selmy|Barristan]] tells [[Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys]] that [[Rhaegar Targaryen|Rhaegar]] enjoyed singing to the [[smallfolk]] of [[King's Landing]] on the Hook and the [[Street of Seeds]].{{Ref|GOT504}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the [[riots in King's Landing]] ensue on the Hook.{{Bookref|ACOK|41}}
