'''Dwarf elephants'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} are a small species of [[elephant]] known to inhabit [[Volantis]]. They are used to pull [[hathay]]s, carts pulled by [[Slavery|slaves]] whose faces are tattooed with wheels.{{Ref|H&L503}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, dwarf elephants are only known to be found in Volantis, where they are a common mode of transport. Their skin is white and they emit a honking noise. [[Tyrion Lannister]] mistakes one of them for [[Illyrio Mopatis]].
