'''William''' was a soldier in the who fought for [[House Lannister]]. He was killed in the [[Battle of the Goldroad]].

==Biography==
===Background===
William was known to frequent the brothels of [[King's Landing]] and was a favorite of [[Craya]]. He took part in the [[sack of Highgarden]] and later fought in the Battle of the Goldroad, where he was killed by [[Drogon]].{{Ref|GOT801}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
William was one of the soldiers mentioned by [[Marei]], Craya, and [[Dirah]] when they discuss the casualties of the Battle of the Goldroad. Craya told the others that William was burned to ashes, with his remains being able to fit in a wine glass.{{Ref|GOT801}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there are several characters whose first name is William (William Foxglove, William Mooton), but they do not serve the Lannisters.
