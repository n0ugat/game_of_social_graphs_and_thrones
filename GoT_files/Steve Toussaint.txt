'''Stephen Toussaint'''{{Note|[tus√≥nt]; to-<small>SAUNT</small>}} (born 22 March 1965) is an English actor and writer, best known for his roles in ''Prince of Persia: Sands of Time'', ''Judge Dredd'', and ''Shooting Dogs''. He portrays Lord [[Corlys Velaryon]] in ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.<ref>[https://deadline.com/2021/02/house-of-the-dragon-rhys-ifans-steve-toussaint-eve-best-sonoya-mizuno-cast-game-of-thrones-prequel-hbo-lord-corlys-velaryon-sea-snake-1234692290/ Deadline]</ref>

==Biography==
Steve Toussaint was born in the United Kingdom to Barbadian parents. He had his last name legally changed to "Toussaint" because his birth name conflicted with a pre-existing member of his actors' union. He chose the name from [[Wikipedia:Toussaint Louverture|Toussaint Louverture]], the Haitain revolutionary leader (as at the time he had been reading about him in "The Black Jacobins" by CLR James).<ref>https://twitter.com/StevieToussaint/status/1468931986853187591</ref>

He made his professional debut as Steve Dixie on ''The Memoirs of Sherlock Holmes''.

Toussaint previously auditioned for several roles in ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', including [[Areo Hotah]].<ref>https://ew.com/tv/house-of-the-dragon-steve-toussaint-auditioned-for-game-of-thrones/?utm_campaign=entertainmentweekly_entertainmentweekly&utm_content=new&utm_medium=social&utm_source=twitter.com&utm_term=62d6bb3e7b696400019a5f9c</ref>

On February 11, 2021, it was announced that Toussaint would be playing [[Corlys Velaryon]] in ''House of the Dragon''.
