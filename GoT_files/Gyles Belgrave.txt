Ser '''Gyles Belgrave'''{{Con|F&B}} is a member of [[Aegon II Targaryen]]'s [[Kingsguard/House of the Dragon|Kingsguard]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Gyles Belgrave and [[Willis Fell]] are escorting Aegon when he walks by [[Helaena Targaryen|Helaena]]. They later break their fast with Targaryen and [[House Hightower/House of the Dragon|Hightower]] [[Knighthood|knights]], and leave when [[Criston Cole|Criston]] begins to berate [[Arryk Cargyll]].{{Ref|HOTD202}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', it is unknown when Gyles joined the Kingsguard.
