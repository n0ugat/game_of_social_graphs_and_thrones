'''Anthony Flanagan''' (born '''Anthony Bonello'''; 11 April 1972) is an English actor.

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he portrays [[Steffon Darklyn]].<ref>https://www.independenttalent.com/actors/anthony-flanagan/</ref><ref>https://www.imdb.com/name/nm01340424/</ref>

==Background==
Anthony Flanagan was born in Stockport, Cheshire, on 11 April 1972. He decided to become an actor after seeing a friend of his sister perform at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London. He is married to actress Rebekah Staton.

His acting debut was as Pat Harford in Peter Whelan's play ''A Russian in the Woods''. He is best known for his portrayal of Tony in Channel 4's comedy-drama series ''Shameless''. He has also appeared in ''Doctor Who'' and ''The Crown'', in which fellow ''House of the Dragon'' cast member [[Matt Smith]] starred as the Eleventh Doctor and Prince Philip respectively.

Flanagan was spotted on location in Cornwall and Devon, where he appeared to be playing a member of the [[Kingsguard]].<ref>https://www.reddit.com/r/HouseOfTheDragon/comments/ptg16l/the_man_seen_on_the_beach_is_anthony_flanagan/</ref>
