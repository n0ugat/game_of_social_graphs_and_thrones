'''Symun Fossoway''' is a member of [[House Fossoway]]. He is betrothed to [[Talla Tarly]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
[[Talla Tarly]] eagerly greets [[Samwell Tarly|Sam]] as he arrives with [[Gilly]] and [[Sam|her baby]], complaining about her arranged marriage to Symun Fossoway. Talla appears unenthusiastic about the arrangement, presumably due to his physical appearance.{{Ref|GOT606}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
With the deaths of [[Randyll Tarly|Randyll]] and [[Dickon Tarly|Dickon]], Symun's betrothed, Talla, becomes the head of [[House Tarly]].{{Ref|GOT705}} It is unknown whether as Lady Tarly she will follow through with the match arranged by her father.
