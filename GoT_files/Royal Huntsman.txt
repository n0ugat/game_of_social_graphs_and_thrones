'''Royal Huntsman'''{{Ref|HOTD103}} is a title in the [[Seven Kingdoms]]. The holder of the office is tasked with overseeing royal hunts.

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Ser [[Howland Sharp]] is the current Royal Huntsman. He oversees the hunt on Prince [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon]]'s second [[nameday]].<ref name="Second">"[[Second of His Name]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', no such title is mentioned.
