'''Matija Petrovic''' (born 1992) is a Croatian stuntman and horse wrangler.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[The Old Gods and the New]]".
