'''Lord Serrett''' is the head of [[House Serrett]] and a bannerman of [[House Lannister]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
Lord Serrett is mentioned in a letter regarding troop movements sent to [[Tywin Lannister|Tywin]] at [[Harrenhal]].{{Ref|GOT206}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lord Serrett participates in the [[battle on the Green Fork]], riding alongside [[Kevan Lannister|Kevan]] in the center of [[Tywin Lannister|Tywin]]'s army. When Tywin names [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] acting [[Hand of the King]], Tyrion suggests Lord Serrett instead, among others.
