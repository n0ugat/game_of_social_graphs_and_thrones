The '''Arsenal of Braavos'''{{Ref|H&L504}} is an island citadel guarding the sea entrance to the [[Free Cities|Free City]] of [[Braavos]]. It is located at the entrance to the lagoon, just beyond the [[Titan of Braavos|Titan]].<ref>"[[Braavos (short)|Braavos]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels the Arsenal sits upon a knob of rock, fortified with stone battlements and bristling with scorpions, trebuchets, and spitfires. The Arsenal is also a dock with room for dozens of galleys. It houses the defense fleet of the city and is the center of shipbuilding in Braavos. It is said that a war galley can be built in a day there.
