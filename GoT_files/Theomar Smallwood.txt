==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lord Theomar Smallwood is the head of [[House Smallwood]] and the Lord of Acorn Hall. He is married to Lady Ravella Swann with whom he has a daughter, Carellen.
