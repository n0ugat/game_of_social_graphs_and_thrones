'''''The Official Game of Thrones Podcast: House of the Dragon'': Season 1''' is the first season of ''The Official Game of Thrones Podcast: House of the Dragon''. It consists of a trailer and eighteen episodes. It premiered with "[[Everything We Know About House of the Dragon (with Paddy Considine and Matt Smith)|Everything We Know About ''House of the Dragon'' (with Paddy Considine and Matt Smith)]]" on August 3, 2022, and concluded with "[[Live from GOT Fan Con with Kit Harington]]" on December 22, 2022.
