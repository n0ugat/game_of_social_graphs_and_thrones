Lord '''Lucas Blackwood''' was the Lord of [[Raventree Hall]] during [[Aegon's Conquest]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Lucas supported [[Aegon I Targaryen|Aegon the Conqueror]] when the conquest reached the [[Riverlands]], turning on his [[Ironborn]] king, [[Harren Hoare|Harren the Black]].{{Ref|HOTD104}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Lucas's great-great-grandson, [[Willem Blackwood]], mentions him whilst vying for [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]]'s hand.{{Ref|HOTD104}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', the Lord Blackwood who supported Aegon I in the Conquest is unnamed. He is known to have had daughters. In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lucas Blackwood is the second son of Lord Tytos Blackwood.
