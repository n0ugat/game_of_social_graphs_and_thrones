'''Hewett's Town'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a town on the island of [[Oakenshield]] in the [[Reach]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lord Hewett's Town is the seat of House Hewett. It is twice as large as [[Lordsport]].

Oakenshield is conquered by [[Euron Greyjoy]] during his invasion of the Reach and granted to Nute the Barber.
