'''James Grogan''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[Fire and Blood]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[The Prince of Winterfell]]", "[[Blackwater]]" and "[[Valar Morghulis]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episodes "[[And Now His Watch Is Ended]]" and "[[Kissed by Fire]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[The Laws of Gods and Men]]" and a stunt player in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[The North Remembers]]", "[[The Night Lands]]", "[[Garden of Bones (episode)|Garden of Bones]]", "[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]", "[[The Old Gods and the New]]" and "[[A Man Without Honor]]".
