'''Driftmark''',{{Ref|HOTD106}}{{Note|[[High Valyrian]]: ''Driftmāki''}} also known as the '''Seat of the Sea''',{{Ref|HOTD107}}{{Note|[[High Valyrian]]: ''Embrurlion''}} is an island in [[Blackwater Bay]] near [[Dragonstone (island)|Dragonstone]]. It is ruled by [[House Velaryon]] from the castle [[High Tide]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Driftmark is ruled by Lord [[Corlys Velaryon]], the successor of [[Daemon Velaryon]].
[[File:0 20092022-HouseoftheDragon1.jpg.webp|thumb|[[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys]] travels on the wheelhouse along the causeway to High Tide.]]

==Behind the scenes==
Driftmark was depicted by the island castle of St Michael's Mount in Cornwall in [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]].<ref>https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-cornwall-56919320</ref>
