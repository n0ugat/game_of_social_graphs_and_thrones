'''Ulf''' is the leader of the [[Moon Brothers]], one of the [[hill tribes]] of the [[Mountains of the Moon]] recruited by [[Tyrion Lannister]] to fight for him in the initial stages of the [[War of the Five Kings]] in exchange for gold, weaponry, and armor the hill tribes could employ against [[House Arryn]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Tyrion reports to [[Tywin Lannister|his father]] and [[Kevan Lannister|his uncle]], that a Moon Brother stabbed a [[Stone Crows|Stone Crow]] over a sausage; in retaliation, three Stone Crows seized the Moon brother and opened his throat; Ulf demands blood money, but [[Shagga]] and [[Gunthor]] refuse to pay.{{Ref|GOT109}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Ulf son of Umar is also the leader of the Moon Brothers, who accompany Tyrion to his father's encampment on the promise of weapons and armors.

Ulf is killed at the [[battle on the Green Fork]]. Tyrion finds him laying in a pool of congealing blood, his arm gone at the elbow, a dozen of his Moon Brothers sprawl around him.

Ulf is succeeded by Crawn son of Calor as the leader of the Moon Brothers.
