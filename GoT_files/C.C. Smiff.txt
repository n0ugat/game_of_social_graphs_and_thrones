'''Neil 'C.C.' Smiff''' is one of the award-winning stunt coordinators working on ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.  He is specifically in charge of coordinating the sword fight sequences in the TV series. He was also a stunt coordinator in ''[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.

Smiff previously worked as a stunt performer in such films as ''Gladiator'', ''Batman Begins'', ''Children of Men'', ''Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1'', and ''Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2''.  He previously worked as a fight coordinator on ''Captain America: The First Avenger''.

He also worked on ''Star Wars Episode VII: The Force Awakens''.
