'''Nicholas Blane''' is a British actor. He has had several recurring roles in British drama series such as ''[[wikipedia:Coronation Street|Coronation Street]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Little Dorrit|Little Dorrit]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Cracker (UK TV series)|Cracker]]'' (which also featured [[Clive Russell]], [[Liam Cunningham]], [[Ron Donachie]] and Colin Tierney, husband of [[Indira Varma]]) and ''[[wikipedia:Trial & Retribution|Trial and Retribution]]''. On film he has appeared in ''[[wikipedia:Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:The Illusionist (2006 film)|The Illusionist]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he is playing the role of the [[Spice King]]. He was confirmed in the role on 7 October 2011.<ref>[http://winter-is-coming.net/2011/10/day-75-casting-news-plus-more-set-photos-from-dubrovnik/ Winter is Coming]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].

He is currently starring the BBC/FX period drama series ''Taboo'', along with his ''Game of Thrones'' co-stars [[Jonathan Pryce]], [[Jefferson Hall]], [[Oona Chaplin]], [[Roger Ashton-Griffiths]], and [[Mark Gatiss]].
