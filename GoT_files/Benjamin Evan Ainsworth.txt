'''Benjamin Evan Ainsworth''' is British actor. He will appear as Prince [[Daeron Targaryen]], youngest son of [[Alicent Hightower]], in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 3|third season]] of ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.

He has previously appeared in ''The House on Bly Manor'', ''The Sandman'', and ''Pinnochio'', and has been cast as main character Link in the live-action ''Legend of Zelda'' movie.
