'''Lady Fell''' is the head of [[House Fell]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Otto Hightower]] demands that the assembled lords in [[King's Landing]] swear that [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon II]] is the new king. Lady Fell is one of the three lords who refuse to betray their oaths to [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]]. She is shortly thereafter removed from the [[throne room]] along with [[Lord Merryweather]].{{Ref|HOTD109}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Lady Fell was the Lady of Felwood and the head of House Fell during the reigns of kings Viserys I and Aegon II Targaryen. Her given name is unknown.

Lady Fell was arrested and later beheaded for refusing to renounce Rhaenyra and swear allegiance to Aegon II. Her head was mounted on a spike above the gates of King's Landing, along with those of [[Lord Merryweather]] and [[Allun Caswell|Lord Caswell]].

In the approved online roleplaying game ''Blood of Dragons'', she is named Alora Fell.
