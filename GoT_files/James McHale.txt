'''James McHale''' (died 24th August 2015)<ref>[https://rip.ie/showdn.php?dn=264586 Death Notice Of James McHale, Thomastown, Kilkenny, Ireland - RIP.ie]</ref> was an actor from Hells Kitchen, NY who moved to Ireland to reside in Thomastown, Kilkenny.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he portrayed Ser [[Axell Florent]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[The Lion and the Rose]]".
