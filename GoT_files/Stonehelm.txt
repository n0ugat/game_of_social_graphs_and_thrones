'''Stonehelm'''{{Ref|H&L204}} is a castle in the [[Stormlands]] and the seat of [[House Swann]], a vassal house holding fealty to [[House Baratheon]] of [[Storm's End]]. The castle is located southwest of Storm's End on the northern shore of the [[Sea of Dorne]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Stonehelm is located at the mouth of a river, at the south end of Cape Wrath and east of the Dornish Marches. It was built to oversee a major river route inland to the Stormlands.
