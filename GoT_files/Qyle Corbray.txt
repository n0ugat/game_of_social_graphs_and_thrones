King '''Qyle Corbray'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} was the King of the [[Fingers]] during the [[coming of the Andals]].

==Biography==
Qyle and his fellow [[Andals|Andal]] warriors fought against the [[First Men]] in the Vale under King [[Robar II Royce]] in the [[Battle of the Seven Stars]]. Robar killed Qyle and took his [[Valyrian steel]] sword [[Lady Forlorn]].{{Ref|H&L615}}

==In the books==
In ''The World of Ice & Fire'', Qyle Corbray ruled as King of the Fingers during the [[coming of the Andals]]. He was the first of the [[Andals|Andal]] kings of the [[Vale of Arryn|Vale]] to fall to King [[Robar II Royce]], who was said to have personally killed him after striking [[Lady Forlorn]] from his grasp.
