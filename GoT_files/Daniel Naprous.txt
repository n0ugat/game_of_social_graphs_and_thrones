}}'''Daniel Naprous''' is a French actor and stunt performer. He is the son of [[Gerard Naprous]] and the brother of [[Camilla Naprous]]. He and his fellow ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' actor [[Spencer Wilding]] share the role of {{WPS|Darth Vader}} in the ''{{WPS|Star Wars}}'' anthology film ''{{WPS|Rogue One}}''.

He appeared in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] of ''Game of Thrones'' as [[Oznak zo Pahl]], a champion for [[Meereen]], in the episode "[[Breaker of Chains]]".

He was also as a stunt performer in numerous episodes from [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|Season 2]] to [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|Season 8]].
