Ser '''Kevan''' was a [[Knighthood|knight]] connected to [[Sharp Point]], a castle in the [[Crownlands]].{{Note|It is unknown if Kevan was from [[House Bar Emmon]] or was simply a sworn sword at Sharp Point.}}

==Biography==
===Background===
Kevan married [[Theresa Baratheon]] at Sharp Point. They had a son named [[Petyr]].{{Ref|GOT106}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Kevan is mentioned in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the only character named Kevan is [[Kevan Lannister]].
