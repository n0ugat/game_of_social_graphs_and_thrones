'''Blazewater Bay'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a region off the north-western coast of the continent of [[Westeros]]. It is part of the [[Sunset Sea]]. The bay is located between [[Cape Kraken]] and the south coast of the [[Rills]]. [[Flint's Finger]] is located on the southern edge of the bay.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Blazewater Bay forms the mouth of the Saltspear, a long, narrow bay between the [[Barrowlands]] and the [[Neck]], which in turn is fed by the Fever River.
