'''One-Eyed Joe''' is a member of the [[Night's Watch]], and apparently chief among the stewards stationed in the stables at [[Castle Black]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
When the newly graduated recruits are assigned to their orders [[Luke]] is told by [[Order of Maesters|Maester]] [[Aemon]] to report to One-Eyed Joe in the stables.{{Ref|GOT107}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there's no member of the Night's Watch called One-Eyed Joe.
