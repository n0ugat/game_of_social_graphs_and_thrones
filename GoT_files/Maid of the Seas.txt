{{Quote|On the New Year, to bring good fortune to Pentos, this Prince must deflower the Maid of the Field and the Maid of the Seas. I confess I don’t know how each is chosen, or what becomes of them after serving their purpose.|Ser [[Jorah Mormont]]|The Free Cities}}
'''Maid of the Seas'''{{Ref|H&L215}} is the title given to one of the two maidens that, by tradition, the [[Prince of Pentos]] must deflower each new year to bring good fortune to the [[Pentos|city]]. The other maiden is known as the [[Maid of the Field]].{{Ref|H&L215}}
