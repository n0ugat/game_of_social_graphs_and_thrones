'''House Hunter'''<ref>[[:File:House Royce lineage.jpeg]]</ref> of [[Longbow Hall]] is a vassal house from the [[Vale of Arryn|Vale]].<ref name="Book">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref> It holds fealty to [[House Arryn]]. It is one of the major houses in the Vale of Arryn. Its head is Lord [[Eon Hunter]].

==Members==
*Lord [[Eon Hunter]], Lord of [[Longbow Hall]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Hunter are loyal bannermen of House Arryn. Their seat is Longbow Hall. According to semi-canon sources, they blazon their arms with five silver arrows, fanned, on a brown field.

===Members===
* Lord {Eon Hunter}, Lord of Longbow Hall. An old man with gouty legs, who has been ruling his house for sixty years, known as Old Lord Hunter. Died under suspicious circumstances.
** Ser Gilwood Hunter, his first son and heir.
** Ser Eustace Hunter, his second son.
** Ser Harlan Hunter, his third son.
