Ser '''Serwyn Errol''' was [[Lord Commander of the Kingsguard]] during the reign of King [[Aenys Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Serwyn was [[Lord Commander of the Kingsguard]] during the reign of King [[Aenys Targaryen]] and knighted [[Mychal Baratheon]].{{Ref|GOT106}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He is mentioned in the book ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there has been no mention of the name of the Lord Commander of the Kingsguard during the reign of Aenys I Targaryen. The only character by that name is the legendary Ser Serwyn of the Mirror Shield, who allegedly killed the dragon Urrax.
