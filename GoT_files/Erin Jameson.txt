'''Erin Anna Jameson''' is an stuntwoman and actress.

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', she was a stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episodes "[[The Heirs of the Dragon]]", "[[Second of His Name]]", "[[King of the Narrow Sea (episode)|King of the Narrow Sea]]", "[[We Light the Way]]" and "[[Driftmark (episode)|Driftmark]]" and the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[The Red Dragon and the Gold]]".
