'''Feastfires'''{{Ref|GOT105}} is a castle in the [[Westerlands]], the region controlled by [[House Lannister]] of [[Casterly Rock]]. The castle is located on the tip of a peninsula west of Casterly Rock.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Feastfires is the seat of House Prester. It is located close to a town called Kayce.
