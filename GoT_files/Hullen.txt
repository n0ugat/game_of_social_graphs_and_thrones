'''Hullen''' is the horse master serving [[House Stark]] at [[Winterfell]].

==Behind the scenes==
Hullen appears in a version of the ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' [[Pilot (Game of Thrones)|pilot]] script.{{Scriptref|GOT100}} It is unknown if he appears in the final version of the pilot, and he does not appear in "[[Winter Is Coming]]."

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Hullen is the master of horse at Winterfell. His son, Harwin, is a member of [[Eddard Stark]]'s household guard.{{Bookref|AGOT|1}}

Hullen is present when the six [[Direwolf|direwolves]] are discovered.{{Bookref|AGOT|1}} He later accompanies Ned to [[King's Landing]], where he is killed when Ned is [[Massacre in the Red Keep|arrested]].{{Bookref|AGOT|50}}
