The '''House of Loraq'''{{Ref|GOT406}}{{Note|Hizdahr's surname is Loraq.}}{{Note|[[Low Valyrian#Ghiscari Low Valyrian|Meereenese Low Valyrian]]: ''Lentor hen Loraq''}} is one of the ancient noble [[Slavery|slaver]] families from the city of [[Meereen]], known as the "[[Great Masters]]."

==Known members==
*The {head of the House of Loraq}, crucified under [[Daenerys Targaryen]]'s orders.
**{[[Hizdahr zo Loraq]]}, his son, and new head of the House of Loraq. Murdered by the [[Sons of the Harpy]] at the Great Games.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the House of Loraq is one of the ancient and most prestigious slaving families of Meereen, having produced famous figures. The family colors are purple, indigo, and lilac.

There is no mention in the novels that Hizdahr's father is one of the Great Masters who were executed by Daenerys.

===Known members===
*Hizdahr zo Loraq, formerly King of Meereen and husband of Daenerys. Imprisoned by Ser [[Barristan Selmy]] for alleged attempt to assassinate Daenerys and collaboration with her enemies.
*Marghaz zo Loraq, Hizdahr's cousin. Commander of the Brazen Beasts.
*Bhakaz zo Loraq, one of Daenerys's [[cupbearer]]s.
