{{Cast|Title = Sam Redford|Image = Sam Redford.jpg|birthplace = England|role = [[Tully bannerman]]|status = |Appearances = "[[No One]]"|imdb = http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0714866|twitter = http://www.twitter.com/samredford7}}'''Sam Redford''' is an English actor, son of renowned actor, [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ian_Redford_(actor) Ian Redford]. He has appeared in ''One Life to Live'', ''The Hurt Locker'', and ''The Night Manager''. He also appears in ''Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them''.

In the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[No One]]", he portrays a [[Tully bannerman]].
