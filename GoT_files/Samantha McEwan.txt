'''Samantha McEwan''' (born 8 October 1987), also credited as '''Samantha Bentley''', is a former adult performer and actress.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she portrays an unnamed bathhouse prostitute in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] and appears in "[[The Laws of Gods and Men]]".

She reappears in [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] playing two different characters in the episode "[[High Sparrow (episode)|High Sparrow]]", a [[Volantis|Volantene]] [[Prostitution|prostitute]] dubbed the "[[Mother of Dragons (High Sparrow)|Mother of Dragons]]" and a [[Westeros]]i prostitute roleplaying as the [[Stranger]]. All told, she has played three separate prostitute characters in the TV series.
