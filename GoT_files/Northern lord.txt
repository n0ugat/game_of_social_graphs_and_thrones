A '''Northern lord''' is among the people who take part in a [[Great Council in the Dragonpit]], to determine [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]]'s fate as well as the new [[King of the Andals and the Rhoynar and the First Men|King]] of the [[Seven Kingdoms]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
The [[North]]ern lord sits next to [[Yara Greyjoy]]. Like the rest of the assembled council, he accepts the ascension of [[Bran Stark]] as the new King, although the result of the council makes him a vassal to the newly independent [[King in the North|Queen in the North]], [[Sansa Stark]].{{Ref|GOT806}}

==Behind the scenes==
The name and House of this lord are not specified in the TV series. The principal surviving northern houses whose lords are as of yet unseen are [[House Tallhart]], [[House Flint]], and [[House Dormand]].
