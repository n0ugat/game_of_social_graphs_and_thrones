'''Qal'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is an island to the east of the [[Jade Gates]], south of the [[Bone Mountains]], southeast of [[Qarth]], and north of [[Great Moraq]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Qal is under the jurisdiction of Qarth. It has two port cities on its northwestern and southeastern ends.
