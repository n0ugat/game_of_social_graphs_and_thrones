[[Sylvi]]'s '''brothel''' is on the [[Street of Silk]] in [[King's Landing]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Criston Cole]] and [[Aemond Targaryen]] visit Sylvi at her establishment for information regarding the whereabouts of Prince [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon]]. She informs them that Aegon no longer frequents the Street of Silk.{{Ref|HOTD109}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
[[File:OT2 Copper.png|thumb|Aegon attends a performance.]]
Aemond begins to visit the establishment again, exclusively seeing Sylvi, to whom he lost his virginity at a very young age.{{Ref|HOTD202}}

While taking [[Martyn Reyne]]'s squire to the brothel, Aegon discovers Aemond in bed with Sylvi, and mocks him for still being attached to his first.{{Ref|HOTD203}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', the known brothels in King's Landing are the House of Kisses, the Blue Pearl, the Mother's, and Chataya's brothel. It is unknown if Sylvi's establishment is meant to be either of these.
