|imdb = http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0937869/}}'''Matthew Wolf''' is an English actor. He has appeared in television series such as ''Emmerdale'', ''Grange Hill'', ''Family Affairs'', ''24'' and ''Hart of Dixie'' and films such as ''Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End'', ''Mr. Popper's Penguin'', ''The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo'' and ''The Fault in Our Stars''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a Lannister Soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]".

In ''[[Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series]]'', he voices a Whitehill soldier and a Lannister soldier.
