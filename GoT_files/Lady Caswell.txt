'''Lady Caswell'''{{Con|F&B}} is the widow of Lord [[Allun Caswell]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Lady Caswell is with her husband when he congratulates [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] on the birth of Prince [[Joffrey Velaryon|Joffrey]].{{Ref|HOTD106}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Lady Caswell ruled [[Bitterbridge]] on behalf of her young son after the execution of her husband.
