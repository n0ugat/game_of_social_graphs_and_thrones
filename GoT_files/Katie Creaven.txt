'''Katie Creaven''' is an English actress, based in Ireland, who portraysÂ [[Marianne Frey]] in [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|Season 3]] of ''Game of Thrones''.

Originally working in Musical Theatre, she has been nominated by the Association of Irish Musical Societies (AIMS) for Best Actress (2005), Best Comedienne (2008), Best Supporting Actress (2009) and Best Female Singer (2011).

In 2012 she was nominated for Best Comedienne at the Waterford International Festival of Light Opera (WIFLO) Awards.

In June 2017 she won the coveted award for "Best Female Singer". This was her second time being nominated for the award and her fifth AIMS nomination.
