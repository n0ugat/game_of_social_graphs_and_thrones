'''Sherrer'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a village in the [[Riverlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Sherrer is one of the first settlements affected by the [[War of the Five Kings]]. During [[House Lannister]]'s early raids against the riverlands, Sherrer is subjected to a brutal attack in which girls as young as six and seven are raped and babies are killed.
