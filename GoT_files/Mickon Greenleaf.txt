'''Mickon Greenleaf''' was the Warden of [[Last Hearth]], the seat of [[House Umber]], a little less than a century before the death of [[Robert Baratheon]]. His daughter, [[Marya Greenleaf]], married [[Finn Umber]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He is mentioned in his daughter's husband's entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
Mickon Greenleaf is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
