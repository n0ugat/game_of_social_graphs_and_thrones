'''Philip McGinley''', (born June 6, 1981 in Liverpool, Merseyside, England, UK) is an actor based in the UK.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he plays [[Anguy]]. He was announced as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] at San Diego Comic-Con 2012.<ref name=ComicCon>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/Massive_Comic-Con_Casting_Reveals/ Massive Comic-Con Casting Reveals]</ref>

He appeared in the 2012 film ''Prometheus'', which was cast by ''Game of Thrones'' casting director [[Nina Gold]]. He has also appeared in ''Falling'', ''Coronation Street'', and ''Nina Argentina''.
