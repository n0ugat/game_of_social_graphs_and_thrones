'''David Petrarca''' (born 10 November 1965) is an American Online Film & Television Association winner director and producer, working in theatre, film and television. His only film credit is ''[[wikipedia:Save the Last Dance 2|Save the Last Dance 2]]'', but his TV credits include ''[[wikipedia:Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip|Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Dawson's Creek|Dawon's Creek]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Eli Stone|Eli Stone]]''. He is most notable for his work for [[HBO]], having directed episodes of ''[[wikipedia:True Blood|True Blood]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Hung (TV series)|Hung]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Big Love|Big Love]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Boardwalk Empire|Boardwalk Empire]]''.

For ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', Petrarca directed episodes of the series starting in its [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
