'''Petyr Baelish's great-grandfather''' was the founder of [[House Baelish]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He was a [[Mercenary|sellsword]] from [[Braavos]] who traveled to the [[Vale of Arryn]] at the invitation of [[House Corbray]]. Once there he founded [[House Baelish]].{{Ref|H&L402}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
Littlefinger mentions his great-grandfather when questioned by Lord [[Yohn Royce]] about his family history.{{Ref|GOT408}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, his story is the same.

In ''A Storm of Swords'', Littlefinger briefly tells [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]] about his great-grandfather when they stay at the [[Fingers]].
