The '''cod queen'''<ref>[[:File:Cod queen concept art.png]]</ref> is a [[mermaid]]-like figure depicted on a statue owned by [[Corlys Velaryon]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Corlys's statue of the cod queen is kept in the Hall of Nine at [[High Tide]].{{Ref|HOTD105}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Corlys captained a fishing boat named the ''Cod Queen'' when he was sixteen years old. The origin of the boat's name is not revealed, however.{{Bookref|F&B|12}}
