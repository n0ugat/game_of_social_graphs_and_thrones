'''''Game of Thrones: Season 5 (Music from the HBO Series)''''' was composed by [[Ramin Djawadi]] and was released on June 9, 2015. It was preceded by ''[[Game of Thrones: Season 4 (Music from the HBO Series)]]'', released in 2014, and followed by ''[[Game of Thrones: Season 6 (Music from the HBO Series)]]'', released in 2016.
