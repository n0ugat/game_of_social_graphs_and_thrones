'''''Game of Thrones: Ascent''''' was a social network game based on the [[HBO]] [[Game of Thrones|television series]]. The game was developed by Disruptor Beam in association with HBO. Each time a new episode aired, new content for the game was released that was based on that episode. The game officially shut down on January 3, 2019.

In the game, the player could become a Lord or Lady and swear fealty to one of the [[Great House]]s: [[House Baratheon|Baratheon]], [[House Greyjoy|Greyjoy]], [[House Lannister|Lannister]], [[House Stark|Stark]], or [[House Targaryen|Targaryen]].
