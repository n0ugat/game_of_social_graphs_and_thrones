'''Gerald''' is a groom in the army of [[King]] [[Renly Baratheon]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
He is a groom in the army of [[King]] [[Renly Baratheon]]. He was injured by a horse but recovered well. Renly greets him when he takes [[Catelyn Stark]] on a tour of his camp in the [[Stormlands]].{{Ref|GOT203}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no equivalent character. The only character by that name is Ser Gerald Gower, a knight in service to [[Stannis Baratheon]] whose role in the books is very minor.
