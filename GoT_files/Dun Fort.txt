The '''Dun Fort'''{{Ref|H&L805}} is a castle in the port-town of [[Duskendale]]. It formerly served as the seat of [[House Darklyn]], and the administrative center of Duskendale.{{Ref|H&L805}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, [[Brienne of Tarth|Brienne]] visits the Dun Fort during her search for [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]]. She wishes to speak with Lord Renfred Rykker, but he is not there; the guards tell her he rode to [[Maidenpool]] with [[Randyll Tarly]]. The [[castellan]] who was assigned to look after Lady Rykker and her children, Ser Rufus Leek, cannot help Brienne, and sends her to the [[Order of Maesters|maester]] of the castle, a bald man with a freckled scalp and a red mustache. The maester tells Brienne about the Darklyns and whatever he knows about [[Dontos Hollard]].
