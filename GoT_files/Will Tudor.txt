}}'''Will Tudor''' (b. 11 April 1987) is an English actor.

In Game of Thrones he played the role of [[Olyvar]].

He was starring in the British science-fiction drama series [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Humans_(TV_series) Humans], alongside fellow ''Game of Thrones'' cast member [[Paul Kaye]] and in ''[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shadowhunters Shadowhunters] ''an American supernatural drama television series'' ''portraying Sebastian Verlac.
