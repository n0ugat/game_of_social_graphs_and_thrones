The '''Street of Looms'''{{Ref|H&L801}} is a street in [[King's Landing]] known for its tailors.<ref>"[[King's Landing (short)|King's Landing]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] sees a naked corpse being torn apart by feral dogs in a gutter near the Street of Looms, when he arrives in King's Landing to serve as [[Hand of the King]] in [[Tywin Lannister|his father]]'s absence.
