'''Susan Elizabeth Brown''' (born 6 May 1946) is a British actress. She is an experienced actress with many roles on stage and screen. She had a major role as Bridget Spears in ''[[wikipedia:Torchwood: Children of Earth|Torchwood: Children of Earth]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' she played the role of Septa [[Mordane]]. She was confirmed in the role on 16 July 2010.
