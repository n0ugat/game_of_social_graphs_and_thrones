'''Dorian Hightower's first wife''' was the first wife of [[Dorian Hightower]], the head of [[House Hightower]] during the [[coming of the Andals]].

==Biography==
When the [[Andals]] invaded [[Westeros]], Lady Hightower had been married to Dorian for twenty years. Dorian told her that wars have a negative impact on trade, and abandoned her to marry [[Dorian Hightower's second wife|a young Andal princess]].{{Ref|H&L612}}
