Lady '''Conn Blackbrow''' was the wife of Lord [[Erich Blackbrow]] and a member of [[House Blackbrow]] through marriage.

==Biography==
===Background===
Conn Blackbrow married [[Erich Blackbrow]], and bore him at least one child, [[Jore Blackbrow]]. It is unknown which house she was originally from.{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She is referenced in the entry of [[Emilia Umber]], her daughter-in-law, in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
