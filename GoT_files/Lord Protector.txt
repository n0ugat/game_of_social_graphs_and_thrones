'''Lord Protector'''{{Ref|GOT107}} is a temporary title of [[Regent|regency]] that may be held on behalf of a [[Lordship|lord]] or lady who is unable or unfit to rule. 

[[Petyr Baelish]] was named Lord Protector of the [[Vale of Arryn|Vale]], on behalf of [[Robin Arryn]], after he married [[Lysa Arryn]].{{Ref|GOT707}}
