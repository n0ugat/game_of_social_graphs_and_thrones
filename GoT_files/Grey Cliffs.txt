The '''Grey Cliffs'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} are located along the coastline of northern [[Westeros]], east and north-east of [[Karhold]] in the [[North]]. The cliffs form the southern shoreline of the [[Bay of Seals]] and part of the western shore of the vast [[Shivering Sea]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Grey Cliffs are in the same location. They are controlled by [[House Karstark]].
