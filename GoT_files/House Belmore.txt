'''House Belmore'''<ref>[[:File:Maynard Royce detail.png]]</ref>{{Note|Mavis's surname is Belmore.}} of [[Strongsong]] is a vassal house from the [[Vale of Arryn]] that holds fealty to [[House Arryn]] of the [[Eyrie]].

==History==
[[Mavis Royce|Mavis Belmore]] is mentioned in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' as the wife of [[Maynard Royce]].<ref name="Prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Members==
*{[[Mavis Royce|Mavis Belmore]]}, wife of {[[Maynard Royce]]}, a [[Lord of Runestone]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Belmore is one of the principal houses of the Vale of Arryn and one of the most powerful vassals of House Arryn.
