}}'''Graeme Peacock''' is a Northern Irish actor. He has appeared in the film ''{{WPS|Starred Up}}'' and the television series ''{{WPS|The Fall (TV series)|The Fall}}''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he has portrayed a Field Worker in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[The North Remembers]]" and "[[Garden of Bones (episode)|Garden of Bones]]", a Marker Trader in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[Blackwater]]", a member of Littlefinger's Crew in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[Oathkeeper (episode)|Oathkeeper]]", a member of Stannis' Crew in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[The Laws of Gods and Men]]", a Volantis Fighter in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episode "[[The Gift]]", a Baratheon Soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]" and "[[Mother's Mercy]]", an Umber Soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]" and a Silence Crew member in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episode "[[Stormborn]]" and "[[The Queen's Justice]]".
