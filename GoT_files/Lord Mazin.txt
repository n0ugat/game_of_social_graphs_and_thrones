'''Lord Mazin''' is a [[North|Northern]] [[Lordship|lord]], the head of [[House Mazin]], and a bannerman of [[House Stark]]. He remains loyal to the Starks during their campaign to take back [[Winterfell]] from [[House Bolton]], contributing [[Man-at-arms|soldiers]] during the [[Battle of the Bastards]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Lord Mazin sends a message to [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch|Lord Commander]] [[Jon Snow]], in which he agrees to send men to the [[Night's Watch]].{{Ref|GOT504}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
Lord Mazin sends 143 men to [[Jon Snow|Jon]] and [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]] to fight against [[House Bolton]] and take back [[Winterfell]] in the [[Battle of the Bastards]].{{Ref|GOT607}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of any House Mazin. The name is an in-joke reference to fellow scriptwriter and friend of the showrunners, Craig Mazin.
