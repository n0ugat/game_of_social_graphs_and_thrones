'''Matthew Jensen''' is a online cinematographer who has worked mostly in television, short films and commercials. His television credits include cinematography work on ''Numb3rs'', ''CSI'', ''Sleeper Cell'', and ''True Blood''.

Matthew is doing the cinematography of ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.
