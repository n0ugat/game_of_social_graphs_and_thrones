'''Sylvi Stark''' was a [[prince]]ss of [[House Stark]] who lived during the [[Age of Heroes]]. She was a relative of Princess [[Maiev Stark|Maiev]] and [[Stark (Bloodmoon)|another Stark]], and was married to the [[Casterly king]].

==Behind the scenes==
On January 8, 2019, ''Entertainment Weekly'' announced that [[Georgie Henley]] had been cast as a series regular in ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', a ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' [[World of Westeros|prequel]] [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]].{{Webref|name=EW |author=James Hibberd |date=January 8, 2019 |url=https://ew.com/tv/2019/01/08/game-of-thrones-prequel-cast-director/ |title=''Game of Thrones'' prequel announces diverse full cast, female director |website=Entertainment Weekly |access-date=January 9, 2019 }} On February 2, 2024, Wake the Dragon revealed that Henley's character was a Stark princess who gets married to a Casterly at [[Casterly Rock]].<ref name="Princess" /> On February 10, 2024, Wake the Dragon leaked storyboards from the pilot, one of which depicted a character named [[Alden]] helping another character named Sylvi exit a gondola at Casterly Rock as members of House Casterly arrive.{{Webref|name=WTD |author=Wake the Dragon |date=February 10, 2024 |url=https://twitter.com/WtDasoiaf/status/1756284710118998245 |title=üö®üêâ Cancelled GoT prequel 'BLOOD MOON' storyboards |website=X |access-date=February 11, 2024 }} On February 18, 2024, Wake the Dragon revealed that Henley had portrayed Princess Sylvi Stark.<ref name="Starks" />

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and its supplementary material, there is no mention of a character named Sylvi Stark. She was an invention of [[Jane Goldman]] for ''Bloodmoon''.
