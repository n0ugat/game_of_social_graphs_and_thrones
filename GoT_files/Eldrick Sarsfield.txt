Ser '''Eldrick Sarsfield''' is a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Sarsfield]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Ser Eldrick Sarsfield is a member of [[House Sarsfield]], a vassal of [[House Lannister]].{{Ref|GOT310}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
As [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] and [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]] walk through the [[Red Keep]]'s gardens, Ser Eldrick and Lord [[Desmond Crakehall]] walk by, clearly laughing at Tyrion, who notes their names for future humiliation.{{Ref|GOT310}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Eldrick Sarsfield.
