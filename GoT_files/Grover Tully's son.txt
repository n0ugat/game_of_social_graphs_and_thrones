'''Grover Tully's son''' was a member of [[House Tully/House of the Dragon|House Tully]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
He was the son of Lord [[Grover Tully]] and the heir to [[Riverrun]]. As Grover grew old and frail, the son became de facto ruler of Riverrun.{{Ref|HOTD106}} He died suddenly while his son [[Oscar Tully|Oscar]] was still young, and Grover raised Oscar in his stead.{{Ref|HOTD204}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Grover Tully's son is not mentioned.
