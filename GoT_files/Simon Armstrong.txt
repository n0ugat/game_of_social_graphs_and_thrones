}}'''Simon Armstrong''' is a British actor with numerous credits on stage and screen, including a recurring role on the British soap opera ''[[wikipedia:Casualty (TV series)|Casualty]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Armstrong is playing the role of [[Qhorin]]. He was announced in the role on 13 October 2011.<ref>[http://grrm.livejournal.com/243753.html George R.R. Martin's blog]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
