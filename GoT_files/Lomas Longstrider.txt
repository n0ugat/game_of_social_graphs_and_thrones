==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
''[[Legends of the Long Night]]'', the book [[Gilly]] is reading from the [[Citadel]] in [[Oldtown]], refers to Lomas Longstrider and his ''Wonders Made by Man''.{{Ref|GOT701}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lomas Longstrider was a famous scribe who travelled all over the [[known world]] and documented his adventures in the books ''Wonders'' and ''Wonders Made by Man''.

The places he visited and/or wrote about included [[Qarth]], [[Old Ghis]], [[Norvos]], [[Braavos]], [[Volantis]], the [[Shivering Sea]], [[Yi Ti]] and [[Bayasabhad]].

He is long dead when the series takes place. [[Tyrion Lannister]] has memorised many passages from his books, and [[Barristan Selmy]] is also familiar with his work.
