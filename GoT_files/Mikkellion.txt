'''Mikkellion''' was a [[Order of Maesters|maester]] living during the late reign of [[Aegon I Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Mikkellion wrote the text ''[[Shrubbery, Being a History on the Flora of the Valyrian Freehold]]'' in [[36 AC]].<ref name="Shrubbery" />

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]] has a copy of ''Shrubbery'' in his library in [[Pentos]].{{Ref|HOTD106}}{{Ref|THTDB106}}

==Behind the scenes==
Mikkellion is named after [[Ti Mikkel]], the real author of ''Shrubbery''.

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Mikkellion.
