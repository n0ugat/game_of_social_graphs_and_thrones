The '''Casterly king''' lived during the [[Age of Heroes]]. He was a relative of Lady [[Morven Casterly|Morven]], [[Aurelia Casterly|Aurelia]], and Princess [[Ianthe Casterly]]. Though he was married to Princess [[Sylvi Stark]], he had [[Casterly king's lover|other lovers]] including [[Lann the Clever]].

==Behind the scenes==
On January 8, 2019, ''Entertainment Weekly'' announced that [[Toby Regbo]] had been cast as a series regular in ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', a ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' [[World of Westeros|prequel]] [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]].{{Webref|name=EW |author=James Hibberd |date=January 8, 2019 |url=https://ew.com/tv/2019/01/08/game-of-thrones-prequel-cast-director/ |title=''Game of Thrones'' prequel announces diverse full cast, female director |website=Entertainment Weekly |access-date=January 9, 2019 }} On February 18, 2024, Wake the Dragon revealed that he had portrayed the young Casterly king.<ref name="King" />

==In the books==
The Casterly king was an invention of [[Jane Goldman]] for ''[[Bloodmoon]]''.
