'''''Game of Thrones: Kingsroad'''''{{Webref|name=GS |author=Game of Thrones: Kingsroad |date=November 13, 2024 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-1vn964YfxE |title=<nowiki>[</nowiki>Game of Thrones: Kingsroad<nowiki>]</nowiki> G-Star Trailer |website=YouTube |access-date=March 3, 2025 }} is a forthcoming role-playing video game set to be released in 2025. Many of the game's events will take place parallel to the events of the [[Game of Thrones|television series]]. [[Jon Snow]] and other figures in the [[Night's Watch]] will feature prominently in the story.<ref>https://ew.com/game-of-thrones-kingsroad-trailer-jon-snow-return-8760945</ref> The game is expected to introduce heavier [[Magic|magical]] and fantastic elements to the franchise, and will provide the first on-screen appearances of several creatures previously believed to be completely mythical, such as [[Ice spider|giant ice spiders]], griffins, [[Unicorn|unicorns]] and cockatrices.<ref>https://winteriscoming.net/check-out-the-monsters-and-bosses-you-ll-fight-in-game-of-thrones-kingsroad</ref><ref>https://www.cbr.com/game-of-thrones-kingsroad-ice-spiders-unicorns/</ref>

Player choice plays a role in the game. Players will be able to explore [[Westeros]], from [[the Wall]] to [[King's Landing]]. Aside from Jon Snow, confirmed characters include [[Cersei Lannister]], [[Petyr Baelish]], and [[Ramsay Bolton]].{{Webref|name= |author=Katelyn Mitchell Jewett
