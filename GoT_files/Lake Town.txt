'''Lake Town'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a town in the [[Riverlands]], near the [[Gods Eye]] lake.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the town on the southern shore of the Gods Eye is unnamed. [[Yoren]] brings his band of [[Night's Watch]] recruits there, only to find the town completely abandoned.
