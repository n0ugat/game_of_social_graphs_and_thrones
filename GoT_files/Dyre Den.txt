The '''Dyre Den'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a castle on [[Crackclaw Point]] in the [[Crownlands]]. It lies on the coast to the north of the [[Whispers]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
The Dyre Den{{Note|Misspelled as "Dire Den."}} appears on the large floor map of [[Westeros]] that [[Cersei Lannister|Cersei]] observes.{{Ref|GOT701}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Dyre Den is ruled by [[House Brune]] of the Dyre Den. It was built on a cliff overlooking the Bay of Crabs.
