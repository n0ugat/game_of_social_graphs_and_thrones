'''Freddie Hill''' is a British actor, director, and producer with numerous television and theatre credits to his name. He has also directed and produced several short films, and has been recognised for his work at the Mumbai International Film Festival.

He portrays "Qarl Correy's Comrade" in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] of ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.<ref>https://www.spotlight.com/5412-5615-4227</ref>
