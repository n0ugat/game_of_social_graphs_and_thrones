'''Charlotte Hope''' is a British actress.

She plays [[Myranda]] in ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. She was announced in the role on 28 June 2012.<ref>[http://wicnet.tumblr.com/post/26084130142/charlotte-hope-joins-the-cast  WiC.net Tumblr]</ref> She has appeared in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth]] seasons.

Beside her role in ''Game of Thrones'', she has appeared in ''Waking the Dead'', ''Missing,'' and ''Casualty''. She appeared in ''The Theory of Everything'' alongside fellow ''Game of Thrones'' actors [[Harry Lloyd]] and [[Enzo Cilenti]], in ''The Testament of Youth'' with [[Kit Harington]], and in the upcoming movie ''The Invisible Woman'' with [[Michelle Fairley]].
