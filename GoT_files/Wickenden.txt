'''Wickenden'''{{Ref|GOT701}} is a town in the [[Vale of Arryn]], the region controlled by [[House Arryn]] of the [[Eyrie]]. The town is on the southern coast of the Vale, just across the bay from [[Maidenpool]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, it is the seat of House Waxley and is on the northern edge of the Bay of Crabs.
