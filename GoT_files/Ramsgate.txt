'''Ramsgate'''{{Ref|GOT402}} is a castle in the [[North]], the region ruled by [[House Stark]] of [[Winterfell]]. Ramsgate is located south-east of Winterfell, near the mouth of the [[Broken Branch]] river.

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
Ramsgate is seen on a map of the North that [[Roose Bolton]] has in the [[Dreadfort]], when he is pointing at [[Moat Cailin]] to the west.{{Ref|GOT402}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Ramsgate is in the same area. The rulers of Ramsgate have not been mentioned, but they are sworn to [[House Manderly]] of [[White Harbor]].
