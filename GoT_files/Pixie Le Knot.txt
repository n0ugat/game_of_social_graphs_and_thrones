}}'''Pixie Le Knot''' (born '''Jennifer Keith''') is a London contortionist. She portrays [[Kayla]] in [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|Season 3]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|4]].

Le Knot's participation was first mentioned in a tweet by Sophie O'Brien.<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2013/02/more-season-three-casting-updates/ More season three casting updates]</ref> Her appearance was later confirmed with the airing of the Season 3 trailer.<ref>http://wicnet.tumblr.com/post/44784928981/leyton-contortionist-pixie-le-knot-set-to-appear-in</ref>
