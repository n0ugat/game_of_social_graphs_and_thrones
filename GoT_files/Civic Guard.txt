The '''Civic Guard'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} is the force that protects the city-state of [[Qarth]]. They also serve as bodyguards to the members of the [[Thirteen]], the rulers of the city.

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
When [[Daenerys Targaryen]] and her ''[[khalasar]]'' arrive the gates of Qarth, a cadre of Civic Guards appear before them to protect the Thirteen.{{Ref|GOT204}}

The members of the Civic Guard guarding the manse of [[Xaro Xhoan Daxos]] are murdered during the robbery of Daenerys's dragons.{{Ref|GOT206}}

==Equipment==
Members of the Civic Guard wear bronze armor above their torsos. They carry long lances and round shields.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Civic Guard is commanded by the Pureborn of Qarth. It includes some camelry and works in partnership with the Pureborn fleet.
