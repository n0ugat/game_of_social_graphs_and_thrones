The '''Ghiscari Strait'''<ref>https://entertainment.ha.com/itm/movie-tv-memorabilia/props/essos-map-from-game-of-thrones-hbo-r-original-2011-2019-/a/7366-89866.s</ref> is a narrow body of water separating the islands of [[New Ghis]] from the mainland of [[Essos]]. It is located in [[Ghiscar]]i territory. The strait links the [[Gulf of Grief]] with the [[Summer Sea]].{{Mapref|Viewer's Guide}}

==In the books==
In ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'', the Ghiscari Strait separates the islands of New Ghis and Ghaen from the mainland.
