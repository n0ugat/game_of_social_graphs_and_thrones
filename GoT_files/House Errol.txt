'''House Errol'''{{Guideref|The Aftermath of Unclean Methods}} of [[Haystack Hall]] is a vassal house from the [[Stormlands]] that holds fealty to [[House Baratheon]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
House Errol, along with most noble houses of the Stormlands, pledge support to [[Renly Baratheon]]'s claim to the [[Iron Throne]].{{Guideref|The Aftermath of Unclean Methods}}

Following the [[Assassination of Renly Baratheon|Renly's death]], the Errols go over to [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]] and participate in the [[Battle of the Blackwater]].{{Guideref|The Aftermath of Unclean Methods}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Errol's history is the same.

===Known members===
*Lady {Shyra Errol}, Lady of Haystack Hall.
*Lord Sebastion Errol, Lord of Haystack Hall, Shyra's successor.
