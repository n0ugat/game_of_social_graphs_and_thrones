'''Atranta'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a castle in the [[Riverlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Atranta is the seat of a branch of [[House Vance]]. Its name is a reference to a place in ''Bad Ronald'' by Jack Vance.
