'''Robert of Oxbridge''' was a [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
===Background===
After the death of [[Osric Stark]], Robert was unanimously chosen to succeed him. Two years later, however, he was stripped of his command and confined to an ice cell after being found abed with his personal [[Steward (Night's Watch)|steward]], [[Morros Charlton]]. He was quickly succeeded by [[Willem of the Dun Fort]].<ref>[[:File:5x2 Night's Watch Book.jpg]]</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Robert is mentioned in a book that [[Samwell Tarly]] is reading.{{Ref|GOT502}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Robert of Oxbridge.
