}}'''Richard E. Grant''' (born '''Richard Grant Esterhuysen'''; 5 May 1957) is a Swazi-born English actor, screenwriter, and director. He is best known for his acting roles in films such as ''Withnail & I'' and ''Bram Stoker's Dracula'' and in TV shows such as ''Dig'', ''Downton Abbey'', ''Doctor Who'', and ''Girls''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrays [[Izembaro]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2015/09/12/richard-e-grants-role-revealed-plus-the-babadooks-essie-davis-joins-the-cast/ Richard E.
