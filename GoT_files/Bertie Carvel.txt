'''Robert Hugh Carvel''' (born 6 September 1977) is an English actor.

Carvel portrays [[Baelor Targaryen]] in ''[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Webref|name=FirstLook |author=Nick Romano |date=June 18, 2024 |url=https://ew.com/game-of-thrones-a-knight-of-the-seven-kingdoms-first-look-cast-confirmed-8665658 |title=''Game of Thrones'' spinoff unveils its Hedge Knight in first look at ''A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms'' |website=Entertainment Weekly |access-date=June 18, 2024}}

From 2011 to 2024, Carvel voiced the male Imperial Agent player character in the MMORPG ''Star Wars: The Old Republic'', a role that is now filled by [[Wilf Scolding]], who played [[Rhaegar Targaryen]] in ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.
