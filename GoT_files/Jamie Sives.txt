'''Jamie Sives''' (born 1973) is a Scottish actor. He has a long list of TV and film credits, including recent appearances in ''[[wikipedia:Get Him to the Greek|Get Him to the Greek]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Clash of the Titants (2009 film)|Clash of the Titans]]'' and as Captain Reynolds in the ''[[wikipedia:Doctor Who|Doctor Who]]'' episode ''[[wikipedia:Tooth and Claw (Doctor Who)|Tooth and Claw]]''. He also appeared in ''[[wikipedia:The Passion (film)|The Passion]]'' alongside [[Joseph Mawle]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played the role of [[Jory Cassel]]. He was confirmed in the role via Twitter on 11 July 2010.
