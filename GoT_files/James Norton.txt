'''James Norton''' is a British actor who will be playing [[Ormund Hightower]] in [[House of the Dragon: Season 3|''House of the Dragon'': Season 3]].{{Webref|name=JN |author=Joe Otterson |date=January 31, 2025 |url=https://variety.com/2025/tv/news/house-of-the-dragon-season-3-cast-james-norton-ormund-hightower-1236292789/ |title=‘House of the Dragon’ Season 3 Casts James Norton as Ormund Hightower (EXCLUSIVE) |website=Variety |access-date=February 1, 2025 }} Norton is best known for his roles in ''Happy Valley'' and ''Grantchester'', as well as ''McMafia'' and the 2016 miniseries adaptation of ''War & Peace'' as Andrei Bolkonsky. In the upcoming BBC historical drama series ''King and Conqueror'', Norton appears as Harold Godwinson opposite [[Nikolaj Coster-Waldau]] as William the Conqueror.
