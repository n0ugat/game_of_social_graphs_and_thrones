'''Ector Swann''' was the head of [[House Swann]] sometime around the early reign of [[Aerys II Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Ector Swann married [[Eleanor Swann]], who bore him at least one child, [[Arwen Swann]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He is referenced in the entry of [[Iain Umber]], his son-in-law, in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}
