'''David Birkbeck''' is an actor based in {{WPS|Northern Ireland}}. He has appeared in films such as ''{{WPS|Cherrybomb (film)|Cherrybomb}}'' and ''{{WPS|Five Minutes of Heaven}}''

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a Northman archer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]".
