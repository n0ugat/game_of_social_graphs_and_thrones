'''Mujja''' is a [[Summer Isles|Summer Islander]] resident of [[King's Landing]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Mujja is a drinking companion of [[Ulf]] and [[Cley]].{{Ref|HOTD203}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Mujja and Cley are drinking with Ulf while the latter tells a [[Dornishmen|Dornish]] merchant about his supposed [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|Targaryen]] heritage.{{Ref|HOTD203}}
[[File:2x3 Ulf Still.jpg|thumb|Ulf, Mujja and Cley at the Cock Inn.]]
When [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] sends out a call to all [[dragonseed]]s in [[King's Landing]], Mujja and Cley urge Ulf on to go to [[Dragonstone]] and claim a [[dragon]].{{Ref|HOTD207}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Mujja.
