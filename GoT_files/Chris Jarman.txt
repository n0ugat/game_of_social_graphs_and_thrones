'''Chris Jarman''' is a British stage and television actor. He has appeared in the films ''{{WPS|Johnny English Reborn}}'' and ''{{WPS|The Lady of Heaven}}'' and the television series ''{{WPS|Grange Hill}}'', ''{{WPS|State of Play (TV series)|State of Play}}'', ''{{WPS|Spartacus (miniseries)|Spartacus}}'', ''{{WPS|Holby City}}'', ''{{WPS|The Bill}}'', ''{{WPS|The Omid Djalili Show}}'', ''{{WPS|Doctor Who}}'', and ''{{WPS|Thunderbirds Are Go (TV series)|Thunderbirds Are Go}}''. He also portrayed {{WPS|Rubeus Hagrid}} in the stage play ''{{WPS|Harry Potter and the Cursed Child}}'' and voiced {{WPS|Nick Fury (Ultimate Marvel character)|Nick Fury}} in the video game ''{{WPS|Lego Marvel Super Heroes 2}}''.
