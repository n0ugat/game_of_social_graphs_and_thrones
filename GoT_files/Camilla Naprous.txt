'''Camilla Naprous''' is a French horse master and stuntwoman. She is the daughter of [[Gerard Naprous]] and the sister of [[Daniel Naprous]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she has been a horse master in every episode of the series to date except for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episode "[[The Bear and the Maiden Fair (episode)|The Bear and the Maiden Fair]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[The Mountain and the Viper]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[The Bells]]" and "[[The Iron Throne]]".
