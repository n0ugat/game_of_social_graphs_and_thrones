Ser '''Eddard Waters''', also known as '''Red Ned''', is a [[bastard]] [[hedge knight]] from the [[Crownlands]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]], who briefly served as a member of King [[Aegon II Targaryen]]'s [[Kingsguard/House of the Dragon|Kingsguard]].

==Biography==
Eddard is currently in the service of King [[Aegon II Targaryen]].<ref name="WBD" />

==Behind the scenes==
Eddard Waters's character description reads as follows:
:''"A bastard hedge knight serving King Aegon II."''<ref name="WBD" />

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Eddard Waters. "Waters" is the surname used for noble-born bastards from the crownlands.
