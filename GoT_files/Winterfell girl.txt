A '''girl''' is present at [[Winterfell]] during the celebration of the victory in the [[Battle of Winterfell]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
She is sitting at a table across from [[Podrick Payne|Pod]], when the two of them see each other and share a smile. They later approach [[Sarra]], who has been rejected by the [[Sandor Clegane|Hound]]. Pod charms Sarra, and the three of them leave the hall together.{{Ref|GOT804}}
