Ser '''Leon Estermont''' was a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Estermont]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]], who briefly served as a member of King [[Aegon II Targaryen]]'s [[Kingsguard/House of the Dragon|Kingsguard]].

==Biography==
Leon is a friend and flatterer of King [[Aegon II Targaryen]].<ref name="WBD" />

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Leon is killed during a [[riot in King's Landing]].{{Ref|HOTD206}}

==Behind the scenes==
Leon Estermont's character description reads as follows:
:''"A knight. Friend and flatterer of King Aegon II."''<ref name="WBD" />

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Leon Estermont.
