'''Daniel Sackheim''' is an Emmy-winning director. He is previously known for his work on ''The X-Files'' and ''The Americans''. He joined ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' in [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|Season 6]] to direct the [[Oathbreaker|third]] and [[Book of the Stranger|fourth]] episodes.
