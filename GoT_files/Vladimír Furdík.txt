| twitter= https://twitter.com/VladimirFurdik/}}'''Vladimír 'Furdo' Furdík''' (born 1 July, 1970) is a Slovak actor and stuntman.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed the [[Night King]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[The Door]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[Dragonstone (episode)|Dragonstone]]", "[[Eastwatch]]", "[[Beyond the Wall (episode)|Beyond the Wall]]", and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]", and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Long Night]]". He took over the role from [[Richard Brake]] for undisclosed reasons.

He was also a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[Sons of the Harpy (episode)|Sons of the Harpy]]" and "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[The Red Woman]]", "[[Oathbreaker]]", and "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]", and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[Dragonstone (episode)|Dragonstone]]", "[[Stormborn]]", "[[The Queen's Justice]]", "[[The Spoils of War]]", "[[Eastwatch]]", "[[Beyond the Wall (episode)|Beyond the Wall]]", and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]" and [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]", and a stunt supervisor in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[Home]]" and "[[The Winds of Winter]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]" and a stunt safety technician in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eight season]] episode "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]"

Since, 1990 Furdík has worked in Hollywood as an actor and stuntman, appearing in films such as ''{{WPS|Tristan & Isolde (film)|Tristan and Isolde}}'', ''{{WPS|Eragon (film)|Eragon}}'', ''{{WPS|War Horse (film)|War Horse}}'', ''{{WPS|Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time (film)|Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time}}'' and ''{{WPS|Skyfall}}'' as a stuntman. He also appeared as a Andrzej in ''{{WPS|Sherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows}}'' and as one of the four ship guardians in ''{{WPS|Prometheus (film)|Prometheus}}''.
