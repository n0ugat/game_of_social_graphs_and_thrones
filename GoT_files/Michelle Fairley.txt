'''Michelle Fairley''' is a Northern Irish actress. She has a lengthy resume of British television productions, including a well-received turn as the alcoholic mother of [[wikipedia:George Best|George Best]] in the drama ''[[wikipedia:Best: His Mother's Son|Best: His Mother's Son]]''. She appeared as the mother of [[wikipedia:Hermione Granger|Hermione Granger]] in the final two ''[[wikipedia:Harry Potter|Harry Potter]]'' movies.

She stars in [[HBO]]'s ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' as [[Catelyn Stark]]. She replaced [[Jennifer Ehle]], who had departed the project following amicable discussions with HBO. Ehle's departure and Fairley's casting were announced simultaneously on 19 March 2010. She was cast for the show after being seen as "Lady Macbeth" in a play opposite Ewan Mcgregor. She was part of the initial starring cast and remains a member of the starring cast for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third]] seasons.

Fairley and actor [[wikipedia:Keith Allen (actor)|Keith Allen]], co-star [[Alfie Allen]]'s real life father, previously played a married couple in the film ''[[wikipedia:The Others (2001 film)|The Others]]''.
