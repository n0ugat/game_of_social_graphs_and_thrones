King '''Royce IV Bolton''',{{Con|TWOI&F}} also known as '''Royce Redarm''', was a [[Red King]] and head of [[House Bolton]].

==Biography==
Royce earned the nickname "the Redarm" due to his fondness of plunging his arm into the bellies of captive [[House Stark|Starks]] and removing their entrails with his bare hand.{{Ref|H&L609}}

==In the books==
In ''The World of Ice & Fire'', Royce IV Bolton succeeded in burning [[Winterfell]] during one of the wars between [[House Bolton]] and [[House Stark]], which his namesake Royce II Bolton had done three hundred years earlier.
