'''Magnar'''{{Ref|GOT502}}{{Note|[[Common Tongue]]: ''Lord''}} is a word in the [[Old Tongue]] of the [[First Men]] and also the title held by the leader of the [[Thenns|Thenn]], a tribe of the [[Free Folk]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, "Magnar" is also the name of a noble house established at Kinghouse, in the island of [[Skagos]].

===Known Magnars===
*{[[Styr]]}. Killed at the [[Battle for the Wall]]
*Sigorn, son of Styr. The current Magnar of the Thenns
