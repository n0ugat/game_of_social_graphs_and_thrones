'''Kerr Logan''' is a British actor, born in Bangor, Northern Ireland, raised in Lancashire, England and based in London, England.<ref>Muir, Marie-Louise (August 7, 2010). [http://www.bbc.co.uk/blogs/artsextra/2010/08/object-width640-height385param.shtml Vincent River Irish premier] BBC.</ref> He was trained at RADA and ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' is his first television role.

In ''Game of Thrones,'' Logan portrayed the role of [[Matthos Seaworth]]. Word of his casting leaked on 7 July 2011, but was not officially confirmed until 2 August. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
