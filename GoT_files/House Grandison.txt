'''House Grandison'''{{Ref|GOT806}}{{Note|Harlan's surname is Grandison.}} of [[Grandview]] is a vassal house from the [[Stormlands]] that holds fealty to [[House Baratheon]]. Their sigil is a black sleeping [[lion]] on a yellow field.

==History==
House Grandison was one of the three loyalist houses that defied their liege lord, [[Robert Baratheon]], at the beginnings of [[Robert's Rebellion]]. Along with two other noble houses, House Grandison was defeated at the [[Battle of Summerhall]] in the course of a single day.{{Ref|H&L314}}

==Members==
*Ser {[[Harlan Grandison]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] of the [[Kingsguard]] of [[Aerys II Targaryen]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Grandison is a vassal house sworn to [[Storm's End]]. Their seat is Grandview and their words are "Rouse Me Not". 

Ser Harlan Grandison, an old knight, served in the [[Kingsguard]] of [[Aerys II Targaryen|Aerys II]]. He died in his sleep at the [[White Sword Tower]], and was replaced by [[Jaime Lannister]]. 

The current head of the family is Lord Hugh Grandison, an old man dubbed Greybeard.
