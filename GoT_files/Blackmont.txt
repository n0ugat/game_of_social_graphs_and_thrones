'''Blackmont'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in [[Dorne]], the region controlled by [[House Martell]] of [[Sunspear]]. The castle is located in the far west of Dorne near the border with the Reach. It is north of [[Starfall]] and east of [[Skyreach]], in the foothills of the [[Red Mountains]]. It is the seat of the eponymous [[House Blackmont]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the location of Blackmont is the same.
