'''Dirah''' is a [[Prostitution|prostitute]] in [[King's Landing]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
Along with [[Marei]] and [[Craya]], Dirah is hired by [[Bronn]]. They lament that some of their favorite boys were killed by [[Drogon]] in the [[Battle of the Goldroad]]. As Marei mounts Bronn, Dirah is told by Craya that a soldier named [[Eddie]] survived the battle but returned to [[King's Landing]] with his face burned off, leaving him without eyelids, to which Dirah asks how he sleeps without eyelids. The encounter comes to an early end when [[Qyburn]] enters and informs Bronn that he has an urgent request from [[Cersei Lannister|Cersei]].{{Ref|GOT801}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character with this name.
