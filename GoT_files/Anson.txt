'''Anson''' was a [[Order of Maesters|maester]] of the [[Citadel]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
Anson is mentioned in a book about [[dragonglass]] that [[Samwell Tarly|Sam]] is reading.{{Ref|GOT701}}

==In the books==
In ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', Anson wrote the book ''Truth'', in which he challenged the idea that [[dragon]]s could change sex at need, calling it a misunderstanding of a metaphor used by [[Septon]] [[Barth]].
