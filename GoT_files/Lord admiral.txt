'''Lord admiral'''{{Ref|H&L806}} is a title in the [[Seven Kingdoms]] sometimes given to the head of the [[royal fleet]].

==History==
[[Daemon Velaryon]] was the lord admiral during the reigns of [[Maegor Targaryen|Maegor]] and [[Jaehaerys I Targaryen|Jaehaerys]].{{Ref|H&L806}} His grandson, [[Corlys Velaryon|Corlys]], served as lord admiral under Jaehaerys and [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].{{Videoref|BTD}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the position of lord admiral is most often given to the [[Master of Ships|master of ships]].

The current lord admiral is [[Paxter Redwyne]].
