'''Samwell''' is a court minstrel in service to [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]] at the [[Red Keep]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Samwell plays the lute and sings a [[Under the dragon's eye|song]] whilst [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] read ''[[Ten Thousand Ships (book)|Ten Thousand Ships]]'' in the [[godswood]]. Queen [[Alicent Hightower|Alicent]] then arrives and sends him away.{{Ref|HOTD103}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', this character has no counterpart.
