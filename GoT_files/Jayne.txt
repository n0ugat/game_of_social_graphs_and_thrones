'''Jayne''' is an agent of [[Mysaria]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Jayne works for Mysaria in [[King's Landing]].{{Ref|HOTD109}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
At a child fighting pit in [[Flea Bottom]], Jayne approaches [[Arryk Cargyll|Arryk]] and [[Erryk Cargyll]] on behalf of Mysaria to help them find [[Aegon II Targaryen|Prince Aegon]].{{Ref|HOTD109}}

==In the books==
