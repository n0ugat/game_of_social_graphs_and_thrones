Lord '''Marlyn Dormand''' is a [[Northmen|northern]] [[Lordship|lord]] and the head of [[House Dormand]]. He is a bannerman of [[House Stark]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
During a war council meeting at [[Harrenhal]], [[Tywin Lannister|Tywin]] berates his bannerman [[Amory Lorch]] for his illiteracy; he sent a letter detailing [[House Lannister]]'s infantry movements to Lord [[Damon Marbrand]], but Lorch accidentally delivered it to Lord Marlyn due to his reading disorder.{{Ref|GOT206}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of any character named Marlyn Dormand or House Dormand. One of [[Tormund]]'s sons is named Dormund. He is slain by one of [[Stannis Baratheon]]'s knights during the [[battle for the Wall]].
