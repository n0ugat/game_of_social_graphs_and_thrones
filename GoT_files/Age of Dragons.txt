{{Quote|We honor the crown's oldest and fiercest ally, House Velaryon. Reaching back to the days of Old Valyria and the Age of Dragons.|Viserys I Targaryen|We Light the Way}}
The '''Age of Dragons'''{{Ref|HOTD105}} was a golden age in the [[Valyrian Freehold]]. Its exact placement in history is unknown.

==History==
===Background===
The [[House Targaryen|Targaryens]] and [[House Velaryon|Velaryons]] became close allies during the Age of Dragons.{{Ref|HOTD105}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
At [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] and [[Laenor Velaryon|Laenor]]'s wedding feast, [[Viserys I Targaryen]] expresses his desire to create a second Age of Dragons in [[Westeros]].{{Ref|HOTD105}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', no mention is made of the "Age of Dragons."
