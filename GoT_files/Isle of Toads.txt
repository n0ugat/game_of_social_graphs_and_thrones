==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the people of the Isle of Toads have fishlike facial features and webbed hands and feet. Among other remnants of a mysterious ancient civilization on the island is the Toad Stone, forty feet tall and made of a greasy [[black stone]].
