A '''Dothraki crone''' is a member of the ''[[Dosh Khaleen]]'', the widows of deceased ''[[Khal|khals]]'' who preside over [[Dothraki]] religious rituals.

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
[[File:Dosh khaleen and Daenerys.jpg|thumb|The Dothraki crone with ''Khaleesi'' Daenerys Targaryen.]]
She presides over a ceremony to predict the future of [[Daenerys Targaryen]]'s [[Rhaego|unborn child]], and declares that he will be the [[stallion who mounts the world]].{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the lead member of the ''dosh khaleen'', who gives the prophecy about Rhaego being the stallion who mounts the world, has only one eye.
