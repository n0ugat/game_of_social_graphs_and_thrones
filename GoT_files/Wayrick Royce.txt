'''Wayrick Royce''' was a member of [[House Royce]] and a brother of the [[Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
Wayrick Royce was the secondborn son of [[Donal Royce]] and [[Priscilla Royce]]. At the age of eighteen, he joined the [[Night's Watch]].<ref name = "s1prop"/>

==Behind the scenes==
Wayrick Royce is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Wayrick Royce is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
