'''Steffon Baratheon''' was an early member of [[House Baratheon]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Steffon was the son of [[Serac Baratheon|Serac]] and [[Monica Baratheon]].{{Ref|GOT106}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He is mentioned in his father's entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the only character named [[Steffon Baratheon]] is the father of [[Robert Baratheon|Robert]], [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]], and [[Renly Baratheon|Renly]].
