'''Haystack Hall'''{{Ref|GOT201}} is a castle in the [[Stormlands]]. It is the seat of [[House Errol]], located northeast of [[Bronzegate]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
[[Daisy]], a newly acquired prostitute at Lord [[Petyr Baelish]]'s brothel, was originally from Haystack Hall.<ref>"[[The North Remembers]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the books, Haystack Hall is the seat of House Errol. It is located to the east of the [[Kingsroad]] and north of [[Storm's End]].
