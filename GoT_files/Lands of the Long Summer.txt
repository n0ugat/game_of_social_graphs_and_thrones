The '''Lands of the Long Summer'''<ref>[https://dedalvs.com/work/house-of-the-dragon/misc/map_request_v2.pdf Map request, version 2 | dedalvs.com]</ref>{{Note|[[High Valyrian]]: ''Bōso Jaedo Tegun''}} are a region on the [[Valyrian peninsula]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Lands of the Long Summer were the heartlands of the [[Valyrian Freehold]].{{Bookref|TWOI&F|2|8}}{{Bookref|TWOI&F|8|2}} [[Aenar Targaryen]] owned holdings there, but sold them when he moved to [[Dragonstone]] to escape the coming [[Doom of Valyria|Doom]].{{Bookref|TWOI&F|3|1}} Before the Doom, they were the most fertile lands in the [[known world]], but since then they have become scorched, drowned, and blighted.{{Bookref|ADWD|33}}
