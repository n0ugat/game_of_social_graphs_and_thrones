'''Asabhad'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is a city in the western provinces of [[Yi Ti]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Asabhad is situated on a trade route between [[Qarth]] and [[Yin]]. It is a starting point for trading caravans making their way to the Mountains of the Morn.
