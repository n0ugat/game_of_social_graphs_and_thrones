'''Matthar''' is a newly made [[Ranger]] of the [[Night's Watch]], of the same group of recruits as [[Jon Snow]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Matthar is present during the speech [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch|Lord Commander]] [[Jeor Mormont]] gave to the "graduating" recruits. Matthar is assigned to the Rangers.{{Ref|GOT107}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Matthar is also called Matt. Similarly to the show, he is assigned to the Rangers. 

Like [[Grenn]] and [[Pypar|Pyp]], Matthar becomes friends with Jon. He is one of the friends who stop Jon from abandoning the Watch.

According to the appendices of the novels, Matthar is stationed at Castle Black.
