'''Oliver Ford Davies''' (born 12 August 1939) is a British actor experienced with roles on both stage and screen. He is best known for his role as Sio Bibble in the three ''[[wikipedia:Star Wars|Star Wars]]'' prequel movies, but has roles in many films such as ''[[wikipedia:Sense and Sensibility (film)|Sense and Sensibility]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Mrs. Brown|Mrs. Brown]]''. He has a successful stage career and won a [[wikipedia:Laurence Olivier Award|Laurence Olivier Award]] in 1990 for his role in the play ''[[wikipedia:Racing Demon (play)|Racing Demon]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Davies played the role of Maester [[Cressen]]. He was announced in the role on 19 July 2011 by [[George R.R. Martin]]. He only appeared as a guest star in the [[The North Remembers|first episode]] of the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
