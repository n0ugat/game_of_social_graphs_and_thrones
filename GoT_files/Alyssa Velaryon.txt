Queen '''Alyssa Velaryon''' was the wife of King [[Aenys Targaryen|Aenys I Targaryen]].{{Ref|THTDB202}}

==Biography==
Alyssa was the sister of Lord [[Daemon Velaryon]].{{Con|F&B}} She had 6 children with King Aenys: [[Rhaena Targaryen (daughter of Aenys)|Rhaena]], [[Aegon Targaryen (son of Aenys)|Aegon]], [[Jaehaerys I Targaryen|Jaehaerys]], [[Alysanne Targaryen|Alysanne]], [[Viserys Targaryen (son of Aenys)|Viserys]], and [[Vaella Targaryen|Vaella]].{{Ref|THTDB202}}

==Family==
{{Velaryon tree}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Alyssa served as queen [[regent]] during the early reign of her son Jaehaerys. She remarried to Lord Rogar Baratheon, with whom she had two children, [[Boremund Baratheon|Boremund]] and [[Jocelyn Baratheon]].

Alyssa's granddaughter, [[Alyssa Targaryen]], was named after her.
