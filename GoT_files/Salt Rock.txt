'''Salt Rock'''{{Ref|GOT210}} is a town in [[Westeros]]. They have a local river market.<ref name="E20">"[[Valar Morghulis]]"</ref>

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
[[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]] and [[Brienne of Tarth|Brienne]] encounter a patrol of [[House Stark|Stark]] [[Man-at-arms|men-at-arms]]. [[Tom]], the leader of the group, suspects he has seen Jaime before, and inquires whether he has been to the river market at Salt Rock - which Jaime denies, falsely implying he has never left [[Ashemark]] prior.{{Ref|GOT210}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no town of this name.
