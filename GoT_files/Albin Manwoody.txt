King '''Albin Manwoody'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} was the last king of [[House Manwoody]].

==Biography==
During [[Nymeria's War]], Albin was defeated by [[Nymeria]], who had him sent to the [[Wall]] in golden chains.{{Ref|H&L509}}

==In the books==
Albin Manwoody is mentioned in ''The World of Ice & Fire''. He was a troublesome madman and claimed dominion over the [[Red Mountains]].
