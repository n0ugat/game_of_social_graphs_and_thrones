'''Nathalie Joanne Emmanuel''' (born 2 March 1989) is an English actress, best known for her former regular role on the soap opera ''[[wikipedia:Hollyoaks|Hollyoaks]]''.

Via Twitter, Emmanuel revealed she would be working on the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] of ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. She tweeted a picture of a the front page of the script for the sixth episode of the season. At the San Diego Comic-Con on 13 July 2012, it was confirmed that she would be playing the role of [[Missandei]].<ref>[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rpSDSgChsaI HBO's YouTube announcement]</ref>

Emmanuel joined the TV series as a recurring cast member in Season 3, and continued as a recurring cast member into Season 4. Emmanuel was later promoted to a starring cast member for seasons 5 and 6.

Emmanuel first caught Benioff and Weiss's eyes when they saw her work in ''Misfits''.<ref>[http://www.denofgeek.us/tv/game-of-thrones/243771/67-things-you-didnt-know-about-game-of-thrones-season-4 ]</ref>

Nathalie has starred in ''[[Wikipedia:Furious 7|Furious 7]]'', the seventh installment in the ''[[Wikipedia:The Fast and the Furious|The Fast and the Furious]]'' franchise as well in the recent installment The Fate of the Furious where she reprises her role as the computer hacker, Ramsey.

In addition, Nathalie has also been featured in the minor role of Harriet alongside costar [[Thomas Brodie-Sangster]] in ''[[w:c:mazerunner:Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials|Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials]]'' and its sequel ''[[w:c:mazerunner:Maze Runner: The Death Cure|The Death Cure]]''.
