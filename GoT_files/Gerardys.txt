'''Gerardys''' is a [[Order of Maesters|maester]] of the [[Citadel]] in service to [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] at [[Dragonstone]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Gerardys is Rhaenyra's household maester.{{Guideref|Maester Gerardys}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Gerardys witnesses the wedding of Rhaenyra and [[Daemon Targaryen]].{{Ref|HOTD107}}

Six years later, Gerardys tutors [[Jacaerys Velaryon]] in [[High Valyrian]] by reading to him the history of [[Aegon's Conquest]] in that language.{{Ref|HOTD108}}

Maester Gerardys attends Rhaenyra as goes into labor pains, he comforts [[Lucerys Velaryon|Lucerys]], when Rhaenyra tells him and his [[Jacaerys Velaryon|brother]] of the death of their grandfather and the [[coup in King's Landing]]. Later, Gerardys watches the funeral of [[Visenya Targaryen (daughter of Rhaenyra)|Visenya]], he kneels after Rhaenyra is crowned as Queen. At the [[Black Council]], Maester Geradys gathers around the [[Painted Table]], he informs Queen Rhaenyra about the support of Houses [[House Celtigar|Celtigar]], [[House Massey|Massey]], [[House Darklyn|Darklyn]], [[House Staunton|Staunton]], and [[House Bar Emmon|Bar Emmon]]. Geradys proposes, with the acquiescence of [[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]], to send a [[raven]] to Lord [[Grover Tully]], as [[Riverrun]] was an old friend of her [[Viserys I Targaryen|father]], to gain the support of the [[Riverlands]].{{Ref|HOTD110}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Gerardys is present at the [[Black Council]] when Rhaenyra returns from the [[Stormlands]] and the funeral of [[Lucerys Velaryon]].{{Ref|HOTD201}}
