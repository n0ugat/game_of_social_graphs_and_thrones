'''Weyland''' was a [[Order of Maesters|maester]] of the [[Citadel]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
Weyland's body was examined following his death by [[Archmaester]] [[Ebrose]] and [[Samwell Tarly]], who removed his organs for inspection. Ebrose suspected that Weyland's excessive drinking had been the cause of his death.{{Ref|GOT701}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Weyland.
