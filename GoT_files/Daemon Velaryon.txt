Lord '''Daemon Velaryon'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} was the head of [[House Velaryon]], [[Lord of the Tides]], and [[lord admiral]] during the reigns of [[Maegor Targaryen|Maegor]] and [[Jaehaerys I Targaryen|Jaehaerys]].

==Biography==
Lord Velaryon served King Maegor, until the realm [[Jaehaerys Targaryen's uprising|rose up]] against him in favor of the intelligent young Prince Jaehaerys in 48 AC. Daemon gave the [[royal fleet]] to Jaehaerys.{{Ref|H&L806}}

==Family==
{{Velaryon tree}}

==In the books==
In ''The World of Ice & Fire'' and ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Daemon served as [[Hand of the King|Hand]] to King Jaehaerys after the dismissal of Rogar Baratheon. He resigned following the death of his sister, Queen Alyssa Velaryon, and the murder of his niece, Lianna Velaryon. Daemon outlived all of his sons and was succeeded by [[Corlys Velaryon]], the son of his eldest son Corwyn.
