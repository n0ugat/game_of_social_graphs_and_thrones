'''House Marsh'''{{Ref|GOT107}}{{Note|Bowen's surname is Marsh.}} is a vassal house from the [[Neck]]. They are sworn to [[House Reed]] of [[Greywater Watch]].

==Members==
*{[[Bowen Marsh]]}, former [[First Steward]] of the [[Night's Watch]]. Executed for [[Mutiny at Castle Black|mutinying]] against [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch|Lord Commander]] [[Jon Snow]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Marsh are crannogmen sworn to House Reed. Their sigil is ten green frogs, 4-3-2-1, on yellow.

Bowen Marsh is the only member of the house is mentioned in the novels.
