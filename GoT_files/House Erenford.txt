'''House Erenford'''{{Guideref|Viewer's Guide}}{{Note|Joyeuse Frey's maiden name is Erenford.}} is a knightly house of the [[Riverlands]]. They are sworn to [[House Frey]] of the [[Twins]].

==Known members==
*Lady {[[Joyeuse Frey]]}, the eighth wife of Lord [[Walder Frey]].{{Guideref|Viewer's Guide}} Killed by [[Catelyn Stark]] during the [[Red Wedding]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Erenford is a lesser knightly house sworn to House Frey. Their sigil is a golden heron, beak and legs black, standing with a silver fish in its beak, on pink.
