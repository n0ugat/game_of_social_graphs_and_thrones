'''House Crane'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} of [[Red Lake]] is a [[noble house]] of the [[Reach]]. Their sigil displays a chevron of five gold cranes on a pale blue field.<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref>{{Note|name=Colors}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Crane was founded by Rose of Red Lake, a daughter of Garth Greenhand, the legendary High King of the [[First Men]]. The women of House Crane are said to be [[Warg|skinchangers]] with the ability to control cranes.{{Bookref|TWOI&F|7|7}}

House Crane joined the [[Reach|Kingdom of the Reach]] during the reign of King Garth III Gardener.{{Bookref|TWOI&F|7|7|2}} Their seat, Red Lake, was the site of a battle during a [[House Lannister|Lannister]] invasion of the Reach.{{Bookref|TWOI&F|7|6}}

The Cranes are a part of [[Renly Baratheon]]'s army during the [[War of the Five Kings]].{{Bookref|ACOK|22}} Ser Parmen Crane joins Renly's [[Kingsguard#Renly Baratheon's Kingsguard|Rainbow Guard]] as "Parmen the Purple."{{Bookref|ACOK|Appendix}} After Renly's death, he joins [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]],{{Bookref|ACOK|42}} though other members of House Crane are present at the royal court in [[King's Landing]].{{Bookref|ASOS|16}}
