====Notes====
*First season "co-starring" cast member promoted to the starring cast for the second season is [[Kurt Egyiawan]].
*New cast members joining the starring cast for the second season are [[Freddie Fox]], [[Gayle Rankin]], [[Tom Bennett]], [[Kieran Bew]], [[Ellora Torchia]], [[Abubakar Salim]], [[Clinton Liberty]], [[Tom Taylor]], and [[Simon Russell Beale]]
*D'Arcy is credited ahead of Cooke for the first time.
*Beale receives the "and" modifier, the second actor in the series to do so, after Ifans.
*Alcock returns, credited as "guest starring" for the episode she appears in.
