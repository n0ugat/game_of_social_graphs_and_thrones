'''House Moore'''{{Ref|GOT210}}{{Note|Mandon's surname is Moore.}} is a vassal house from the [[Vale of Arryn|Vale]]. It holds fealty to [[House Arryn]].

==Members==
* Ser {[[Mandon Moore]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] of the [[Kingsguard]]. Killed by [[Podrick Payne]] during the [[Battle of the Blackwater]] for attempting to murder [[Tyrion Lannister]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Moore is a vassal House sworn to the [[Eyrie]]. Their sigil is three bronze spearheads on white with a bronze border embattled.
