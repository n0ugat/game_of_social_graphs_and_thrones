'''''Game of Thrones: A Pop-Up Guide to Westeros''''' is a book inspired by the ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' TV series, published on April 10, 2014. The book is 10 pages long.<ref>[https://www.amazon.co.uk/Game-Thrones-Pop-up-Guide-Westeros/dp/0593073452 Amazon]</ref>

The book features pop-up recreations of the key locations that appear in the opening title sequence in every episode of the series. These are displayed in five spreads that "fold out to create a remarkable pop-up map of [[Westeros]]." There are also mini pop-ups featuring "iconic elements" including the [[White Walkers]] and [[dragon]]s, with text detailing the "history of the [[Seven Kingdoms]] and beyond." Pop-ups of locations include the castle of [[Winterfell]], [[King's Landing]], and the [[Wall]].
