'''Robert Sterne''' is a casting director and actor. He has worked with [[HBO]] on the series ''Rome'' and ''John Adams''. He was a casting associate for the ''Red Riding'' miniseries (which starred [[Sean Bean]]). He was the casting director for the British comedies ''Free Agents'', ''Threesome'' and ''Friday Night Dinner'' and a casting associate for the drama miniseries ''The Crimson Petal and the White'', ''Any Human Heart'' and ''The Devil's Whore''.

He is a casting director for ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', along with [[Nina Gold]]. He also guest stars as the [[Royal Steward]] in [[King's Landing]].

Robert Sterne won the Outstanding Casting for a Drama Series 2015 Emmy Award for [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] of ''Game of Thrones.<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/game-of-thrones-breaks-the-record-for-most-emmy-wins-in-a-year/ ]</ref>''
