'''Rusalia Egen''' was the the wife of [[Xander Egen]] and mother of [[Elena Egen]], through her being the grandmother of [[Yohn Royce]].

==Biography==
Rusalia married Lord [[Xander Egen]], and bore him at least one daugher, [[Elena Egen]].<ref name = "s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Rusalia Egen is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Rusalia Egen is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
