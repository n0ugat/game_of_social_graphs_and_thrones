'''Miltos Yerolemou''' is an actor. He is a Greek Cypriot based in London.<ref name="Interview of Miltos Yerolemou"> [http://mywalkoffame.wordpress.com/2013/05/10/conversation-with-miltos-yerolemou/ Conversation with Miltos Yerolemou]</ref> He has a number of theatrical and television appearances to his name, most notably in the British comedy series ''[[wikipedia:Absolutely Fabulous|Absolutely Fabulous]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Black Books|Black Books]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:My Family|My Family]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Yerolemou played [[Syrio Forel]]. He confirmed he had been cast in the role via Twitter on 6 July 2010. He portrays this character with an accent "a little Greek, a little Italian and a little Spanish, and then pushed it East into Persia".<ref name="Interview of Miltos Yerolemou"/>
