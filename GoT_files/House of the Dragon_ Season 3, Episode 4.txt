'''''House of the Dragon'': Season 3, Episode 4''' is the fourth episode of the [[House of the Dragon: Season 3|third season]] of ''[[House of the Dragon]]''. It is the twenty-second episode of the series overall. It will premiere in 2026 on [[HBO]] and [[HBO Max]]. It will be directed by [[Clare Kilner]].
