'''Thomas Wlaschiha''' (born 20 June, 1973) is a German actor. His ancestors were ethnic Czechs who came to Germany around the beginning of the 19th Century, hence his Czech-sounding surname. He has appeared in films including ''[[wikipedia:Enemy at the Gates (film)|Enemy at the Gates]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Munich (film)|Munich]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Brideshead Revisited (film)|Brideshead Revisited]],'' and ''[[wikipedia:Valkyrie (film)|Valkyrie]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he portrayed the role of the [[Faceless Men|Faceless Man]] [[Jaqen H'ghar]] in [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|Season 2]]. He was confirmed in the role on 3 August 2010.<ref>[http://winter-is-coming.net/2011/08/exclusive-tom-wlaschiha-is-jaqen-hghar/ Winter is Coming]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].

In [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|Season 6]] Tom reappears as Jaqen.<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2014/06/23/familiar-face-returning-game-thrones-season-5/]</ref> Since then, he is credited as a starring cast member. He also provides Jaqen's voice for the German dub.
