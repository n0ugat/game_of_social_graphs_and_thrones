'''Skane'''{{Mapref|Viewer's Guide}} is an island in the [[North]]. It is located off the north-eastern coast of [[Westeros]], in the [[Bay of Seals]]. It is located to the north of its larger sister island, [[Skagos]], making it the northernmost land in the [[North|Kingdom of the North]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Skane is noted as being uninhabited. Skane was raided long ago by the Skagosi, with its inhabitants supposedly killed and [[Cannibalism|eaten]].
