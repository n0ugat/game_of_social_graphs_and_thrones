'''Jamie Metcalfe Campbell Bower''' (born 22 November 1988) is an English actor, musician, and model. He is arguably best known for his role as Caius in ''[[wikipedia:The Twilight Saga: New Moon|The Twilight Saga: New Moon]]'' also as Number 11-12 in ''[[wikipedia:The Prisoner (2009 miniseries)|The Prisoner]]'', and in the up-and-coming City of Bones film as semi-protaginist, Jace Wayland. He portrayed [[wikipedia:Gellert Grindelwald|Gellert Grindelwald]] in ''{{WPS|Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1}}'' and ''{{WPS|Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald}}''.

[[File:Jamie as Waymar.jpg|thumb|left|Bower as [[Waymar Royce]] in the unaired pilot]]
He portrayed [[Lann the Clever]] in the [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]] of ''[[Bloodmoon]]''.{{Webref|name=Lann |author=Wake the Dragon |date=February 18, 2024 |url=https://twitter.com/WtDasoiaf/status/1759440092576235882 |title=Jamie Campbell Bower as Lann the Clever, a prisoner of House Stark and the ancestor of House Lannister! |website=X |access-date=February 19, 2024 }}

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Campbell Bower was supposed to be playing Ser [[Waymar Royce]]. He was confirmed in the role on 20 October 2009, and played in the unscreened [[Pilot (Game of Thrones)|pilot]]. However, he was replaced by [[Rob Ostlere]] for the series proper. Campbell Bower has gone on to play [[wikipedia:King Arthur|King Arthur]] in the drama series ''[[wikipedia:Camelot (TV series)|Camelot]]''.
