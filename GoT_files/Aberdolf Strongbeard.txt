'''Aberdolf Strongbeard'''{{Ref|tlw}} is a soldier, initially in service to [[House Baratheon of Dragonstone|House Baratheon]], and later to [[House Stark]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Aberdolf serves in [[Stannis Baratheon]]'s army. He witnesses the burning of [[Shireen Baratheon|Shireen]].{{Ref|GOT509}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
Having survived the [[battle in the ice]], Aberdolf fights in the [[Battle of the Bastards]], serving under [[Jon Snow]] to take the [[North]] back from [[House Bolton]].{{Ref|GOT609}} He later hails Jon as [[King in the North]], along with the Northern and the [[Vale of Arryn|Vale]] lords.{{Ref|GOT610}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
Aberdolf is among the soldiers who witness the surprise arrival of [[Bran Stark|Bran]] back to Winterfell, accompanied by [[Meera Reed]].{{Ref|GOT703}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
During the [[Great War]], Aberdolf serves as a guard at Winterfell while the Stark and [[House Targaryen|Targaryen]] forces prepare to fight the coming [[Wight|army of the dead]].{{Ref|GOT801}} He is disgusted upon seeing [[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]], who has come to fight for the living.{{Ref|GOT802}} He takes part in the castle's defense during the [[Battle of Winterfell]].{{Ref|GOT803}} Afterwards, he takes part in the mass funeral for those who fell in the battle.{{Ref|GOT804}}

Aberdolf later takes part in the [[Battle of King's Landing]].{{Ref|GOT805}} He accompanies Jon when he confronts [[Grey Worm]] about executing the defeated Lannister soldiers, and prepares to fight when the [[Unsullied]] ready their spears at him and the rest of Jon's men. The situation, however, is defused by [[Davos Seaworth|Davos]], and they proceed to the [[Red Keep]].{{Ref|GOT806}}

After the [[exile]] of Jon to the [[Night's Watch]] for [[assassination of Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys's murder]] and the election of Bran as the [[Lord of the Seven Kingdoms|Lord of the Six Kingdoms]], Aberdolf returns to Winterfell and stands guard during the coronation of [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]] as the [[King in the North|Queen in the North]].{{Ref|GOT806}}

==Behind the scenes==
Although never stated on-screen, the extra that portrays this character, [[Andrew McClay]], stated in the documentary ''[[Game of Thrones: The Last Watch]]'' that the name of his character, who had fought for Stannis and Jon Snow, was "Aberdolf Strongbeard".{{Ref|tlw}} The name "Aberdolf Strongbeard" is also used on his Twitter page with that spelling.<ref>https://twitter.com/AndyMcClayGoT1/ Twitter</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, this character has no equivalent. There are many northmen who join [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]'s army during his campaign against the [[House Bolton|Boltons]].
