'''Abulu'''<ref name="Book">[[:File:BTS Nymeria Book 2.jpg]]</ref> is an island in the [[Summer Sea]], part of the archipelago known as the [[Summer Isles]].

==History==
Abulu was visited by [[Nymeria]] and her ten thousand [[ships]] during the [[Rhoynar migration]]. Many of her followers chose to stay on the island, and were left behind when Nymeria continued to her final destination of [[Dorne]].<ref name="Book" />

==In the books==
In ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', Abulu is more commonly known as the Isle of Women. It is located northeast of Walano, the northernmost of the major Summer Isles.
