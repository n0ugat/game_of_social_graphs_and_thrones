His last TV credit was a role on television drama ''[[wikipedia:A Touch of Frost|A Touch of Frost]]'' in 1996.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he is playing the role of [[Tobho Mott]] and was confirmed in the role on 24 November 2010. He initially appeared as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and returned in this role for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
