==Biography==
===Background===
This child is an orphan living at an orphanage in [[King's Landing]].{{Ref|GOT301}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
The orphanage he is living at is visited by [[Margaery Tyrell]] as she passes through the streets of [[Flea Bottom]]. Margaery asks him if his father was a soldier, to which he replies yes. Through King [[Joffrey Baratheon|Joffrey]]'s reign, Margaery explains how the soldiers helped saved the city in the [[Battle of the Blackwater]].{{Ref|GOT301}}
