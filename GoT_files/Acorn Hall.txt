'''Acorn Hall'''{{Ref|GOT107}} is a castle in the [[Riverlands]], the region controlled by [[House Tully]] of [[Riverrun]]. The castle is located south east of Riverrun, near the hill [[High Heart]]. It is the seat of [[House Smallwood]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
[[Pypar]] mentions that he once sang at Acorn Hall.<ref>"[[You Win or You Die]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels Acorn Hall is the seat of [[House Smallwood]], a minor House sworn to House Vance of Wayfarer's Rest. It is an oaken keep surrounded by stone curtain walls.

In ''A Storm of Swords'', [[Arya Stark|Arya]], [[Gendry Baratheon|Gendry]], and several members of the [[Brotherhood Without Banners]] arrive at Acorn Hall, where Lady Smallwood welcomes them amiably.
