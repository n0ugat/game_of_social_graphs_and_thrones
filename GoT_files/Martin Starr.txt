}}'''Martin James Pflieger Schienle''' (born July 30, 1982), known professionally as '''Martin Starr''', is an American actor and comedian. He is best known for his roles as Bill Haverchuck in the short-lived television series ''{{WPS|Freaks and Geeks}}'', Bertram Gilfoyle in the [[HBO]] series ''{{WPS|Silicon Valley (TV series)|Silicon Valley}}'' and Mr. Harrington in the {{WPS|Marvel Cinematic Universe}} films ''{{WPS|The Incredible Hulk (film)|The Incredible Hulk}}'', ''{{WPS|Spider-Man: Homecoming}}'' and ''{{WPS|Spider-Man: Far From Home}}''.

He has also appeared in films such as ''{{WPS|Walk Hard: The Dewey Cox Story}}'', ''{{WPS|Knocked Up}}'', ''{{WPS|The Incredible Hulk (film)|The Incredible Hulk}}'', ''{{WPS|Adventureland (film)|Adventureland}}'', ''{{WPS|This Is the End}}'' and ''{{WPS|Amira & Sam}}'' and television series such as ''{{WPS|King of the Hill}}'', ''{{WPS|How I Met Your Mother}}'', ''{{WPS|Parks and Recreation}}'', ''{{WPS|New Girl}}'', ''{{WPS|The Goldbergs (2013 TV series)|The Goldbergs}}'', ''{{WPS|Bill Nye Saves the World}}'' and ''{{WPS|Future Man (TV series)|Future Man}}''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he made a cameo appearance as an Ironborn soldier in "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]".<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DkdbetJTZGA Game of Thrones | Season 8 Episode 1 | Game Revealed (HBO)]</ref>
