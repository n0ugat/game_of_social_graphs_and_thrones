|twitter = https://twitter.com/richardbrake}}'''Richard Brake''' (born 30 November 1964) is a British-American actor. He is best known for his portrayal of Joe Chill, the murderer of Bruce Wayne's parents, in ''Batman Begins'' (which also featured [[Jack Gleeson]]), as Corporal Portman in the film adaptation of ''Doom'' (which also featured [[DeObia Oparei]]), and as Enrikas Dortlich in Hannibal Rising. He had a one-episode role in ''The Mandalorian'' season 2, portraying Valin Hess.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he previously played the [[Night King]].
