The '''River Gate''',{{Ref|HOTD103}} also known as the '''Mud Gate''',{{Ref|GOT208}} is a gate in the southern wall of [[King's Landing]] that opens at the mouth of the [[Blackwater Rush]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
On [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon]]'s second nameday, the baggage train for the royal hunt masses at the River Gate.{{Ref|HOTD103}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
[[Tyrion Lannister]] predicts that the Mud Gate will be the focal point of King [[Stannis Baratheon]]'s attack because it is weak and easy to land near.{{Ref|GOT208}}

It is where the [[Battle of the Blackwater]] mainly takes place, as Stannis and his forces attempt to take King's Landing through the gate, but are repelled by the city's defenders led by Tyrion and reinforcements from the [[House Lannister|Lannisters]] and the [[House Tyrell|Tyrells]].{{Ref|GOT209}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the River Gate is located near the center of the southeast wall of King's Landing.
