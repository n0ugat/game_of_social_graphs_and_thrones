'''House Greenleaf'''{{Ref|GOT104}}{{Note|Marya and Mickon's surname is Greenleaf.}} is a [[North]]ern noble house, vassals of [[House Umber]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Two members of House Greenleaf are recorded in the entry of [[Finn Umber]] in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==Members==
===Historical members===
*{[[Mickon Greenleaf]]}, a warden at the [[Last Hearth]]
**{[[Marya Greenleaf]]}, his daughter, who married {[[Finn Umber]]}

==In the books==
House Greenleaf is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
