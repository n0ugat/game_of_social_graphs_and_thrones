'''Wayfarer's Rest'''<ref>[[:File:Cersei Westeros Map Puzzle.jpg]]</ref> is a castle in the [[Riverlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Wayfarer's Rest is the seat of a branch of [[House Vance]]. Its name is a reference to Liane the Wayfarer from ''The Dying Earth'' by Jack Vance.

Captives from the [[Battle of Oxcross]], including Ser Lymond Vikary, are held at Wayfarer's Rest.
