"'''The Bear and the Maiden Fair'''" is the seventh short of the [[Game of Thrones: Inside the Episode: Season 3|third season]] of ''[[Game of Thrones: Inside the Episode]]''. It is the twenty-seventh short of the series overall. It was released on May 13, 2013.
