'''Mavis Royce''', née '''Belmore''', was a member of [[House Belmore]] and the wife of [[Maynard Royce]], the [[Lord of Runestone]]. She is the grandmother of [[Yohn Royce]].

==Biography==
Mavis Belmore married [[Maynard Royce]] and bore him two sons: [[Andar Royce|Andar]] and [[Paxter Royce|Paxter]], and two daughters: [[Junia Royce|Junia]] and [[Serena Royce|Serena]].<ref name = "s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Mavis Royce is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Mavis Royce is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
