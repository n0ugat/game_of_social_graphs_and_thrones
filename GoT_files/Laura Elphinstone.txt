|Appearances = "[[The Bells]]"}}'''Laura Elphinstone''' is an English actress who portrays [[Nora]] in [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|Season 8]]. She is known for roles such as Carolyn in ''Vera'', Helene "Nym" Demuth in ''National Theatre Live: Jane Eyre'', and Starkey / Ali in ''Peter and Wendy''.<ref>https://www.independenttalent.com/actors/laura-elphinstone/</ref> She has worked with fellow ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' actor [[Owen Teale]] ([[Alliser Thorne]]) in a revival of the play ''Rutherford and Son''.<ref>https://theatreweekly.com/first-look-rutherford-and-son-at-sheffield-theatres-in-rehearsal/</ref>
