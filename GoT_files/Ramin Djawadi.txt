'''Ramin Djawadi'''{{Note|Pronunciation: "''java-DEE''"}} (born July 19, 1974) is a Grammy and Emmy-nominated German-Iranian composer of music for film and television, and is the composer for ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' and its prequel series, ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.

==Career==
Djawadi is of German and Iranian descent. A protégé of [[wikipedia:Hans Zimmer|Hans Zimmer]], he produced individual pieces of music for ''[[wikipedia:The Time Machine (2002 film)|The Time Machine]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Basic (film)|Basic]]'', ''[[wikipedia:The Recruit|The Recruit]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl|Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl]]''. He collaborated with Zimmer on the music for ''[[wikipedia:Thunderbirds (film)|Thunderbirds]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Something's Gotta Give (film)|Something's Gotta Give]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Batman Begins|Batman Begins]]''.

Striking out on his own, Djawadi has produced the theme music for the movies ''[[wikipedia:Blade: Trinity|Blade: Trinity]]'', ''[[wikipedia:The Unborn (2009 film)|The Unborn]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Mr. Brooks|Mr. Brooks]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Deception (2008 film)|Deception]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Ask the Dust (film)|Ask the Dust]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Open Season (film)|Open Season]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Open Season 2|Open Season 2]]''. His highest-profile work in film has been providing the theme music for ''[[wikipedia:Iron Man (film)|Iron Man]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Clash of the Titans (2010 film)|Clash of the Titans]]''. In television he has provided the main title music for ''[[wikipedia:FlashForward|FlashForward]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Prison Break|Prison Break]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Person_of_Interest_(TV_series)|Person of Interest]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Westworld (TV series)|Westworld]]'', also having worked on ''[[wikipedia:Threshold (TV series)|Threshold]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Blade: The Series|Blade: The Series]]''. He has also worked in computer gaming, providing the soundtrack for [[wikipedia:Medal of Honor (2010 video game)|the 2010 reboot of the ''Medal of Honor'' series]].

==''Game of Thrones''==
[[Stephen Warbeck]] was the original composer during early production on ''Game of Thrones'' but he withdrew from the project on February 2, 2011, and none of his work subsequently appeared in the TV series.

Djawadi was then hired to be the TV series's composer, which he continued to work on from Season 1 through Season 8. In addition to many [[Themes and leitmotifs (Game of Thrones)|themes and leitmotifs]], Djawadi also composed the [[Themes and leitmotifs (Game of Thrones)#Game of Thrones main theme|main series theme]] used in the [[Opening credits (Game of Thrones)|opening credits]].

==''House of the Dragon''==
On February 1, 2021, it was confirmed that Djawadi would return as the composer for the ''Game of Thrones'' [[World of Westeros|successor show]], ''House of the Dragon''<ref>[https://ew.com/tv/house-of-the-dragon-ramin-djawadi/ Djawadi to compose for ''House of the Dragon'']</ref>.
