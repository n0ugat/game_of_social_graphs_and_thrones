|Last = "[[The Last of the Starks]]"}}'''Benjamin Lorton Crompton''' (born 1974) is a British actor. He has starred in the [[wikipedia:BBC|BBC]] sketch comedy ''[[wikipedia:Man Stroke Woman|Man Stroke Woman]]'', the sitcom ''[[wikipedia:Ideal (TV series)|Ideal]]'' and the films ''[[wikipedia:All or Nothing (film)|All or Nothing]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:102 Dalmatians|102 Dalmatians]]''. His debut role was a guest appearance in ''The Thin Blue Line'', a series which also featured fellow ''Game of Thrones'' actors [[Mark Addy]], [[Owen Teale]] and [[Rupert Vansittart]], as well as Kevin Allen, the uncle of [[Alfie Allen]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrays the role of [[Eddison Tollett]], a member of the [[Night's Watch]]. Crompton was confirmed in the role on August 9, 2011.<ref>[http://grrm.livejournal.com/232992.html?thread=14481440#t14481440 George R.R. Martin's blog]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
