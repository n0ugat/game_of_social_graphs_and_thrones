'''Mark Southworth''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]", "[[The Old Gods and the New]]", "[[A Man Without Honor]]", "[[The Prince of Winterfell]]" and "[[Valar Morghulis]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episodes "[[The Rains of Castamere (episode)|The Rains of Castamere]]" and "[[Mhysa]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[Two Swords]]".
