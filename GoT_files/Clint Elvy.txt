'''Clinton Elvy''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[The House of Black and White]]", "[[Sons of the Harpy (episode)|Sons of the Harpy]]", "[[Kill the Boy]]", "[[Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken]]", "[[The Gift]]", "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]", "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]" and "[[Mother's Mercy]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[The Spoils of War]]" and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[The Long Night]]", a stunt supervisor in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episode "[[The Wars To Come]]" and a fire safety technician in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episode "[[Kill the Boy]]".
