'''Amanda Segel Marks''' is an American writer and producer who has worked on television series such as ''{{WPS|Without a Trace}}'', ''{{WPS|The Good Wife}}'', ''{{WPS|Nikita (TV series)|Nikita}}'', ''{{WPS|Person of Interest (TV series)|Person of Interest}}'', and ''{{WPS|Helstrom (TV series)|Marvel's Helstrom}}''. She is also writing the pilot script for a TV adaptation of the Ubisoft video game ''Skull and Bones'', about female pirates in the Golden Age of Piracy. 

She was officially hired by HBO to write a pilot script for ''[[Ten Thousand Ships]]'', a potential ''Game of Thrones'' [[World of Westeros|successor show]] that will chronicle the rise of [[Nymeria]] and her conquest of [[Dorne]].<ref>[https://deadline.com/2021/05/game-of-thrones-amanda-segel-write-10000-ships-spinoff-1234763526/ ‘Game Of Thrones’: Amanda Segel To Write ‘10,000 Ships’ Spinoff]</ref>
