King '''Benedict Blackmont''' was the last king of [[House Blackmont]].

==Biography==
Benedict was defeated in [[Nymeria's War]], and [[Nymeria]] had him sent to the [[Wall]] in golden chains.{{Ref|H&L509}}

==In the books==
In ''The World of Ice & Fire'', Benedict Blackmont is described as having worshiped a dark god and was said to have the power to turn himself into a vulture of enormous size.
