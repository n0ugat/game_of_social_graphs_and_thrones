The '''Red Temple of Braavos''' is a place of religious worship for followers of the [[Lord of Light]], located in [[Braavos]].<ref>"[[Braavos (short)|Braavos]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Temple of the Lord of Light is connected to the rest of [[Braavos]] by a large bridge. It is located near the [[Isle of the Gods]]. It is built from red stone and on top of its great square tower is an iron brazier twenty feet across containing a great fire, with smaller fires in iron braziers near its doors. It is smaller than the [[Red Temple of Volantis|Temple of the Lord of Light]] in [[Volantis]].
