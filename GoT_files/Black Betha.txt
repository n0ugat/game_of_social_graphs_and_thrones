'''''Black Betha'''''{{Con|ASOIAF}} is a ship captained by [[Davos Seaworth]]. Its first mate is [[Matthos Seaworth]], Davos's son and the [[squire]] of King [[Stannis Baratheon]].

''Black Betha'' serves as Davos's flagship during the [[Battle of the Blackwater]], leading the vanguard of the fleet. The ship is destroyed by a [[wildfire]] explosion, which also kills Matthos and throws Davos overboard.<ref>"[[Blackwater]]"</ref>
