'''Donna C. Williams''' is a stuntwoman and actress.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[The Mountain and the Viper]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]".
