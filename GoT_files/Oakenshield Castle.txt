'''Oakenshield Castle'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a castle on the eponymous island of [[Oakenshield]] in the [[Reach]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the castle is identified only as "Lord Hewett's castle." It is located in Lord [[Hewett's Town]].

Oakenshield is conquered by [[Euron Greyjoy]] during his invasion of the Reach and granted to Nute the Barber.
