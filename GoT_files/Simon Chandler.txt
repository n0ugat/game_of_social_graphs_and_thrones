'''Simon Chandler''' (born 4 June, 1953) is a British film, television and theatre actor. He voiced {{WPS|Merry Brandybuck}} in {{WPS|Ralph Bakshi}}'s ''{{WPS|The Lord of the Rings (1978 film)|The Lord of the Rings}}''. He has also appeared in the films ''{{WPS|The Bounty (1984 film)|The Bounty}}'', ''{{WPS|The Man Who Knew Too Little}}'', ''{{WPS|Spice World (film)|Spice World}}'', ''{{WPS|Stoned (film)|Stoned}}'', ''{{WPS|The King's Speech}}'', ''{{WPS|The Iron Lady (film)|The Iron Lady}}'', ''{{WPS|Fast Girls}}'', ''{{WPS|The Theory of Everything (2014 film)|The Theory of Everything}}'' and ''{{WPS|The Old Guard (2020 film)|The Old Guard}}'' and the television series ''{{WPS|Shine on Harvey Moon}}'', ''{{WPS|Jonny Briggs}}'', ''{{WPS|Silent Witness}}'', ''{{WPS|Teachers (British TV series)|Teachers}}'', ''{{WPS|Hitler: The Rise of Evil}}'', ''{{WPS|Holby City}}'', ''{{WPS|Judge John Deed}}'', ''{{WPS|The Bill}}'', ''{{WPS|My Family}}'', ''{{WPS|Coronation Street}}'', ''{{WPS|Midsomer Murders}}'', ''{{WPS|The Thick of It}}'', ''{{WPS|House of Anubis}}'', ''{{WPS|Doctors (2000 TV series)|Doctors}}'', ''{{WPS|Mr. Selfridge}}'', ''{{WPS|Wallander (British TV series)|Wallander}}'', ''{{WPS|The Crown (TV series)|The Crown}}'', ''{{WPS|Casualty (TV series)|Casualty}}'' and ''{{WPS|Call the Midwife}}''.
