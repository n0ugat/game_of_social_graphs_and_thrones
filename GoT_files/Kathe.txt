'''Kathe''' was a freed [[Slavery|slave]] of [[Meereen]].

==Behind the scenes==
Kathe appears in the [https://storyfactory.uk/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/Game-of-Thrones-S06E09-Battle-Of-The-Bastards.pdf script] for "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]," in the scene where the [[Sons of the Harpy]] are massacring escaping freedmen at the gate of Meereen.{{Scriptref|GOT609}} She does not appear in the filmed version.{{Ref|GOT609}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Kathe.
