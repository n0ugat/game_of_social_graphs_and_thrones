The '''Gate of the Gods'''{{Ref|HOTD202}} is a gate in the walls of [[King's Landing]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
[[Blood]] is caught attempting to flee through the Gate of the Gods with the head of Prince [[Jaehaerys Targaryen (son of Aegon II)|Jaehaerys Targaryen]].{{Ref|HOTD202}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
[[Gilly]] reads the journal of a [[High Septon (Robert's Rebellion)|High Septon]], in which he records that it takes 993 steps to walk from the Gate of the Gods to the [[Great Sept of Baelor]].{{Ref|GOT701}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the [[Kingsroad]] enters King's Landing from the north at the Gate of the Gods.
