The '''Mother of Mountains'''{{Ref|GOT107}}{{Note|[[Dothraki (language)|Dothraki]]: ''Maisi Krazaaji''}} is a mountain in [[Essos]], located within sight of [[Vaes Dothrak]], a lone mountain in the middle of the plains of the [[Dothraki Sea]]. It is considered sacred in the [[Great Stallion|Dothraki religion]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
During his vow to take the [[Iron Throne]] for his unborn son, [[Rhaego]], [[Khal]] [[Drogo]] swears by the Mother of Mountains.<ref>"[[You Win or You Die]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'', the Mother of Mountains is a mountain sacred to the Dothraki people, located within sight of Vaes Dothrak and a lake known as the Womb of the World, from which, according to myth, the first man emerged riding on the first horse. Only men are allowed to set foot on the Mother of Mountains.
