'''Christopher Adam Painter''' (born August 21, 1992) is an English stuntman and actor.

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episodes "[[We Light the Way]]" and "[[Driftmark (episode)|Driftmark]]".
