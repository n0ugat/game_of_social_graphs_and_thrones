'''Paxter Royce''' was a member of [[House Royce]], a son of Lord [[Maynard Royce]].

==Biography==
Paxter Royce was the secondborn son of Lord [[Maynard Royce]] and Lady [[Mavis Royce]]. At 21 years old he married [[Wylla Waynwood]], who bore him one daughter, [[Edwina Royce]]. He died of an unknown illness at the age of 40.<ref name="s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Paxter Royce is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Paxter Royce is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books. The name of Yohn Royce's father has not been mentioned in the books.
