'''Elliott John Tittensor''' (born 3 November 1989) is an English actor. He is known for his role as {{WPS|Carl Gallagher}} in the television series ''{{WPS|Shameless (British TV series)|Shameless}}''. He has also appeared in the films ''{{WPS|Spike Island (film)|Spike Island}}'' and ''{{WPS|Dunkirk (2017 film)|Dunkirk}}''.

He portrays Ser [[Erryk Cargyll]] in ''[[House of the Dragon]]''. His identical twin brother [[Luke Tittensor|Luke]] portrays his character's twin brother, Ser [[Arryk Cargyll]], in the series.<ref>[https://winteriscoming.net/2021/04/01/game-of-thrones-prequel-house-of-the-dragon-casts-twin-kingsguard-brothers/ Game of Thrones prequel casts twin Kingsguard brothers (winteriscoming.net)]</ref>
