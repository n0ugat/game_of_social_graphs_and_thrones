'''Thomas Flanagan''' is a Scottish actor. He is best known for portrayed Filip "Chibs" Telford in the television series ''{{WPS|Sons of Anarchy}}''. He has also appeared in films such as ''{{WPS|Braveheart}}'', ''{{WPS|Face/Off}}'', ''{{WPS|Gladiator (2000 film)|Gladiator}}'', ''{{WPS|Sin City (film)|Sin City}}'' and ''{{WPS|Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2}}'' and television series such as ''{{WPS|Peaky Blinders (TV series)|Peaky Blinders}}'', ''{{WPS|Gotham (TV series)|Gotham}}'', ''{{WPS|Wu Assassins}}'' and ''{{WPS|Westworld (TV series)|Westworld}}''.
