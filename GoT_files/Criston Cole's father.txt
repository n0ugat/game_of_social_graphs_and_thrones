'''Criston Cole's father''' is the [[steward]] to Lord [[Beric Dondarrion (son of Simon)|Beric Dondarrion]] of [[Blackhaven]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He is the head of [[House Cole]], a minor house of the [[Dornish Marches]] sworn to [[House Dondarrion]]. He is the father of Ser [[Criston Cole]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Ser [[Harrold Westerling]] mentions Criston's father during the [[Heir's Tournament]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

Criston tells Rhaenyra that his father served at Blackhaven.{{Ref|HOTD103}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', no additional information is given on Criston Cole's father.
