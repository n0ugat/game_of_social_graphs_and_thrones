'''Serac Baratheon''' was a son of [[Orys Baratheon|Orys]] and [[Argella Baratheon]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Serac Baratheon is an early member of [[House Baratheon]], a son of [[Orys Baratheon]] and a distant ancestor of King [[Robert Baratheon]] and his brothers, [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]] and [[Renly Baratheon|Renly]].<ref name="s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

Serac married [[Monica Baratheon|Monica Velaryon]] at the age of 17 at [[Driftmark]]. He died at the age of 68. Serac was the father of [[Reginald Baratheon|Reginald]], [[William Baratheon|William]], [[Steffon Baratheon (son of Serac)|Steffon]], [[Padraic Baratheon|Padraic]], and [[Flynn Baratheon]].<ref name = "s1prop"/>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'', which notes the fact that he, along with all of his ancestors and descendants were born "black of hair".{{Ref|GOT106}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
