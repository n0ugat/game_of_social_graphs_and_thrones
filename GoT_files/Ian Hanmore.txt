'''Ian Hanmore''' is a Scottish actor, noted for his roles on British television and in film. In TV he has appeared in ''[[wikipedia:Doctor Who|Doctor Who]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Life on Mars (TV series)|Life on Mars]]'', and in films such as ''[[wikipedia:The Magdalene Sisters|The Magdalene Sisters]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he is playing the role of [[Pyat Pree]]. He was announced in the role on 24 August 2011.<ref>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/New_Casting_Pyat_Pree_and_Balon_Greyjoy/ Westeros.org]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
