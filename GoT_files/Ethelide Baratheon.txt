'''Ethelide Baratheon''' was a daughter of [[Orys Baratheon|Orys]] and [[Argella Baratheon]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Ethelide Baratheon is an early member of [[House Baratheon]], the daughter of [[Orys Baratheon|Orys]] and [[Argella Baratheon]]. At the age of fifteen, she eloped with a [[hedge knight]] and was never seen again.{{Ref|GOT106}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT106}}
