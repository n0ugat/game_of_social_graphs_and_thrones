'''Bill Kelliher''' (born 23 March 1971) is an American musician. He is the rhythm guitarist of the heavy metal band {{WPS|Mastodon (band)|Mastodon}}.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a [[Free Folk|wildling]] in the episode "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]" alongside his bandmates [[Brent Hinds]] and [[Brann Dailor]].

He performs on the song "White Walker" on ''[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catch_the_Throne Catch the Throne]''.
