The '''Northern Wars'''{{Ref|GOT104}} were a long series of conflicts that took place in the [[North]] some years prior to [[Robert's Rebellion]]. It is known to have involved [[House Umber]], out of which at least two members, [[Brandon Umber|Brandon]] and [[Iain Umber (son of Gareth)|Iain Umber]], died in the fighting. The fighting occurred from at least [[257 AC/Non-canon|257]]{{Dateref|Northern Wars}} to [[270 AC]].{{Dateref|Northern Wars}}

==In the books==
There is no corresponding conflict described in the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' book series.
