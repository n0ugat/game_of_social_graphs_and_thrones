'''Varly'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} was a guardsman sworn to [[House Stark]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Varly accompanies [[Eddard Stark]] south when he becomes the [[Hand of the King]]. He warns the [[Royal Steward]] to stay back when he surprises them in the corridor. He accompanies Eddard to the throne room to confront [[Joffrey Baratheon]]. He is killed by men of the [[City Watch of King's Landing|City Watch]] when they [[Massacre in the Red Keep|betray]] Eddard.{{Ref|GOT107}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Varly is a guardsman sworn to House Stark. He is killed by [[Janos Slynt]] during the [[Massacre in the Red Keep]] as his fellow guards are massacred by the Gold Cloaks.
