'''Diana Royce''' was the wife of [[Robar Royce (lord)|Robar Royce]], the [[Lord of Runestone]]. She is the great-grandmother of [[Yohn Royce]].

==Biography==
Diana Royce, who originally came from an unknown noble house, married [[Robar Royce (lord)|Robar Royce]] and bore him several sons, the oldest two being [[Maynard Royce]], who inherited [[Runestone]] after his father's death, and [[Donal Royce]].<ref name="s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Diana Royce is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Diana Royce is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
