Ser '''Deziel Dalt'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} is a landed [[Knighthood|knight]] and the head of [[House Dalt]], a knightly house sworn to [[House Martell]] of [[Dorne]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Deziel Dalt is the Knight of Lemonwood.{{Ref|GOT401}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
Ser Deziel is a member of the group of [[Dorne|Dornish]] lords and knights and that accompany Prince [[Oberyn Martell]] to [[King's Landing]].{{Ref|GOT401}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Ser Deziel Dalt is one of the many Dornish nobles that escort Prince Oberyn to [[King's Landing]].
