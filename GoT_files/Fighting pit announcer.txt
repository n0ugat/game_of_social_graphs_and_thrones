==Biography==
===Background===
This man is a [[Ghiscari]] citizen of [[Meereen]].{{Ref|GOT509}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
He announces the [[Fighting pit#Great Games|Great Games]] at the [[Great Pit of Daznak]]. During the attack by the [[Sons of the Harpy]], his dead body can be seen when [[Daario Naharis]] leads [[Daenerys Targaryen]] across the pit.{{Ref|GOT509}}
