'''House Selmy'''{{Ref|GOT106}}{{Note|Barristan's surname is Selmy.}} of [[Harvest Hall]] is a minor vassal house from the [[Stormlands]] that holds fealty to [[House Baratheon]] of [[Storm's End]].

==Known members==
*Ser {[[Lyonel Selmy]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[Harvest Hall]].
**Ser {[[Barristan Selmy]]}, his son. Known as "Barristan the Bold." Former [[Lord Commander of the Kingsguard]] of [[Robert Baratheon]]. Since being dismissed by [[Joffrey Baratheon]], he tracked down [[Daenerys Targaryen]] and swore his loyalty to her, joining her Queensguard. Slain when fighting the [[Sons of the Harpy]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Selmy is a house sworn to Storm's End. Their seat is Harvest Hall and their sigil is three stalks of yellow wheat on brown.

At the [[parley]] with [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]] and [[Catelyn Stark|Catelyn]], [[Renly Baratheon|Renly]] braggingly lists all the houses that support him, among them House Selmy.{{Bookref|ACOK|31}}

Ser Barristan Selmy is by far the House's most famous member. The current lord of Harvest Hall is his great-nephew Arstan, but nothing is known about him except his name and title.

So far in the books, Ser Barristan is alive.

===Known members===
*Ser Lyonel Selmy, Knight of Harvest Hall.
**Ser Barristan Selmy, his son.
*Lord Arstan Selmy, current Lord of Harvest Hall, Lyonel's great-grandson and Barristan's great-nephew.
