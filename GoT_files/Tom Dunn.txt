'''Tom Dunn''' was a member of the [[Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
Tom was among the Night's Watchmen who didn't escape the [[Massacre at Hardhome]].{{Scriptref|GOT510}}

==Behind the scenes==
Tom is mentioned in the script for "[[Mother's Mercy]]."{{Scriptref|GOT510}} In the filmed version, the line mentioning him is cut.{{Ref|GOT510}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Tom Dunn. No members of [[House Dunn]] have appeared or been mentioned as of ''A Dance with Dragons''.
