'''Valryon''' was a [[dragon]] who was bred by [[House Targaryen]]. After its death, its skull decorated the [[Red Keep]] in [[King's Landing]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Valryon is one of the dragons mentioned by [[Viserys Targaryen (son of Aerys II)|Viserys]] as he baths with [[Doreah]] in [[Vaes Dothrak]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of a dragon named Valryon.
