| Appearances = 4 episodes}}'''Eros Vlahos''' (born 1995) is an English actor. He is best known for his role as Cyril in ''[[wikipedia:Nanny McPhee and the Big Bang|Nanny McPhee and the Big Bang]]''. He has a recurring role as a young [[wikipedia:Niccolò_Machiavelli|Nico Machiavelli]] in Starz Original Series ''[[wikipedia:Da Vinci's Demons|Da Vinci's Demons]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played [[Lommy|Lommy Greenhands]]. He was announced in the role on 5 October 2010. He initially appeared as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and returned in this role for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
