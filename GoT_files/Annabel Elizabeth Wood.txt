'''Annabel Elizabeth Wood''' is a stuntwoman and actress.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[Breaker of Chains]]", [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[Sons of the Harpy (episode)|Sons of the Harpy]]", "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]", "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]" and "[[Mother's Mercy]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[The Red Woman]]" and "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[Dragonstone (episode)|Dragonstone]]", "[[Beyond the Wall (episode)|Beyond the Wall]]" and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[The Long Night]]" and "[[The Bells]]".
