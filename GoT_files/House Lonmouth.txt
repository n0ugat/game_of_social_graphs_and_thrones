'''House Lonmouth'''{{Ref|HOTD105}}{{Note|Joffrey's surname is Lonmouth.}} is a minor [[noble house]] from the [[Stormlands]] that holds fealty to [[House Baratheon/House of the Dragon|House Baratheon]].

==History==
[[Knighthood|Ser]] [[Richard Lonmouth]] attended the [[Great Tourney at Harrenhal]], and was beaten by [[Lord of Storm's End|Lord]] [[Robert Baratheon]] in a drinking game.{{Ref|H&L605}}

==Members==
*Ser {[[Joffrey Lonmouth]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] in service of [[House Velaryon]]. Known as the "Knight of Kisses." Killed by Ser [[Criston Cole]].
*Ser [[Richard Lonmouth]], a knight who participated in the [[Great Tourney at Harrenhal]]. Known as the "Knight of Skulls and Kisses."

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Lonmouth is said to blazon their arms with quartered of six: red lips strewn on yellow, yellow skulls strewn on black (hence the nicknames of its two known members). Their words are "The Choice Is Yours," in reference to their heraldry.

===Known members===
*Ser [[Richard Lonmouth]], known as the Knight of Skulls and Kisses, a companion and former squire of Prince [[Rhaegar Targaryen]].

===Historical members===
*Ser [[Joffrey Lonmouth]], known as the Knight of Kisses. He was close friends with [[Laenor Velaryon]], who named his son Prince [[Joffrey Velaryon]] after him.
