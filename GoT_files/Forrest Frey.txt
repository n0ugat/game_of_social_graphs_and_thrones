Lord '''Forrest Frey''' is the [[Lord of the Crossing]] and head of [[House Frey]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Forrest and his wife [[Serena Frey|Serena]] treat with Prince [[Jacaerys Velaryon]] at the [[Twins]]. He swears allegiance to [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] in exchange for the promise of being granted [[Harrenhal]] after the war.{{Ref|HOTD205}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Forrest Frey was a suitor to Rhaenyra when she visited the [[riverlands]] in 112 AC. After boldly asking for Rhaenyra's hand openly, he was dubbed "Fool Frey."
