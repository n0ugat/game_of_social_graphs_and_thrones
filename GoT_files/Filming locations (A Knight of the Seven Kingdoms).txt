==United Kingdom==
===Northern Ireland===
[[File:AKOTSK tree.jpg|thumb|250px|left|The iconic tree from the opening pages of ''The Hedge Knight''.]]
[[Ashford]] is portrayed by Myra Castle near Downpatrick, County Down. It is also used for scenes set in [[Flea Bottom]], [[King's Landing]]. Previously, Myra Castle was used as the exterior for the [[Dreadfort]] in ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.<ref name="Redanian" />

A location on the {{WPS|Glenarm Castle}} estate, in County Antrim, portrays [[Ashford Meadow]].<ref name="Redanian" />

The {{WPS|Tollymore Forest Park}} is used for scenes set in the [[Crownlands]]. It was previously used to film the introduction of the [[direwolf|direwolves]] in "[[Winter Is Coming]]," the first episode of ''Game of Thrones''.<ref name="Redanian" />

The scene in which [[Duncan the Tall|Dunk]] buries [[Arlan of Pennytree]] is filmed near the {{WPS|Mourne Mountains}}, which were previously used as a backdrop for the [[North]] in ''Game of Thrones''.{{Webref|name=Redanian2 |author=@RedanianIntel |date=July 2, 2024 |url=https://x.com/RedanianIntel/status/1808157012598964734 |title=A tree prop (probably the site where Dunk buries Ser Arlan) on the filming location of A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms near Mourne Mountains, NI (photo credit @Cathal50404944) |website=X |access-date=July 16, 2024 }}
