|imdb = http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0734654/|First = "[[The Lion and the Rose]]"|Last = "[[The Children]]"}}'''Struan Rodger '''(born 18 September 1946) is a British actor. In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he briefly played [[Brynden Rivers]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]]. He debuts in an uncredited voice cameo in "[[The Lion and the Rose]]" and makes his only onscreen appearance in "[[The Children]]". Absent throughout the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]], the character reappears in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]], yet the role is taken over by [[Max von Sydow]].

Rodger is best known for his role as Sandy McGrath in the film ''[[wikipedia: Chariots of Fire| Chariots of Fire]]''. He has also appeared in films such as ''[[wikipedia: Stardust (film)| Stardust]]'', ''[[wikipedia: Four Weddings and a Funeral|Four Weddings and a Funeral]]'' and ''[[wikipedia: Kill List|Kill List]]''. He also provided the voice of the Face of Boe in several episodes of ''[[wikipedia:Doctor Who|Doctor Who]]''.
