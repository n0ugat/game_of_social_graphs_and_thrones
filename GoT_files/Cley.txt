'''Cley''' is a resident of [[King's Landing]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Cley is a drinking companion of [[Ulf]] and [[Mujja]].{{Ref|HOTD203}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Cley drinks with Ulf and Mujja at a tavern in King's Landing, and teases Ulf about his lack of silver hair when he boasts about his supposed [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|Targaryen]] heritage.{{Ref|HOTD203}}
[[File:2x3 Ulf Still.jpg|thumb|Cley, Mujja and Ulf at the Cock Inn.]]
When [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] sends out a call to all [[dragonseed]]s in King's Landing, Cley and Mujja urge Ulf on to go to [[Dragonstone]] and claim a [[dragon]].{{Ref|HOTD207}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Cley. The only character named Cley is [[Cley Cerwyn]] from the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels.
