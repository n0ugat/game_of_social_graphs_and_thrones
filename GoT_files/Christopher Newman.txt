'''Christopher Newman''' is a Lebanese producer for ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. In the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and a [[A Man Without Honor|single episode]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]], he was credited as a line producer, and as a co-producer in the whole of Season 2.

He also had a cameo appearance as [[Hoster Tully|Hoster Tully]]'s corpse in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|Season 3]] episode "[[Walk of Punishment (episode)|Walk of Punishment]]".
