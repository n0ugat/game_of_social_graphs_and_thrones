'''Vermax'''{{Note|[[High Valyrian]]: ''Vermaks''}}{{Note|<small>VƎR</small>-maks}} is a [[dragon]] that belongs to [[House Targaryen]] during the reign of King [[Viserys I Targaryen]]. He is bonded to [[Jacaerys Velaryon]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Vermax hatched around the same time as [[Tessarion]]{{Ref|HOTD208}} and bonded with [[Jacaerys Velaryon|Jacaerys]] from a young age. He has olive-green scales, orange eyes, and orange frills.{{Ref|HOTD106}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
In the [[Dragonpit]], [[Jacaerys Velaryon]] trains Vermax to heel and to breathe fire onto a goat under the supervision of the [[Dragonkeepers]] with [[Lucerys Velaryon]], [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon Targaryen]], and [[Aemond Targaryen]] as their audience.{{Ref|HOTD106}}
[[File:TBQ Dragons.png|thumb|[[Meleys]], Vermax and [[Arrax]] leaving [[Dragonstone]].]]
After the coronation of Queen [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]], Jacaerys and Vermax start to fly to the [[Eyrie]] and to [[Winterfell]] to affirm the supports of [[House Arryn/House of the Dragon|House Arryn]] and [[House Stark/House of the Dragon|House Stark]].{{Ref|HOTD110}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Vermax waits near the shores of the [[Trident]] while [[Jacaerys Velaryon|Jacaerys]] negotiates safe passage for [[House Stark]] with [[House Frey]].{{Ref|HOTD205}}

Vermax flies around [[Dragonstone (island)|Dragonstone]] with [[Moondancer]] as [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] and [[Mysaria]] discuss the impending war against the [[Greens]].{{Ref|HOTD208}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Vermax is the mount of Jacaerys Velaryon.
