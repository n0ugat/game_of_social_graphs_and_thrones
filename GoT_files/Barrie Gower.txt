'''Barrie Gower''' worked as a Prosthetics Supervisor in [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|Season 4]] through [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|Season 8]] of ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei2K3sX8_JQ Game of Thrones Season 5: Episode #5 - The Stone Men of Valyria (HBO)]</ref> In addition to ''Game of Thrones'', he is known for his work on ''Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2'', ''The Iron Lady'', ''The Witcher'', and ''Stranger Things''.

He will return to work on ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.
