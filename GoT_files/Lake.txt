'''Lake''' was a [[Children of the Forest|Child of the Forest]] who lived during the [[Age of Heroes]].

==Behind the scenes==
On July 21, 2020, ''Redanian Intelligence'' revealed that [[Felicia Mukasa]] had portrayed a Child of the Forest named Lake in ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', the rejected ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' [[World of Westeros|prequel]] [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]].<ref name="Redanian" />

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and its supplementary material, there are two noble houses named Lake (one from [[Dorne]] and the second from the [[North]]), but there is no mention of an individual character named Lake who lived during the Age of Heroes. The character was invented by [[Jane Goldman]] for ''Bloodmoon''.
