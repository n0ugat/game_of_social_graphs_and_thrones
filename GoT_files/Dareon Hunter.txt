'''Dareon Hunter''' is a member of [[House Hunter]]. His status is uncertain.

==Biography==
Dareon Hunter is the firstborn son of Ser [[Stentey Hunter]] and [[Serena Royce]].<ref name = "s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Dareon Hunter is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Dareon Hunter is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
