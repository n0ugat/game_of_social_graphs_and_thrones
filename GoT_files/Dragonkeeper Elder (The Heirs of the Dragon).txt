A '''Dragonkeeper Elder''' served [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]] in the [[Dragonpit]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He is a senior member of the [[Dragonkeepers]], a monastic order guarding and raising House Targaryen's [[dragon]]s.{{Ref|HOTD101}} He speaks [[High Valyrian]] exclusively.{{Ref|HOTD102}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
The Elder and three other Dragonkeepers bring [[Syrax]] into the Dragonpit after [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] lands her at the entrance. He later brings Syrax to the funeral of Queen [[Aemma Arryn|Aemma]] and Prince [[Baelon Targaryen|Baelon]]. He watches stoically as Rhaenyra gives the command to cremate them.{{Ref|HOTD101}}

The Elder brings the [[Small Council]] news of [[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]] attacking him and his [[Dragonkeeper Acolyte (The Heirs of the Dragon)|Acolyte]], then stealing one of [[Dreamfyre]]'s eggs. He later accompanies [[Otto Hightower]] to [[Dragonstone]] to retrieve the egg.{{Ref|HOTD102}}
