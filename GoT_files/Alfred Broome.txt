==Biography==
===Background===
Ser Alfred is from [[House Broome]], a [[noble house]] of the [[Westerlands]]. He is a member of [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]]'s [[Black Council]].{{Webref|name=WBD2 |author=Unknown author |date=Unknown date |url=https://press.wbd.com/us/bio/house-dragon-s2-character-descriptions |title=House of the Dragon S2 <nowiki>|</nowiki> Character Descriptions |website=Warner Bros. Discovery |access-date=June 5, 2024 }}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Alfred is present at the Black Council when Rhaenyra returns from the [[Stormlands]].{{Ref|HOTD201}}

Alfred is present when word of Prince [[Jaehaerys Targaryen (son of Aegon II)|Jaehaerys]]'s [[Assassination of Jaehaerys Targaryen|murder]] and funeral reaches Dragonstone. He proposes countering the propaganda being spread by the [[Greens]] blaming Rhaenyra for ordering the killing by suggesting she did so in a moment of grief over the [[Fight above Shipbreaker Bay|death]] of her son [[Lucerys Velaryon|Lucerys]]. An offended Rhaenyra asks him if he thinks she would order a child's beheading out of her grief; when Alfred tries to defend his proposal of selling the killing as an act of vengeance made in haste, Princess [[Rhaenys Targaryen|Rhaenys]] rebukes him, and Alfred falls silent.{{Ref|HOTD202}}

==Behind the scenes==
On December 2, 2023, [[HBO]] announced that [[Jamie Kenna]] had been cast in the role of Ser Alfred Broome for [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]].<ref name="WBD" /> His character description reads as follows:
:''"A gruff knight on Queen Rhaenyra’s black council."''<ref name="WBD2" />

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Alfred joined the garrison at [[Dragonstone]] during the reign of [[Jaehaerys I Targaryen|Jaehaerys I]], and continued to serve there under [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]], the [[Prince of Dragonstone|Princess of Dragonstone]].
