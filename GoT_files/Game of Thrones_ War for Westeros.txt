'''''Game of Thrones: War for Westeros''''' is an upcoming real-time strategy game set for release in 2026.

==Gameplay==
The game allows players to control the [[Great House]]s such as the [[House Stark|Starks]], [[House Lannister|Lannisters]], and [[House Targaryen|Targaryens]] or the [[Night King]] to battle for control of [[Westeros]]. The game will feature a campaign as well as PvP multiplayer. Players will be able to form alliances between houses. Units include infantry, cavalry, siege engines, [[giant]]s, and [[dragon]]s. Confirmed locations include the [[Wall]] and [[King's Landing]].{{Webref|name= |author=Dalton Cooper |date=June 7, 2025 |url=https://gamerant.com/game-of-thrones-war-for-westeros-trailer-gameplay-screenshots/ |title=Game of Thrones: War for Westeros Video Game Announced, But There's a Catch |website=GameRant |access-date=June 8, 2025 }}
