'''Three Towers'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in the [[Reach]], the region controlled by [[House Tyrell]] of [[Highgarden]]. The castle is located south-west of Highgarden and [[Oldtown]], on the edge of the [[Redwyne Straits]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Three Towers is the seat of House Costayne. It is located on the southern coast of Whispering Sound, where it empties into the Redwyne Straits. Along with [[Blackcrown]], it guards the entrance to the Sound and the approaches to Oldtown.
