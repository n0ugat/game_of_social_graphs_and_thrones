'''Lord of the Dreadfort'''{{Ref|GOT310}} is a title held by the ruler of the [[Dreadfort]] and its surrounding lands in the [[North]]. The title is traditionally held by the head of [[House Bolton]]. The current Lord of the Dreadfort is uncertain with the death of [[Ramsay Bolton]] and the extinction of House Bolton in the aftermath of the [[Battle of the Bastards]].
