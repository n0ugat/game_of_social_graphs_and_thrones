'''House Fell'''{{Ref|HOTD109}} of [[Felwood]] is a [[noble house]] in the [[Stormlands]]. They hold fealty to [[House Baratheon/House of the Dragon|House Baratheon]] of [[Storm's End]].

==Members==
*[[Lady Fell]], a supporter of [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] at the outset of the [[Dance of the Dragons]].
*Ser [[Willis Fell]], a [[Knighthood|knight]] of the [[Kingsguard]] of [[Aegon II Targaryen]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the arms of House Fell are blazoned per fess, a white crescent moon in the first above a green field, a sprucetree line between.

House Fell fought against the army of [[Orys Baratheon]] during [[Aegon's Conquest]], taking part in an ambush on Orys at the [[Wendwater]].

During the [[First Dornish War]], Lord Fell was smothered by [[Dornishmen|Dornish]] assassins in a brothel.

Lady Fell was executed for refusing to renounce her oath to [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] and swear fealty to [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon II]].

House Fell remained loyal to [[House Targaryen/House of the Dragon|House Targaryen]] at the start of [[Robert's Rebellion]]. After [[Robert Baratheon|Robert]] killed Lord Fell in single combat at [[Summerhall]], his son Silveraxe joined Robert's side.

Lord Harwood Fell participates in [[Stannis Baratheon]]'s [[Battle in the ice|campaign against the Boltons]]. He dies from a chill after falling into a river during their march to [[Winterfell]].
