This unnamed '''Great Master''' was a [[Slavery|slave-trader]] from the city of [[Meereen]], on the coast of [[Slaver's Bay]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
He is one of the many [[Great Masters]] that gather over the walls of Meereen, when [[Daenerys Targaryen]] and her army appear before the city gates to lay [[Siege of Meereen|siege]] to the city.{{Ref|GOT403}}

When the Meereenese slaves rise against the Great Masters, he is ambushed and killed on the streets by a mob.{{Ref|GOT404}}
