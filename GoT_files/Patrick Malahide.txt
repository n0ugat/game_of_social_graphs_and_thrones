'''Patrick Gerald Duggan''' (born 24 March 1945 in Reading, Berkshire, England), known professionally as '''Patrick Malahide''', is a British actor who portrays [[Balon Greyjoy]] on ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.

He first appeared on screen in 1976 in the British TV series ''Flight of the Heron'', followed by guest appearances in ''[[wikipedia:Sutherland's Law|Sutherland's Law]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:The New Avengers|The New Avengers]]''. From 1979 to 1988 he had a recurring role in ''[[wikipedia:Minder (TV series)|Minder]]'' as Arthur Daley's nemesis Detective Sergeant Chisholm.

Malahide became a mainstay of British television, appearing in series such as ''[[wikipedia:Middlemarch (1994 TV serial)|Middlemarch]]'', ''[[wikipedia:The Singing Detective|The Singing Detective]]'' and in the first season of ''[[w:c:blackadder:The Black Adder|The Black Adder]]'' (in "The Black Seal" as Guy de Glastonbury, co-starring with [[Tim McInnerny]], who killed his character in the episode). His most recent television appearance was in the 2008 version of ''[[wikipedia:Survivors (2008 TV series)|Survivors]]''. On film he has appeared in movies such as ''[[wikipedia:The World is Not Enough|The World is Not Enough]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Brideshead Revisited (film)|Brideshead Revisited]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Captain Corelli's Mandolin|Captain Corelli's Mandolin]]''.

In ''Game of Thrones'' he plays the role of [[Balon Greyjoy]]. He was announced in the role on 24 August 2011.<ref>[http://winter-is-coming.net/2011/08/patrick-malahide-cast-as-balon-greyjoy/ Winter is Coming]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
