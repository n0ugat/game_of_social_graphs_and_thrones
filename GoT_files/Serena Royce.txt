'''Serena Royce''' is a member of [[House Royce]], a daughter of Lord [[Maynard Royce]].

==Biography==
Serena Royce is the fourthborn child, and secondborn daughter, of Lord [[Maynard Royce]] and Lady [[Mavis Royce]]. She married Ser [[Stentey Hunter]] and has born him ten children; seven sons and three daughters. She has green eyes and auburn hair.<ref name="s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Serena Royce is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Serena Royce is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
