Lord '''Alyn Cockshaw'''{{Con|D&EN}} is a [[Lordship|lord]] from the [[Reach]].

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Alyn is a childhood friend of [[Daemon Blackfyre (son of Daemon)|Daemon Blackfyre the Younger]]. He and Daemon were bullied by [[Aegon Blackfyre|Aegon]] and [[Aemon Blackfyre|Aemon]], Daemon's older brothers. Alyn wept when Daemon was forced into [[exile]] with [[Aegor Rivers|Bittersteel]] after the [[Blackfyre Rebellion]].
