King '''Tywell II Lannister'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} was a [[King of the Rock]], identified by some as the king who was served his own son in a pie by the legendary [[Rat Cook]].

==Biography==
===Background===
According to legend, a king and his son once visited the [[Nightfort]], where the latter was killed and served to his oblivious father in a pie. The king enjoyed the pie so much that he asked for a second helping.{{Ref|GOT310}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
[[Bran Stark|Bran]] tells the legend of the Rat Cook to [[Meera Reed|Meera]] and [[Jojen Reed|Jojen]].{{Ref|GOT310}}

==In the books==
In ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', the legend is the same. In some tellings, the king was [[Oswell I Arryn]], not Tywell II Lannister.{{Bookref|TWOI&F|7|1}}
