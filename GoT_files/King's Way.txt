'''King's Way'''{{Ref|HOTD109}} is a street in [[King's Landing]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Erryk Cargyll]] and [[Rhaenys Targaryen]] find themselves south of King's Way as they escape the [[Red Keep]].{{Ref|HOTD109}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the King's Way is one of four streets that starts at the great square in the centre of [[King's Landing]], with the other three being the God's Way, the Muddy Way, and the Street of the Sisters.
