King '''Brandon Stark''', also known as '''Brandon the Breaker''',{{Con|ASOIAF}} was [[King in the North]] during the [[Age of Heroes]].

==Biography==
According to legend, he allied with [[King-Beyond-the-Wall]] [[Joramun]] to [[Fall of Night's King|defeat]] [[Night's King]] and his [[corpse queen]], freeing the [[Night's Watch]] from their rule.{{Ref|H&L212}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, his story is the same. After defeating Night's King, he obliterated his name from memory. In some versions of the tale, he is the brother of Night's King.
