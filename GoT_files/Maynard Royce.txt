'''Maynard Royce''' was the [[Lord of Runestone]] and head of [[House Royce]]. He is the grandfather of [[Yohn Royce]].

==Biography==
Maynard Royce was the firstborn son of Lord [[Robar Royce (lord)|Robar Royce]] and inherited [[Runestone]] upon his father's death. He had green eyes and flaxen hair. When he was eighteenth, he married Lady [[Mavis Royce|Mavis Belmore]], who bore him two sons: [[Andar Royce|Andar]] and [[Paxter Royce|Paxter]], and two daughters: [[Junia Royce|Junia]] and [[Serena Royce|Serena]]. He died at the age of 38 of a fever, Runestone being inherited by his eldest son, Andar.<ref name="s1prop">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop from Season 1</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Maynard Royce is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Maynard Royce is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
