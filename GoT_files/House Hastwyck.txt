'''House Hastwyck'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} is a [[noble house]] from the [[Reach]]. Their sigil is barry olive and ivory.{{Ref|H&L803}}

==History==
Ser [[Morgil Hastwyck]] was killed by [[Prince]] [[Aemon Targaryen (son of Viserys II)|Aemon the Dragonknight]] in a [[trial by combat]] after slandering [[King|Queen]] [[Naerys Targaryen]] with the encouragement of [[Aegon IV Targaryen|King Aegon IV]].{{Ref|H&L803}}

==Historical members==
*Ser {[[Morgil Hastwyck]]}, throat slit by [[Aemon Targaryen (son of Viserys II)|Aemon the Dragonknight]] at [[King's Landing]].

==In the books==
In ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', House Hastwyck is the same. According to ''The Citadel'', their words are "None so Dutiful".
