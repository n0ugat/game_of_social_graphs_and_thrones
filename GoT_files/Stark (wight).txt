'''Stark''', whose first name is unknown, was an ancient member of [[House Stark]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He was a member of House Stark. He was buried in the crypt beneath [[Winterfell]].{{Ref|GOT803}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
During the [[Battle of Winterfell]], he is raised as a [[wight]] by the [[Night King]] and escapes his sarcophagus. He is later slain by [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] or [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]] along with another Stark wight.{{Ref|GOT803}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, no such character has appeared.
