'''Jaryd Headley''' (born November 2, 1992) is an English actor. He has appeared in the film ''{{WPS|The Danish Girl (film)|The Danish Girl}}''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed an [[Unsullied]] soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]", "[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms (Game of Thrones)|A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]", and "[[The Long Night]]".
