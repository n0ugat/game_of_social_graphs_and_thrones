Lord '''Leo Tyrell''', called '''Longthorn''', is the [[Lord of Highgarden]] and head of [[House Tyrell]] during the reign of [[Daeron II Targaryen]].<ref>https://redanianintelligence.com/2024/08/11/a-knight-of-the-seven-kingdoms-casts-a-tyrell/</ref>

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Leo Tyrell is a slender man with silvery hair and a grey beard. Some consider him the finest lancer in history.

Leo fought in the [[Blackfyre Rebellion]], winning notable victories against [[Daemon Blackfyre]] in the Reach. He did not arrive in time for the [[Battle of Redgrass Field|Battle of the Redgrass Field]].
