'''Tion Tarbeck''' was a member of [[House Tarbeck]]. He was the son of [[Walderan Tarbeck]] and [[Ellyn Reyne]]. He was named after [[Tion Lannister]], his mother's first [[Marriage|husband]].

==Biography==
Tion was killed alongside his mother when [[Tywin Lannister]] attacked [[Tarbeck Hall]] with trebuchets, causing the keep to collapse and crush them.{{Ref|H&L708}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, his story is the same.
