'''Morros Charlton''' was a [[Steward (Night's Watch)|steward]] of the [[Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Morros was the personal steward of [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch|Lord Commander]] [[Robert of Oxbridge]]. When the two were found abed together, Robert was stripped of his command and imprisoned in an ice cell while Morros was sent to the [[Shadow Tower]].<ref>[[:File:5x2 Night's Watch Book.jpg]]</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Morros is mentioned in a book that [[Samwell Tarly]] is reading.{{Ref|GOT502}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Morros Charlton.
