|Appearances = "[[Garden of Bones (episode)|Garden of Bones]]"}}'''David Fynn''' is a British actor. He has appeared in ''Pete Versus Life'', ''Peep Show'', ''Doctor Who'', ''The Inbetweeners'', ''Spooks'', ''Teachers'', ''Sherlock'' and ''Black Mirror''. He also had a role in the HBO series ''Life's Too Short''. He trained at The Webber Douglas Academy of Dramatic Art and has worked in film and theatre as well as his television roles. He is currently appearing in the National Theatre production of She Stoops to Conquer in London. He has forthcoming roles in the British comedy series ''Big Bad World'' and ''Parents''.

He is also starring in the American NBC comedy series Undateable, where he plays a gay, British man. While playing this character, he referenced Game of Thrones.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he is played the role of [[Rennick]]. he joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
