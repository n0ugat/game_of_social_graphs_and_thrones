'''Chris Newton''' is a stuntman.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[The Old Gods and the New]]", "[[The Prince of Winterfell]]" and "[[Blackwater]], the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episodes "[[Valar Dohaeris]]" and "[[The Climb]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episodes "[[First of His Name]]" and "[[The Watchers on the Wall]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[The Long Night]]", "[[The Last of the Starks]]" and "[[The Bells]]" and a stunt rigger in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Iron Throne]]".
