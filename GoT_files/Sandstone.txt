'''Sandstone'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in [[Dorne]], the region controlled by [[House Martell]] of [[Sunspear]], and the seat of [[House Qorgyle]]. The castle is located in the south-west of Dorne, near the [[Brimstone]] in the deepest part of the Dornish desert. It is west of the [[Hellholt]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Sandstone is the seat of House Qorgyle. Prince Oberyn Martell was a [[ward]] at Sandstone for a time.
