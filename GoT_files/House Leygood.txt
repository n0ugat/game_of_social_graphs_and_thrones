'''House Leygood'''{{Ref|GOT402}}{{Note|Bronn refers to a knight named Leygood.}} is a vassal house from the [[Reach]] that holds fealty to Lord [[Bronn]] of [[Highgarden]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
[[Bronn]] mentions to [[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]] he's having an affair with the wife of a [[Leygood|knight of House Leygood]]. He mentions that the knight's sigil consisted of thunderbolts.{{Ref|GOT402}}

==Members==
*[[Leygood]], a knight.
**His wife. Had a secret affair with [[Bronn]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, no member of House Leygood has been mentioned by name, and the house itself is mentioned only once: in ''A Feast for Crows'', when Brienne arrives at the market of [[Duskendale]], she notices among the merchandise a shield that bears the sigil of House Leygood.{{Bookref|AFFC|9}}

The house is briefly mentioned in ''The Hedge Knight'' (the first of the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]]), ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', and in ''[[Fire & Blood]]''.

===Historical members===
*Lord Leygood, one of the Caltrops.
**Ser Lucas Leygood, his son, Commander of the City Watch.
*Ser Simon Leygood, a knight during the reign of [[Aerys Targaryen (son of Daeron II)|Aerys I Targaryen]].
