'''Kevin Morgan''' is a stuntman.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt rigger in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episodes "[[Valar Dohaeris]]", "[[The Climb]]" and "[[The Bear and the Maiden Fair (episode)|The Bear and the Maiden Fair]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Oathbreaker]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episode "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]" and a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[Home]]" and "[[The Broken Man]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episode "[[The Queen's Justice]]".
