'''Axel Baratheon''' was a son of [[Orys Baratheon|Orys]] and [[Argella Baratheon]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Axel Baratheon is an early member of [[House Baratheon]], being the firstborn son of [[Orys Baratheon]].{{Ref|GOT106}} He married [[Alanna Penrose]] at roughly the age of 18. He was killed at the age of 42 at [[Gallowsgrey]] in a duel with [[Steffon Trant]], who had dishonored his wife.{{Notes|Lineages}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He has an entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'', which notes the fact that he, along with all of his ancestors and descendants were born "black of hair".{{Ref|GOT106}}
