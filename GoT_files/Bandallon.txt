'''Bandallon'''{{Ref|GOT701}} is a castle in the [[Reach]], the region controlled by [[House Tyrell]] of [[Highgarden]]. The castle is located west of [[Brightwater Keep]] on the southwestern coast of the continent of [[Westeros]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Bandallon is the seat of House Blackbar.
