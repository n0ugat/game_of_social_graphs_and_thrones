The '''liberation of Raventree Hall''' is a military engagement in the [[War of the Five Kings]]. It is part of the Northern campaign to liberate the [[Riverlands]] from the [[House Lannister|Lannister]] occupation.{{Guideref|Retaking the Riverlands}}

==History==
Following the liberation of [[Riverrun]] at the [[Battle of the Camps]], [[Robb Stark|Robb]] tasks [[Greatjon Umber]] with the expulsion of Lannister forces from the lands of the Riverlords while he invades the [[Westerlands]]. Umber's first liberates [[Raventree Hall]], the ancestral seat of [[House Blackwood]], and then moves to [[Liberation of Stone Hedge|restore]] [[Stone Hedge]] to [[House Bracken]], thus forcing the Lannisters to fall back to [[Harrenhal]] and cutting them off from [[Casterly Rock]].{{Guideref|Retaking the Riverlands}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, after the liberation of Riverrun and his proclamation as [[King in the North]], Robb gives leave to the Riverlords to reclaim their seats. Lord Tytos Blackwood reclaims Raventree, though the Lannisters plunder his harvest and scorch the land as they flee.{{Bookref|ACOK|8}}
