'''Katherine Chappell''' was a visual effects editor working on ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.  

In June 2015, during [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]], Chappell was killed in a lion attack while on vacation in South Africa.  She was attacked while her car windows were down and a lion lunged at her neck.<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/george-r-r-martin-on-violence-against-women-game-of-thrones-editor-killed-in-lion-attack/]</ref>
