A '''Velaryon knight''' fought in the [[War for the Stepstones]].

==Biography==
===Background===
He is a [[Knighthood|knight]] sworn to [[House Velaryon]].{{Ref|HOTD103}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
At [[Bloodstone]], the knight is staked by [[Craghas Drahar]] to be eaten by carnivorous crabs. Before the execution can continue, [[Daemon Targaryen]] arrives on [[Caraxes]] and the Crabfeeder flees inside a cave. The knight is relieved, shouting for Daemon to save him, but he is crushed when Caraxes lands directly on top of him.{{Ref|HOTD103}}
