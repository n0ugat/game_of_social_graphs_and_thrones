{{Quote|House Lefford, who guards the Golden Tooth, the eastern pass through the mountains and the old and frequent chaos of the Seven Kingdoms. Though after [[Robb Stark]]'s recent incursions... perhaps we need a new gatekeeper.|[[Tywin Lannister]]|The Westerlands}}
'''House Lefford'''{{Ref|H&L315}} of the [[Golden Tooth]] is a vassal house that holds fealty to [[House Lannister]] of [[Casterly Rock]]. Their lands are the main entrance to the Westerlands from the east, and the current head of the family is [[Leo Lefford]]. The Lefford sigil is a golden pile on sky blue, a sun to the right in the sky.

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
A Lefford knight travels to [[King's Landing]] to participate in the [[Heir's Tournament]], he is defeated by a [[House Mallister|Mallister]] knight.{{Ref|HOTD101}}

A member of House Lefford swears allegiance to King [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon II]] at the start of the [[Dance of the Dragons]].{{Ref|HOTD109}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Lord [[Jason Lannister]] starts amassing the armies of the [[Westerlands]], including the forces of House Lefford, at the [[Golden Tooth]].{{Ref|HOTD201}}

House Lefford has prepared 500 [[men-at-arms]] for the march to [[Harrenhal]].{{Ref|HOTD206}}

==In the books==
[[File:House Lefford ASOIAF.svg|thumb|200px|right|The Lefford coat of arms in the ''A Song of Ice and Fire'' novels: a golden inverted pile on sky-blue, a yellow sun in the first.]]
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Lefford of the Golden Tooth is a noble house from the Westerlands. Their coat of arms is a golden inverted pile on a sky blue field, a yellow sun in the first. The seat of the House is at the Golden Tooth. The castle controls the main entrance to the Westerlands from the east.

===Known members===
*Lord {Leo Lefford}, drowned during the [[Battle of the Fords#In the books|Battle of the Fords]].
*Lady Alysanne Lefford, Leo's heiress.

With unspecified familiar relationship to the main branch there are also:

*Leonella Lefford, the wife of [[Lothar Frey]]. She has four daughters: Tysane, Walda, Emberlei and Leana.
*Lady Myranda Lefford, the wife of Ser {[[Stafford Lannister]]}. She has three children: Ser Daven, Cerenna and Myrielle.
