'''Domonkos Pardanyi''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a set supervisor in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] episodes "[[Winter Is Coming]]", "[[The Kingsroad]]", "[[Lord Snow]]", "[[Cripples, Bastards, and Broken Things]]", "[[You Win or You Die]]", "[[The Pointy End]]" and "[[Fire and Blood]]" and a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] episodes "[[The Wolf and the Lion]]", "[[A Golden Crown]]", "[[The Pointy End]]" and "[[Baelor]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[The Prince of Winterfell]]", "[[Blackwater]]" and "[[Valar Morghulis]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episodes "[[And Now His Watch Is Ended]]" and "[[The Bear and the Maiden Fair (episode)|The Bear and the Maiden Fair]]".
