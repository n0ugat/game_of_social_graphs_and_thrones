Ser '''Royce Caron''' is a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Caron]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Royce Caron's son, Ser [[Desmond Caron]], is presented before Princess [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] when [[Lord Commander of the Kingsguard|Lord Commander]] [[Ryam Redwyne]] of the [[Kingsguard/House of the Dragon|Kingsguard]] passes away, and a vacancy becomes available. Ser [[Harrold Westerling]] speaks of Ser Desmond's father when he lists his various accomplishments.{{Ref|HOTD102}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Royce Caron is a prominent vassal of House Baratheon. He is particularly notable because his daughter Elenda is married to [[Borros Baratheon]], who is in line to Storm's End.
