'''George Georgiou''' is a British actor who plays [[Razdal mo Eraz]] in ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. He appeared in "[[The Bear and the Maiden Fair (episode)|The Bear and the Maiden Fair]]" in [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|Season 3]], and came back in [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|Season 6]]. George trained at the Drama Centre London and gone on to appear in various high profile TV and Films including The Honourable Woman, AD-Beyond the bible, The Americans, Bones, Law&Order and many more. On film he has small roles in Mamma Mia, RED2, 300-Rise of an Empire, The Cut. George gained most of his early acting experience in leading roles on the London stage in the West End, Off West End and Fringe theatres.
