'''Emun Elliott''' (born '''Emun Mohammadi''', 28 November 1983) is a Scottish actor. He appeared in the film ''[[wikipedia:Black Death (film)|Black Death]]'' with fellow ''Game of Thrones'' actors [[Sean Bean]] and [[Carice van Houten]]. He also had a recurring role in the television series ''[[wikipedia:Paradox (TV series)|Paradox]]''. In [[wikipedia:Ridley Scott|Ridley Scott]]'s film ''[[wikipedia:Prometheus (film)|Prometheus]]'' he plays the role of Chance, a starship pilot.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he portrayed the role of [[Marillion]]. He was confirmed in the role on 16 July 2010.
