'''Natalia Gastiain Tena''' (born 1 November 1984) is a British actress and musician. She is best known for playing the role of [[wikipedia:Nymphadora Tonks|Nymphadora Tonks]] in the ''[[wikipedia:Harry Potter|Harry Potter]]'' film series, which also featured [[David Bradley]], [[Michelle Fairley]], [[Freddie Stroma]], and [[Jim Broadbent]]. She also appeared in the film ''[[wikipedia:About a Boy|About a Boy]]''. As a musician she sings and plays accordion in the band [[wikipedia:Molotov Jukebox|Molotov Jukebox]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', Natalia Tena plays the role of [[Osha]] and was confirmed for the role on July 17, 2010. She initially appeared as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] and returned in this role for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third]] season. Tena had not appeared in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]]. On October 6, 2014, fellow actor [[Art Parkinson]], who plays the role of [[Rickon Stark]], confirmed that Tena's character would also not be returning for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]].<ref>[http://winteriscoming.net/2014/10/06/art-parkinson-confirms-rickon-osha-still-awol/ Art Parkinson Confirms Rickon, Osha Still AWOL]</ref> Tena's character returned in [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|Season 6]] for two episodes ("[[Oathbreaker]]" and "[[Book of the Stranger]]"), before her character was killed off.

In 2015, she co-starred in the TV series ''Residue'' alongside [[Iwan Rheon]] who portrayed [[Ramsay Bolton]] in ''Game of Thrones''.

In 2018, Tena portrayed the character Lana in the television show ''Origin''.
