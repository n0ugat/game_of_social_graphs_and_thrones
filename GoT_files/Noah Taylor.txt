'''Noah Taylor '''is an Australian actor. He has appeared in a number of well-known films including "''Shine''", "''Almost Famous''", "''Vanilla Sky''", "''The Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou''", "''Charlie and the Chocolate Factory''" and "''Lawless''". He also starred in the film "''Lara Croft: Tomb Raider''" with fellow "''Game of Thrones''" star [[Iain Glen]].

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he played [[Locke]], a renamed version of the popular character of '''Vargo Hoat''' from the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]].
