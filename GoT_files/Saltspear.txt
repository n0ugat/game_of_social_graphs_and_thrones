The '''Saltspear'''{{Guideref|War of the Five Kings}} is an inlet in the [[North]]. It is located in [[Blazewater Bay]] and west of [[Moat Cailin]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Saltspear is fed by the Fever River, a river in the [[Neck]]. It opens into Blazewater Bay.
