'''Kerry Danielle Ingram''' is an English actress. She plays Princess [[Shireen Baratheon]] in ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. She was announced in the role on 1 June 2012.<ref>[http://www.spoilertv.com/2012/06/game-of-thrones-season-3-shireen.html SpoilerTV]</ref> She appeared in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth]], and [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth]] seasons.

Ingram got her start in musical theater when she was nine years old, when her mother found her an audition for a local production of ''Annie ''where she played Molly. Afterwards, she worked her way up to West End productions. She won the Laurence Olivier Award for her role as "Matilda" in ''Matilda the Musical ''in which she played the role for 2 years starting in 2010 when the show first opened in Stratford Upon Avon before moving with it to London until 2012.<ref>[http://www.getreading.co.uk/entertainment/theatre_and_dance/s/2112159_matilda_star_kerry_ingram_thrilled_by_olivier_award Get Reading.co.uk]</ref> She has a minor part in the film ''[[wikipedia:Les Misérables (2012 film)|Les Misérables]].''

Other projects include singing at the Royal Albert hall along side Alan Clayton with ''The rings of akhaten ''from ''Doctor Who'' where she played a young girl named Merry for the 50th anniversary proms. Ingram has also this year appeared an episode of ''Doctors'' starring as Lios Wren and has filmed another project which is to be released in 2015.

Ingram has a form of [[Wikipedia:Osteogenesis imperfecta|brittle bone disease]] (''Osteogenesis imperfecta''). She has regularly fractured bones since she was born. Kerry manages to cope with the help of [[Wikipedia:Pamidronic acid|Pamidronate]] drug infusions which are delivered in four-hour sessions, once a day, for three days every four months at Heatherwood Hospital and Wexham Park. She has broken a total of around 30 bones in her life time. Her condition also makes her short for her age, Ingram is currently 5 feet tall which is a little below average and was told she had stopped growing completely around a year ago.<ref>[
https://www.maidenhead-advertiser.co.uk/news/10391/Redroofs-pupil-to-star-in-smash.html Redroofs pupil to star in smash-hit TV series Game of Thrones]</ref><ref>[http://www.getreading.co.uk/news/local-news/mum-pleads-heatherwood-hospital-not-4236318 Mum pleads with Heatherwood Hospital not to close wards]</ref>Kerry revealed at 2013 TitanCon that this made it easy for her to relate to Shireen.
