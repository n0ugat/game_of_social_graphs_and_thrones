'''Southshield'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is one of the four [[Shield Islands]], west of the [[Reach]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Southshield is ruled by House Serry. After [[Euron Greyjoy]] is elected King of the Isles and the North, he conquers Southshield and names Andrik the Unsmiling its lord.
