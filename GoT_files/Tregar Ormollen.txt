'''Tregar Ormollen'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} is a [[Lys]]ene merchant lord. [[Lynesse Hightower (daughter of Leyton)|Lynesse Hightower]], the estranged wife of [[Jorah Mormont]], is his concubine.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Tregar Ormollen's backstory is the same. He shares his manse in Lys with Lynesse Hightower, one of his many concubines- but even his lawful wife fears her.{{Bookref|ACOK|12}}

In ''A Dance with Dragons'', Jorah tells [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] that Tregar threatened to enslave him for debt, unless he gave Lynesse up and left the city; Jorah had no choice but to comply.{{Bookref|ADWD|27}}
