'''Felwood'''{{Ref|GOT105}} is a castle in the [[Stormlands]], the region controlled by [[House Baratheon]] of [[Storm's End]]. The castle is located north-west of Storm's End and south of [[King's Landing]], at the edge of the [[Kingswood]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Felwood is the seat of House Fell.
