This "'''little bird'''" is part of [[Varys]]'s intercontinental network of informants and spies, whom he refers to as "[[little birds]]".

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
He delivers a royal pardon to [[Jorah Mormont]] in the markets of [[Vaes Dothrak]]. Jorah realizes that the timing is suspicious and quickly returns to the side of [[Daenerys Targaryen]].{{Ref|GOT107}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, this character does not appear but Jorah does leave Daenerys in the markets outside [[Vaes Dothrak]] enigmatically citing personal business before reappearing. Varys is known for employing orphans and urchins as his "little birds".
