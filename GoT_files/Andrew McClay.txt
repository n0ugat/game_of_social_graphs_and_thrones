In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a Baratheon soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episode "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]" and a Stark soldier in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]" and "[[The Winds of Winter]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episode "[[The Queen's Justice]]", and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]", "[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms (Game of Thrones)|A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]] ", "[[The Long Night]]", "[[The Last of the Starks]]", "[[The Bells]]", and "[[The Iron Throne]]".

In the documentary ''[[Game of Thrones: The Last Watch]]'', McClay said his character was named [[Aberdolf Strongbeard]].<ref>[https://comicbook.com/tv-shows/2019/05/27/game-of-thrones-fans-celebrating-true-mvp-andrew-mcclay/ Game of Thrones Fans Are Celebrating the Show's True MVP Andrew McClay]</ref>
