'''Kimberley Pope''' is one of the award-winning major concept artists working on ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.

She has previously worked as a concept artist on other productions such as the motion pictures ''Elizabeth: The Golden Age'', ''Scott Pilgrim vs. The World'', and ''Attack the Block''.
