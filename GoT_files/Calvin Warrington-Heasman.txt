}}'''Calvin Warrington-Heasman''' is a stuntman based in the United Kingdom.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a Stone Man in the episode "[[Kill the Boy]]" and a Burning Lannister in the episode "[[The Spoils of War]]".

He was also a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[Sons of the Harpy (episode)|Sons of the Harpy]]", "[[Kill the Boy]]", "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]" and "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[Stormborn]]", "[[The Queen's Justice]]" and "[[The Spoils of War]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]", "[[The Long Night]]", "[[The Last of the Starks]]" and "[[The Bells]]".

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[The Red Dragon and the Gold]]" and "[[The Red Sowing]]".
