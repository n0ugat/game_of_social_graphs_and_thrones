'''Lord Confessor'''{{Ref|HOTD109}} is a position in the royal court of the [[Seven Kingdoms]]. The Lord Confessor is tasked with extracting information from people, mostly from prisoners and often by means of torture.

Lord [[Larys Strong]] served as Lord Confessor under [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]] and [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon II]].{{Ref|HOTD109}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there are several officials known as "confessors" overseen by the Lord Confessor, who is in turn overseen by the [[King's Justice]].{{Bookref|AFFC|27}}

[[Maegor Targaryen|Maegor I]]'s Lord Confessor was arrested upon the accession of [[Jaehaerys I Targaryen|Jaehaerys I]].{{Bookref|F&B|5}}

Larys Strong served as a confessor and later Lord Confessor under Viserys I and Aegon II.{{Bookref|F&B|12}}

Since the reign of [[Daeron II Targaryen|Daeron II]], the office of Lord Confessor has remained vacant.{{Bookref|AFFC|27}}
