'''Ramon Tikaram''' (born 16 May 1967) is a British actor of Indo-Fijian and Malaysian descent. He is best known for his recurring roles on British drama series ''[[wikipedia:This Life|This Life]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:EastEnders|EastEnders]]'', but has appeared in numerous films, TV shows and on stage. He voiced Dorian Pavus, a major supporting character, in the 2014 video game Dragon Age: Inquisition. Ramon currently voices [https://tardis.fandom.com/wiki/Vikram_Shindi Colonel Shindi ] in [https://tardis.fandom.com/wiki/UNIT:_The_New_Series UNIT: The New Series] .

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he played the role of [[Prendahl na Ghezn]]. He was announced in the role on 18 August 2012.<ref>[http://www.westeros.org/GoT/News/Entry/Two_More_for_S3_Ramon_Tikaram_Dan_Hildebrand_Join_Cast/ Westeros.org]</ref>
