'''Richard Roberts''' is a Set Decorator working on ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. He is particularly responsible for [[Food and drink|food prep]].

He has previously worked on the medieval detective series ''Mystery!: Cadfael'', and films such as ''Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith'', ''Elizabeth: The Golden Age'', ''The Da Vinci Code'', ''Wing Commander'', ''Titanic'', and all four of the Pierce Brosnan-era James Bond films (''Goldeneye'', ''Tomorrow Never Dies'', ''The World Is Not Enough'', and ''Die Another Day'').
