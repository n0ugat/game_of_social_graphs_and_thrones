'''Archie''' was a soldier in the who fought for [[House Lannister]]. He was killed in the [[Battle of the Goldroad]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Archie was known to frequent the brothels of [[King's Landing]] and was a favorite of [[Craya]]. He took part in the [[sack of Highgarden]] and later fought in the Battle of the Goldroad, where he was killed by [[Drogon]].{{Ref|GOT801}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
Archie was one of the soldiers mentioned by [[Marei]], [[Craya]], and [[Dirah]] when they discuss the casualties of the Battle of the Goldroad.{{Ref|GOT801}}
