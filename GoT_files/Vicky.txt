'''Vicky''' is a commoner from [[King's Landing]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
Shortly before the [[Battle of King's Landing]], the [[smallfolk]] are being evacuated into the [[Red Keep]]. When the gate to the Red Keep closes, a commoner shouts for Vicky.{{Ref|GOT805}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Vicky.
