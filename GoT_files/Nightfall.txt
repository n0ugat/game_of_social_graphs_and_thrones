'''Nightfall'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} was a [[Valyrian steel]] blade owned by [[Dalton Greyjoy]].<ref>"[[The Old Way]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, [[Dalton Greyjoy]] wielded Nightfall. A blade of unknown origin, it was taken from a dead pirate. Nightfall was eventually passed on to House Harlaw through unknown means. The Valyrian steel sword is currently in the possession of Ser Harras Harlaw.{{Bookref|AFFC|19}}{{Bookref|AFFC|29}}
