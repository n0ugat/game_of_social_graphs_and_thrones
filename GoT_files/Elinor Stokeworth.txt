'''Elinor Stokeworth''' is a member of [[House Stokeworth]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Elinor is the daughter of [[Lord Stokeworth]]. She is betrothed to the [[Gormon Massey's son|son]] of Lord [[Gormon Massey]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Alicent Hightower|Alicent]] and [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] gossip about her and her betrothed during the [[Heir's Tournament]]. Alicent alleges that Elinor is already pregnant.{{Ref|HOTD101}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', there is no character named Elinor Stokeworth. There is, however, a character named Elinor Massey, though she is a young maiden 20 years after this point in time.
