'''Grassy Vale'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in the [[Reach]], the region controlled by [[House Tyrell]] of [[Highgarden]]. The castle is located on the [[Blueburn]] river near the border with the [[Stormlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Grassy Vale is the seat of House Meadows. Its location is the same.
