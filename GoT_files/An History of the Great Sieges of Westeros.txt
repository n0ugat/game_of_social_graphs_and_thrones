'''''An History of the Great Sieges of Westeros'''''{{Ref|GOT208}} is a book detailing the history of the Great Sieges of [[Westeros]] by [[Archmaester]] [[Ch'Vyalthan]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
[[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] reads this book when preparing the defense of [[King's Landing]] from [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]'s impending [[Battle of the Blackwater|attack]]. [[Varys]] already read it, and while he says it is a thrilling subject, he muses on how Ch'Vyalthan was a flawed writer.{{Ref|GOT208}}
