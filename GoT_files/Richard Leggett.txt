'''Richard John Leggett''' is a stuntman.

In the [[Pilot (Bloodmoon)|pilot]] of ''[[Bloodmoon]]'', he was a stunt performer.

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episodes "[[Second of His Name]]", "[[King of the Narrow Sea (episode)|King of the Narrow Sea]]" and "[[We Light the Way]]".
