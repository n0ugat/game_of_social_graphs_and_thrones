|Appearances = "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]"}}'''Adam Basil''' is an actor and stuntman.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he portrayed a Strong Fighter in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episode "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]".

He was also a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]" and "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[Beyond the Wall]]" and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]".
