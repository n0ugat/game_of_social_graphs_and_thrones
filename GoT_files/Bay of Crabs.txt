The '''Bay of Crabs'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a large estuary that extends from the mouth of the [[Trident]], and empties into the [[Narrow Sea]]. It divides the [[Vale of Arryn]] from the [[Crownlands]] and the [[Riverlands]]. [[Gulltown]] and [[Wickenden]] are on its northern shore, [[Saltpans]] sits at the mouth of the Trident, and [[Maidenpool]] and [[Crackclaw Point]] sits on its southern shore.
