==Members==
*Ser {[[Alfred Broome]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] in service of [[House Targaryen]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of House Broome. In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', the Broomes were among the houses who sided with the [[Faith Militant uprising]]. Their castle was burned by King [[Maegor Targaryen|Maegor the Cruel]]'s dragon [[Balerion]].{{Bookref|F&B|4}}

Lady Lucinda Broome was married to Lord Prentys Tully.{{Bookref|F&B|6}}

It is unknown whether House Broome is related to [[awoiaf:House Broom|House Broom]], one of the principal vassals of [[House Lannister]]. In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', Ser Alfred Broome is shown wearing the sigil of House Broom on his armor, suggesting that House Broome and House Broom are in fact the same.
