The '''''Gay Abandon'''''{{Ref|HOTD206}} is a [[Pentos]]hi merchant cog.

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Corresponding with Prince [[Reggio Haratis]], Lady [[Jeyne Arryn]] arranges for [[Rhaena Targaryen|Rhaena]], [[Aegon Targaryen (son of Rhaenyra)|Aegon]], and [[Viserys Targaryen (son of Rhaenyra)|Viserys]] to be sent to [[Pentos]] on the ''Gay Abandon''.{{Ref|HOTD206}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', the ''Gay Abandon'' went to [[Dragonstone]] to retrieve Aegon and Viserys, not the [[Vale of Arryn|Vale]]. Rhaena was not sent with them.
