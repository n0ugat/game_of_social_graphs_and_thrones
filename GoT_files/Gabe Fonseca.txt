'''Gabe Fonseca''' is an American screenwriter and producer. He is a member of the writing staff on ''[[House of the Dragon]]'' for its [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]].

==Biography==
Gabe Fonseca previously worked as a staff writer on ''Private Practice'' (2011 to 2013) and ''The Night Shift'' (2016). He then worked as a production assistant on Marvel's ''Jessica Jones'' in its second season, in which he was also credited with writing one episode. In 2018 he was a producer on ''Wu-Tang: An American Saga'' (2019), for which he wrote two episodes.

Fonseca runs a YouTube channel named ''Cereal Time TV'', founded in 2013, where he reviews breakfast cereals.
