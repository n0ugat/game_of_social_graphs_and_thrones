'''Alexis Raben''' (born August 25, 1980) is a Russian-born American actress. She has appeared in television series such as ''{{WPS|Banshee (TV series)|Banshee}}'' and ''{{WPS|Krypton (TV series)|Krypton}}''. In 2006, she married English film director [[Miguel Sapochnik]].

Raben was a producer on the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] of ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', of which her husband was a showrunner. According to a report by ''Puck'', she could not return as a producer on the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]], with HBO citing her lack of experience. In the report, it is further claimed that Miguel Sapochnik left the series because of this.<ref>https://tvline.com/2023/01/03/miguel-sapochnik-wife-house-of-the-dragon-season-2-controversy/</ref>

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she portrayed Xela{{Cite}} in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[The Bells]]". In ''House of the Dragon'', she portrays [[Talya]] in the first season.
