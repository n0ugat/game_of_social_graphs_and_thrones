'''Harkon Umber''' was a member of [[House Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Harkon Umber was the firstborn son of Lord [[Hothor Umber]] and his wife, Lady [[Amarylis Umber|Amarylis]] of [[House Manderly]]. Born in [[183 AC]]{{Dateref|Harkon Umber}} at [[Last Hearth]], he had blue eyes, brown hair, and a fair complexion. He was tragically killed in a bear hunt at the age of fourteen, leaving no heirs behind.{{Ref|GOT104}}
