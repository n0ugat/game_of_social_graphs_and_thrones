'''Gary Lightbody''' (born 15 June 1976) is a Northern Irish singer, musician and songwriter. Since 1994 he has been the lead singer and rhythm guitarist in the band [[wikipedia:Snow Patrol|Snow Patrol]]. He is credited with writing "[[wikipedia:Chasing Cars|Chasing Cars]]", their biggest-selling and most famous song.

On 23 July 2012, Lightbody announced via Facebook that he was cameoing in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] of ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', including a picture of himself in costume<ref>[http://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10151059011597072&set=a.10150310316677072.362699.6191917071&type=1 Snow Patrol's Facebook page]</ref>. He told fans it was not a particularly large or notable role. Lightbody had previously revealed himself to be a huge fan of the series, hanging out with some of the actors in Belfast and even engaging in a Twitter dispute with a BBC Radio 1 DJ over spoilers for the series.

He played the Bolton soldier who sings "[[The Bear and the Maiden Fair]]."
