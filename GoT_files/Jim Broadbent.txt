'''Jim Broadbent''' (born 24 May 1949) is an English actor who portrayed [[Archmaester]] [[Ebrose]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] of ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.<ref>http://screencrush.com/jim-broadbent-game-of-thrones-character-maester/</ref><ref>http://www.ew.com/article/2016/08/31/game-thrones-jim-broadbent-season-7</ref>

Broadbent won the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor in 2001 for his role in ''Iris''. He is probably best known for his role as Horace Slughorn in the ''{{WPS|Harry Potter}}'' film series (with [[David Bradley]], [[Natalia Tena]], [[Freddie Stroma]], and [[Michelle Fairley]]), as Digory Kirke in ''The Chronicles of Narnia'', as Pod Clock in 1997's ''The Borrowers'', as DCI Roy Slater in ''Only Fools and Horses'', ''Moulin Rouge'', Chief Inspector Frank Butterman in ''Hot Fuzz'' (alongside [[Rory McCann]] and David Bradley), and numerous roles in ''Blackadder'' (alongside [[Tim McInnerny]] and [[Patrick Malahide]]).
