'''Faye Elaine Marsay''' is an English stage and screen actress. She is known for her role as Anne Neville in ''The White Queen'' and as the recurring character Candice in ''Fresh Meat''.

She also prominently appeared in the ''Doctor Who'' 2014 Christmas special as Shona - a character who was determined to marathon ''Game of Thrones'' over Christmas break.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she portrays the [[Waif]] in [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|Season 6]].
