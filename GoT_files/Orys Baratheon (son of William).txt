'''Orys Baratheon''' was an early member of [[House Baratheon]].

==Biography==
Orys was the son of [[William Baratheon]] and [[Martyna Mullendore]].<ref name="Lineages">''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]'' prop ([https://www.factinate.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/Catspaw-Season-1.png see image], Orys's father's entry is in the lower right)</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Orys Baratheon is mentioned in the section on House Baratheon in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. His name does not appear on screen.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
