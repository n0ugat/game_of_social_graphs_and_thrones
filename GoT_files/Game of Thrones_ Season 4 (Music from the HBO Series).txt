'''''Game of Thrones: Season 4 (Music from the HBO Series)''''' was composed by [[Ramin Djawadi]] and was released on June 10, 2014.<ref>[http://www.hypable.com/2014/06/10/game-of-thrones-season-4-soundtrack-available-today/ Game of Thrones season 4 soundtrack available today]</ref> It was preceded by ''[[Game of Thrones: Season 3 (Music from the HBO Series)]]'', released in 2013, and followed by ''[[Game of Thrones: Season 5 (Music from the HBO Series)]]'', released in 2015.

==Behind the scenes==
*On the vinyl edition of the soundtrack, Sigur Ros is misspelled.
*The seventh track on the album, "Mereen", is also misspelled instead of the actual spelling of [[Meereen]].
*"Feral Love" by [[Chelsea Wolfe]], "So Close to Being Free" by [[Eivør Pálsdóttir]] and "Turn Around" by [[Losers]] were featured in the trailers.
