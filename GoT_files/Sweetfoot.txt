==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Sweetfoot is a mare palfrey initially owned by Ser [[Arlan of Pennytree]]. She later passed into his [[squire]] Dunk's possession. She is calm and sure of foot.{{Bookref|THK}}
