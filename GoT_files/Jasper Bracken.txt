Ser '''Jasper Bracken''' was a [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Jasper was a member of [[House Bracken]]. He was elected Lord Commander of the [[Night's Watch]] after the death of [[Rodrik Velaryon]], and held the [[Wall]] for sixty-three years.<ref>[[:File:5x2 Night's Watch Book.jpg]]</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Jasper is mentioned in a book that [[Samwell Tarly]] is reading.{{Ref|GOT502}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Jasper Bracken.
