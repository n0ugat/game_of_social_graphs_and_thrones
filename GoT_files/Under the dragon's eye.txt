'''''Under the dragon's eye'''''{{Ref|HOTD103}} is the refrain of a song about the [[Rhoynar|Rhoynish]] warrior-queen [[Nymeria]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Samwell]], a royal minstrel, sings the song to [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] while she reads [[Ten Thousand Ships (book)|''Ten Thousand Ships'']]. She orders him to keep singing it after he finishes, but Queen [[Alicent Hightower|Alicent]] arrives and sends him away.{{Ref|HOTD103}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'' and the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, this song is neither sung nor mentioned.
