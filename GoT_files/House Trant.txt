'''House Trant'''{{Ref|GOT301}}{{Note|Meryn's surname is Trant.}} is a vassal house from the [[Stormlands]] that holds fealty to [[House Baratheon]].

==Members==
[[File:Meryn-Trant-201.jpg|thumb|Ser Meryn Trant, a member of House Trant.]]
*Ser {[[Meryn Trant]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] of the [[Kingsguard]]. Killed by [[Arya Stark]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Trant is a vassal house sworn to [[Storm's End]]. Their seat is Gallowsgrey (according to a semi-canon source), their sigil is a hanged man, sable, on a field of blue, and their words are "So End Our Foes".
