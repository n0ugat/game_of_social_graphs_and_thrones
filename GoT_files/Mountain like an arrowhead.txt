The '''mountain like an arrowhead'''{{Ref|GOT706}} is a landmark [[Beyond the Wall|north]] of [[Eastwatch-by-the-Sea]].{{Ref|GOT706}}

==History==
===Background===
Circa [[11200 BC]],{{Dateref|Night King}} the [[Night King]] was created by the [[Children of the Forest]] near this mountain.{{Ref|GOT605}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
[[Bran Stark|Bran]] [[Sight|observes]] the creation of the Night King near the mountain. He later sees the [[Wight|army of the dead]] assembled at the foot of the mountain, before being discovered and marked by the Night King.{{Ref|GOT605}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
[[Sandor Clegane|The Hound]] sees the mountain in a vision while staring into a fireplace. The dead are marching past.{{Ref|GOT701}}

The mountain serves as the location of a [[Wight hunt|battle]] between the [[White Walkers]] and [[Jon Snow|Jon]]'s party.{{Ref|GOT706}}

==Behind the scenes==
[[wikipedia:Kirkjufell|Kirkjufell]] (Icelandic: ''Church Mountain'') is a mountain on the north coast of the Sn√¶fellsnes in Iceland. It is 463 meters high and is the most photographed mountain in the country. It was called "Sukkertoppen" (Sugar peak) by Danish sailors and is sometimes described as the most peculiar if not the most beautiful mountain on the peninsula. It is possible to walk all the way around the mountain, as it is cut off from the rest of the mountain range. An average journey around the mountain takes about 3 hours.
