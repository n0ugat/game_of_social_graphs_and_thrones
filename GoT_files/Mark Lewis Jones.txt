'''Mark Lewis Jones''' (born 16 November 1964) is a Welsh actor, born in Rhosllanerchrugog, North Wales. He began acting with the Clwyd Youth Theatre in 1985 and has worked with the [[wikipedia:Royal Shakespeare Company|Royal Shakespeare Company]]. In television he is best known for his roles as police offers in ''[[wikipedia:55 Degrees North|55 Degrees North]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Murder Prevention|Murder Prevention]]'' and has also guest-starred in British television series such as ''[[wikipedia:Torchwood|Torchwood]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Merlin (TV series)|Merlin]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Waking the Death|Waking the Dead]]''. His biggest movie roles have been as Hogg the Whaler in ''[[wikipedia:Master and Commander: The Far Side of the World|Master and Commander: The Far Side of the World]]'', the soldier Tecton in ''[[wikipedia:Troy (film)|Troy]]'', Captain Moden Canady in ''[[wikipedia:Star Wars: The Last Jedi|Star Wars: The Last Jedi]]'', and Thomas Longstride in ''[[wikipedia:Robin Hood (2010 film)|Robin Hood]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he is playing the role of [[Shagga]] and was confirmed in the role on 20 August 2010.
