{{Quote|Present yourself to Borcas when you arrive; make no comment about his nose.|[[Order of Maesters|Maester]] [[Aemon]] to [[Dareon]]|You Win or You Die}} 
'''Borcas''' is a member of the [[Night's Watch]], and apparently chief among the stewards stationed at [[Eastwatch-by-the-Sea]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
When the newly graduated recruits are assigned to their orders [[Dareon]] is told by [[Order of Maesters|Maester]] [[Aemon]] to report to Borcas at [[Eastwatch-by-the-Sea]], and warned not to comment on his nose.{{Ref|GOT107}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
Following the [[Breaching of the Wall]], Borcas's fate remains unknown.{{Ref|GOT707}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Borcas is also mentioned, but the book makes no description whatsoever about his nose.
