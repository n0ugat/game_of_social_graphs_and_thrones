'''David Garrick''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[Blackwater]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episode "[[Two Swords]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episode "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episode "[[Stormborn]]".
