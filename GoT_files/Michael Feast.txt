}}'''Michael Feast''' (born 25 November 1946) is an English actor of stage and screen. He was born in {{WPS|Brighton}}, and trained at the {{WPS|Central School of Speech & Drama}}. He performed in the original 1968 London production of ''{{WPS|Hair (musical)|Hair}}''. He also worked several times with {{WPS|John Gielgud}}, whom he later played in {{WPS|Nicolas de Jongh}}'s biographical play ''{{WPS|Plague Over England}}''. He also had a significant role in the acclaimed TV series ''{{WPS|State of Play (TV serial)|State of Play}}''. He has also appeared in the films ''{{WPS|Velvet Goldmine}}'' and ''{{WPS|Sleepy Hollow (film)|Sleepy Hollow}}''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrays [[Aeron Greyjoy]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].
