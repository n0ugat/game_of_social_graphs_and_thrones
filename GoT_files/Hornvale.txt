'''Hornvale'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in the [[Westerlands]], the region controlled by [[House Lannister]] of [[Casterly Rock]]. The castle is located in the center of the region, among the mountains east of Casterly Rock. It is south of [[Golden Tooth]], near the headwaters of the [[Red Fork]] of the [[Trident]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Hornvale is the seat of House Brax, a loyal vassal house of the Lannisters.
