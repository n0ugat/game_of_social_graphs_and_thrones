}}'''Kelly Long''' is an Irish actress.<ref>[http://web.archive.org/web/20131207212039/http://thefilmlobby.com/users/77 The Film Lobby: Kelly Long] (archived December 7, 2013 from [http://www.thefilmlobby.com/users/77])</ref> She portrays [[Joyeuse Frey]], [[Walder Frey]]'s eighth wife, in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[Baelor]]" and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episode "[[The Rains of Castamere (episode)|The Rains of Castamere]]".

She also works as a stand-in for [[Emilia Clarke]], [[Michelle Fairley]], [[Lena Headey]], and [[Sophie Turner]]. She was a stunt horse riding double for [[Michelle Fairley]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[The Wolf and the Lion]]" and [[Carice van Houten]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episode "[[Garden of Bones (episode)|Garden of Bones]]". In Season 2, Long began working full-time for ''Game of Thrones'' art department.<ref>[http://www.thetimewarriors.co.uk/blog/?p=8002 EGâ€™s very own Princess, Kelly Long]</ref>
