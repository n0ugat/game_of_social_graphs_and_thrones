Lord '''Simon Staunton''' is the head of [[House Staunton]] and Lord of [[Rook's Rest]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
Lord Simon attends the [[Black Council]] at [[Dragonstone]], where he bickers with Lord [[Bartimos Celtigar]].{{Ref|HOTD110}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Simon is present at the Black Council when [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] returns from the [[Stormlands]].{{Ref|HOTD201}}

Simon is present at the Black Council when word of the [[assassination of Jaehaerys Targaryen]] and the subsequent propaganda campaign of the Greens to vilify Rhaenyra for ordering the killing reaches Dragonstone.{{Ref|HOTD202}}

When [[Baela Targaryen|Baela]] informs the Council she witnessed [[Criston Cole]] marching a Green army through the Crownlands, Simon fears that Cole may target Rook's Rest on his march to confront Prince [[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]] at [[Harrenhal]] and requests Rhaenyra's leave to depart Dragonstone so he may return to his seat and fortify it.{{Ref|HOTD203}}
[[File:Simon witnesses the army..png|thumb|Simon faces off against the Greens.]]
Simon commands the garrison of Rook's Rest when Criston Cole's Green army [[Battle at Rook's Rest|besiege]] it, and witness the aerial dragon battle between [[Meleys]], [[Sunfyre]], and [[Vhagar]].{{Ref|HOTD204}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Lord Staunton's first name is not stated. Variations of the name "Simon" are common in House Staunton. He was one of the lesser lords who sat on the black council, a version of the [[Small Council]] that advised Rhaenyra during the [[Dance of the Dragons]]. 

Whereas the fate of his show counterpart has not been mentioned, in ''Fire & Blood'' Lord Staunton is beheaded following the fall of Rook's Rest to the green army and his head is mounted above the Old Gate of King's Landing.
