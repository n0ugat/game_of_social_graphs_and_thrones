Lord '''Jason Mallister''' is the head of [[House Mallister]]. He is a loyal bannerman of [[House Tully]].

==Biography==
During the [[Greyjoy Rebellion]], Lord Jason led the victorious defense of his ancestral seat of [[Seagard]] against an [[Assault on Seagard|attack of ironborn forces]], led by [[Rodrik Greyjoy]], and killed him.{{Ref|H&L202}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Lord Jason Mallister is a loyal bannerman of House Tully. He has at least one son, named Patrek.

As a young knight, Lord Jason participated in a tourney at [[Storm's End]], where he was defeated by [[Rhaegar Targaryen|Rhaegar]]. During the early stage of [[Robert's Rebellion]], he attended the wedding of [[Eddard Stark]] and [[Catelyn Stark|Catelyn Tully]], giving them lavish gifts.{{Bookref|AGOT|28}} He participated in the [[Battle of the Trident]], slaying three of Rhaegar's bannermen, thus avenging the death of his kinsman Jeffory Mallister, who had been killed under orders of the [[Aerys II Targaryen|Mad King]].{{Bookref|AGOT|29}}

Lord Jason takes part in the [[Tournament#Tourney of the Hand|Tourney of the Hand]];{{Bookref|AGOT|29}} later, during the [[War of the Five Kings]], he takes part in the [[Battle of Whispering Wood|battle in the Whispering Wood]]{{Bookref|AGOT|63}} and the liberation of [[Riverrun]] at the [[Battle of the Camps]].{{Bookref|AGOT|69}} He returns to Seagard after [[Robb Stark|Robb]] is declared the [[King in the North]].

Lord Jason is one of the seven nobles who are chosen to launch the funeral boat of [[Hoster Tully]].{{Bookref|ASOS|35}} Jason meets Robb on his march north to the [[Twins]]. Robb orders him to send two ships into the [[Neck]], one carrying [[Galbart Glover]] and the other [[Maege Mormont]], to find [[Greywater Watch]].{{Bookref|ASOS|45}}

In the fourth novel, Jason is forced to surrender Seagard to the [[House Frey|Freys]], since they threaten to kill his son Patrek, who was taken captive at the [[Red Wedding]].{{Bookref|AFFC|38}} Currently he and his son are imprisoned in Seagard.
