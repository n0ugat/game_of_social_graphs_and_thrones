'''Spottswood'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a castle in eastern [[Dorne]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Spottswood is the seat of House Santagar.

Queen [[Rhaenys Targaryen (sister of Aegon I)|Rhaenys Targaryen]] descended upon Spottswood with her [[dragon]] [[Meraxes]] during the [[First Dornish War]], but found only old women and children.
