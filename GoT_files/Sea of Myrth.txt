The '''Sea of Myrth'''{{Mapref|Viewer's Guide}} is a large inlet of the [[Narrow Sea]], located in south-western [[Essos]] where it separates the [[Disputed Lands]] from the lands further north. The [[Free Cities|Free City]] of [[Myr]] lies on the eastern coast of the Sea of Myrth, while [[Tyrosh]] lies on an island at the mouth of the sea.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Sea of Myrth is in the same location.
