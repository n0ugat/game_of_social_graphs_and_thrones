'''Fair Isle'''{{Ref|H&L201}} is an island of the [[Sunset Sea]], located near the coast of the [[Westerlands]]. The island is the site of [[Faircastle]]. During the [[Greyjoy Rebellion]], [[Stannis Baratheon]] destroyed the [[Iron Fleet]] in a [[sea battle off Fair Isle]].<ref>"[[Greyjoy Rebellion - Theon Greyjoy]]"</ref><ref>"[[Greyjoy Rebellion - Stannis Baratheon]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Fair Isle is controlled by House Farman, a vassal of [[House Lannister]].
