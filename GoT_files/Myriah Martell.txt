Queen '''Myriah Martell''' was the wife of [[Daeron II Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Myriah Martell was a [[Dorne|Dornish]] princess of [[House Martell]] of [[Sunspear]]. She married King [[Daeron II Targaryen]] and became the mother of his four sons: [[Baelor Targaryen|Baelor]], [[Aerys Targaryen (son of Daeron II)|Aerys]], [[Rhaegel Targaryen|Rhaegel]], and [[Maekar Targaryen|Maekar]].{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
Queen Myriah, along with her progeny, is mentioned in [[House Targaryen]]'s entry of the book ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Queen Myriah married Daeron II as part of the marriage pact that brought [[Dorne]] under the rule of the [[Iron Throne]].
