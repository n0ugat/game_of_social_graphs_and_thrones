'''Rila Fukushima''' is a Japanese model and actress. She is best known for her co-starring role in ''The Wolverine'' and for her role as Tatsu Yamashiro/Katana in  ''Arrow''.

In [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] she portrayed a [[Red priestess (High Sparrow)|red priestess]].<ref>[http://watchersonthewall.com/the-trial-of-benioff-and-weiss/#comment-289812]</ref>
