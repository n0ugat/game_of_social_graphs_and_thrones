'''Calla''' is a Meereenese noble.

==Biography==
===Background===
Calla is the seven-year-old daughter of [[Great Masters|Master]] [[Mighdal]], one of the [[Great Masters]] of [[Meereen]]. She was tutored by a [[slavery|slave]] named [[Fennesz]] before [[Daenerys Targaryen]] abolished slavery in the city.{{Ref|GOT410}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
When Fennesz explains his background to Daenerys, he mentions that Calla has come to admire her after learning about [[House Targaryen|her family's history]] and her accomplishments.{{Ref|GOT410}}

==Behind the scenes==
The name "Calla" was selected by the show writer [[D.B. Weiss]] in honor of Carla, the wife of his beloved childhood friend David Migdal ([[Mighdal]]).

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no equivalent to this character. The only other person named "Calla" is Calla Blackfyre, a daughter of [[Daemon Blackfyre]].
