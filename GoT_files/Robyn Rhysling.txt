Ser '''Robyn Rhysling'''{{Con|D&EN}} is a [[Knighthood|knight]] during the reign of [[Daeron II Targaryen|Daeron II]].{{Ref|H&L416}}

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], Robyn is a grizzled old knight with a salt-and-pepper beard.

In 204 AC, he lost an eye to a splinter from a broken lance.
