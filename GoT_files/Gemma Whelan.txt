'''Gemma Elizabeth Whelan''' (born 23 April 1981) is a British actress and comedian who has appeared in films such as ''[[wikipedia:Gulliver's Travels (2010 film)|Gulliver's Travels]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:The Wolfman (2010 film)|The Wolfman]]''. In 2010 she won the Funny Women Variety Act at the Leicester Square Theatre for her character of Chastity Butterworth.

On ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Whelan played the role of [[Yara Greyjoy]], renamed from Asha Greyjoy in the books. Rumors of her casting leaked on 7 July 2011<ref>[http://winter-is-coming.net/2011/07/asha-renamed-and-cast/ Winter is Coming]</ref> but were not officially confirmed by [[HBO]] until 2 August 2011.<ref>[http://insidetv.ew.com/2011/08/02/game-of-thrones-greyjoy-asha/ Entertainment Weekly]</ref> She joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] and returned for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth]], [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth]], and [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh]] seasons.
