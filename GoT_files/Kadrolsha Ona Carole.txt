'''Kadrolsha Ona Carole''' is an actress and paranormal investigator.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she portrayed a North Woman Rider in "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]", a Meereen Guard in "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]" and a Winter Town Woman in "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]".
