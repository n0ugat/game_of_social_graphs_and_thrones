'''Morne'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a castle on [[Tarth]] in the [[Stormlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Morne is a ruined castle that once served as the seat of petty kings who ruled from the eastern shore of Tarth. According to Maester Hubert, Morne was built by the [[Andals]], not the [[First Men]].
