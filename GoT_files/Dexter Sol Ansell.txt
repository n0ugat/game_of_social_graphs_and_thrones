'''Dexter Sol Ansell''' (born September 16, 2014) is an English actor. He has portrayed a young {{WPS|Coriolanus Snow}} in ''{{WPS|The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes}}'' and has appeared in television series such as ''{{WPS|Emmerdale}}'' and ''{{WPS|The Midwich Cuckoos (TV series)|The Midwich Cuckoos}}''.
