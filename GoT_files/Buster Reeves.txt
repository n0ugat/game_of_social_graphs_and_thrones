'''Buster Reeves''' (born January 7, 1975) is a British stuntman and actor who served as the stunt coordinator for the first season of ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. He did not return after [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|Season 1]].

He has worked as a stuntman in a number of large Hollywood productions, including ''{{WPS|Troy (film)|Troy}}'', ''{{WPS|War of the Worlds (2005 film)|War of the Worlds}}'', ''{{WPS|Batman Begins}}'' and ''{{WPS|Clash of the Titans (2010 film)|Clash of the Titans}}''. He has also appeared in uncredited minor roles in ''{{WPS|The Dark night (film)|The Dark Knight}}'' and ''{{WPS|Terminator: Salvation}}''.
