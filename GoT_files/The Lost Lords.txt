"'''The Lost Lords'''"{{Ref|ATGS02}} is the second episode of ''[[Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series]]''. It premiered on February 3, 2015. It was written by Nicole Martinez, Meghan Thornton, and Brad Kane, and directed by Kent Mudle.
