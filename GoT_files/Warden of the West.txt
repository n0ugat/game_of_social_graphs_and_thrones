'''Warden of the West'''{{Ref|GOT108}} is a title traditionally held by the [[Lord of Casterly Rock]], the head of [[House Lannister]] of [[Casterly Rock]], since [[Loren I Lannister]], the last [[King of the Rock]], [[Bending the knee|submitted]] to [[Aegon I Targaryen]]. Previously held by [[Lordship|Lord]] [[Tywin Lannister]] and [[King|Queen]] [[Cersei Lannister|Cersei I Lannister]], the title is currently held by Lord [[Tyrion Lannister]].
