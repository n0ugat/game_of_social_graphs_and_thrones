A '''wildling girl''' was a [[wight]] in the [[Haunted Forest]].

==Biography==
===Background===
She was originally a member of the [[Free Folk]] before turning into a wight.{{Ref|GOT101}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
During his investigation of a [[Free Folk|wildling]] camp, [[Will]] of the [[Night's Watch]] observes her corpse nailed to a tree. A short time later, he sees her again, now standing and walking; she turns to him, and is revealed to have the blue eyes of a [[wight]]. Will flees before she reacts in any way.{{Ref|GOT101}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]]===
When [[Bran Stark|Bran]] is on the other side of the [[Wall]], he uses the [[Sight]] on a [[heart tree]] and sees the wildling girl in his vision.{{Ref|GOT402}}
