}}'''Nonso Anozie''' is an English actor. After several notable appearances on the London stage, his first film appearance was 2007's ''[[wikipedia:The Last Legion|The Last Legion]]'', in which his co-stars included [[Iain Glen]], [[Owen Teale]], [[Alexander Siddig]], [[Robert Pugh]], [[James Cosmo]], [[Murray McArthur]] and [[Thomas Brodie-Sangster]]. Anozie's other films include ''[[wikipedia:Atonement (film)|Atonement]]'', ''[[wikipedia:RocknRolla|RocknRolla]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Nanny McPhee and the Big Bang|Nanny McPhee and the Big Bang]]'', ''[[wikipedia:Conan the Barbarian (2011 film)|Conan the Barbarian]]'', and ''[[wikipedia:Ender's Game|Ender's Game]]''.

On stage, he is noted for being one of the youngest actors to portray ''[[wikipedia:King Lear|King Lear]]'' and won the [[wikipedia:Ian Charleson Award|Ian Charleson Award]] in 2005 for his portrayal of William Shakespeare's Othello.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he is playing the role of [[Xaro Xhoan Daxos]] and was officially announced in the role on 2 August 2011. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
