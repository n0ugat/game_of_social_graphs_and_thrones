'''Willam'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} was the oldest brother of [[Ray]], a [[septon]] in the [[Riverlands]].

==Biography==
Willam was a potboy from a village. He fought alongside his brothers [[Owen]] and [[Ray]] in the [[War of the Ninepenny Kings]], in which he collapsed during a march and died of a fever.{{Ref|H&L604}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Willam's story is the same, except that he is the brother of Septon Meribald, not Ray, and he shared his fate with his brother Robin.
