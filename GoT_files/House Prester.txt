'''House Prester'''{{Guideref|Viewer's Guide}} of [[Feastfires]] is a [[Noble house|vassal house]] that holds fealty to [[House Lannister]] of [[Casterly Rock]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Prester is the same. Their sigil is a red ox on ermine and their motto is "Tireless."

===Known members===
*Lord Garrison Prester, Lord of Feastfires.
**Ser Forley Prester, his cousin.
