'''Reuben Williams''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episodes "[[Second of His Name]]", "[[We Light the Way]]", "[[The Princess and the Queen]]" and "[[The Lord of the Tides]]" and the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[A Son for a Son]]", "[[Rhaenyra the Cruel]]" and "[[The Red Dragon and the Gold]]".
