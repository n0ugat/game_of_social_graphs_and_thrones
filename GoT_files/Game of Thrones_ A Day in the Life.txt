'''''Game of Thrones: A Day in the Life'''''{{Ref|ADITL}} is a half-hour documentary following a day of production of the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] of ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' across its sets spanning three different countries: Belfast, Northern Ireland; Dubrovnik, Croatia; Sevilla and Osuna, Spain.<ref>[https://ew.com/article/2015/01/19/game-of-thrones-season-5-special/?hootPostID=a7fc93aeb91f3cf02e78fda0036b0096 'Game of Thrones' season 5 'mystery' special details revealed]</ref>

==Description==
Glimpse the epic scale of ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' in this featurette that spends one day touring [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]] sets in Croatia, Spain and Ireland.<ref name="HBO Max" />
