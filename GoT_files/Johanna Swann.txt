'''Johanna Swann''' is a lady of [[House Swann]] during the reign of [[Viserys I Targaryen|Viserys I]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
While present at a hunt in the [[Kingswood]], Queen [[Alicent Hightower]] and the ladies of the court discuss how Johanna Swann has been kidnapped by the [[Triarchy]] during the [[War for the Stepstones]]. Alicent expresses concern when learning that she is to be [[Slavery|sold]] to a pleasure house in [[Lys]].{{Ref|HOTD103}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Johanna Swann's fate is much the same.
