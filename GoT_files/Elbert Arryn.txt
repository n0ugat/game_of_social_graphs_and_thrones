Ser '''Elbert Arryn'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} was a [[Knighthood|knight]] of [[House Arryn]] and nephew and heir to [[Defender of the Vale|Lord]] [[Jon Arryn]].

==Biography==
After [[Lyanna Stark]] was allegedly [[Abduction of Lyanna Stark|kidnapped]] by [[Prince of Dragonstone|Prince]] [[Rhaegar Targaryen]], her brother [[Brandon Stark|Brandon]] rode to [[King's Landing]] with four companions, including Elbert. Brandon demanded justice from [[King of the Andals and the Rhoynar and the First Men|King]] [[Aerys II Targaryen]], but the [[king]] responded by [[Execution|executing]] the group.{{Ref|H&L111}}
