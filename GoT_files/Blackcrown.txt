'''Blackcrown'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in the [[Reach]], the region controlled by [[House Tyrell]] of [[Highgarden]]. The castle is located southwest of Highgarden and [[Oldtown]], on the northern side of Whispering Sound, the estuary of the River [[Honeywine]]. The castle overlooks the [[Redwyne Straits]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Blackcrown is the seat of House Bulwer.
