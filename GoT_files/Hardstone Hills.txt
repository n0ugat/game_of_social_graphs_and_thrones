The '''Hardstone Hills'''{{Guideref|Great Wyk}} are where [[Hammerhorn]], the seat of [[House Goodbrother]], is located.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Hardstone Hills are a series of hills on the island of Great Wyk. It is the source of ores which are mined by the [[smallfolk]] in service to House Goodbrother.
