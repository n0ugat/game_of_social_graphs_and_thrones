The '''Street of the Sisters''',{{Scriptref|HOTD101}} also called the '''Street of Sisters''',{{Ref|GOT104}} is a street in [[King's Landing]] that runs from the [[Dragonpit]] to the [[Great Sept of Baelor]].

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]] and [[Alicent Hightower|Alicent]] ride a wheelhouse through the Street of the Sisters on their way to the [[Red Keep]].{{Ref|HOTD101}}{{Scriptref|HOTD101}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Street of Sisters is one of four streets that starts at the great square in the centre of [[King's Landing]], with the other three being the God's Way, the King's Way and the Muddy Way. The [[Guildhall|Guildhall of the Alchemists]] is located upon it and the [[Prostitution|brothel]] known as the House of Kisses is located at the end of the street atop Visenya's Hill.

Lady Baela Targaryen was known to take part in drunken [[horse]] races along the street.

In ''A Game of Thrones'', a drunken horse race takes place along the street during the build up to the [[Hand of the King|Hand]]'s [[Tournament|Tourney]]. [[Arya Stark|Arya]] travels along the street when she makes her way to the Great Sept of Baelor, to witness the [[Execution of Eddard Stark|her father's execution]].
