'''Martha''' is a [[Northmen|Northern]] girl and one of the [[little birds]] working for [[Varys]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Martha is a girl from Winterfell who works in the kitchens and secretly spying for Varys.{{Ref|GOT805}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 8|''Game of Thrones'': Season 8]]===
She is at [[Winterfell]] during the [[Battle of Winterfell]], hiding in the crypt with Varys. They hide behind the statue of [[Lyanna Stark]] when the dead are [[Wight|reanimated]] by the [[Night King]].{{Ref|GOT803}}

Later, she works in the kitchens at [[Dragonstone]]. She reports to Varys that [[Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys]] has not been accepting food, to which Varys responds that they will "try again tomorrow", suggesting that Varys is attempting to [[poison]] Daenerys.{{Ref|GOT805}}

==Behind the scenes==
Martha is portrayed by [[Bronte Carmichael]], and appears in both of the season 8 episodes directed by [[Miguel Sapochnik]].<ref>https://www.independenttalent.com/actors/bronte-carmichael/</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character by that name.
