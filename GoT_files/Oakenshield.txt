'''Oakenshield'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is one of the four [[Shield Islands]], west of the [[Reach]]. It has at least two settlements, [[Oakenshield Castle]] and [[Hewett's Town]].{{Ref|THTDB201}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Oakenshield is ruled by House Hewett. Owen Oakenshield was a son of Garth Greenhand who conquered the Shield Islands, but his relation to House Hewett is unknown.

After he is elected King of the Isles and the North, [[Euron Greyjoy]] conquers the island and names Nute the Barber its new lord.
