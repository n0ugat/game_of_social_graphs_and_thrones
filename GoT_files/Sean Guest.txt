'''Sean Guest''' is an American director who served as first assistant director for the [[Winter Is Coming|first episode]] of ''[[Game of Thrones]].'' Sean Guest was the first assistant director for ''Death at a Funeral'' which also stars [[Peter Dinklage]] (the actor who plays [[Tyrion Lannister]]).
