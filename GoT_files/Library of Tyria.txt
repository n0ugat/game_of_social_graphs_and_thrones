The '''Library of Tyria'''<ref>[https://dedalvs.com/work/house-of-the-dragon/misc/shrubbery_v4.pdf ''Shrubbery'' version 4 | dedalvs.com]</ref> was a library in the [[Valyrian Freehold|Valyrian]] city of [[Tyria]]. It was the largest and most renowned repository of knowledge of its day, considered greater even than the [[Citadel]] of [[Oldtown]]. It was destroyed along with the city during the [[Doom of Valyria]].

==In the books==
In ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels and their supplementary material, there is no mention of the Library of Tyria.
