The '''Dragonmont'''{{Ref|HOTD110}} is a volcano on the island [[Dragonstone (island)|Dragonstone]] in [[Blackwater Bay]]. It is used as a lair for [[dragon]]s.

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]] climbs into the Dragonmont to retrieve three eggs laid by [[Syrax]].{{Ref|HOTD108}}

At the start of the [[Dance of the Dragons]], Daemon enters the Dragonmont and summons [[Vermithor]] with a [[dragonsong]].{{Ref|HOTD110}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
[[File:S2 Dragonkeepers.png|thumb|Platform for dragonriders.]]The [[dragonrider]]s use a cave in the Dragonmont as a place to land their dragons.{{Ref|HOTD201}}{{Ref|HOTD204}}{{Ref|HOTD206}}

Ser [[Steffon Darklyn]] attempts to claim [[Seasmoke]] in the Dragonmont, and is burned alive.{{Ref|HOTD206}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Dragonmont is a rich source of [[dragonglass]]. On the foot of the volcano are villages bound to the castle [[Dragonstone]].
