}}'''Tim Plester''' is an English actor. He has appeared in several TV series such as ''Silent Witness'', ''Casualty'', ''Life On Mars'','' Doctors'', ''Waterloo Road'', ''The Bill'' and ''Doctor Who''. He has also appeared in feature films like ''It's All Gone Pete Tong'' (alongside [[Paul Kaye]]), ''Kick-Ass ''and ''Lockout''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he is playing [[Walder Rivers]]. He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]], and returned to reprise his role in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].

He is also a playwright. He wrote an award winning short film called ''Ant Muzak'' (2002), which starred fellow third season cast member MacKenzie Crook.
