'''Zabhad'''<ref name="Map">[[:File:HotD Jade Gates Map.jpg]]</ref> is a city on the [[Isle of Elephants]],<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> located in the [[Jade Sea]].<ref name="Map" />

==In the books==
In ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', Zabhad is the chief port of the Isle of Elephants. Lord [[Corlys Velaryon]] visited the island on one of his nine voyages, reporting that it was ruled by a ''shan'', who rules from a palace made of ivory.
