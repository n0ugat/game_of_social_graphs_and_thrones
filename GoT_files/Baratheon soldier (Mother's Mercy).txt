A '''Baratheon soldier'''<ref name="Script"/> fought for King [[Stannis Baratheon]] in the [[War of the Five Kings]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
This soldier serves in [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]'s army, and participates in the [[battle in the ice]]. He is one of the few to survive the initial fighting, but is later killed by [[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay]], although he surrenders.{{Ref|GOT510}}
