'''Sean Francis Bobbitt''' (born 29 November 1958) is a Texan cinematographer who served as director of photography for the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] of ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.

He has worked on films such as ''The Place Beyond the Pines'' and ''12 Years a Slave''.
