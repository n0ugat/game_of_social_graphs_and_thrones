'''Rowan''' is a woman living during the reign of [[Daeron II Targaryen]].<ref name="Redanian1">https://x.com/RedanianIntel/status/1815803857110257706</ref>

==In the books==
In the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]], there is no character named Rowan. There are two characters named Rowan elsewhere: in the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Rowan is a [[Free Folk|wildling]] spearwife; in ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', Rowan Gold-Tree is an ancestor of [[House Rowan]].
