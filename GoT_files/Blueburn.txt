The '''Blueburn'''{{Mapref|Viewer's Guide}} is a river in the [[Reach]], the region of [[Westeros]] under the control of [[House Tyrell]] of [[Highgarden]]. It is a tributary of the [[Mander]]. It flows west from headlands near the border with the [[Stormlands]] into the Mander near [[Longtable]]. It runs past [[Grassy Vale]].
