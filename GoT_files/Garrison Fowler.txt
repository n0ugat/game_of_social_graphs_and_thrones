King '''Garrison Fowler''' was the last king of House Fowler.

==Biography==
After being defeated in [[Nymeria's War]], Garrison was sent to the [[Wall]] in golden chains by [[Nymeria]].{{Ref|H&L509}}

==In the books==
In ''The World of Ice & Fire'', Garrison Fowler is described as aged and blind. Despite this, he was still feared for his cunning.
