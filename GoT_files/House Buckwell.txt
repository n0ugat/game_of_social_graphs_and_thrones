'''House Buckwell''' of the [[Antlers]] is a [[noble house]] from the [[Crownlands]]. Their sigil is a golden rack of antlers on vair.<ref name="Intro" />

==History==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
House Buckwell declared for [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].<ref name="Intro" />

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the words of House Buckwell are "Pride and Purpose." Their involvement in the Dance of the Dragons is unknown.

===Members===
*Lord Buckwell, one of [[Maegor Targaryen|Maegor I Targaryen]]'s last supporters.
*Ser Jarman Buckwell, a senior [[ranger]] of the [[Night's Watch]].
