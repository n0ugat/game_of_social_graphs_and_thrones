Lord '''Gunthor Darklyn''' was the head of [[House Darklyn]] and Lord of [[Duskendale]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Gunthor is the father of Ser [[Steffon Darklyn]], the [[Lord Commander of the Kingsguard|Lord Commander]] of [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]]'s [[Kingsguard/House of the Dragon|Queensguard]].{{Ref|HOTD204}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
[[Daemon Targaryen|Daemon]] orders [[Bartimos Celtigar]] to send a raven to Lord Darklyn. Maester [[Gerardys]] later confirms that House Darklyn has declared for [[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]].{{Ref|HOTD110}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
Lord Gunthor refuses to bend the knee to [[Aegon II Targaryen]] after the [[sack of Duskendale]], one of the first military victories by Ser [[Criston Cole]] at the start of the [[Dance of the Dragons|war]]. He is subsequently [[Execution|beheaded]] by Ser Criston, causing his soldiers to swear fealty to Aegon and join the [[Greens]].{{Ref|HOTD204}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Gunthor was a member of the [[Black Council|black council]]. He was the nephew of Ser [[Steffon Darklyn]], not his father.
