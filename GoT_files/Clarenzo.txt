'''Clarenzo''' is a member of [[Izembaro]]'s [[Mummers|comedic theatre troupe]] in [[Braavos]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 6|''Game of Thrones'': Season 6]]===
Clarenzo plays [[Joffrey Baratheon]] in a production of ''[[The Bloody Hand]]'', and is later seen by [[Arya Stark]] backstage complaining of several warts on his penis.{{Ref|GOT605}}

==In the books==
In the novels version of ''The Bloody Hand'', Joffrey Baratheon is also a character, but the name of the performer has yet to be revealed.
