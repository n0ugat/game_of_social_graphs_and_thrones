'''Neyela Frey '''is one of the many granddaughters of [[Walder Frey]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Neyela is one of the many granddaughters of [[Walder Frey]], the [[Lord of the Crossing]].{{Ref|GOT309}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
Along with several of Lord Frey's other daughters and granddaughters, [[Robb Stark]] had the option of marrying her. When he arrives at the [[Twins]] for his uncle [[Edmure Tully|Edmure's]] [[Red Wedding|wedding]], Robb apologizes for his slight against them.{{Ref|GOT309}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there's no mention of a granddaughter of Lord Walder Frey named "Neyela", nor for that matter are there any other Freys named "Neyela".
