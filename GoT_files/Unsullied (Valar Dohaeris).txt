==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]===
[[Kraznys mo Nakloz]] cuts off his nipple to demonstrate the obedience of the Unsullied and their inability to feel pain for [[Daenerys Targaryen]]. The [[Unsullied]] does not flinch while Kraznys mutilates him and then thanks his master for having been of service.{{Ref|GOT301}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, this Unsullied is a towering [[Lys]]ene youth with blue eyes and flaxen hair.
