'''Ellyn Baratheon''' is a noblewoman from [[House Baratheon/House of the Dragon|House Baratheon]].

==Biography==
Ellyn is a daughter of Lord [[Borros Baratheon]] of [[Storm's End]]. She has three sisters: [[Cassandra Baratheon|Cassandra]], [[Maris Baratheon|Maris]], and [[Floris Baratheon|Floris]].{{Ref|HOTD109}}

==Family==
{{Baratheon tree (House of the Dragon)}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Ellyn and her sisters Cassandra, Maris, and Floris were known as the "Four Storms". Their mother was Lady Elenda of [[House Caron]]. Not much is known about Ellyn among the four of them: Cassandra was the eldest, Floris the prettiest, and Maris the cleverest. Ellyn may have been the youngest.
