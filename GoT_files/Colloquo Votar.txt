'''Colloquo Votar''' is the author of the ''[[Jade Compendium]]'', a book considered a major source of legends from [[Qarth]], [[Yi Ti]], and other lands around the [[Jade Sea]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
Colloquo Votar and his ''Jade Compendium'' are mentioned on a page that [[Gilly]] is seen reading in a history book.{{Ref|GOT701}}<ref>[[:File:GoT-Sn7 FirstLook 04.jpg]]</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Colloquo Votar is stated to have been an adventurer from [[Volantis]]. He is apparently long dead by the time of the main novels, though exactly when he lived is unclear.
