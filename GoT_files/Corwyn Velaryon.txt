'''Corwyn Velaryon''' was a member of [[House Velaryon]].

==Biography==
Corwyn was the son of Lord [[Daemon Velaryon]], and father of [[Corlys Velaryon|Corlys]] and [[Vaemond Velaryon]]. The ship ''[[Corwyn]]'' is named after him.{{Ref|HOTD202}}
