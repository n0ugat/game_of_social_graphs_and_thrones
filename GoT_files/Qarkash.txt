'''Qarkash'''<ref>[[:File:HotD Far East Map.jpg]]</ref> is a town in [[Essos]], located west of [[Qarth]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Qarkash was built by the ancient Qaathi civilization, the ancestors of the Qartheen.
