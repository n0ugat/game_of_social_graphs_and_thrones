'''Ziggy Barker''' is an English child actress.

Barker played the infant [[Helaena Targaryen]] in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] of ''[[House of the Dragon]]''. Her sister [[Lulu Barker|Lulu]] portrays [[Jaehaera Targaryen]] in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]].<ref>[https://www.derbyshiretimes.co.uk/news/people/it-was-an-amazing-experience-peak-district-girl-makes-screen-debut-in-hbo-hit-house-of-the-dragon-4675484 "It was an amazing experience": Peak District girl makes screen debut in HBO hit House of the Dragon (derbyshiretimes.co.uk)]</ref>
