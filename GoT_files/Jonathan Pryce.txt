|imdb = http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000596/|Last = "[[The Winds of Winter]]"}}'''Sir Jonathan Pryce''' CBE (born '''John Price''', 1 June 1947) is a Welsh actor who has appeared in films such as ''Brazil'', ''Jumpin' Jack Flash'', ''The Adventures of Baron Munchausen'', ''Glengarry Glen Ross'', ''Evita'', ''Tomorrow Never Dies'', ''Stigmata'', ''Ronin'' (alongside fellow ''Game of Thrones'' star [[Sean Bean]]) and the ''Pirates of the Caribbean '' (also with [[Mackenzie Crook]]) and ''G.I. Joe ''film series.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' he plays the part of the [[High Sparrow]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth]] seasons.

He is currently starring the BBC/FX period drama series ''Taboo'', along with his ''Game of Thrones'' co-stars [[Jefferson Hall]], [[Nicholas Blane]], [[Oona Chaplin]], [[Roger Ashton-Griffiths]], and [[Mark Gatiss]].
