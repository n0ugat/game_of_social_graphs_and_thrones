'''Jonathan Paul Cohen''' is a stuntman and actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[Fire and Blood]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[Blackwater]]" and "[[Valar Morghulis]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]] episode "[[And Now His Watch Is Ended]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|fourth season]] episodes "[[The Mountain and the Viper]]", "[[The Watchers on the Wall]]" and "[[The Children]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|fifth season]] episodes "[[The Wars To Come]]", "[[Sons of the Harpy (episode)|Sons of the Harpy]]", "[[Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken]]", "[[Hardhome (episode)|Hardhome]]", "[[The Dance of Dragons (episode)|The Dance of Dragons]]" and "[[Mother's Mercy]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] episodes "[[Oathbreaker]]" and "[[Battle of the Bastards (episode)|Battle of the Bastards]]", the [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]] episodes "[[The Spoils of War]]", "[[Eastwatch]]" and "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]"  and the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episodes "[[The Long Night]]", "[[The Last of the Starks]]" and "[[The Bells]]" and a stunt player in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]] episodes "[[The North Remembers]]", "[[The Night Lands]]", "[[Garden of Bones (episode)|Garden of Bones]]", "[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]", "[[The Old Gods and the New]]", "[[A Man Without Honor]]" and "[[The Prince of Winterfell]]".

In ''[[House of the Dragon]]'', he was a stunt performer in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 1|first season]] episode "[[We Light the Way]]".
