'''Janei Lannister''' is the daughter of [[Kevan Lannister|Kevan]] and [[Dorna Lannister]], the younger sister of [[Lancel Lannister|Lancel]] and [[Martyn Lannister|Martyn]] and the older sister of [[Willem Lannister]].<ref name="finalebook">[https://www.makinggameofthrones.com/production-diary/season-8-episode-6-series-finale-props-song-of-ice-and-fire See] the family tree of [[House Lannister]] in Archmaester [[Ebrose]]'s ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire (Ebrose)|A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' (as featured in "[[The Iron Throne]]")</ref>

==Family==
{{Lannister tree}}

==Behind the scenes==
Janei is featured in the family tree of House Lannister in Archmaester [[Ebrose]]'s ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire (Ebrose)|A Song of Ice and Fire]]''.<ref name="finalebook" /> She is not mentioned or featured elsewhere in the series.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Janei is only listed in the appendices, not appearing in the story itself. She is the youngest child of Kevan and Dorna.
