'''Grimston'''{{Ref|THTDB201}} is a castle on the island of [[Greyshield]] in the [[Reach]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Grimston is the seat of House Grimm.

Grimston is taken by Ser Harras Harlaw during [[Euron Greyjoy]]'s invasion of the Reach. Euron then names him Lord of Greyshield.
