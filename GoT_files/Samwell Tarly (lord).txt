Lord '''Samwell Tarly''',{{Con|F&B}} also known as '''Savage Sam''', was the head of [[House Tarly]] during the reign of [[Aenys Targaryen]].

==Biography==
When the [[Dornishmen|Dornish]] [[Vulture King]] rebelled against King Aenys, Sam played a leading part in the [[Vulture Hunt]] to defeat him. He wielded the family's ancestral [[Valyrian steel]] sword [[Heartsbane]], which was said to run red from point to hilt with rebel blood.{{Ref|H&L616}}

==In the books==
In ''[[The World of Ice & Fire]]'', Savage Sam's story is the same.
