Ser '''Mors Umber''' was a [[First Ranger]] of the [[Night's Watch]] during the command of [[Osric Stark]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Mors was a candidate for the position of [[Lord Commander of the Night's Watch]]. When he lost the [[Elections|choosing]] to the ten-year-old Osric Stark, he accused that the [[King in the North|King of Winter]] had intervened to support his brother. He later relented and was appointed First Ranger by Osric.<ref>[[:File:5x2 Night's Watch Book.jpg]]</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Mors is mentioned in a book that [[Samwell Tarly]] is reading.{{Ref|GOT502}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, this character is not mentioned. Mors Umber, called Crowfood, is the uncle of [[Greatjon Umber]] and serves as [[castellan]] of [[Last Hearth]] with his younger brother Hother Whoresbane.
