Lady '''Amarylis Umber''', née '''Manderly''',{{Notes|Lineages}} was a member of [[House Manderly]]. She married Lord [[Hothor Umber]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Amarylis Umber married Lord [[Hothor Umber]]{{Ref|GOT104}} and bore him five sons: [[Harkon Umber|Harkon]], [[Mycah Umber|Mycah]], [[Lucan Umber|Lucan]], [[Gareth Umber|Gareth]] and [[Joseth Umber|Joseth]], and two daughters, [[Regan Karstark|Regan]] and [[Corin Umber|Corin]].{{Notes|Lineages}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
She is referenced in her son Hothor's entry in ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''.{{Ref|GOT104}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no such character.
