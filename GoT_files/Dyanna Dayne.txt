'''Dyanna Dayne'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} was the wife of [[Maekar Targaryen]].

==Biography==
Dyanna Dayne was the wife of [[Maekar Targaryen]], and the mother of his sons [[Daeron Targaryen (son of Maekar)|Daeron]], [[Aerion Targaryen|Aerion]], [[Aemon]], and [[Aegon Targaryen (son of Maekar)|Aegon]].{{Ref|GOT507}}

==Family==
{{Targaryen tree (A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms)}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Dyanna Dayne was the wife of Maekar Targaryen and the mother of Daeron, Aerion, Aemon, Daella, Aegon, and Rhae Targaryen. She died by [[209 AC]].
