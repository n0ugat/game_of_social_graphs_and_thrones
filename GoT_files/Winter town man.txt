A '''winter town man''' is a resident of the [[winter town]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
[[Brienne of Tarth]] and [[Podrick Payne]] arrive at an inn near Winterfell looking for [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]]. Their conversation is interrupted by the Winter town man who pledge loyalty to House Stark. Brienne convinces the man to get a message through to Sansa to light a candle in the highest tower of the Broken Tower if she needs help. The message is later carried through to Sansa's [[Old woman|maid]] who tells Sansa.{{Ref|GOT505}}
