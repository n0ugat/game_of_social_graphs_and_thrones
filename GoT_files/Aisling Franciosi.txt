|Last = "[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]"}}'''Aisling Franciosi''' (born 6 June 1993) is an Italian-Irish actress. She is known for playing the part of Katie Benedetto in the BBC Two crime drama television series ''The Fall''.

In 2014, she portrayed the role 'Apprentice' in the short film ''Ambition'', with would-be ''Game of Thrones'' co-star [[Aidan Gillen]] as the 'Master'.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]],'' she portrays the older [[Lyanna Stark]] (in flashbacks) in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]] and [[Game of Thrones: Season 7|seventh season]].
