The '''Rosby Road'''{{Ref|HOTD203}} is a road in the [[Crownlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Rosby road runs from the Iron Gate at [[King's Landing]] through [[Rosby]] to [[Duskendale]]. There, it connects to the Duskendale road running north to [[Maidenpool]].
