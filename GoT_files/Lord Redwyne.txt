'''Lord Redwyne'''{{Con|F&B}} is the head of [[House Redwyne]] during the reign of [[Aegon II Targaryen]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Lord Redwyne's relation to Lady [[Joselyn Redwyne]] is unknown.

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 1|''House of the Dragon'': Season 1]]===
The day after the death of King [[Viserys I Targaryen]], Lord Redwyne kneels before [[Otto Hightower]], swearing allegiance to [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon II]].{{Ref|HOTD109}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', Lord Redwyne was kin to [[House Hightower/House of the Dragon|House Hightower]] and a friend of Lyonel Hightower, the son of [[Ormund Hightower]].

Lord Redwyne declared for Aegon II at the start of the [[Dance of the Dragons]].
