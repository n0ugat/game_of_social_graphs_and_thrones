'''Simon Brindle''' is a Costume Armor Supervisor working on the ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' TV series since [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|Season 1]]. His work overlaps with the overall [[Costumes|costume designs]] created by [[Michele Clapton]].  

He has also worked on ''Alexander'' (2004), ''Clash of the Titans'' (2010), ''Prince of Persia'' (2010), ''Your Highness'' (2011), ''Immortals'' (2011), ''Wrath of the Titans'' (2012), ''Thor: The Dark World'' (2013), and ''Guardians of the Galaxy'' (2014).

According to IMDB, Simon Brindle will return to work on the prequel series ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.
