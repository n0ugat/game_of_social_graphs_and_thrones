'''Antlers'''{{Guideref|Map of Westeros}} is a castle in the [[Crownlands]], the region controlled directly by the [[Iron Throne]] in [[King's Landing]]. The castle is located north of King's Landing near the border with the [[Riverlands]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Antlers is the seat of House Buckwell. It is alternatively called "the Antlers."
