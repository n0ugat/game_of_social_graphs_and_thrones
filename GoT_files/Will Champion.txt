'''Will Champion''' (born 31 July 1978) is a British musician. Since 1997 he has been the drummer (and occasional guitarist, bassist, keyboard player and singer) in the band [[wikipedia:Coldplay|Coldplay]].

On 6 November 2012 it was announced that Champion would have a cameo appearance in the third season of ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. It was confirmed that his character would be a drummer<ref>[http://insidetv.ew.com/2012/11/05/game-of-thrones-coldplay/ Entertainment Weekly]</ref>. Champion plays a drummer, one of the musicians/assassins hired by [[Lothar Frey]] for the [[Red Wedding]].

Along with the rest of his band, Champion later did ''[[Game of Thrones: The Musical]]'' for Red Nose Day.
