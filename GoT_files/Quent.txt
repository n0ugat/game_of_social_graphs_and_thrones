'''Quent''' is a guard serving [[House Stark]] in the [[War of the Five Kings]].

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
He is warmly greeted by [[King]] [[Robb Stark]] as he tours the camp.{{Ref|GOT206}} He is present when [[Knighthood|Ser]] [[Jaime Lannister]] is recaptured and returned to the camp.{{Ref|GOT207}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Quent is a House Stark guardsman. He accompanies [[Bran Stark|Bran]] and Robb to the [[Wolfswood]] during Bran's first ride since being crippled. After the outbreak of the [[War of the Five Kings]], Quent marches south with the rest of the Northern force, and fights under the command of [[Hallis Mollen]], the Winterfell captain of the guards after [[Jory Cassel]]'s departure. Quent is one of the guardsmen ordered to accompany [[Eddard Stark]]'s remains to [[Winterfell]] after they are returned to Robb Stark as a gesture of goodwill by [[Tyrion Lannister]].
