'''Donal Royce''' is a member of [[House Royce]]. His status is uncertain.

==Biography==
Donal Royce is the firstborn son of his [[Donal Royce|father by the same name]] and [[Priscilla Royce]]. He is the third of his house to be named Donal, and has blue eyes and light brown hair. When he was 22 years old, he married [[Catherine Hornwood]], who bore him a stillborn son and six daughters.<ref name = "s1prop"/>

==Behind the scenes==
Donal Royce is mentioned in the section on House Royce in the Season 1 prop for ''[[The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. The section on House Royce never appears on-screen in the TV series, as its planned appearance, where [[Eddard Stark]] would have read from it, was cut.

==In the books==
Donal Royce is referenced only in the TV adaptation of ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' and has no corresponding counterpart in the books.
