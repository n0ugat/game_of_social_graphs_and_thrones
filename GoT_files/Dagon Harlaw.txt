'''Dagon Harlaw''' was a [[First Ranger]] of the [[Night's Watch]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Dagon was a member of [[House Harlaw]]. After the death of [[Lord Commander Stone]], he lost the [[Elections|choosing]] to [[Rodrik Velaryon]]. At Maester [[Gavin]]'s counsel, Rodrik appointed Dagon as [[First Ranger]]. He continued to serve as First Ranger under [[Jasper Bracken]].<ref>[[:File:5x2 Night's Watch Book.jpg]]</ref>

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 5|''Game of Thrones'': Season 5]]===
Dagon is mentioned in a book that [[Samwell Tarly]] is reading.{{Ref|GOT502}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no character named Dagon Harlaw.
