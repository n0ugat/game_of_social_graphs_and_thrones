'''Anthony Morris''' is an Irish actor who has appeared in a number of TV shows, including ''[[wikipedia:Rough Diamond (TV series)|Rough Diamond]]'' and ''[[wikipedia:Killinaskully|Killinaskully]]''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', Morris played the role of the [[Tickler]]. He was announced in the role on 6 September 2011.<ref>[http://grrm.livejournal.com/236666.html George R.R. Martin's blog]</ref> He joined the cast as a guest star in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|second season]].
