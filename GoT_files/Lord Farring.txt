'''Lord Farring''' is the head of [[House Farring]] during the [[Dance of the Dragons]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Lord Farring is a supporter of [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]].{{Ref|HOTD203}}

===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
It is mentioned at the [[Black Council]] that Lord Farring has reported the movements of [[Criston Cole]]'s army in the northern [[Crownlands]].{{Ref|HOTD203}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', House Farring is not mentioned. The only known Farrings are the ones that appear in the main ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels.
