'''Jerard''' is a shepherd who resides in the [[Crownlands]].

==Biography==
===[[House of the Dragon: Season 2|''House of the Dragon'': Season 2]]===
On the cusp of winter, a tenth of Jerard's flock is taken by the Crown. Jerard travels to the [[Red Keep]] to petition to King [[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon II]] for the return of his sheep. Counsel from [[Otto Hightower]] leads Aegon to refuse Jerard's request.{{Ref|HOTD201}}
