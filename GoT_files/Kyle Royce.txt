'''Kyle Royce'''{{Con|ASOIAF}} was a companion of [[Brandon Stark]] in the time leading up to [[Robert's Rebellion]].

==Biography==
Kyle accompanied Brandon Stark to [[King's Landing]], demanding justice for the alleged kidnapping of Brandon's sister [[Lyanna Stark]] by Prince [[Rhaegar Targaryen]]. In response, King [[Aerys II Targaryen]] had them arrested and executed.{{Ref|H&L111}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Kyle Royce's background and fate are the same.
