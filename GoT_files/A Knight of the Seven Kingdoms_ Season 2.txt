'''''A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms'': Season 2''' is the second season of ''[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]''. It is expected to consist of six episodes. It is based on ''[[The Sworn Sword]]'', the second of the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]] by [[George R.R. Martin]].

==Production==
On May 26, 2023, Francesca Orsi stated that ''A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms'', then titled ''The Hedge Knight'', was envisioned to run "ideally year-to-year and arcing out a three-season series, which maps out the three novellas that George wrote."{{Webref|name=THK |author=Nellie Andreeva |date=May 26, 2023 |url=https://deadline.com/2023/05/francesca-orsi-hbo-qa-succession-finale-the-last-of-us-house-of-the-dragon-white-lotus-euphoria-perry-mason-1235374588/ |title=HBO Drama Chief Francesca Orsi On ‘Succession’ Finale & WGA Strike; ‘The Last Of Us’, ‘House Of The Dragon’, ‘White Lotus’, ‘Euphoria’ Updates & More |website=Deadline |access-date=December 6, 2023 }}

On February 12, 2025, Orsi revealed that [[HBO]] are considering filming Seasons 2 and [[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms: Season 3|3]] back-to-back.<ref name="Orsi" />

On March 9, 2025, George R.R. Martin confirmed in an interview with ''Collider'' that Ira Parker was "working on the second season."<ref name="Collider" />

==In the books==
The season is adapted from ''The Sworn Sword'', the second of the [[Dunk & Egg novellas]].<ref name="Orsi" />
