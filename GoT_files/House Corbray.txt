'''House Corbray'''{{Ref|H&L402}} of [[Heart's Home]] is a vassal house which holds fealty to [[House Arryn]] of the [[Eyrie]].

==History==
The Corbrays are descended from [[Andals]], and are proud to hold one of the purest Andal bloodlines in Westeros. A [[Petyr Baelish's great-grandfather|Braavosi sellsword]] in service to House Corbray resettled in the Vale of Arryn. His great-grandson is Lord [[Petyr Baelish]].{{Ref|H&L316}}

==Known members==
*Ser [[Vance Corbray]], a [[Knighthood|knight]].

===Ancestors===
*King {[[Qyle Corbray]]}, a king during the Andal Invasion of the Vale.
*Ser {[[Gwayne Corbray]]}, knight of the [[Kingsguard]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Corbrays are an old but poor house. They hold the [[Valyrian steel]] blade known as [[Lady Forlorn]]. Their seat is [[Heart's Home]].

The heraldry of House Corbray in the books is three black ravens in flight, each holding a separate red heart, on a white field.

===Known members===
*Lord Lyonel Corbray, the Lord of Heart's Home. A childless man of forty-odd years.
*Ser Lyn Corbray, his eldest brother and heir. A quarrelsome knight, secretly on Littlefinger's payroll.
*Ser Lucas Corbray, his brother.
