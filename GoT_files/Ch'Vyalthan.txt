'''Ch'Vyalthan''' was an [[archmaester]] of the [[Citadel]] and the author of the books ''[[An History of the Great Sieges of Westeros]]'' and ''[[An History of the House of Lannister]]''.

==Biography==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 2|''Game of Thrones'': Season 2]]===
[[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] refers to his book ''[[An History of the Great Sieges of Westeros]]'' when preparing the defense of [[King's Landing]] from [[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]'s imminent [[Battle of the Blackwater|attack]]. [[Varys]] criticizes him as an uninspired writer. Tyrion and [[Bronn]] debate how to correctly pronounce his name, but Varys offhandedly reveals the proper form: "sh(uh)-VY-al-thun".{{Ref|GOT208}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 7|''Game of Thrones'': Season 7]]===
[[Archmaester]] [[Ebrose]] hands his book ''[[An History of the House of Lannister]]'' to [[Samwell Tarly|Sam]], claiming that it might be useful when writing ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire (Ebrose)|A Chronicle of the Wars Following the Death of King Robert I]]''. He criticizes Ch'Vyalthan for his writing, but praises him for his research.{{Ref|GOT702}}

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, there is no mention of this character.
