The '''Painted Dogs''' are one of the [[Hill tribes]] of the [[Mountains of the Moon]].

==History==
===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
The Painted Dogs are among the hill tribes that [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] recruits. They subsequently take part in the [[battle on the Green Fork]].<ref>"[[Baelor]]"</ref>

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, the Painted Dogs are briefly mentioned as one of the Vale mountain clans,{{Bookref|ACOK|49}} but no further details are given about them. They are not among the clans whom Tyrion encounters and recruits.
