'''Frank Doelger''' is a triple Emmy-winnning executive producer and director on the HBO series ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. He began as a producer in [[Game of Thrones: Season 1|Season 1]], and was promoted to executive producer in [[Game of Thrones: Season 2|Season 2]]. He has continued to work on the TV series through [[Game of Thrones: Season 5|Season 5]].

Like [[David Benioff]] and [[D.B. Weiss]], Doelger graduated from Trinity College (though much earlier than they did, in 1978).<ref>[https://vimeo.com/95966993 ]</ref>
