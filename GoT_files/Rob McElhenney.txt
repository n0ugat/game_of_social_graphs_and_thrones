'''Robert McElhenney''' (born April 14, 1977) is an actor, director, producer, and screenwriter. He is best known for his role as {{WPS|Mac (It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia)|Ronald "Mac" McDonald}} in the comedy series ''{{WPS|It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia}}'', which he also created and executive produces as well as writing and directing several episodes.

He has also appeared in television series such as ''{{WPS|Law & Order}}'', ''{{WPS|ER (TV series)|ER}}'', ''{{WPS|Lost (TV series)|Lost}}'', ''{{WPS|The Mindy Project}}'' and ''{{WPS|Fargo (season 3)|Fargo}}''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he made a cameo appearance as an [[Ironborn]] soldier in "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]".<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DkdbetJTZGA Game of Thrones | Season 8 Episode 1 | Game Revealed (HBO)]</ref>
