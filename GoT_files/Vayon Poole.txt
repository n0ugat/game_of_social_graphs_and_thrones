'''Vayon Poole''' served [[House Stark]] as the [[Steward]] of [[Winterfell]].

==Biography==
===Background===
Vayon Poole serves House Stark as the Steward of Winterfell. He is the father of five daughters, one of whom, [[Jeyne Poole]], is [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]]'s best friend.{{Ref|GOT104}}

===[[Game of Thrones: Season 1|''Game of Thrones'': Season 1]]===
When [[Luwin]] approaches [[Catelyn Stark|Catelyn]] to talk about the costs of the royal visit to Winterfell and the need to replace the members of the household that accompanied [[Eddard Stark|Eddard]] to [[King's Landing]], she, grieving for [[Bran Stark|Bran]], tells Luwin to have Poole see to the task. Luwin notes that Poole also went south with Lord Stark.{{Ref|GOT102}}

Poole is later killed by [[House Lannister|Lannister]] men during the [[Massacre in the Red Keep|purge of the Stark household]] ordered by King [[Joffrey Baratheon|Joffrey]] after Eddard tries to remove him from the [[Iron Throne|throne]]. He is attacked and killed while he and his men are packing for an evacuation Eddard has ordered in anticipation of his move against Joffrey.{{Ref|GOT108}}
