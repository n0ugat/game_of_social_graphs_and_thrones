'''Rebecca Calder''' is an English actress. She has appeared in the films ''{{WPS|King Arthur: Legend of the Sword}}'' and ''{{WPS|Wrath of Man}}'' and the television series ''{{WPS|Primeval (TV series)|Primeval}}'', ''{{WPS|The Bill}}'', ''{{WPS|Doctors (2000 TV series)|Doctors}}'', ''{{WPS|Silent Witness}}'' and ''{{WPS|I May Destroy You}}''.

Calder plays a Harrenhal servant and a Dragonkeeper Elder in the [[House of the Dragon: Season 2|second season]] of ''[[House of the Dragon]]''.
