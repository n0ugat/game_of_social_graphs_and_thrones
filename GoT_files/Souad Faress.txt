'''Souad Adel Faress''' (born March 25, 1948) is an Ireland-based Ghanian actress. She is best known for her radio role in ''The Archers'', and the films ''My Beautiful Laundrette'', ''Bhaji on the Beach'' and ''Sixth Happiness''.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', she portrays the [[High Priestess (Oathbreaker)|High Priestess]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 6|sixth season]].<ref>[http://narrowroad.co.uk/news/souad-faress--game-of-thrones The Narrow Road — Souad Faress in Game of Thrones]</ref>
