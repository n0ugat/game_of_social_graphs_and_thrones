'''House Targaryen'''{{Ref|GOT101}}{{Note|[[High Valyrian]]: ''Targārio Lentrot''}} of [[King's Landing]], known poetically as the '''House of the Dragon''',{{Ref|HOTD101}} is the royal [[Noble house|house]] of the [[Seven Kingdoms]] of [[Westeros]]. [[2 BC|Two centuries]] ago, House Targaryen [[Aegon's Conquest|conquered and unified]] the realm under the leadership of [[Aegon I Targaryen|Aegon the Conqueror]] and his sister-queens [[Visenya Targaryen|Visenya]] and [[Rhaenys Targaryen (sister of Aegon I)|Rhaenys]].

The Targaryens are of pureblood [[Valyrians|Valyrian]] descent. Their [[Heraldry|sigil]] is a three-headed red [[dragon]] on a black field, and their [[House words|words]] are "Fire and Blood."{{Note|name=Words}}
