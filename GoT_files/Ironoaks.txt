'''Ironoaks'''{{Ref|C&R07}} is a castle in the [[Vale of Arryn]]. The castle is located east of the Eyrie.<ref>"[[House Arryn, Kings of the Vale]]"</ref> It is the seat of [[House Waynwood]]. House Waynwood are one of the major vassals that hold fealty to [[House Arryn]] of the [[Eyrie]].

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Ironoaks, also known as Ironoaks Castle, is the seat of [[House Waynwood]] in the Vale of Arryn. It is east from the Eyrie and west of Old Anchor and is situated by a lake.
