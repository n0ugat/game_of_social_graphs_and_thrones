}}'''Paul Mackenzie Crook''' (born 29 September 1971) is a British actor. He is best known for playing the role of Gareth on the original version of ''[[wikipedia:The Office (UK TV series)|The Office]]'' and for his role as Ragetti in the first three ''[[wikipedia:Pirates of the Caribbean|Pirates of the Caribbean]]'' movies (which have also featured [[Jonathan Pryce]], [[Ian McShane]], [[DeObia Oparei]] and [[Anton Lesser]]).

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'' Crook played the role of [[Orell]], debuting in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|third season]]. This was announced at the San Diego Comic-Con on 13 July 2012.<ref>[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rpSDSgChsaI HBO's YouTube channel]</ref>
