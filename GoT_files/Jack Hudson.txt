|imdb = https://www.imdb.com/name/nm9297569/}}'''Jack Hudson''' is an Irish actor.

In ''[[Game of Thrones]]'', he portrayed a [[wight]] in the [[Game of Thrones: Season 8|eighth season]] episode "[[Winterfell (Game of Thrones)|Winterfell]]".<ref>https://www.imdb.com/title/tt5924366/?ref_=ttep_ep1</ref>

== Personal life ==
Hudson was born in Dublin, Ireland. He studied acting at the Gaiety School of Acting. As an actor, he is best known for ''Vivarium'' and ''The Desk''. He wrote and directed a drama short called ''Absence''. He has appeared as a contestant on ''Fx Makeup Academy Fashion Show'' and ''Mr Ireland'' in 2017 and 2018 respectively.
