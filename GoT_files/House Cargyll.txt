'''House Cargyll'''{{Guideref|Arryk Cargyll}}{{Note|Arryk and Erryk's surname is Cargyll.}} is a vassal house from the [[Crownlands]].

==Known members==
*Ser {[[Arryk Cargyll]]}, a [[Knighthood|knight]] of the [[Kingsguard]] of [[Viserys I Targaryen]] and [[Aegon II Targaryen]]. Killed by Erryk in a [[duel on Dragonstone]].
*Ser {[[Erryk Cargyll]]}, Arryk's twin brother, also a knight of the Kingsguard of Viserys I Targaryen and [[Rhaenyra Targaryen]]. Killed himself shortly after killing Arryk.

==In the books==
In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, House Cargyll is extinct. However, their sigil is mentioned in ''The Hedge Knight'', as a member of the house participates in the [[Ashford]] Tourney.
