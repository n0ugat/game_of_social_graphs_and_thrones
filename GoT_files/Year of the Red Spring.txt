The '''Year of the Red Spring'''{{Con|TWOI&F}} is a name that describes the year [[126 AC]].{{Dateref|The Princess and the Queen}}{{Dateref|Driftmark}}

==In the books==
In ''[[Fire & Blood]]'', the year 120 AC is remembered by this name. It was so-named for the high amount of tragic deaths and accidents that occurred within that year:
*A fire at [[Harrenhal]], claiming the lives of [[Lyonel Strong|Lyonel]] and [[Harwin Strong]].
*[[Laena Velaryon]]'s death in childbirth.
*The death of Laena's son, an hour after her.
*[[Aemond Targaryen]]'s confrontation with his nephews [[Jacaerys Velaryon|Jacaerys]], [[Lucerys Velaryon|Lucerys]], and [[Joffrey Velaryon]], in which he lost an eye.
*[[Laenor Velaryon]]'s fatal duel with [[Qarl Correy]].
